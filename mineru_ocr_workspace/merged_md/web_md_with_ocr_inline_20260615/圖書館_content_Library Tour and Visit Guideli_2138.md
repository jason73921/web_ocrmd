- **文章簡介**: Eligible Participants


Guests hosted by the university"s departments.

Library professionals and faculty or student groups from library and information science-related programs.

Student groups led by high school or vocational school teachers.


Service Hours


Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 1:30 PM – 4:30 PM.

Closed on weekends, national holidays, and during university examination periods. Please refer to library announcements for updates.


Application Process


To provide better service, please submit your application at least one week before the visit to schedule the tour and related arrangements.

External organizations must attach an official letter from their institution when applying.


Tour Details


The tour includes a guided walkthrough and a presentation covering the library’s facilities and services.

A minimum of five participants is required per tour, with a maximum limit of 30 people per session.

Each tour lasts approximately 20–30 minutes, with one session scheduled in the morning and one in the afternoon.

For more detailed information about the Library Tour, please contact the University Library at ispc@saturn.yzu.edu.tw
