- **文章簡介**: The Global Affairs Office of Yuan Ze University invited over one hundred foreign students from different universities like National Taiwan University and National Taiwan University of Science and Technology to gather at Yuan Ze University. YZU took the famous movie, The Avengers, as the theme of event, allowing students to incorporate the movie plot and main character of Marvel heroes in the activities. The title is names as The And Game that is homophonic to The End Game, hoping to convey the concept of connection and continuation. In addition to international students’ getting to know each other through games and solving each other"s difficulties to complete tasks, YZU expected to achieve a win-win situation through the integration of different nationalities.
