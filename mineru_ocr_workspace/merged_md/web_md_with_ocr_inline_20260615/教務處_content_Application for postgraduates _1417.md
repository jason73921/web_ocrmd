- **文章簡介**: Notice: Procedures for Graduate Students Applying for a Change of Major in the 1st Semester of the 114th Academic Year

Date: November 21, 2025

Specifications:

I. Application period: From November 24, 2025 (Monday) to December 3, 2025 (Wednesday).

II. Applicable objects:

1. Students who have completed one semester of study;

2. Students with a Grade Point Average of over seventy;

3. Students who meet the requirements set forth by the accepting departments.

III. Application procedures:

1. Complete the application form (submit along with their transcripts of the previous semesters and relevant documents) → approved by the current department (the advising professor, department chair and college dean) → submit to Registration Division of Office of Academic Affairs for preliminary examination.

2. Each department may establish its own criteria for reviewing applications for a change of major. The request shall be discussed and reviewed by the accepting departments. The review results shall be approved by the accepting department chair and college dean, then a list of accepted applicants will be published upon approval of the Provost.

IV. Notifications:


Each student can only apply one time and transfer to one department. Students granted approval to change their majors shall not file for a change in or withdrawal of their application.

Students who are still on their leave period shall not file an application.

Those students, who have been admitted by means of entrance exam or screening selection, or who are On-job Students of Master Program, shall not apply for a change of major between these programs.

Graduate students applying to change a major within the division of the same department shall process the application in accordance with “Yuan Ze University Regulations for Change of Major for Graduate Students of Master/Doctoral Programs”.

Masters’ or doctoral students changing a major (graduate institute, degree program), the time period for study in both departments (graduate institute, degree program) shall be counted.

Applications from the Mainland China Student are limited to the Mainland Chinese student enrollment quota of the accepting department (graduate institute) of the current academic year.


V. Please see the following link for further information about change of major:

YZU → Administration → Office of Academic Affairs →Student Enrollment Matters → Application for postgraduates Change of Department/Institute

# Yuan Ze University Regulations for Change of Major for Graduate Students of Master/Doctoral Programs

Passed by the 7th Academic Affairs Meeting of the 2019 School Year, June 17, 2020   
Approved for future reference by Ministry of Education in Letter Tai-Kao-2-Tzu No. 1090105783, July 29, 2020   
Approved for future reference by Ministry of Education in Letter Tai-Kao-2-Tzu No. 1090122371, August 21, 2020

Article 1 These regulations are provided for processing matters relating to graduate students’ applications for a change of majors, departments, tracks or programs of study (hereafter, ‘a change of major’). These regulations are provided pursuant to Paragraph 1of Article 59 of “YZU Study Regulations”.

Article 2 Graduate students who are not interested in the current major to which they are enrolled may apply for a change of major. The application and admission dates each year accord with the University’s school calendar.

Article 3 Graduate students who meet all of the following criteria may apply for a change of major:

1. Students who have completed one semester of study;

2. Students with a Grade Point Average (GPA) of over seventy;

3. Students who meet the requirements set forth by the accepting departments.

Article 4 Application for a change of major shall be processed with the following procedures:

1. Graduate students who wish to change their majors shall complete the application form, and then submit along with their transcripts of the previous semesters and relevant documents to Registration Division of Office of Academic Affairs for preliminary examination by the deadline announced. Applications to change the current major shall be approved by the advising professor, the department chair and college dean.

2. Each department may establish its own criteria for reviewing applications for a change of major. The request shall be discussed and reviewed by the accepting departments. The review results shall be approved by the accepting department chair and college dean, submitted to Registration Division of Office of Academic Affairs, and then published upon approval of the Provost (Dean of Office of Academic Affairs).

Article 5 Each student can only apply one time. Students granted approval to change their majors shall not file for a change in or withdrawal of their application. They must fulfill the graduation requirements set forth by the accepting departments and meet the requirements for the length of time in which the study must be completed pursuant to “YZU Study Regulations”. The maximum length of study shall not be extended following a change of major. Disapproved applicants may continue to register in their current departments.

Article 6 Credits obtained from courses taken at the student’s previous major may be waived; students shall file an application for credit exemption in accordance with “Yuan Ze University Regulations for Credit Exemption” by the date stipulated in the University’s school calendar.

Article 7 Inter-division transfers within the same department/institute of a graduate school shall be processed in accordance with these foregoing regulations.

Article 8 Matters not specified herein shall be governed by relevant provisions of the Ministry of Education and the University.

Article 9 These regulations will be adopted by the Academic Affairs Meeting before being reported to the Ministry of Education for ratification and implementation, as shall amendments when they are made.
