The office of library and information service hold 2018 YZU Innovative Apps Challenge to stimulate students to put into research & creative thinking on APP, and the winner is the team “WEGO-YZU” grouped by the students of Departments of Information Communication and Computer Science & Engineering.

The team member of WEGO-YZU are Lin Zi Heng, Lee Yong Ying, Chang Chen-yo, Wu Jin-ze and Huang Yi-Xuan, respectively. WEGO is a multifunctional emergency APP including several technologies, like Fire Base, WebRTC, gyroscope and Google Remark.
