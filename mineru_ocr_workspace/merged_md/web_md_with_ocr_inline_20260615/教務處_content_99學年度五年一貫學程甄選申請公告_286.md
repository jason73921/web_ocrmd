中華民國一00年一月十二日

                                                       (99)智大教內第0027號

 

主旨：99學年度元智大學學生修讀學碩士五年一貫學程(預備研究生)甄選申請公告

說明：相關申請事項如下：

·             98學年第一學期 99.1.13 98-3教務會議決議，申請日改為開學日後二週內提出申請。 

·             元智大學學生修讀學、碩士五年一貫學程辦法已放於教務處網頁。

·             大學部三年級學生、二年制在職專班生三年級及四年級學生，合於各系所(組)學位學程甄選標準者皆可提出申請。

·             每位學生只限申請一系所（組），否則一經發現取消其報名或錄取資格。

·             各系所提供之甄選名額得不足額錄取。

·             99學年度申請期限為100年3月11日前。

·             預計100年3月24日放榜為原則。

·             100學年度起更名者為：先進能源研究所(100年更名為先進能源碩士學位學程)、管理學院管理科學碩士班(100年更名為管理學院商學碩士班)

·             五年一貫申請表請自行下載使用。

·             相關規定查詢路徑如下：

元智首頁/行政單位/教務處/學生專區/學籍異動/五年一貫學程申請http://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/content/view/144/277/

Announcement

Office of Academic Affairs

 

Subject：Apply fro 2010 Academic Year Yuan Ze University students taking the five-year Bachelor/Master program selection.



              Content：

            ·             Based on the 98-3 Academic Affairs Meeting Resolution on January 13th, 2010, the date of application has been fixed to within two weeks; individuals must submit their application within two weeks from the day of school opens. 

            ·             Yuan Ze University Students Taking the Five-Year Bachelor/Master Program Rules and Regulations can be found on the Office of Academic Affairs website. 

            ·             Junior undergraduate students and two-year executive program students qualified to the criteria of each department are entitled to apply.

            ·             Each student must apply for one department (group) only or may risk losing his/her qualification of admission or application.

            ·             Actual number of persons admitted can be fewer than the number offered in each department.

            ·             Deadline for application: March 11th, 2011

            ·             List of successful candidates is supposed to be released on March 24th.

            ·             Download the five-year program application form

            ·             Relevant regulations can be found via：
