You will also gain practical experience to build your capabilities. This is not just a classroom; it"s your first step toward interdisciplinary exploration!

Introduction to the Semiconductor Industry and Manufacturing Processes

Curious about the semiconductor industry but don"t know where to start?

This course takes you through the entire supply chain, from upstream materials to downstream applications! Learn about IC design, wafer manufacturing, packaging, and testing. Grasp the development context of the global and Taiwanese semiconductor industries, and learn to perform basic analysis from trends, policies, to technological evolution. 

👉 Want to enter the semiconductor field? This is your first stop!

3D Computer Simulation Modeling and Decision Analysis

Want to see how a factory "moves"?

In this course, you will learn to use 3D simulation software to construct production and supply chain processes. You will practically operate and identify process bottlenecks to make more effective decisions. Everything from modeling, simulation, to analysis is done hands-on! 

👉 Suitable for those who love visualization, hands-on work, and problem-solving!

Exploring Intelligent Robots: Design, Simulation, and Practice

Want to build your own small robot?

This course introduces you to smart robotics from scratch. From basic concepts, applications in daily life, to perception and motion control, you will gradually build your own 3D robot model. Finally, you will complete a small practical project to experience the possibilities of interdisciplinary innovation! 

👉 A must-take for those who enjoy hands-on work and creative challenges!

 

Exploration of Semiconductor Wonderland

How close is the semiconductor to us?

This course starts with technology in daily life, leading you to understand the principles behind semiconductors. Hands-on experiments turn abstract concepts into fun experiences, guiding you into the world of high-tech in the most relaxed way. 

👉 Science popularization × Experiments × Fun, to explore the magic of chips!

 

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence in Robotics

Want to know how AI makes robots smart?

This course introduces the world of intelligent robotics in a beginner-friendly way: from "How to perceive," "How to learn," to "How to act". Through demonstrations, real-life examples, and discussions, you will understand how robots influence modern life.

👉 You can easily get started, regardless of your engineering background!

Exploring International Business Management

You don"t need a business background to understand global commercial operations!

This course introduces you to how multinational corporations operate in a globalized environment in the most accessible way. From cultural differences and global strategies to actual business cases, it helps you build an international perspective. 

👉 Suitable for anyone who wants to understand how the world is connected by business!

Introduction to Investment and Financial Planning   

Want to start managing your finances but don"t know where to begin?

This course is tailored for zero-basis students. It introduces common investment tools, risks, and financial concepts, helping you build financial literacy and apply it to your life. 

👉 Want to start getting rich? Start with this course to avoid pitfalls!

AI Easy Japanese Learning

Interested in learning languages easily with AI?

This course covers the 50-sound table (Go-jūon), basic sentence patterns, and daily conversations. It integrates tools like AI voice recognition and image generation, allowing you to learn Japanese in the most relaxed, fun, and efficient way, while also getting to know Japanese culture.

👉 Language × Technology, making Japanese learning super fun!

 

AI Applications and Practices in Healthcare

How is AI changing the healthcare landscape?

This course covers everything from vital signs measurement, anatomy and physiology, CPR, to the application of the Internet of Things (IoT) in health care. Finally, you will personally experience the real-world scenario of "Technology × Caregiving" in an immersive nursing laboratory. 

👉Want to see what the future of healthcare looks like? Come here to experience it!

Cybersecurity Awareness Campaign

Do you know you face cybersecurity risks every day? 

Suspicious links, phishing emails, fake websites, weak passwords—all of these can lead to data breaches. This course teaches you to identify dangers and strengthen your information protection capabilities in the most practical way, ensuring you are no longer a hacker"s target. 

👉 Daily life × Practical × Essential cybersecurity self-defense skills!

 

Net-zero Planning & Carbon Management

How is net-zero related to you?

This course helps you understand the concept of net-zero, the principle of carbon sinks, and natural carbon removal methods. You will also conduct actual tree carbon sink measurements on campus and use GIS tools for spatial analysis. Sustainability will no longer be just a slogan but an action you can take.

👉 Sustainability × Practice × Technical skills, all in one go!

 

I Ching Divination: Understanding Life through Ancient Wisdom 

Want to know how ancient wisdom views modern life? 

This course is centered around the I-Ching (Book of Changes). Combined with practical divination and real-life cases, it guides you to understand the thinking of "change and ease" and contemplate its application in psychology, management, technology, and design. 

👉 A new, interdisciplinary way to understand life and decision-making!
