- **文章簡介**: Dept. of Art and Design presents the 7th Graduate Exhibition, starting from May 15 to 19 at the Library, the Seventh Building and the student activity center. Graduates display what they have learned for the past four years and show 33 works including paintings, decoration arts, video works, product design and community artworks.

For years, Art and Design introduce students art accomplishments to the public; it is the first time the department hosts the graduate exhibition on campus, says Professor Chen Kuan-Hua (陳冠華). “We hope to aggressively reward people in YZU with our artworks and engage in the campus culture,” he notes. After the university presentation, the graduate exhibition will be shown at Songshan Cultural and Creative Park (June 3-6).
