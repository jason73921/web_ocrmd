Since the Art and Design Department established, the department has been cooperating with community residents in the way of "artistic intervention" to jointly create new value for the community and reconnect the relationship between people. This year, the Wetland Art Festival worked with the residents in Banqiao District to make cloth slippers in biological colors of the wetland, reed corsages and wishing bamboos.
