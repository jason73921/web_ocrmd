I am a new teacher, but my account is locked. Why?

If you previously used Turnitin as a student and had not logged in for over six months, your account may have been automatically locked when upgraded to a teacher account.




The password reset link validity period has been shortened: The validity period for the password reset link has been reduced from 24 hours to 1 hour. Please be sure to change your password within the time limit.

To ensure Quick Submit remains a reliable tool for its intended purpose of spot checking one-off papers, on December 18th, the maximum number of submissions will be reduced from 100 to 25 per instructor within a 24-hour period.
