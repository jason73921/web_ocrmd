Subject: South Hua University Indigenous Cultural Week - "Shine Bright, Embrace Love" Series of Events - Join Us!

Dear Campus Community,

The South Hua University Indigenous Resource Center cordially invites you to participate in our upcoming "Shine Bright, Embrace Love" Indigenous Cultural Week series of events. This event aims to foster emotional connections among our Indigenous students, strengthen the foundation of a friendly campus environment, and promote understanding and respect for different ethnic groups. We welcome your active participation in making this event a meaningful and enjoyable experience for all!

Here are the event details:

Event Dates:




Dates: October 2nd (Monday), October 3rd (Tuesday), October 4th (Wednesday), 112th Year of the Republic of China





Event Location:


Address: South Hua University, No. 55, Section 1, Nan Hua Road, Chiayi County





Contact Information: (1) Contact Person: Ms. Yung-Hsin Mai, South Hua University Indigenous Student Resource Center (2) Phone Number: 05-2721001 ext. 1282 (3) Email: mys99@nhu.edu.tw




Special Reminder: Please ensure that you bring your campus access pass, and on the event day, kindly present your temporary access pass for parking on campus.




We look forward to welcoming you to Indigenous Cultural Week and sharing, learning, and building friendships together. Your participation is essential in creating a campus rich in cultural diversity!

Thank you once again for your participation, and we eagerly anticipate your presence.

Warm regards, South Hua University Indigenous Resource Center
