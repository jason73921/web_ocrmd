- **文章全文**: hosted by the Taoyuan City Government’s Department of Youth Affairs and organized by Yuan Ze University. The festival—aimed at promoting exchange between international and local students and building a student-friendly international campus—drew about 300 participants and featured food from around 15 countries, 10 cultural booths, sports friendlies (badminton, pickleball, bubble/space soccer), and performances and cultural-experience zones showcasing local and indigenous Taiwanese arts. The event’s theme centered on “sports × culture × exchange,” highlighting cross-cultural dialogue and Taoyuan’s effort to become a more international, youth-friendly city.
