Scholarship for Low-Income Students from the Educational Production Fund in the 112-2 Academic Semester.
The scholarship is limited to citizens of the Republic of China (Taiwan) who can provide proof of low-income household status.


說明：


附上實施要點及申請書。

申請者請備妥申請書、歷年成績單，於113/3/4（一）前送至生輔組（8203室）辦理，逾期不予受理。

具低收入戶資格之大學部及學業成績及格者（依實施要點規定），皆能申請本助學金5千元。
