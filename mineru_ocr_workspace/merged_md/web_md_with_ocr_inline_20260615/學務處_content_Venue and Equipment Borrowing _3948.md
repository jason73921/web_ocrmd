※ Notes for Venue and Equipment Borrowing:


 If you need to borrow a venue or equipment, it is mandatory to submit an activity request through the portal system and receive approval before proceeding with the borrowing.

 For borrowing from the Extracurricular Activities Section, there is no need to print a physical application form. However, for borrowing from other divisions, the application form with the Extracurricular Activities Section"s endorsement should be submitted to the borrowing unit.

 Please allow sufficient time for the portal activity request to be processed and approved. It is recommended to complete the application procedure at least 10 days before the event (please ensure that the club advisor is notified to complete the approval process). Last-minute or on-the-day requests for venue and equipment borrowing will not be accepted. (Failure to complete the application process may result in the inability to borrow the venue or equipment, and the club will be responsible for any consequences.)

 Before the event, please bring your identification documents and visit the Extracurricular Activities Section counter during the designated working hours to collect the venue key or equipment. (Working hours for counter service are Monday to Friday: 11-13 / 13-15 / 16-18, three time slots.)

 The venue key and equipment must be returned no later than 1:00 PM on the next working day following the event. This allows the subsequent borrowing units to use them. Failure to return the items within the specified timeframe may result in the suspension of borrowing privileges or appropriate disciplinary action, depending on the circumstances.
