- **文章簡介**: Indonesian Alumni Dinner Brings YZU Community Together

Professor Yun-Chia Liang, Chief of the Global Affairs Office at Yuan Ze University; Assistant Professor Panca Jodiawan, currently teaching at YZU; and Billy, a PhD student, visited Indonesia and hosted the Indonesian Alumni Dinner as part of their trip.

The event provided a meaningful opportunity to reconnect with alumni, including Arya Ondrio, President of the Indonesian Alumni Association, and Twin Yoshua Rahajo, Vice President, further strengthening ties within the global YZU community.

Thank you to all alumni who participated and continue to support the YZU community abroad.
