## 疫，齐共好

以為穩定的疫情突然再次升溫，起伏不定的生活，充滿不確定的規劃，讓生活變得不便.

確診了，害怕同學怎麼看自己、擔心影響身旁朋友家人。

隔離出來面對同學感到緊張，  
大家相處上好像變得  
有些疏離…

## 照顧 自己

辨識及整理自身情緒。即使隔離仍找尋喜歡/興趣/可控之事。

規律生活作息、 課程、均衡飲食睡眠。

## 關照彼此

適時與人連結，傾訴感受，同理關懷代替抱怨。

不獵巫、不責怪，維持日常訊息聯繫，讓彼此不孤單。

## 友善 共存

面對變動，彈性接 納，適度瞭解疫情資 訊。

瞭解住家附近医院、藥局、快篩站、親友協助，必要時求助專業。

若身心狀態不佳持續兩週以上，可求助醫師或聯繫本校學務處諮商與就業輔導組系心理師。

## Stay with Covid-19, Stay in Peace.

The pandemic is growing again. The uncertainties make life inconvenient.

When I am confirmed, I'm worry about that may affect my friends and family and how my classmates think of me.

After the quarantine, I get nervous when I faced my classmates, and feel everyone alienated from each other.

## Take Care of Yourself

Identify your emotions and organize them.

Even in quarantine, find likes, interests, and controllable things.

Regular daily routine, classes, balanced diet and sleeping.

## Take Care of Others

Instead of complaining and blaming, talk with others, discuss feelings, and try to put yourself in somebody élse's shoes.

You are not alone. Don't start witch-hunts, don't blameothers, and maintain daily contact with soméone you can trust.

## Friendly Coexistence

Adopting a flexible approach to accepting epidemic information and understanding it appropriately.

Know where is the hospitals, pharmacies, quick screening stations, and classmates/friends are located near your home, and seek professional help if necessary.
