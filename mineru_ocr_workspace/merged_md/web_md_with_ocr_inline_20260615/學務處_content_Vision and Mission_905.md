- **文章全文**: Our Vision

建構核心價值與特色校園文化，營造友善校園並促進學生自我實現，培養具良好品德的社會公民，提升學務與輔導工作品質與績效。

To build core values and a distinctive campus culture, create a friendly campus environment, promote students" self-fulfillment, cultivate socially responsible citizens with good character, and enhance the quality and effectiveness of student affairs and counseling work.



Our Mission

創造充滿生活智慧與溫暖互助的成長環境，

以激發潛能的豐沛挑戰，

來蘊育具備多元智能、美感創意、健康快樂、社會關懷的世界公民與活力領袖。

To create a growth environment that is filled with wisdom and warmth, where mutual assistance thrives,

To provide abundant challenges that stimulate potential,

In order to cultivate world citizens and dynamic leaders who possess multiple intelligences, aesthetic creativity, health and happiness, and social concern.

Mission

Headquarters of OSA

以妥適傳達上級交辦事項為經，以充分統整各組提報資訊為緯，促進組織運作順暢與成長學習。

To effectively convey tasks assigned by superiors and comprehensively integrate information provided by various units, in order to promote smooth organizational operation and growth through learning.



Extracurricular Activities Section

激發社團的熱情與活力，鼓勵積極服務社會，以培育多元智能、團隊合作與樂於永續創新的領袖人才。

Igniting the passion and vitality of student clubs, encouraging active community service, and fostering leaders with multiple intelligences, teamwork, and a commitment to sustainable innovation.



Counseling & Career Development Section

提供學生心靈港灣與成長資源；安適心靈，激發熱忱以成就職涯發展，進而邁向適才適所精彩人生。

Providing students with a haven for their mental well-being and growth resources; nurturing a healthy mind, igniting passion for career development, and embarking on a fulfilling life in suitable positions.



Life Guidance Section

秉持熱忱服務精神，提供適切協助。

Upholding a spirit of enthusiastic service and providing appropriate assistance.



Sanitary & Health Care Section

邁向健康促進學校、提升自主健康管理知能、營造健康照護環境。

Advancing the promotion of health in the school, enhancing self-management capabilities for health, and creating a healthy care environment.



Student Housion Service Section
