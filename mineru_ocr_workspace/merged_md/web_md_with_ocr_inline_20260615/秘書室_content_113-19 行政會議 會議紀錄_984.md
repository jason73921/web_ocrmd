113-19 行政會議摘要 Executive Summary of Minutes

一、通過本校「教師授課時數及抵免辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations for faculty’s teaching hours and deduction” was approved.

二、通過本校「校園霸凌防制實施辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “campus bullying prevention implementation regulations” was approved.

三、通過本校「產學研鏈結中心設置暨管理辦法」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “YZU regulations for the establishment and management of the industry-academia-research collaboration center” was approved.

四、通過本校「大陸地區碩博生獎助學金設置辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations of scholarships for students from mainland China” was approved.

五、備查本校教職員安全衛生在職教育訓練成效報告案 The report of “effectiveness of occupational safety and health on-the-job training for faculty and staff” was approved.
