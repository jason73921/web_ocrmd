Closure Dates: 2025/01/12 22:00 – 2025/01/14 18:00

Description:

1. A waterproofing project will be carried out in the south area bathrooms on the 7F to 9F of Female Dorm 1. To facilitate the construction, the bathroom floors need to be completely dried in advance, necessitating a temporary closure.

2. 2025/1/12(Sun.)22:00 - 1/14(Tue.)18:00

　Shower areas in south area on the 8-9F will be closed. Please use north area on the same floor or bathrooms on other floors.

3. 2025/1/13(Mon.) 8:00 - 18:00

　Shower areas in south area on the 7F will be closed. Please use bathrooms on other floors.

4. The bathrooms will be reopened for use immediately after the maintenance is completed.

5. Please note that noise may occur during the construction period.

6. We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your cooperation.
