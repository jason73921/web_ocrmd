. ・°°🌱2023.05.04🌿・°°・.

Today"s coffee gathering, I am honored to invite Director Pan Junnan of the Public Affairs and Alumni Service Center to talk with us and discuss the school"s new plans and policies for alumni services in the future.

When talking about content, the most important core is still cohesion and connection. Therefore, in terms of activities, I always hope to connect with school activities. In addition to the relationship with alumni, it also strengthens the connection with current students so that the two can be combined without disconnection. And this project also hopes that with the help of the departmental alumni associations of each department, the members of each department will be gathered together, so that the group of the General Assembly can be more complete. In addition, for the services and benefits of alumni, the General Assembly and the Alumni Center are also working hard to fight for alumni, hoping to provide alumni with help.

The growth of a strong tree and a dense forest must have the participation of sunshine, breeze, rain, and birds to scatter seeds for it. Alumni are just like these nurturing elements, they are an indispensable and important existence of the association.

Dear alumni, if you have time, you may wish to come back to the Tianhe Bookstore for a sit-down, experience the relaxed and comfortable atmosphere of the bookstore together, sit down and drink a cup of coffee, exchange each other"s current situation, and also share suggestions, so that the Yuan Zhi Alumni Association can learn from it. Be strong and perfect! 🍀
