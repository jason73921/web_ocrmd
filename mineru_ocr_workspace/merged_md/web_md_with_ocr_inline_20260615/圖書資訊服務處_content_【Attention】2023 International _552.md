- **文章全文**: The 2023 International Conference on Security and Information Technologies with AI, Internet Computing and Big-data Applications (SITAIBA’23 for short) will be held in Chihlee University of Technology, New Taipei City, Taiwan, December 7-9, 2023. SITAIBA’23 will provide a platform for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals from all over the world to present their research results and development activities in Security and Information Technologies with AI, Internet Computing and Big-data Applications. It aims at strengthening the international academic cooperation and communications, and exchanging research ideas. All accepted regular papers (at least 10-page) in SITAIBA’23 are going to be published in Springer Nature (APPROVAL) by the series “Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies” (SIST) which is indexed by EI Compendex, Furthermore, extended versions of selected papers will be recommended in the following SCI-indexed Journals and EI-indexed Journals :
