- **文章全文**: But most of the time we get too carried away with this busy world, and it makes it hard to even just stop and rest. Taking a step back for a short moment to restart our brain and recharge our energy by traveling really is worth it.

Solo-traveling is not my cup of tea but then I realized that once you take a step out there, you’ll be amazed at how amazing the places you visited and the people you met along the journey! It was such a heartwarming feeling to be able to explore new places around Tainan, try different kinds of foods, and get to know a lot of amazing people.
