The warm sunlight shines slantingly into the bookstore, creating a gentle and comfortable atmosphere. This was my initial feeling when I entered the bookstore"s dining and relaxation area, or rather, the intentional atmosphere created by the bookstore, which allows travelers who come to the bookstore to slow down their pace and dissolve their fast rhythms. In such a space, conversations become more relaxed and enjoyable.

During the first coffee gathering, I had the privilege of learning about the chairman"s vision of "unity, mutual assistance, and sustainability." In order to achieve this vision, the chairman, the activity director, and the alumni service center representative discussed future activity planning and team direction together. Emotional connection and resource integration are the main core of the alumni association, and they all require the participation and support of the alumni.

Yuan Ze University has talented individuals from various departments, each with their own expertise and strengths. However, like the tea drinks in the bookstore, while individual drinks can taste their unique flavors, they can exhibit ultimate charm when combined with other tea drinks. The logo of the alumni association, designed to depict hand in hand, hopes to integrate the characteristics of each department, allowing each individual"s talent to shine on a larger stage and to connect alumni friendship, pulling together an inseparable network for sustainability.

Senior brothers and sisters, why not come back to Tianhe Bookstore when you have time, experience the relaxed and comfortable atmosphere of the bookstore, sit down and have a cup of coffee, and exchange updates with each other! ☕️
