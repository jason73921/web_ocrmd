■Description：



■相關注意事項Important Notices：


Gold and Silver awards: Only applicable for achievements in the first semester of the academic year of

graduation(112/8/1~113/1/31).      

Achievement award: Graduates must apply, supported by achievements over the years.

If the achievements of recent graduates fall outside the calculation period, certificates will be granted but no monetary awards.

For the same achievement, applicants can only apply for either the Gold or Silver award, not both, and if multiple achievements are submitted for the same category of award, only one will be granted.

The allocation of the prize money will be a fixed ratio of the annual budget, with 60% for the Gold award and 40% for the Achievement award. The prize money for each category of award will be evenly distributed among the "awards," subject to the maximum amount available for that award. Any remaining balance of the prize money may be reallocated.

# Reward Details for Students at Yuan Ze University

08.13.2009 Approved by Letter 0980137289 of Tai-hsun (2)

05.21.2021. Ministry of Education Taijiao (2) Zi No. 1100068988 letter for reference

Clause 1 In order to recognize YZU students’ excellent performance and achievement during their study at the university and encourage the students to live up to their potentials with the goal of building the spirit of YZU members, this set of details is established based on Clause 32 in the College Laws.

Clause 2 The rewards items include the following:

I. General rewards.

II. YZU Gold Award、SilverAward and Achievement Award.

Clause 3 The rewards categories are divided into: Studies, academics, activities,

service, talent, sports, versatility and more.

Clause 4 When the students’ achievement meet one of the following conditions, they will receive general rewards.

I. The students have attend the activities held outside of the university and have won honors for the school.

II. The students have been elected as departmental staff and perform

responsibly with achievements.

III. The students attend or held the activities recommended by the divisions at YZU.

IV. The students have held more than 10 extracurricular activities in an entire academic year.

V. The students have served as the extracurricular club staff for an entire academic year.

VI. The students have served as a YZU volunteer for an entire academic year.

VII. The students have served on different university committees for an entire academic year.

VIII.The students have participated special activities approved by the YZU divisions.

IX. The students have represented the university and won in the inter-collegiate competitions and contests.

X. The students have represented the university to participate in both the on-campus and off-campus activities with achievements.

XI. The students have served both on-campus and off-campus with acknowledged excellence in performance.

XII. The students have other achievements and should be the models of other students.

Clause 5 When the students’ achievements meet the following conditions, they can be recommended for the YZU Silver Award.

I. Silver Award for Academic Performance: the students who have performed excellently in their academic grades and have been recommended by their departments. Each class should elect one student in each year from each department.

II. Silver Award for Academic Achievements: the students who have won the first three places in on-campus or off-campus academic competitions. The undergraduate and Master’s degree students whose learning or research outcomes have been published in famous journals in or outside of Taiwan are qualified. The journals have to be classified as Level B by the departments or colleges.

III. Silver Award for Activities: the club members who have served in the students’ organizations (related to academic performance, music, recreation, physical education, and friendship building) that have been evaluated at Level A in the academic year assessment are qualified for this award.

IV. Silver Award for Service: the students who perform excellently in serving their organizations (student-governed and service-providing) or volunteering in on-campus and off-campus activities with Level B evaluation in the academic year assessment are qualified for this award.

V. Silver Award for Talents: the students who have participated in the talent contests at YZU and won the first place or those attending regional contests and won the first three places are qualified for this award.

VI. Silver Award for Physical Education Performance: those who have been evaluated by the Physical Education Office and have met one of the following conditions are qualified.

(1) Those who represent YZU to attend national physical education contests and won the fourth to the eighth places are qualified.

(2) Those who represent YZU to attend regional physical education contests and won the first place or special honors are qualified.

(3) Those who have set new records in physical education categories at YZU are qualified.

VII. Silver Award for Versatility: Pass the State Examination General Exam (including examinations of the same level), Recognized by each college as a Grade B National Technician Skills Test or with excellent results in Chinese tests (refer to the comparison table of various English examinations and CEF for Yuan Ze University award application).

Clause 6 When the students’ achievements meet one of the following conditions, they can be recommended to apply for YZU Gold Award.

I. Gold Award for Academic Performance: the students who have graduated with excellent performance can be recommended by their departments for this award. Each department can only recommend one student.

II. Gold Award for Academic Achievements: the students who meet one of the following conditions are qualified for this award.

(1) The students have won the first three places in international academic competitions are qualified.

(2) The students have won the first place or special honors in national academic competitions are qualified for this award.

(3) The undergraduate and Master’s degree students whose learning or research outcomes have been published in famous journals in or outside of Taiwan are qualified. The journals have to be classified as Level A by the departments or colleges.

III. Gold Award for Activities: the students who have won Silver Award and met one of the following conditions are qualified for this award.

(1) The student is the head of the student organization and, in his/her

term, the club has been evaluated as an excellent club.

(2) The students have won the first three places in national competitions and brought honors to YZU.

IV. Gold Award for Service: the students who have won Silver Award and met one of the following conditions are qualified for this award.

(1) The student is the head of the student organization and, in his/her term, the club has been evaluated as an excellent club.

(2) The students have served as volunteers for more than a year and have been responsible for major activities taking place at YZU. With excellent performance, they can be recommended for this award when passing the evaluation of the Volunteer Consultation Evaluation Committee.

(3) The students who have performed excellently and won the first three places in national competitions to bring honors to YZU.

V. Gold Award for Talents: the students who have participated and won the first place in either the international or national talent competitions.

VI. Gold Award for Physical Education Performance: those who have been evaluated by the Physical Education Office and have met one of the following conditions are qualified.

(1) Those who represent YZU to attend international national physical education contests and won the first three places are qualified.

(2) Those who represent YZU to attend national physical education contests and won the first three places or special honors are qualified.

(3) Those who have set new records in physical education categories at YZU are qualified.

Ⅶ Gold Award for Versatility: Pass the National Examination Higher Examination (including examinations of the same level), Those who have been recognized by each college as having excellent results in the national technical ability test or language test (refer to the comparison table of various English examinations and CEF for Yuan Ze University award application).

Clause 7 When the students’ achievements have met the following conditions and have been qualified again for Gold Award can apply for Achievement Award based on recommendation, However, the Gold Award is only awarded with a certificate but not a bonus:

I. Academic Achievement Award: Those students who have won twice Silver

Award and one time Gold Awards are qualified.

II. Achievement Award for Activities: Those who have won twice Silver Awards for Activities and one Gold Award for Activities are qualified.

III. Achievement Award for Service: Those who have won twice Silver Awards for Service and one Gold Award for Service are qualified.

IV. Achievement Award for Talents: Those who have won twice Silver Awards for Talents and one Gold Award for Talent are qualified.

V. Achievement Award for Physical Education: Those who have won twice Silver Awards for Physical Education and one Gold Award for Physical Education are qualified.

VI. Achievement Award for Versatility: Those who have won twice Silver Awards for Versatility and one Gold Award for Versatility are qualified.

Clause 8 The details for evaluating the students and actual evaluation for each medal will be established by different divisions: the Office of Academic Affairs (academic performance), each college (academic achievement), Physical Education Office (physical education) and the Office of Student Affairs (activities, service, talents). All the details have to be sent to the Student Affair meeting for approval. The applicants or their departments can prepare the documents required for recommendation procedures. The information includes activity records, physical education or talent competition scores. After the evaluation of related evaluation procedures, the decision will be sent to the Student Affair meeting for approval before the winner lists are announced.

Clause 9 The medals in the same scope can only be applied once each academic year. The Achievement Medal is limited to only the students graduating that year. The application timeline is as follows:

I. Gold and Silver Medals: the students can submit their applications in the fall semester of each academic year based on their performance in the previous two academic years.

II. Achievement Medals: the graduating students of the year can submit their applications in the spring semester of their graduating year based on the records of the previous years.

Clause 10 If the winners are reported to have violated the stipulations, the same procedures can be taken to revoke their qualifications.

Clause 11 When the students’ behaviors meet the qualifications for general rewards and other scholarships, they can prepare the documents and submit their applications to the administrative or teaching divisions at YZU. With the approval of the Level One supervisors at the divisions, the award winning records can be listed in their academic information system. The related documents can be applied at the Joint Service Counter.

Clause 12 The award winners will receive their certificates and medals prepared by each evaluation division. Moreover, the Office of Student Affairs will compile the name list of all winners to publicly acknowledge these winners at important meetings or graduation commencement at YZU. The achievements will be permanently recorded in the students’ files. The record details of the winners’ excellent performance will be announced in the YZU Student Honor Bulletin. The Achievement Medals can be kept permanently by the winners. The Silver and Gold Medals will be returned to the university when the winners graduate. In addition, the Achievement Medal winners and the Gold Medal winners will receive NT\$10,000 and NT\$5,000 respectively.

Clause 13 The winners should accept the invitation from YZU to serve in different public events to demonstrate their award-winning talents or achievements. This will serve as encouragement to other students and honor the university.

Clause 14 This set of details is ratified in the Student Affair meeting and approved by the university affair meeting before implementation. All amendments require the same procedures.
