Dear students,

The shortlist of the priority course selection from the 1081 final questionnaires＋1082 opening questionnaires has been posted on the Yuan Ze University website or the News Section of Office of Academic Affairs website.

If you have further questions, please contact with Teaching Service Section (ext. 2256)  before May. 9th 2020.  It is invalid beyond the deadline.

 

Associate statements, please see as follow :

Priority course selection policy:

1091 semester the priority course selection is in accordance with the filling condition of the 1081 final questionnaires＋1082 opening questionnaires.

 

Quotas set for priority course selection

- For the class with a maximum number of 35 students, the quota of priority enrollment is 5.

- For the class with students between 36 and 70, the quota of priority enrollment is 10.

- For the class with a maximum number of 71 students, the quota of priority enrollment is 15.

 

The statements showed above ONLY FOR “ELECTIVE COURSES”.

 

Teaching Services Section Office of Academic Affairs
