說明：

一、本校機車停車場115年2月26日 09:00am 正式啟用，並同步開放宿舍旁東泰街側門通行。 

二、由桃園、中壢及八德前往東泰街側門交通路線參考附圖一。 

三、因校園車牌辨識系統建置整合中，暫不開放正門-側門不同出入口進出，將自2月25日10:00pm起於活動中心前管制道路通行，管制措施詳附圖二。 

四、管制期間，由遠東路校門入校車輛無法進入第三宿舍停車場：

    (一) 遠東路校門入校汽車請停放於環校道路停車位，已核准入校之教職員機車請停放於休閒中心後方停車棚。 

    (二) 需停放第三宿舍停車場之汽車請於東泰街側門入校，惟第三宿舍地下停車場車位有限，如無實際需求仍請由正門入校。 

五、配合東泰街側門開放後之校園車牌辨識系統刻正建置整合中，俟測試完成後將儘速解除管制措施。 

六、管制期間造成不便，敬請見諒。

Subject: Traffic Flow Update Following the Opening of the Dongtai Street Side Gate (Dormitory Area)

Details:


The new on-campus motorcycle parking area will officially open at 9:00 AM on February 26, 2026. At the same time, the Dongtai Street side gate (next to the dormitories) will be opened for access.

For suggested routes to the Dongtai Street side gate from Taoyuan, Zhongli, and Bade, please refer to Appendix 1.

As the campus license plate recognition system is still under integration and testing, entry and exit through different gates (main gate and side gate) will not be permitted for the time being. Starting at 10:00 PM on February 25, temporary traffic control will be implemented on the road in front of the Activity Center. Please see Appendix 2 for details.

During the control period, vehicles entering through the Yuandong Road main gate will not be able to access the Third Dormitory parking lot:
 (1) Cars entering via the Yuandong Road main gate should park in designated spaces along the campus ring road. Authorized faculty and staff motorcycles should park in the shed behind the Recreation Center.
 (2) Vehicles intending to park in the Third Dormitory parking lot must enter through the Dongtai Street side gate. As parking spaces in the Third Dormitory underground lot are limited, drivers without specific need are advised to continue using the main gate.

The campus license plate recognition system for the Dongtai Street side gate remains under installation and integration. Traffic control measures will be lifted once testing is completed.

We appreciate your patience and apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.


 

總務處敬上  Office of General Affairs
