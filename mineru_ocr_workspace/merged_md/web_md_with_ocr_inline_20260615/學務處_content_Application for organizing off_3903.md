```markdown
## APPLICATION FORORGANIZING OFF-CAMPUS ACTIVITIES

Using the "Personal Portal Student Club Activity System" to complete the "Activity Application" Note: When applying for off-campus activities, it is necessary to provide information related to "First Aid Personnel." Clubs that do not have qualified first aid personnel are not allowed to organize off-campus activities.

## The following documents must be submitted 3 days before the event:

1. Confirmation form for club-organized offcampus activities.

2. Student off-campus activity safety checkist.

3. List of participants and emergency contact persons.

4. Safety checklist for rented vehicles, tour company business registration certificate, tour bus company information, driver's licenses, and vehicle insurance certificates(please ensure they are valid).

5. Parental consent form (required for participants under 18 years old or for dangerous activities.

6. Event proposal and itinerary for the day.

7. Copy of insurance policy certificate.
```

```markdown
1. Confirmation form for submission of documents for off-campus activities.
```
