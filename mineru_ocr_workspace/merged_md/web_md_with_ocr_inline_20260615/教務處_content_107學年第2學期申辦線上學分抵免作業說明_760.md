## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 760
- **文章標題**: 107學年第2學期申辦線上學分抵免作業說明
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/107-2-1080128
- **文章簡介**: 一、申請日期: 108年2月1日~3月4日（逾期網路抵免系統將自動關閉）

二、抵免事項說明

       (一)抵免審核：

              1.共同科目:由通識教育部、國際語言中心、軍訓室、體育室、學務處審核。

                    2.專業科目:由系所審核。

             (二)

       1.本次申請適用107學年度入學學生(含大學部、研究所及轉學生)，第一學期未提出或第一次申請扺免漏列之科目，得於第二學期再行提出，且以

           一次為限， 逾期不予受理。

       2.107學年度轉系學生抵免科目請以書面學分抵免表提出申請。

       3.服務學習課程抵免以書面學分抵免表提出申請。

 (三)轉學生轉二年級適用106學年修業科目表，轉三年級適用105學年度修業科目表，新生適用107學年修業科目表。

     

       (四)申請流程：

               1.元智首頁→portal →輸入帳號、密碼  登入→學習檔案→線上抵免

               2.抵免作業操作流程相關說明：

                            路徑:元智首頁→學校行政業務→教務處→學籍相關→學分抵免

                 https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-03-22-06-44-19  

         3. 必選修科目表查詢路徑:教務處→學生專區→必選修科目表→各系必選修科目表

                              https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-01-14-06-58-46/2016-03-25-13-49-59

             (五)

     1.學生須繳交成績單正本一份至一館二樓教務處註冊組R1209。

                 2.學分班學分證明正本請繳交至各系所審核人                   

                                                                                                                                     教務處註冊組   啟

Procedures for newly admitted students/transferring students applying for on-line Course Credit 

                                      Waiver for the 2nd semester  of 107 School Year


Application period:Feb.1st~Mar.4th, 2019 (Students must apply for it during the period; the on-line system will be closed afterwards.)

Notifications:


1.Verification of credit waiver:

(1)Common core courses: verified by College of General Studies, International Language and Culture Center, Military Office, Physical Education  

     Office, Office of Student Affairs.

             (2)Required core courses: verified by Department of major.

2.

            (1)Course credit waiver is applied to students admitted for 107 School Year, including  undergraduate students, graduate students and transferring  

                 students. Students who didn’t submit the application for credit waiver or who did submit the application but forgot to do so

                 for certain courses during the 1st semester shall apply for it during the 2nd semester. The application shall be only submitted once by the deadline.

            (2)Transferring students admitted for 107 School Year shall submit the application for credit waiver with a written application form.

3.Transferring students (2nd year student) are applied to the list of courses for 106 School Year. Transferring students (3rd year student) are applied to the  

     list of  courses for 105 School Year. Newly admitted students (whose student numbers beginning with 107) are applied to the list of courses for 107  

     School Year.

4.Application procedure:

             (1)YZU → Student Portal → Study File → Course Waiver

              (2)YZU → Administration → Office of Academic Affairs → Students’ Resource →Course Credit Waive

    https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-03-22-06-44-19     

  (3)The path for checking the list of required/elective courses: Office of Academic Affairs→ Students’ Resource → List of Required/Elective Courses
