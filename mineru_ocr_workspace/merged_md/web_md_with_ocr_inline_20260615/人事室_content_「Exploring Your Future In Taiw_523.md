# Exploring Your Future In Taiwan

## Why Taiwan?

Have you ever thought of working in Taiwan? As the number one destination for expatriates, Taiwan boasts a great quality of life, topping thecharts in safety, friendly residents, affordable cost of living, and a world-class national healthcare system available to all residents.

We need your help to make the semiconductors flow, and the software doesn't write itself either. We have a thriving startup scene, but we are also looking for artists, architects, researchers, lawyers,managers, andall other brilliant minds.

After work, come explore our mountain ranges, go scuba diving or surfing, indulge inthe multitude of different foods, or catch a world-class art or music performance.

Come and make your next stop, Taiwan.
