Subject: New Taipei City Government"s "Together Beautiful - Manauaz (Bunun for "Beautiful")" - 2023 Indigenous Cultural Arts Exhibition and Rural Specialty Market Event

Description:



The Rural Specialty Market event and Indigenous Cultural Arts Exhibition are scheduled to take place on November 4, 2023 (Saturday) at 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM, respectively, at the Ruifang Train Station Front Square. We welcome citizens and individuals interested in indigenous culture to join us for a stroll through the indigenous rural specialty market and appreciate the essence and beauty of traditional indigenous music, dance, and culture.




Admission to the event is free, and attendees can exchange for commemorative items by checking in at the event. Additionally, while shopping at the specialty market, you can qualify for a lucky draw ticket with a minimum purchase amount. Prizes for the lucky draw include an iPad 10th generation, a 42-inch TV, a premium dehumidifier, and vouchers for items at the Wellcome Supermarket. We encourage people from all walks of life to actively participate in the event.
