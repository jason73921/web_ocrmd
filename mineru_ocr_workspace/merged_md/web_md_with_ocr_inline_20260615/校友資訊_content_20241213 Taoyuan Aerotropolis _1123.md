- **文章簡介**: 🚩 Yuan Ze University Alumni Association 20241213 Taoyuan Aerotropolis Visit

Last week, the Yuan Ze University Alumni Association led a delegation to visit Taoyuan Aerotropolis Corporation, where General Manager Hung Hsiu-Kuan personally received the group. During the visit, detailed insights were provided into the planning of Taoyuan Aerotropolis, which integrates existing industrial resources in the surrounding areas and leverages Taoyuan’s advantageous industries. The planning focuses on industries characterized by low pollution, low energy consumption, low water usage, and high added value (Three Lows and One High).

The six priority industries targeted for investment include:

Cloud Computing
Smart Vehicle Industry
International Logistics and Distribution
Biotechnology
Aviation Support Industry
Green Energy
By connecting the airport with surrounding infrastructure, the project aims to enhance Taoyuan"s industrial development and progress. Yuan Ze University, as one of the cooperative institutions of Taoyuan Aerotropolis, plays a pivotal role in the industry-academia collaboration alliance. This partnership assists resident companies in recruiting top talent, thereby enhancing their competitiveness and enabling them to establish a strong foothold in Taiwan while expanding globally. Together, these efforts contribute to achieving the ultimate goal of industry-academia collaboration.
