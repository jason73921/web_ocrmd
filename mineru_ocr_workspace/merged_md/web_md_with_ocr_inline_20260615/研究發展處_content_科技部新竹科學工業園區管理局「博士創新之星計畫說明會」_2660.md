## What is LEAP?

Launched by the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) in TAIWAN, LEAP cooperates with high-tech companies, start-ups and venture capitals in the U.S., Israel and France. Through the Program, LEAP enterprise partners will become host companies to host those carefully screened, selected, and matched talents as trainees overseas for one year. The crosscountry knowledge circulation and business development are expectable.

## Our Applicants

Ph.D. or talents with equivalent relevant industry experience.

Backgrounds of major innovative industries including Internet of things, semiconductors and IC design, biomedical, smart machinery, digital economy, green energy, defense, new agriculture, circular economy and cultural innovation technology are preferred.

## Selectees Grant

MOST will provide each admitted Program fellow living allowance and support the fee of VISA application.

## Our Partner Companies

Over 120 participating companies have been enlisted from the U.S., Israel and France since 2017. You may find more information on LEAP website.

## How to become host companies?

Provide your company information and training opening details (such as quota and description) by filling in the form no later than September 10 (Tue.) 2019 https://reurl.cc/K39vj
