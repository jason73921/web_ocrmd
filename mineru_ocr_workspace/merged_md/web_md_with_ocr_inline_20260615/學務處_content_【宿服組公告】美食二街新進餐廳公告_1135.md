Crossroads Café provides not only light meals, European-style bread, and coffee, but also job opportunities to the disabled. Students and faculty members are invited to attend the opening ceremony, which is to be held at 10 am, on Friday, Nov. 18th, 2016. Free snacks and desserts will be provided during the ceremony.
