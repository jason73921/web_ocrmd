## 一、 說明 Introduction

跨域數位人才加速躍升計畫主要目標係促成產業國際人才循環、培育產業所需國際人才，並促進產業與國際人才交流。本計畫擬與海外企業合作、促進人才國際循環，以跨國交流實習(遠距實習結合海外參訪)之方式推動人才與國際產業交流。為培育產業未來所需之國際數位人才，本計畫重點實習領域聚焦於數位經濟產業發展相關主題，其重點實習領域(以下簡稱實習領域)包含但不限於：

The DIGI+ Talent Accelerator & Jumpstart Program aims to promote talent circulation internationally, to cultivate digital talent for industry in Taiwan, and to urge connection between talent and the industry. This program would like to cooperate with overseas companies that are willing to offer internship opportunities (remote internship and onsite visits abroad) for Taiwanese talent and to promote the exchange between talents and international industries. The focus areas of this internship program (hereinafter referred to as “internship area”) are topics related to the development of digital economy and industries, which include but not limited to the following:

(一) 創新科技應用：人工智慧，如機器學習、深度學習、影像辨識、語意分析；資料科學，如資料處理、資料分析、資料視覺化、網路爬蟲；智慧聯網，如物聯網、5G通訊、雲端運算、通訊科技等。

Tech Innovation and Application: artificial intelligence, including machine learning, deep learning, image recognition, semantic analysis; data science, including data processing, data analysis, data visualization, web crawler; smart networks, including Internet of Things, 5G communication, cloud computing, communication technology, etc.

(二) 數位經濟發展：數位行銷、金融科技、電子商務、共享經濟、數位內容(如：AR/VR、3D 設計、UI UX 設計等)、數位經濟政策研究分析、國際市場投資分析等。

Digital Economy Development: digital marketing, financial technology, ecommerce, sharing economy, digital content (e.g., AR/VR, 3D design, UI/UX design, etc.), digital economic policy research and analysis and international market investment analysis, etc.

本申請須知之名詞釋義如下 Definition of Terms：

(一) 跨域數位人才加速躍升計畫：由數位發展部數位產業署推動國際人才循環及國際人才培育之計畫，重要工作項目之一為推動臺灣人才成為產業國際人才。

DIGI+ Talent Accelerator & Jumpstart Program: a program supervised by Administration for Digital Industries, Ministry of Digital Affairs, Taiwan (R.O.C.), which aims to promote talent circulation and international talent training; one of its key features is promoting Taiwanese talent to become international talent in the industry.

(二) 臺灣人才國際交流實習：本計畫協助在臺大學校院二年級以上至畢業三年內之臺灣籍人才(含應屆畢業生，不含海外就讀在學生)媒合至海外企業實習職缺，進行為期5個月的遠距實習(含至少2週海外參訪)執行海外公司之實習內容。

Taiwanese Talent International Internship: one of TCA’s main programs that promotes internship opportunities offered by overseas companies for Taiwanese students who are in their second university year or above (including graduates of the current year but excluding overseas Taiwanese students). Students will participate the internship remotely with overseas companies from July till November 2024 (including 2 weeks of onsite visit to the companies abroad).

(三) 申請人：申請參與本計畫國際交流實習之本國籍人才。

Applicants: Taiwanese talents who apply to this program.

(四) 實習單位：提供本計畫實習機會之海外一般企業、研究機構、非營利組織，或臺灣企業於實習地具海外分公司或辦公室等海外據點或單位設置者(不包含陸港澳)。

Host Organizations: overseas companies, research institutions, non-profit organizations, or Taiwanese companies with overseas branches, offices, bases, or units established in internship locations (organizations in Hong Kong, Macau, and Mainland China are not eligible) that offer internship positions to this program.

(五) 實習生：申請並經實習單位面試甄選後錄取參與本計畫之申請人。Interns: Applicants who are admitted to this program.

實習期程：自2024年7月1日至2024年11月30日止，完成實習時數240小時，以及至少2週海外參訪。

Internship period: Complete at least 240 hours of internship and 2 weeks of onsite visit abroad before November 30th, 2024.

(二) 為民國113年大學校院應屆畢業生或112學年度第二學期(2024年2月後)就讀於我國大學校院大學二年級(含)以上至研究所，不限科系之學生；

(三) 或為民國110年後自我國大專校院畢業且申請時無全職工作之人才。\* 不含海外就讀學生。

Applicants must meet at least two of the following criteria:

1. Must be Taiwan (R.O.C.) citizen;

2. Graduates of 2024, or students who are in their second university year or above (including Masters and PhD) in spring semester 2024 (February to June, 2024); no specific major requirement;

3. Or recent graduates (graduated from universities in Taiwan after 2021) without full-time employment at the time of application.

\* Students studying overseas are excluded.

## 六、 實習生配合事項 Cooperation Matters for Interns

## (一)國際交流實習 International Internship

1. 取得實習單位正式錄取文件，並與計畫辦事處簽訂契約書或切結書。

Obtain internship offer letter from the Host Organization, and sign contract or affidavit with Host Organization and Program Office.

2. 參與實習單位所安排之實習規劃，實習時數不得少於240小時(不包 含海外參訪2週)。 Interns must participate in the program and activities organized by the Host Organization, and complete at 240 hours of remote internship (excluding the 2-week onsite visit abroad).

3. 每月繳交下列文件 Submit following documents monthly.

(1) 學習歷程檔案：須填寫每月實習任務與時數，並經實習單位核實後，由實習生繳交至計畫辦事處。

Learning Portfolio: Record your monthly internship task items and internship hours; then get your learning portfolio verified by your Host Organization; and submit it to the Program Office.

(2) 實習回饋：線上填寫實習情況及相關回饋。

Formative Feedback Survey: Complete an online survey related to your internship experience and feedback

4. 須配合計畫辦事處進行線上定期實習訪查，內容包含實習生適應 狀況、實習生表現與工作執行情形等。 Regular online review meeting: Attend online review meetings held by Program Office.

5. 於11月18日前繳交一千字以上成果報告書(中英文皆可)，其內容應包含國外實習企業或機構之簡介、國際實習之學習心得、遠距實習及海外參訪之生活體驗、實習之具體效益(請條列式列舉)感想與建議等(含4-5張實習與海外參訪照片)。

Submit internship report (no less than 1,000 words, both English and Chinese are acceptable) before November 18th, the report should include company information, internship description, overview of internship experience and ongoing considerations with 4-5 pictures attached.

6. 另須參與計畫辦事處辦理之相關進修課程(實體或線上)，成果交流活動與跨計畫交流會，以及擔任計畫大使協助下屆實習生等相關具公益性質事項。

Interns must attend related courses (face-to-face/online) the Program Completion Presentation and experience exchange events at the end of the program to demonstrate their internship achievement, learning, and experience. Interns are also required to involve in public interest activities such as acting as the ambassadors of this program to assist next year’s interns, etc.

## (二)海外參訪 Onsite Visit Abroad

1. 於出國2週前，完成並提交完整海外參訪規劃(建議事先與實習單位討論)。

Submit a complete and appropriate onsite visit plan of two weeks before the visit (preliminary discussion with the company or organization is preferred).

2. 投保出國海外參訪期間之旅行平安保險，項目包含：意外身故、失能投保金額新臺幣100萬元起；海外突發疾病投保金額新臺幣10萬元起；傷害醫療投保金額新臺幣10萬元起。並於出國前三個工作天提供投保相關證明。

Purchase travel insurance for the internship abroad, including coverage for accidental death and disability of at least NT\$1,000,000 per accident or illness, emergency medical conditions of NT\$100,000, and injury medical treatment of NT\$100,000 per accident or illness. Interns must provide documents of insurance coverage 3 working days before heading for the onsite visit abroad.

## (三)計畫津貼 Program Stipend and Allowance：

## 1. 實習津貼 Stipend

本計畫錄取之實習生自2024年7月1日至2024年11月30日止(共5個月)，給付每人每月實習津貼新臺幣8,800元。

Each trainee will be granted a stipend of \$8,800 per month while attending this training and internship program for 5 months, from July 1st to November 30th of 2024.

## 2. 海外參訪津貼 Allowance

錄取實習生之海外參訪津貼，依本計畫「海外參訪津貼標準表」 (如附件三)辦理。參訪津貼分兩次支給：實習生與實習單位討 論並提交完整海外參訪規劃(50%)、實習生繳交成果報告並完成 計畫成果發表與交流會(50%)。無出國事實者，不予補助此津貼。 Selected interns will be given an allowance (limited slots) based on the “Overseas Internship Allowance Standards” (Attachment 3). The allowance will be allocated respectively based on the interns’ complete of the following requirements: 1) submit a complete/appropriate onsite visit plan after discussing with the organization (50% of the allowance), 2) submit internship report and participate in the end of Internship Presentation and experience exchange events (50% of the allowance). Interns who do not undertake internship abroad will not receive the allowance.

3. 實習生應完成5個月實習(共240小時實習時數以及至少2週的海外參訪)。如未完成者，應依實際實習工作天數比例計算，償還已領取之津貼。

All interns shall complete at least, within 5 months of internship, 240 hours of remote internship and 2 weeks of onsite visit abroad. Those who fail to complete the regulated internship hours (240 hours) shall return the allowance in proportion based on the number of internship hours completed.

(四)追蹤調查 Career Development Survey：

須配合本計畫執行結訓實習生追蹤調查作業至少二年，須告知就業公司、職稱與薪資等資訊。

Intern must participate in the career development survey for at least 2 years after the completion of this program by providing employment information, such as employer, position and salary range, etc.

## 七、 實習終止作業 Termination and Withdrawal

(一) 如因不可抗力之因素須退出本計畫，實習生須提出書面相關證明(例如：診斷證明等資料)，並向實習單位及計畫辦事處提交實習終止申請表(附件一)。

Due to force majeure, interns may terminate internship program by submitting written proof (such as certificate of diagnosis, etc.) and Termination & Withdrawal Form (Attachment 1) to the Host Organization and the Program Office.

(二) 呈上述情形者，實習生之實習津貼須依實際實習工作天數/時數比例計算，償還已領取之實習津貼，且辦理退訓之實習生至多僅能請領40%之實習津貼。

Interns with the above circumstances shall return the stipend based on the number of internship days/hours completed. Those who withdraw from internship program shall not receive more than 40% of the stipend.

(三) 若實習生出國參訪返台後未能於期限內(11/18)繳交成果報告並完成計畫成果發表與交流會(若為不可抗力之因素須提出相關證明)，不發予實習生參訪津貼(50%)並辦理退訓，且須繳回已請領之全額實習/參訪津貼。

Interns who are not able to submit internship report and participate in the end of Internship Presentation and experience exchange events after the onsite visit abroad will not receive the other 50% of the allowance. (Written proof is required, if interns are to withdraw with proper reasons.) Interns will be asked to withdraw from the program and the full amount of the stipend/allowance received by the interns shall be returned to the Program Office.

## (四) 實習生有以下情形將予以實習終止：

Intern with the following circumstances will be withdrawn from internship program:

1. 實習生報名表及履歷資料填寫不實者，經查證屬實，須繳回已請領實習/參訪津貼，且不得再申請本計畫。

Those who enter any false or misleading information will be withdrawn from the program and cannot apply to this program in the future. Stipend/allowance that has already been issued to intern must be returned.

2. 實習生適應不佳，得向本計畫提出實習終止申請。

## 一、 說明 Introduction

跨域數位人才加速躍升計畫主要目標係促成產業國際人才循環、培育產業所需國際人才，並促進產業與國際人才交流。本計畫擬與海外企業合作、促進人才國際循環，以跨國交流實習(遠距實習結合海外參訪)之方式推動人才與國際產業交流。為培育產業未來所需之國際數位人才，本計畫重點實習領域聚焦於數位經濟產業發展相關主題，其重點實習領域(以下簡稱實習領域)包含但不限於：

The DIGI+ Talent Accelerator & Jumpstart Program aims to promote talent circulation internationally, to cultivate digital talent for industry in Taiwan, and to urge connection between talent and the industry. This program would like to cooperate with overseas companies that are willing to offer internship opportunities (remote internship and onsite visits abroad) for Taiwanese talent and to promote the exchange between talents and international industries. The focus areas of this internship program (hereinafter referred to as “internship area”) are topics related to the development of digital economy and industries, which include but not limited to the following:

(一) 創新科技應用：人工智慧，如機器學習、深度學習、影像辨識、語意分析；資料科學，如資料處理、資料分析、資料視覺化、網路爬蟲；智慧聯網，如物聯網、5G通訊、雲端運算、通訊科技等。

Tech Innovation and Application: artificial intelligence, including machine learning, deep learning, image recognition, semantic analysis; data science, including data processing, data analysis, data visualization, web crawler; smart networks, including Internet of Things, 5G communication, cloud computing, communication technology, etc.

(二) 數位經濟發展：數位行銷、金融科技、電子商務、共享經濟、數位內容(如：AR/VR、3D 設計、UI UX 設計等)、數位經濟政策研究分析、國際市場投資分析等。

Digital Economy Development: digital marketing, financial technology, ecommerce, sharing economy, digital content (e.g., AR/VR, 3D design, UI/UX design, etc.), digital economic policy research and analysis and international market investment analysis, etc.

本申請須知之名詞釋義如下 Definition of Terms：

(一) 跨域數位人才加速躍升計畫：由數位發展部數位產業署推動國際人才循環及國際人才培育之計畫，重要工作項目之一為推動臺灣人才成為產業國際人才。

DIGI+ Talent Accelerator & Jumpstart Program: a program supervised by Administration for Digital Industries, Ministry of Digital Affairs, Taiwan (R.O.C.), which aims to promote talent circulation and international talent training; one of its key features is promoting Taiwanese talent to become international talent in the industry.

(二) 臺灣人才國際交流實習：本計畫協助在臺大學校院二年級以上至畢業三年內之臺灣籍人才(含應屆畢業生，不含海外就讀在學生)媒合至海外企業實習職缺，進行為期5個月的遠距實習(含至少2週海外參訪)執行海外公司之實習內容。

Taiwanese Talent International Internship: one of TCA’s main programs that promotes internship opportunities offered by overseas companies for Taiwanese students who are in their second university year or above (including graduates of the current year but excluding overseas Taiwanese students). Students will participate the internship remotely with overseas companies from July till November 2024 (including 2 weeks of onsite visit to the companies abroad).

(三) 申請人：申請參與本計畫國際交流實習之本國籍人才。

Applicants: Taiwanese talents who apply to this program.

(四) 實習單位：提供本計畫實習機會之海外一般企業、研究機構、非營利組織，或臺灣企業於實習地具海外分公司或辦公室等海外據點或單位設置者(不包含陸港澳)。
