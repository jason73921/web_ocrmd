# Yuan Ze University Regulations for Establishment of the Health Committee

December 13, 1999, Approved by the 5th Executive Council Meeting of the 88th Academic Year May 22, 2006, Amended and Approved by the 18th Executive Council Meeting of the 94th Academic Year January 16, 2012, Amended and Approved at the 8th Executive Council Meeting of the 100th Academic Year October 08, 2012, Amended and Approved by the 5th Executive Council Meeting of the 101st Academic Year June 11, 2025, Amended and Approved by the 18th Executive Council Meeting of the 113th Academic Year April 22, 2026, Amended and Approved by the 17th Executive Council Meeting of the 114th Academic Year

Article 1 To strengthen the promotion of health work at the school, and to enhance the physical and mental health of faculty, staff, and students, the Health Committee (hereinafter referred to as "the Committee") is established.

Article 2 The Committee is composed of Vice President,the Dean of Student Affairs, the Dean of General Affairs, the Director of the Personnel Office, the Director of the Accounting Office, the Director of the Physical Education Office, the Director of the Military Training Office, the Director of the Environmental Protection and Occupational Safety and Health Center, the Chairperson of the Faculty and Staff Welfare and Mutual Aid Committee, one faculty representative from the College of Medical and Health Sciences, the Director of the Sanitary & Health Care Section, one school nurse, one male and one female representative from the Student Union, and one male and one female representative from the Student Dormitory Self-Governance Association. Vice President serves as the Chairperson of the Committee, and the Head of the Health Care Group concurrently serves as the Executive Secretary. All members serve without remuneration.

Article 3 The functions of the Committee are as follows:

1. Deliberation, supervision, and assessment of the school's health work.

2. Promotion of health care services for faculty, staff, and students.

3. Promotion of health education and counseling at the school.

4. Supervision and improvement of environmental and food safety and hygiene at the school.

5. Other matters related to health at the school.

Article 4 The Committee shall hold meetings at least once per semester, and ad hoc meetings may be called when necessary.

Article 5 The Chairman of the Committee shall preside over the meetings. In case the Chairman is unable to preside, a designated member of the Committee shall act as the chairperson.

Article 6 Matters resolved by the Committee shall be implemented by the relevant units of the

school respectively.

Article 7 The regulations above and any future amendments shall be implemented upon the approval of the Executive Council Meeting.

In case of any discrepancy between the Chinese and English versions, the Chinese version shall prevail.
