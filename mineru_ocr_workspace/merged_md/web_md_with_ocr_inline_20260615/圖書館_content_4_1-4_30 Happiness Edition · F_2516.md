- **文章簡介**: Happiness doesn’t always arrive with fireworks.
Sometimes, it’s found in a warm drink, a moment of being understood, or a quiet page in a good book.

This April, YZU Library invites you to slow down, turn a page, and rediscover the gentle warmth that reading brings. Let words light up your heart and allow everyday moments of happiness to quietly unfold around you.

 

Book Exhibition: Let Reading Warm the Heart

Period: April 1–30, 2026
Venue: New Title Area, YZU Library

World Book Day × “Heart 100” Reading Marathon

April is the season of reading.
Join us for the World Book Day: Heartfelt Reading Marathon, where books become companions and reading becomes a journey inward.

With a curated list of 100 selected titles, we invite you to explore self-awareness, understand emotions, and let reading be a soft light that brightens daily life.

From April 20 to April 30, participate in the event and enjoy the chance to receive a Starbucks drink voucher, savoring both the aroma of coffee and the joy of reading.

Period: April 20–30, 2026
Eligibility & Reward Rules:


Borrow 10 physical books from the book exhibition during the event period, including at least one title from the “Heart 100” book list.

Eligible participants will receive one Starbucks coffee voucher.

Limited to 30 participants, while supplies last.

Open to YZU faculty, staff, and students only.

Each participant may receive one voucher only.


Let reading be the starting point of happiness—and a cup of coffee the perfect ending to this gentle journey.

E-Reading, Happiness Without Borders

Reading doesn’t stop at paper. Through e-books, you can also nourish the mind and connect with the world.

By making digital reading part of your daily rhythm, every click opens a new path of thought and extends the joy of reading beyond boundaries.

From April 7 to April 30, we invite you to join the E-Reading activity and compete for heartwarming rewards.

Period: April 7–30, 2026
How to Participate:


Log in to iRead Huayi, UDN Reading, or HyRead eBook platforms.

Borrow 30 or more e-books during the event period (cross-platform accumulation allowed).

The top three borrowers will receive special happiness-themed prizes.

Only actual borrowing records count; online previews are not included.


Prizes:


Bluetooth Wireless Headphones

Wooden Aroma Diffuser Lamp

German Tea Set with Japanese 3D Cat Cup-Edge Sugar


Notes:


To encourage meaningful reading, each borrowed e-book must be opened and read for more than 10 minutes to be counted.

The same e-book will only be counted once; participants are encouraged to explore diverse titles.

Any use of automated tools or abnormal borrowing behavior is strictly prohibited. Violations will result in disqualification.

The Library reserves the right to adjust event details and provide final interpretation.
