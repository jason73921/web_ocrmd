主旨：台北扶輪社於明年（112）4月29日舉辦全國大專院校「解決社會問題英文演講比賽」(English SocialSolutions Contest)訊息，請查照。

## 說明：

一、依據台北市台北扶輪社之章程及扶輪精神，為推廣全國大專院校學生英語語文能力並俱有國際思維與未來領袖之孕育，自1955年起每年均舉辦一場大型全國大專院校英語演講比賽EnglishSpeechContest（ESC)。為更深化社會關懷，呼籲年輕學子對社會問題之重視與參與，自107年起比賽更名為「解決社會問題英語演講比賽」（EnglishSocialSolutionsContest，簡稱「ESsC」），重點轉向鼓勵學生發揮其創新力、溝通技巧及團隊合作，提出解決方案讓社會大眾瞭解我們所面臨的社會問題，並付諸行動共同解決。

二、此項比賽全國大專院校同學均可參加，不限國籍。過往數十年間，數千位學生從此項比賽受益良多，現在均已成為各行各業中的中堅領導人物。在過去英語演講的基礎上，本年比賽的重點是：關注社會問題及提出解決方法，增進領導能力、自信心、創業能力、問題解決及溝通能力。希望此一比賽繼續成為本社每年度的重要服務社會活動。

三、本年的ESSC題目：研擬提出可改善所在社區的問題之服務計畫Developandpresent a social project whichwillbenefit your community

日期：2023年4月29日

地點：台北科技大學

四、敬請教育部發文至全國大專院校，鼓勵在校學生報名參加。

五、比賽詳細資訊與報名方式請見附件。

## 2023 English Social Solutions Contest Information

Topic: Develop and Present a social project to benefit your community

First Round: February 12, 2023 –(Video Submission deadline)

Final Round: April 29, 2023 (In-Person Presentation)

Location: National Taipei University of Technology

## TOP PRIZE: 50,000 TWD cash prize + other corporate sponsorships or internships

## Entering the Contest as a team

We encourage our contestants to work with individuals with different backgrounds and skill sets to come up with creative and unique solutions to the current situation. The team may consist of 1 to 3 people.

If you decide to enter as a team, then all members of each team must be present at the presentation.Any absenteefromachtam mayconstitute disqulifiction ulesshaving prior written approval from RCT.

First Round (Video Submission) – Submitted by February 12th, 2023

• List of stakeholders with details of their needs (E.g., beneficiaries,

partnerships, investors, etc.)

Semi-final Presentation – March 18th, 2023

•Winners of the Preliminaries will compete on March 18th, 2023. Semifinal Round will take place in physical rooms.

## Final Presentation –April 29, 2023

• Final round will compete on April 29, 2023.

• Rules and regulations will be the same as the Semi-Finals.

Contest Rules and Regulations

A team can have 1 to 3 team members.

• Business Casual is the preferred dress code for the contest.

•It is not compulsory for all team members to speak in the presentation, but each member is required to show up on stage and participate in the whole event.

•The presentation may include any audio and visual aids that the team considers appropriate.

• RCT will provide a computer with PowerPoint software and projector to assist your presentation. Any team requiring proprietary software and equipment to demonstrate their plan is responsible for its provision and installation.

To maintain fairness to all contestants, please refrain from using cellphones or mobile devices during the contest.
