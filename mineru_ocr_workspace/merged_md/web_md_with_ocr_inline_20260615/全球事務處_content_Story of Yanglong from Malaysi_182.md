- **文章全文**: To get to the dormitory in Yuanzhi, you must walk a steep slope and a long way. The only word for it is exhausting.
While walking alone in Yuan Zhi"s campus, looking at people who are busy helping their children with their luggage and dealing with check-in matters, a feeling of loneliness suddenly bursts into my mind, and I begin to miss my family and parents.

Despite living in the dormitory since middle school, I still feel a little uneasy about Taiwan since I do not have any relatives or friends here.
What about you, newcomers?

Do you feel the same way?

It takes a lot of work to leave your warm home and go abroad alone! !
Seniors and staff from the Global Affairs Office will help you~ (Originally in English)
