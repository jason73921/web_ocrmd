中華民國108年2月26日

(107)智大教內第0024號

主旨：公告108學年度學生轉系時程及相關事項。

說明：

一、申請日期：108年3月18日(星期一)起至4月9日(星期二)止。

 

二、保送升學之離島地區學生不得申請轉系，唯情況特殊報經原保送之地方政府同意者不再此限。

三、大一學生申請自由轉系第一志願填寫同類組者，在原系當學年上下二學期之學業平均成績皆需達到60分以上，否則取消該生自由轉系資格。

四、依前項規定大一學生上學期學業平均成績未達60分者，第一志願請勿再填同類組學系，否則取消轉系資格。

五、請填寫學生轉系申請表及元智大學學生申請轉系自我規劃分析表。

六、轉系標準請參閱大學部轉系審查標準表或至各學系洽詢。

七、其他相關事項及表單請參閱轉系網頁：元智大學/行政單位/教務處/學籍相關/轉系申請

Announcement

Office of Academic Affairs

February 26th, 2019

 (107)智大教內第0024號

Subject: Application period and notification for change of major in the 108 semester.

Content:


1..Application period: 2019/3/18 (Mon.)-2019/4/19(Tue.)

2.Students, who are from offshore islands and were admitted to the university through the guaranteed admission process, apply for change of major MUST have the permission from the original local government or the application will be dismissed.

3.Freshmen apply for change of major in the same field MUST have the average score of 60 or above in the first academic year, or the application will be dismissed.

4. Freshmen whose average score in the first semester of the first academic year is lower than 60 are not allowed to apply for change of major in the same field.

5.Students applying for a change of major (department/college/program) and Self-planning table.

6.Please see the department’s websites for the regulation of change of major.

7.For more details, please refer to the website: YZU homepage/Administrative Offices/Office of Academic Affairs/Student Statuses/Change of Majorhttps://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-03-22-06-44-19/129-2016-03-22-08-03-15
