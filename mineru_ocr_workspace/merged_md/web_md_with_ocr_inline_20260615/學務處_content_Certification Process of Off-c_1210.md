According to Article 11.4 principle for waiver, those students who have received related service learning certificates from government agencies or non-profit groups can attach the related documents to apply for course or activity waivers.

Submit the required documents as follows to the Extracurricular Activities Section R8301:


A copy of service learning certificates from government agencies or non-profit groups

Yuan Ze University Service Learning Log (Download)
