- **文章簡介**: Get ready to dive into the vibrant world of student clubs at Yuan Ze University during the 2023 Club Expo on September 13-14 hosted by the Extracurricular Affairs Section! It"s your chance to connect with fellow freshmen and discover a wide range of interests, from academics to sports and culture. Explore, engage, and jumpstart your university journey as you interact with our dynamic club community!

The expo will be held at the Red Brick Lane in front of Building 7 where each club will have a booth to display their club activities. Feel free to ask them any questions and join any clubs you want!

To see more information about clubs, go to extracurricular affairs section’s website: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/st/index.php/en-us/extracurriculum-activities/club-activity-guidance/3932-student-clubs-overview
