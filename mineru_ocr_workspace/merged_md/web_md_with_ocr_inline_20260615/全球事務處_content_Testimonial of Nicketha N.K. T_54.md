- **文章全文**: Being enrolled at YZU has surely opened many doors for me and has added to my awesome experience as an international student. 

There exists a sense of community at YZU, whether it be within the international student body or your individual departments/colleges. As a Senior within EBSC department, I can boast of the quality and dedication of my lecturers, the department staff, and our director. 

YZU also has an interesting environment, from its scenic gardens to its stately-designed buildings. Be sure to check out the campus library!

I have no regrets that my path has led me to YZU and I would encourage other International students to give it a chance as well

Nicketha N.K. Toney
