In response to the World Health Organization and the National Health Administration’s sugar reduction policy, the Dietitian Association will organize the "I Love Sugar-Free Drinks" campaign in March 2025 (National Nutrition Month).

Yuan Ze University"s Student Affairs Office, in collaboration with the campus dining vendors Ice Cellar at Constellation Food Court and Summer Orange at LeXue Food Court, is supporting the "Drink Sugar-Free, Stay Healthy & Save Money" initiative to promote a low-sugar environment.

On Monday, March 3, 2025, during business hours, enjoy special discounts when purchasing sugar-free tea at the following participating vendors:


Ice Cellar at Constellation Food Court – NT$5 off per cup

Summer Orange at LeXue Food Court – Buy one, get one free


For other participating off-campus vendors and promotions, [click here].

For more details, please inquire at the respective stores.

We look forward to your participation! 😊
