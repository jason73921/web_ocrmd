## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 1283
- **文章標題**: Library services adjustment (effective on May 20)
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/tw/library-services-adjustment-effective-on-may-20
- **文章簡介**: In response to the outbreak of the pandemic, the library will adjust its services effective from May 20 as follows.


Limited hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 to 17:00; Closed on Saturdays and Sundays

Limited Service: Reserved borrowing service only. The reading seat area and book stacks are not accessible. Exploit the “Assisted search” to reserve the books you would like to borrow. Pick up the books at the circulation desk when you get the notification.

Restricted patrons: suspension of non-YZU-affiliated readers (admission available for YZU faculty, staff, and students only)

Loan extension: The due date on all borrowed books and materials will be automatically extended according to the pandemic alert.


 During the crucial period, you are encouraged to explore the licensed e-books, e-resources via remote access authentication. Stay home and stay safe.
