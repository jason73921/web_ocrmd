People said that the first known use of the word art comes from the playing that children and adults all love. The festival, focusing on leisure, pleasure, and exploration, brings people to an adventure of art and lifestyle in the mood of playing in spring.

The one-month art journey started by the warm-up event of the clown’s parade on Feb. 24 and opening ceremony on March 7. The festival arranges rich and diverse activities including several performances, local creation, workshops, film screenings, lectures & art exhibition; and the event of playing in spring lasts till March 30.
