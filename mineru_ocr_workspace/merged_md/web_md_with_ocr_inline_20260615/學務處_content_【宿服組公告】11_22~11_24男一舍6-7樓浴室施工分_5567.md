男一舍6-7樓浴室施工 分區暫停使用

停用日期：2024/11/22 00:00起～2024/11/24

說明：

1.男一舍6-7樓浴室擋水條施工，施作前地板需全部乾涸，將提前分區停用浴室。

2.2024/11/22(五)00:00起A、C區淋浴間暫停使用，請暫至該樓層B區或其他樓層使用。

3.2024/11/23(六)00:00起B區淋浴間暫停使用，請暫至該樓層A、C區或其他樓層使用。

4.施工完成將立即恢復正常使用。

5.施工期間易有噪音產生。

6.造成不便請見諒，謝謝您的合作。

 

 

Male Dorm 1,6th-7th Floor Bathroom Maintenance Temporary Suspension of Use by Sections

Closure Dates: 2024/11/22 00:00 – 2024/11/24

Details:

1.Water barrier maintenance will be conducted in the bathrooms on the 6th  and 7th  floors of Male Dorm 1. To prepare for the work, the bathroom floors need to be completely dried beforehand, requiring a phased suspension of use.

2.From 2024/11/22, 00:00 (Fri.): 
Shower areas in Sections A and C will be closed. Please use Section B on the same floor or bathrooms on other floors.

3.From 2024/11/23, 00:00 (Sat.): 
Shower areas in Section B will be closed. Please use Sections A and C on the same floor or bathrooms on other floors.

4.The bathrooms will be reopened for use immediately after the maintenance is completed.

5.Please note that noise may occur during the construction period.

6.We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your cooperation.

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
