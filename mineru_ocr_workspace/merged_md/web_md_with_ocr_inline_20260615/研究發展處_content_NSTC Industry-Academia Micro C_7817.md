Item
Description





Eligible Institutions
Public and private universities, and public research institutions



Eligibility
Qualifications for Principal Investigator and Co-Investigator must comply with Article 3 of the NSTC Guidelines for Research Project Grants



Funding Categories
- Operating Expenses: Research personnel costs (full-time/part-time assistants, temporary wages, postdoctoral researchers) 
 - Equipment Expenses: Only service-related equipment is eligible; research-related equipment is not covered 
 - International Travel Expenses 
 - Administrative Fees



Execution Period
February 1 to January 31 of the following year (subject to NSTC approval)




 

Project Application and Work Process




Item
Time
Description





1. New Application
May to August (Subject to announcement by the National Science Council)
Submit the first-phase plan online. Applicants who meet the requirements may proceed to submit the second-phase plan.



2. Continuation Plan Report
Before the end of October
Submit the continuation plan report online.



3. Review Stage
November to January of the following year
Includes online review, briefing sessions (presented by applicants), and decision-making meetings.



4. Funding Allocation
First phase: February to April
Second phase: July to September
(Subject to notification by the National Science Council)
The National Science Council will sign a supplementary contract with the executing agency. After both parties sign, funds will be allocated according to regulations.



5. On-site Inspection
March to April (Subject to notification by the National Science Council)
Random inspections based on project execution status to assess implementation and provide improvement suggestions if needed.



6. Results Announcement
May (Subject to notification by the National Science Council)
Joint alliance results presentation.
