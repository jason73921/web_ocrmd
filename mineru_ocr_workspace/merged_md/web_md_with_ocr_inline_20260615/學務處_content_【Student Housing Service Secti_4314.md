# 網站文章資料_【Student Housing Service Section Announcement】Application for Dormitory Living Renewal or Withdrawal_4314

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 4314
- **文章標題**: 【Student Housing Service Section Announcement】Application for Dormitory Living Renewal or Withdrawal
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/st/index.php/tw/?option=com_content&view=article&id=4314
- **文章簡介**: Accommodation period: from the beginning of the first semester of 112 school year (112-2 semester) to the end of the second semester of 113 school year (113-1 semester).  
本次申請之住宿起訖時間：112學年第二學期～113學年第一學期。

Renewal eligibility  續住申請資格：

Foreign students who live in the dormitory from 111-2 semester. 
111-2入住學生宿舍之境外生。

Students with no dormitory violations and point deduction.  
無宿舍違規記點者。

Students who have not paid or are in arrears with accommodation related fees are not allowed to apply.  
未繳或拖欠住宿相關費用者不得申請。




Application time: From 10:00, 2023/12/1 until 17:00, 2023/12/29.
申請時間：2023/12/1 10:00起至2023/12/29 17:00止。

Submission details: Please submit the “Yuan Ze University Accommodation Consent Form” to the Student Housing Service Office (Male dorm, 1st floor, Area C) before 17:00, 2023/12/29. 
資料繳交：「元智大學住宿同意書」請於2023/12/29 17:00前繳交至宿服組辦公室（男一舍1樓C區）。

Payment of accommodation-related fees: Renewal students do not need to repay the deposit, and the accommodation fee will be included into the registration fee of the next semester. Please be sure to complete the payment before moving in.
住宿相關費用繳交：續住生之保證金毋需重新繳納，住宿費則併入下學期註冊單中，進住前請務必完成繳費事宜。

Bed arrangement: For renewal students, you will be assigned with your original bed. If you want to choose the bed by yourself or change bed, please contact Ms. Su at the Student Housing Service Section. 
床位安排：以原床位為原則，如欲自(重)組室友，請洽宿服組蘇小姐。

Deadline for check-out of this semester: 2024/1/13 for Undergraduate students, 2024/1/31 for Graduate students. 
本學期住宿截止日期：大學部2024/1/13，研究生2024/1/31。

If you wish to give up accommodation for 112-2 semester, please fill in the "YZU Student Affidavit for Renouncing Campus Accommodation due to Specified Reason"  and submit it to the Student Housing Service Office before 12/29, and complete the check-out before the check-out deadline for this semester.
112-2如欲放棄住宿者，請填妥「放棄床位切結書」於12/29前繳交至宿服組辦公室，並於住宿截止日前完成退宿。
