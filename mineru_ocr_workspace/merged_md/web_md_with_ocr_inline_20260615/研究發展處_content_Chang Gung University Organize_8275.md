- **文章簡介**: Chang Gung University will launch a 36-hour "2026 CGU STB BIODESIGN Biomedical Innovation and Design Course" this March. Taking the real needs of medical care and patients as the starting point, the course leads participants deep into clinical sites to explore key unsolved problems and develop innovative solutions with clinical value and market potential.

 

Course Features



 Eligible Participants: Faculty members and researchers only. 




Interdisciplinary Team System: Combines different backgrounds such as medicine, engineering, and business management.




Practical Design: Through intensive brainstorming and mentor guidance, participants will design innovative products that meet clinical and industrial needs.




Full Sponsorship: The entire course is free. Those interested in biomedical innovation are welcome to participate.
