- **文章簡介**: The Ministry of Agriculture (MOA) has commissioned the Chinese Management Association (CMA) to organize the "Practical Course on Commercialization of Agricultural Technology" for this year (2025). Please forward this information to relevant personnel and industry professionals under your jurisdiction and encourage active registration.

 



Objective: To enhance the commercialization and development mindset for R&D outcomes among project execution units. The MOA has commissioned the CMA to host this event, featuring experts and scholars with practical experience in industry consultation to share experiences and foster exchange. This aims to guide industry, academia, and research sectors in grasping commercialization keys and overcoming product development bottlenecks.




Target Audience: Priority will be given to teams and cooperative businesses involved in past and future Agricultural Technology Industry-Academia Collaboration Projects, as well as individuals interested in R&D outcome protection, application of patent intelligence, AI technology patenting, or ESG-related topics.




Course Themes and Dates:



Session 1: Protection Strategies for Industry-Academia Collaboration Outcomes (November 12)




Session 2: Impact of Carbon Reduction Trends on Technology Development (November 13)






Location: Classroom A302, Center for Public and Business Administration Education (CPBAE), NCCU.




Estimated Enrollment: 60 participants per session. Registration will close when capacity is reached.




Registration Deadline: From now until November 5, 2025, or earlier if the capacity is filled.




Registration Link: https://reurl.cc/3brM6V




Contact Person: Ms. Liu Yu-Ping, Project Manager, Project Team for Agricultural Technology Industry-Academia Collaboration (Tel: 02-33431119).

## 114 年度農業技術商品化實務課程簡章

## 一、緣起：

近年本部積極推動農業科技創新與產業化，透過「農業科技產學合作計畫鏈結大專院校、法人研究機構與產業界，共同投入關鍵技術研發與應用轉化。隨著計畫成果逐步累積，如何有效保護研發成果、掌握專利佈局，並促進後續商品化應用，已成為推動農業科技成果落地之重要課題。檢視近年輔導與推動過程，多數執行團隊雖具研發能量，惟在成果保護策略之規劃、專利情報之應用，以及ESG導入等面向，仍欠缺系統性認知與實務經驗。

## 二、目的：

本(114)年度旨在協助農業科技產學合作計畫之研究團隊與合作業者，建立研發成果保護與智慧財產佈局觀念，並導入永續發展思維以提升成果商品化能力。透過專題講授與實務案例分享，期能強化共同研發成果之保護策略與專利運用能力，以及掌握 ESG 對技術研發、授權與產業應用之影響，促進成果永續加值與市場拓展。
