Basis: In accordance with the University’s Student Awards Regulations, the University confers Gold Awards, Silver Awards, and Achievement Awards to recognize and encourage students with outstanding achievements.

Application: At the beginning of each semester, students shall submit their applications for recommendation to their respective departments or relevant units within the designated application period. For the 114-2 semester, the application period is February 24 to March 6. Students must apply through their affiliated departments.

Eligibility: Any student who meets the requirements stipulated in the Student Awards Regulations may apply and must submit relevant supporting documents.
