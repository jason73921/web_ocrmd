## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 1118
- **文章標題**: Social distancing in the library
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/tw/social-distancing-in-the-library-2
- **文章簡介**: To help lower the chances of spreading COVID-19, please follow the measures below.

l   Do wear a mask when entering the library.

l   Stay 1.5 meters away from other people, including when studying.

l   A reading table is limited to 2 persons.

l   Be sure to wear a mask when staying in the discussion room and remain the door open to maintain ventilation. Those who are unable to cooperate will lose usage permission.

l   Make good use of the collection of e-books. Have a quick search on the library website or browse the e-book catalog via http://lib.yzu.edu.tw/ERWeb2/ShowEBooks.aspx.

l   The interlibrary cards will suspend from now on. Those who need to borrow books from other libraries should use the Nationwide Document Delivery Service (NDDS) to apply for borrowing and mailing.

~Thank you for practicing social distancing~
