Time: 2023/6/19~8/31

Location: Male 1 Dormitory 1-7th Floors

Project: The Male 1 Dorm 1-7th Floors Replacement of Bedroom Doors.

Notice:

1.Students who stay in the dorm during summer vacation need to move all their items in advance. The bed and transfer time will be announced later.

2.Any damage due to an individual’s failure to comply with the regulations will not be compensated.

3.Students who stay during vacation, please pay attention to the construction staff going in and out. Please bring all personal valuables with you.

4.Entry and exit are prohibited during construction work. Thank you for your cooperation.

 

 

施工日期：2023/6/19~8/31

施工地點：男一舍1~7樓

施作項目：男一舍1~7樓汰換寢室門扇

注意事項：

1.請男一舍同學提前將寢室物品全部清空，暑期留宿床位及轉換時間另行公告。

2.若因個人未依規定而造成受損，不負賠償責任。

3.請留宿同學留意施工人員進出，重要物品務必隨身帶走。

4.施工作業期間禁止出入，造成不便請見諒，謝謝您的合作。
