# Yuan Ze University Regulations Governing the Establishment of Y.Z. Scholarship

Amended and Adopted at the 9th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2014 on 2014.12.10 Amended and Adopted at the 20th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2014 on 2015.04.29 Amended and Adopted at the 2nd Administrative Council of Academic Year 2015 on 2015.08.26 Amended and Adopted at the 4th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2015 on 2015.09.23 Amended and Adopted at the 9th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2015 on 2015.12.02 Amended and Adopted at the 16th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2015 on 2016.04.13 Amended and Adopted at the 3rd Administrative Council of Academic Year 2016 on 2016.09.07 Amended and Adopted at the 9th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2016 on 2016.11.30 Amended and Adopted at the 19th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2017 on 2018.05.23 Amended and Adopted at the 12th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2018 on 2019.01.23 Amended and Adopted at the 22nd Administrative Council of Academic Year 2018 on 2019.07.17 Amended and Adopted at the 5th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2020 on 2020.10.07 Amended and Adopted at the 2nd Administrative Council of Academic Year 2022 on 2022.08.17 Amended and Adopted at the 12th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2022 on 2023.01.04 Amended and Adopted at the14th Administrative Council of Academic Year 2024 on 2025.04.16

Article 1 Yuan Ze University founder Mr. Y. Z. Hsu developed the Regulations to provide scholarship to Yuan Ze University students with academic excellence.

Article 2 The eligibility and amount of admission scholarship or living allowance for baccalaureate programs and master programs (excluding executive master programs) are described below:

1. Students admitted to Yuan Ze University baccalaureate programs via university personal application, university recommendation and screening test or entrance examinations shall be granted for paying their first-year tuition and miscellaneous fees equivalent to those of a national university standard when their performances at the College Entrance Exam in the subjects of Chinese, English and Mathematics finished at the upper half (75%) of all examinees. The aforementioned students who rank top 3 places at class in the academic performance of first year, receiving 80 points in the assessment of personal conduct without receiving grants for overseas exchange funds shall be granted for paying their tuition and miscellaneous fees equivalent to those of a national university standard, too, from the second to the fourth year of enrollment.

2. Graduates of Yuan Ze University students will be granted a grant equivalent to the tuition of one academic year in the first year of admission to the master’s program of the University.

3. Graduates of Asia Eastern University of Science and Technology students will be granted a grant equivalent to one academic year’s miscellaneous charges in the first year of admission to the master’s program of the Yuan Ze University.

4. Non-staff personnel at Yuan Ze University (including university-contracted staff, unit funded personnel, and project personnel), as well as full-time and contracted employees (including contracted assistant personnel) at Far Eastern Memorial Hospital, will be granted a grant equivalent to one academic year’s miscellaneous charges in the first year of admission to Yuan Ze University’s master’s program.

5. The grant, however, may suspend upon the decision of a department (college) affairs meeting approved by the YZU Scholarship Committee considering poor performance of the recipient or other reasons.

6. The amount of aforementioned scholarships shall be determined by the resolution adopted by the review from the Scholarship Committee.

Article 3 The guidelines related to Yuan Ze University doctoral programs (not include of DBA, college of management) admitted with exemption of tuitions are described below:

1. Students with ROC citizenship admitted to the Yuan Ze University doctoral program admission test (including screening test and bachelor/ master’s degree studying directly for doctoral degree) and do not have full time job (who must sign an affidavit letter) shall be exempt for tuition from the first to third year.

2. In case the students of doctoral programs have full-time job, show poor performance or other factors during the period of tuition exemption, as determined by the department (college) affairs meeting, the students will be reported to the Scholarship Committee for approval of reference to cancel the right of tuition exemption.

Article 4 The regulations governing the application and requisition for doctoral financial aids are described below:

1. New students admitted to the doctoral program will be approved at the college meetings for the list of Y.Z. Hsu doctoral student financial aids recipients in the amount of NT15,000 per month, for one to three years, according to the quota allocated for the current academic year. In particular, NT10,000 is a special scholarship and NT5,000is the research funds for teachers.

2. . Doctoral students receiving this financial aid must sign a statement. In case the students hold full-time job during the collection of this financial with proved investigation, Yuan Ze University may claim all scholarships collected during the full-time period and the students will be reported to relevant departments for disciplines.

3. In case the students show poor performance or other factors during the period of collecting the financial aid, as determined by the department (college) affairs meeting, the students will be reported to the Scholarship Committee for approval of reference to cancel the financial aids.

Article 5 The regulations governing the eligibility and amount for applying enrolling student excellence scholarship are described below:

1. Sophomore to senior students from the baccalaureate program having previous academic-year performance ranked in the top 3 places of their class, at least 80 points in conducts while the number of credits taken from previous academic year is at least 30 credits (inclusive) shall be distributed with scholarship of NT10,000 for the first place, NT8,000 for the second place, and NT6,000 for the third place. Unfilled vacancy for students meeting eligibility for certain reasonmay not be refilled.

2. Second-year students of master programs (excluding executive master programs) having previous academic-year performance ranked top 5% or first place per department (degree programs) while the number of credits taken from previous academic year is at least 12 credits (inclusive) and the average GPA of that academic year is at least 80 points (inclusive), shall be distributed scholarship in the amount of NT8,000. Unfilled vacancy for students meeting eligibility for certain reason may not be refilled.

Article 6 Other related regulations are described below:

1. Students from baccalaureate programs conforming to provisions prescribed in Article 2 and Article 5 may apply for only one disbursement.

2. The application and review procedures shall conform to the “Yuan Ze University Y.Z. Hsu Scholarship Examination Rules”for processing.

3. Doctoral students studying for doctoral programs shall pay for the balance of tuition and miscellaneous fees for master program, starting from the semester of voluntary change to master programs.

4. Foreign students, overseas Chinese students and students from Mainland meeting the provisions prescribed in Article 2, 3 and 4, may apply for scholarship if they have not collected other university scholarship and financial aids for that academic year.

Article 7 The Regulations are enacted after the discussion by the Admission Committee with proposal to the Administrative Council for adoption. Any modifications made to the Regulations shall be made in accordance as such.
