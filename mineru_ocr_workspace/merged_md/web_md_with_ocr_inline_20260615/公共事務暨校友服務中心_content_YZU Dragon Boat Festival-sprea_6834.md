- **文章簡介**: The Dragon Boat Festival is one of the three major Chinese festivals. The Office of  Student Affairs at Yuan Ze University, together with volunteer students, organized the Dragon Boat Festival caring activity, "spreading the love with meaningful zongzi (glutinous rice dumplings)."
