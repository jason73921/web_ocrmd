# 2026 圖⽂傳播科技與藝術國際學術研討會

# 徵稿啟事與投稿須知

國立臺灣藝術⼤學圖⽂傳播藝術所將於⺠國 115 年 3 ⽉ 30 ⽇（星期⼀）舉辦「2026圖⽂傳播科技與藝術國際學術研討會」，為推廣學術交流與專業成長，以及增進學術單位的交流情誼，敬邀 貴系所蒞臨指導，並懇請 貴校所協助推廣宣傳，邀請 貴系所之研究⽣積極參與投稿，以擴⼤研討會學術交流之效益。

## 本研討會徵稿領域如下：

1. 印刷適性與發展

2. 影像技術與開發

3. 媒體傳播與趨勢

4. 創意設計與應⽤

5. 圖⽂出版與科技

6. 其他圖⽂藝術創作及設計相關

所有相關的⽂件需以電⼦檔的形式投稿，電⼦檔需包括 Word 與 PDF 格式。請到本研討會網站，透過「投稿」的按鈕投稿，或直接填寫以下投稿表單，並上傳檔案。

# 2026 International Conference on Graphic Communication Technology and Art Call for Papers and Submission Guidelines

The 2026 International Conference on Graphic Communication Technology and Art (ICGCTA 2026) will be hosted by the Graduate Institute of Graphic Communication Arts, National Taiwan University of Arts, on Monday, March 30, 2026. To promote academic exchange and professional development, and to strengthen ties among academic units, we cordially invite your department/ institute to participate and assist with dissemination of this call. We also encourage graduate students from your institution to submit papers to enhance the scholarly impact of the conference.

## Research Topics (including but not limited to):

1. Printability and Development

2. Imaging Technology and Development

3. Media Communication and Trends

4. Creative Design and Applications

5. Graphic Publishing and Technology

6. Other related graphic arts and design

## Submission Method

1. All files must be submitted electronically in both Word and PDF formats. Please submit via the “Submit” button on the conference website (ICGCTA 2026 Website) or upload them through the online submission form.

2. Online Submission Form: https://forms.gle/5YeRuNjTgB4jfNRn6

## Paper Format

1. The conference follows the 7th edition of the Publication Manual of the American Psychological Association (APA).

2. Template and Copyright License Form: Please download the ICGCTA 2026 Template and the Copyright License Form.

3. Abstract: include the paper title in Chinese and English; author name(s); affiliation (institution/school) and title/position; a Chinese abstract of 300–500 characters with 3–5 keywords; and an English abstract with English keywords. Each abstract should be a single paragraph (no paragraph breaks) and limited to one page in both Chinese and English. Please refer to the ICGCTA 2026 Template for details.

4. Full paper: up to 6,500 words, maximum 8 pages. A signed copyright license form must be attached. All submissions must be original (not previously published or under review elsewhere). Duplicate submission of the same manuscript, copyright infringement, and violations of academic ethics are prohibited; authors bear full responsibility for any violations.

## Presentation Format

Authors who pass the two-stage review will be notified of presentation times and sessions via email. Each presentation is allotted up to 20 minutes, including Q&A. A lectern with standard presentation equipment will be provided; each presenter should bring a laptop. For inquiries, please email the organizer at ntua\_gcapaper@ntua.edu.tw.

## Important Dates

1. Abstract submission deadline: Please complete the online submission form by Friday, December 26, 2025. Online Submission Link: https://forms.gle/5YeRuNjTgB4jfNRn6

2. Full paper submission deadline: Please email the full paper files (in Word and PDF) to ntua\_gcapaper@ntua.edu.tw by Monday, January 19, 2026. Email subject format: Submission to ICGCTA 2026\_Research Area\_Name

For any inquiries, please email ntua\_gcapaper@ntua.edu.tw.
