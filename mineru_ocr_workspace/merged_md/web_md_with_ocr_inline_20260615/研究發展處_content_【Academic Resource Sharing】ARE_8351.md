Overview：


This eBook integrates AREE’s digital courses and educational resources on academic writing, along with relevant materials from various external organizations. It is designed to help a new generation of researchers recognize the core values of academic writing and understand the potential and limitations of AI as an auxiliary research tool. By providing practical strategies and resources, this manual serves as an essential reference for the early stages of research, guiding students to build a solid foundation in academic literacy while ensuring their research is both innovative and ethically sound in the era of AI.
