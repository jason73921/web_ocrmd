Application deadline： March 10, 2026

Starting date of employment： August 1, 2026

Qualification for Candidates：


Ph.D. degree in the field of Mechanical Engineering.

Research fields in Automatic Control, Robotics, Smart Manufacturing, and Thermal Fluid Simulation.

Capable of offering undergraduate foundational and advanced courses in relevant fields at the university level. Priority given to the ability to teach courses in areas such as automatic control, mechatronics integration, artificial intelligence, machine vision, programming, engineering drawing, mechanical drawing, applied mechanics, mechanics of materials, or engineering mathematics.


Applications should include：

Candidates are requested to submit the following documents via email (please combine items 2–9 into a single PDF file):

Fill in the document with basic information in the attached  download (另開新視窗) 。

Curriculum Vitae and autobiography.

Photocopy of degree diplomas (foreign degrees must be notarized by the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office; Ph.D. students should submit advisor’s letter with expected graduation date).

Photocopy of transcripts (foreign degrees require notarization as above).

Photocopy of teaching certificate (if available).

Publication list (including journal information).

Research interests (including research plans).

Teaching plans (including course outlines and support information for EMI capability).

Supplementary materials to demonstrate accomplishments (optional).

Doctoral dissertation and certificate.

At least two letters of recommendation (including one from the Ph.D. advisor). Letters must be sent directly by referees via email to  rdijchen@saturn.yzu.edu.tw (開啟郵件程式) 。
