Subject: Taitung University Indigenous Student Resource Center"s Announcement of the "National Indigenous Youth Leadership Development Camp 2023" Event Brochure Held from October 13th to 15th

Description:



Organized in accordance with the Ministry of Education"s "2023 Indigenous Student Resource Center Program for Higher Education Institutions."




To deepen the cultural identity of indigenous students and strengthen their connection to contemporary indigenous issues, this program aims to enhance the understanding of indigenous youth regarding indigenous culture and history. Through the curriculum, it seeks to bring indigenous youth closer to the development and historical context of indigenous communities, fostering talent for participation in indigenous-related affairs.




Participants: Eligible participants include current students from higher education institutions who have attended introductory leadership development camps in various regions or have held leadership positions in on-campus indigenous-related clubs.




The event will take place from October 13th to 15th, 2023, at the Red Brick House in Yilan City (No. 13, Guangfu Road, Yilan City).




Registration for this event is done online at: https://reurl.cc/E1rdoA. Please complete your registration by 12:00 PM on October 6th and check the announcement on the Original Force Network Platform on October 7th.
