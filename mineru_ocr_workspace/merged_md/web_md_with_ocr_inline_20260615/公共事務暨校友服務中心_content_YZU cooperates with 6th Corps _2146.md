- **文章簡介**: Lieutenant General Hsu Yen-pu of 6th Corps Command head 12 Commissioned Officers to visit Yuan Ze University and singed the letter of intent of strategy alliance for cooperation on August 3. With taking responsibility for localization of higher education , YZU will open courses for officers and men in the military of northern Taiwan.

In order to meet the demand for talent development in military, YZU first recommend three school courses including ethical decision making which strengthen military character to make better decision after comprehensive thinking. The course of project management will help students to understand the process and risk aversion. And as for the course of strategy alliance, it focus on the organization and strategy adjusting to be more competitive in the high uncertainty of international environment. YZU will expand more different professional courses for the demand of all crops.
