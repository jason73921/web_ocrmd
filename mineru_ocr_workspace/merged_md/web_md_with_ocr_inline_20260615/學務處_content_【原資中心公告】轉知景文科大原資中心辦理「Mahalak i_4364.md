Subject: Jhongwen University Indigenous Student Resource Center Presents "Mahalak ima" Indigenous Cultural Week - Join Us!

Description:



Jhongwen University"s Indigenous Student Resource Center is organizing the "Mahalak ima" Indigenous Cultural Week. We warmly invite everyone to participate actively.




Event Highlights: (a) "Mahalak ima ": "Cultural Knowledge Challenge," "Traditional Attire Experience," and "Indigenous Cultural Market." (b) "Manauaz 美麗原視界": "Indigenous Culture Night" featuring Indigenous culinary delights, performances by the Daruan Social Group, and performances from friendly schools.




Event Dates, Times, and Locations: (a) "Mahalak ima ": December 12th to December 14th (10:00-15:00), First-floor corridor of the Administration Building. (b) "Manauaz " Indigenous Culture Night: December 14th (18:00-20:00), Fifth-floor Auditorium of the Administration Building.




Attached, please find the event poster and venue map.
