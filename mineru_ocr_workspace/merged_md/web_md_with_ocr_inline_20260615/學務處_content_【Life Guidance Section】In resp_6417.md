- **文章簡介**: A magnitude 6.1 earthquake has occurred in southeastern Taiwan. Aftershocks may continue—please stay safe and take necessary precautions.

If your home suffers severe damage due to a typhoon or earthquake, the school offers the " Unlimited assurance Program" to support affected students and their families in rebuilding their homes and overcoming this difficult time. This program provides subsidies to help students navigate through the crisis and continue their education with peace of mind. The details of the program subsidies are as follows:

1. This applies to formally registered students of the university, excluding students who are extending their studies and professional students in graduate programs.

2. Applicants who meet any of the following conditions may submit an application:
(1) Parents or legal guardians are involuntarily unemployed or laid off.
(2) Households affected by natural disasters.
(3) Families experiencing sudden crisis.
(4) Other special reasons.

3.Applicants must attach the following documents:
(1) Application form. (Include recommendation from relevant counseling personnel.)
(2) Copy of the household registration or a transcript.
(3) Individual Income Tax list and property list in the latest year of family (Including parents and student).
(4) Certificate of involuntary separation or unemployment benefit proof. (required for applying for involuntary separation or unemployment.)
(5) Copy of disaster victim certificate issued by the town/city office or village head, or a casualty certificate issued by a hospital (required for applying for natural disaster subsidies).
(6) Other relevant supporting documents.

4.Application Method and Procedures:
(1) This project is conducted once per semester. Applicants should prepare the necessary documents from the start of the semester and submit their applications to the Student Affairs Office"s Life Guidance Section.
(2) The project liaison will interview the students and assess their overall needs, provide recommendations for assistance, and then submit these to the project evaluation team for consideration and decision based on the specifics of each case.
(3) For other special reasons, applications can be processed at any time.
