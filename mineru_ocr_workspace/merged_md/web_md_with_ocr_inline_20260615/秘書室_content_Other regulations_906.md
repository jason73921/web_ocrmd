## Yuan Ze University Fitness Center Management Regulations

2007.03.19 Passed in the 12th Administrative Meeting of the 2006 academic year

Article 1 These regulations (hereinafter referred to as the Regulations) have been formulated to effectively manage and fully utilize the functions of the University's Fitness Center (hereinafter referred to as the Center).

Article 2 A Management Committee has been established to be the responsible unit in charge of reviewing and supervising the Center's annual plans, budget, relevant management implementation rules, and other relevant matters of importance.

The Management Committee comprises the Dean of Student Affairs, the Vice President of General Affairs, the Secretary-General, the Director of the Personnel Office, the Director of the Accounting Office, the Director of the Physical Education Office, the Chairperson of the Faculty and Staff Welfare Committee, a teacher representative (selected by the President), and a student representative (nominated by the student association). Committee members shall serve one-year terms and can be re-elected to serve consecutive terms.

A chairperson shall be appointed by the President from among the committee members. The Director of the Physical Education Office shall serve as the Executive Secretary and handle committee meeting affairs under the instructions of the Chairperson.

Article 3 The Center was established primarily for education purposes. Students, faculty, relatives of faculty and alumni may apply for membership.

Article 4 A separate set of implementation rules shall be formulated to manage matters such as membership application methods, rules, fee standards, and opening hours. These implementation rules shall be implemented upon approval of the Management Committee.

Article 5 The Regulations shall be announced and implemented upon approval from an Administrative Meeting. The same applies to all subsequent amendments.

# Regulations for the Appointment and Payment of Coaching Fees of External Coaches for the Yuan Ze University Varsity Team

Passed in the 3rd Administrative Meeting of the 2024 academic year on September 25, 2024.

Article 1 In order to enhance the sports culture of our school, professional coaches from outside the school (hereinafter referred to as external coaches) are hired to strengthen sport-specific skill levels and competitive abilities of our varsity teams. These regulations were formulated for this purpose.

Article 2 The selection of external coaches for each varsity team will be reviewed and approved by Physical Education Office meetings and then submitted to the President for approval before being appointed. In principle, the term of employment will be on a yearly basis.

Article 3 External coaches should be responsible for the formation, training, and competition of student varsity teams, as well as the recruitment of outstanding student athletes and other related matters.

Article 4 The following payment standards for coaching fees are based on a minimum of 4 hours of training per week each semester:

I. Coaching fees are calculated based on 4 credits per week, with payments made for 9 months each year.

II. The payment standard for each credit will follow the same rate as the hourly teaching fees for teachers and will be paid according to the coach's position. Coaches with a C-level certification will be paid the hourly rate of a lecturer. Those with a B-level certification will be paid the rate of an assistant professor. Coaches with Alevel certification will be compensated at the associate professor rate, while national-level coaches will be paid the hourly rate of a professor.

Article 5 When an external coach leads a team in external competitions, travel expenses will be paid in accordance with Yuan Ze University’s domestic and international travel expenses standards for faculty and staff.

## Article 6

If a coach fails to actually implement training during their employment or the results are unsatisfactory, damaging the reputation of the school, and if the circumstances are severe, they may be dismissed after review by the Physical Education Office and approval from the President.

Article 7 The Regulations shall be announced and implemented upon approval from an Administrative Meeting. The same applies to all subsequent amendments.
