What is the most interesting part of life? Perhaps, to have a carefree life is everyone thirsts for. Typical art spaces are often quiet and overcautious, or offer arts impenetrable to most of audience. We therefore decide to break the bondage to allow art into our life in an amusing way, starting from our campus. 

The campus is a life field so close to students but also a place most confined to them. Geared by fantasy, we resolve to transform it into a big funny museum of no wall and distance, not just a place only for study and full of stress. By means of subverting the fixed ideas, we mean to turn over our campus into a wonderland with surprises at every corner. With this, we invite 8 artists to present their fascinating art creativity from indoors to outdoors. We look forward to exciting rusted mind and activating potential art DNA of all audience.

Period: April 27 ~ June 23, 2017

Venue: Campus and Aesthetic Path in Library at YZU

Opening: April 27 Thu. 12:00PM 　Grassland by Building 3 at YZU
