Nikki, a Global MBA alumna of Yuan Ze University, shared her journey of adapting to life and work in Taiwan. From communication barriers to cultural differences in the workplace, she highlighted the realities international professionals often face.

Her talk shed light on topics like overtime culture, lunch break habits, and the mutual fear of making mistakes when speaking across languages. Nikki’s story reminded us that career success abroad takes more than qualifications—it requires patience, adaptability, and cultural understanding.
