- **文章簡介**: 2024-2025 YZU Academic Calendar has been amended and approved by the 8th Executive Council Meeting of the 2024 Academic Year on December 18, 2024.

Highlights of the 2024-2025 YZU Academic Calendar Revision:


The event of "End-of-Term Questionnaires for graduating classes" has been removed, and the "End-of-Term Questionnaires" for all classes will now be conducted from May 15 to May 29, 2025.

The event of "Final Examination week for graduating classes" has been removed. The examination has been postponed to the "Final Examination week", scheduled from June 2 to June 7, 2025.

The event of "Deadline for submission of grades for graduating classes", which was originally scheduled on June 9, 2025, has been postponed to June 22, 2025.

To avoid requiring students from other counties or cities to return to school to collect their graduation certificates, the original "Deadline for dormitory check-out" on June 22, 2025 has been revised to " Deadline for dormitory check-out (Graduating students can defer until the Jun 29)".


For further details, please refer to the 2024-2025 YZU Academic Calendar.
