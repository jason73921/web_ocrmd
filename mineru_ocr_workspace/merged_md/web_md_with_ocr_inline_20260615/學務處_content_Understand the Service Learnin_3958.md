- **文章簡介**: There are three ways to accumulate Service Learning hours:


The course in the department or the General Education Service Learning course: Pass the score can get the course hours.

On-campus Service Learning activities (or courses): Clubs or classmates participate in on-campus activities, and give course or activity hours according to the content, and fill in the Service Learning journal within a week after the events, hours will be given depends on the journal and the performance of the activity.

Off-campus Service Learning activities (or courses): The application process please refer to The Approvable Process of Off-campus Service Learning Hours, the Service Learning in Yuan Ze is a compulsory 1 credit course for university students, each student must finish the course hours and activity hours of 18 hours each before graduation.
