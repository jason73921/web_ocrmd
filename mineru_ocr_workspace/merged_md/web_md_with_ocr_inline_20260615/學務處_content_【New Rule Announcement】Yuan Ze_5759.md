# Yuan Ze University Diverse Achievement Certificate: Recording and Certification Guidelines

Ratified at the 1st Student Affair Committee Meeting of the Academic Year on 11 December 2024

Article 1: In order to enhance students’ motivation for independent learning, encourage diverse learning experiences both on and off campus, and foster the development of wellrounded, sustainable talent, and systematically record and certify the learning experiences as an important evidence for further education, employment, or selection for various awards, these guidelines are specially formulated.

Article 2: The Diverse Achievement Certificate records six core competencies acquired by students during their studies, including: Academic Competence, Global Competence, Versatility Competence, Social Competence, Professional Competence and Leadership Competence. Each competency is further divided into subcategories based on university standards. Any additions or revisions to the subcategories must be approved by the Student Affair Committee Meeting before inclusion in the Certificate system.

Article 3: Registration of the Diverse Achievement Certificate is limited to degree students enrolled at the university, and must apply during their enrollment period to qualify for certification.

Article 4: Diverse Achievement Certificate review and certification includes the following three methods:

1. Automatic import of data through internal university information systems.

2. Batch uploads of compiled rosters by responsible administrative units.

3. Individual students uploading supporting documents, which will be reviewed and certified by relevant departments or administrative units.

Article 5: The Diverse Achievements Certificate can serve as supporting documentation for:

1. Applications for various scholarships.

2. Applications for admission to domestic or international educational institutions.

3. Applications for academic research, workplace employment, and internship opportunities.

4. Participation in other selection processes or competitions.

Article 6: These guidelines shall be implemented following approval by the Student Affair Committee Meeting. Any amendments shall follow the same procedure.

If any controversies or disputes occurred regarding the clauses of the regulations, it shall always refer to the Chinese version.
