- **文章簡介**: President of Trustee Board Douglas Hsu gave his speech in Yuan Ze University’s 29th Anniversary and stressed that the school must have the concept of New Economic Integration & broad vision because of rapidly global change. YZU hold sport competition, community art festival, school fair and R&D advancements exhibition. The alumnus Chin Yi Chang and the band ‘Big Change” were also invited to share their story and gave performance.

President Wu Jyh-Yang said YZU has lunched bilingual programs like English courses in each college to cultivate international talents and made programming language as required course in recent two years. All students and faculties are requested to keep learning and obtain competitive advantage.
