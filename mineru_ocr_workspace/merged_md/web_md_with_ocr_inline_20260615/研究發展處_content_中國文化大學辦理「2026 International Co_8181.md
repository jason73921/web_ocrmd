# 2026 International Conference on Global Business

Date: Friday, May 9, 2026

Organizers: Department of Global Business

Venue: Chinese Culture University, Hwa Kang, Yangmingshan, Taipei, Taiwan

Website: https://crbgmba.pccu.edu.tw/p/406-1118-141330,r985.php?Lang=zh-tw

The Department of Global Business at Chinese Culture University (CCU) is committed to advancing scholarly dialogue and exploring innovative perspectives on the challenges and opportunities shaping today’s global business environment. It is with great pleasure that we announce the 2026 International Conference on Global Business, hosted by our department.

This conference brings together researchers, academic scholars, industry professionals, and students from around the world to exchange insights, foster cross-disciplinary collaboration, and ignite forward-thinking discussions. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with distinguished experts, expand professional and academic networks, and explore new pathways for impactful research and innovation.

We are pleased to extend a formal invitation to prospective authors to contribute to the conference through the submission of research abstracts and full papers. We encourage highquality scholarly work that presents original and previously unpublished conceptual, empirical, experimental, or theoretical research relevant to global business. Selected submissions will be featured in conference presentation sessions.

We warmly welcome the participation of academic scientists, researchers, practitioners, scholars, and students worldwide to submit research aligned with the following conference themes:

Conference Themes

• Economics

• Finance and Accounting

• Marketing

• Tourism

• AI and Business Applications

• ESG (Environmental, Social, and Corp. Governance)
