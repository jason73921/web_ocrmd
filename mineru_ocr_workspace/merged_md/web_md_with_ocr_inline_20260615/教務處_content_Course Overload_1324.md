- **文章全文**: Applicant Qualifications






Students with an average score of 80 or more last semester or taking interdisciplinary course programs, a minor or double major may take additional courses for not more than 6 credits in the following semester with the approval of the department/college/program chairperson. The course selection system will automatically increase the maximum credit limit of qualified students by 6 credits, 31 credits in total without the need to apply.






Application Procedure






Students who meet the qualification of Overload Application could add additional credits online during the computer course selection period.






Application Period
