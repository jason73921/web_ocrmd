- **文章簡介**: The Second Integrative Studies, General Studies, and Lifelong Education Seminar, sponsored by Enlightenment & Cultural Creation Association in Taiwan (TECCA) and Microlife Learning Foundation, along with YZU College of General Studies and School of Lifelong Education, takes place at Monarch Plaza Hotel of Taoyuan on September 24. 


Fredrick Chien (錢復), President of Cathy Charity Foundation, gives his speech on reaction to change for Taiwan in the seminar. Shih-Hung Chan (詹世弘), former YZU President, addresses the global energy issue. Sun Ma (馬遜), former President of Huafan University, shares the Wisdom of Success. Yu-Ming Chen (陳右明), Chairman of Chinese Biology electromagnetic Association, delivers his thoughts about the contribution and reflection from contemporary medicine. Hosts for the seminar include YZU President Jyh-Yang Wu(吳志揚), Mao-Chuan Huang (黃茂全), President of Oriental Institute of Technology, and TECCA Chairman Li-Wen Wang (王立文). The guests of honor share a broad range of opinions from global issues to personal health care.
