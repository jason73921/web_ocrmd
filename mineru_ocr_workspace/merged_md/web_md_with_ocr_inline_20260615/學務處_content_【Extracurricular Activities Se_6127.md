- **文章簡介**: Subject: The Ministry of Education has forwarded a notice from the Consumer Protection Committee of the Executive Yuan to safeguard the safety and rights of students participating in off-campus camps during summer vacation. All faculty and students are requested to enhance awareness and promote key precautions regarding such activities.

Details:


This notice is issued in accordance with the Taoyuan City Government’s letter No. 1140198771, dated July 18, 2025.

During the summer break, many private organizations offer various types of camps. To ensure the safety and rights of students during these activities, faculty and students are advised to:

Carefully choose legally registered and certified organizers.

Review the contract terms and the organizer’s commitment to fulfilling their responsibilities.

Pay attention to the organizer’s safety measures and emergency response plans.

Conduct risk assessments.

Promote basic knowledge on safety and emergency rescue measures for outdoor activities, especially those in mountainous or coastal areas, to students and their parents.




Attached is the official letter from the Taoyuan City Government Department of Education for reference.


Student Affairs Office – Extracurricular Activities Section reminds you to stay informed and stay safe.
