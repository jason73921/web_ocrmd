🚀 Want to use AI to transform the way people work?

Cooler Master provides high-performance cooling solutions powering global AI computing. At the same time, we are actively exploring how AI can transform enterprise workflows, productivity, and innovation.

If you are passionate about Engineering × Marketing × Talent Development × Digital Content, we invite you to join the Cooler Master Academy AI Young Talent Program.

During the internship, you will gain hands-on experience in your professional field, while learning how to use AI to optimize workflows, improve efficiency, and design new ways of working.

 

🎓 A Complete AI Practical Training Journey

🔹 Develop Expertise (Hands-on professional training): Gain real-world experience in your field through a learn-by-doing, learn-by-failing approach.

🔹 Workflow AI Transformation: Collaborate with AI consultants and internal AI teams to explore innovative AI-powered workflows.

🔹 Real Business Projects: Work with mentors to complete impactful AI optimization projects within real business environments.

🔹 Demo Day: Present your AI project at the annual showcase and turn your internship experience into a valuable portfolio.

🔥 Apply Now >>> COOLER MASTER AI Young Talent Internship Program

 

🎯 Open Internship Tracks



Engineering / Thermal Technology




Research liquid cooling solutions, evaluate key component performance, assist new product design, 3D modeling (Creo), thermal simulation (FloEFD), and DFM documentation.

 



Marketing / Digital Branding




Explore AI applications in content creation and marketing analytics, EDM production, campaign planning and execution, social media management, and marketing data analysis.

 



Talent Development / Learning & Development (L&D)




Develop AI knowledge assistants and learning content, assist training program planning and delivery, create and manage learning materials, support certification programs, and produce course recordings.

 



Video & Digital Content Creation




Use AI to enhance video production efficiency, create digital visual designs, edit promotional and training videos, and produce multimedia content.

 



Industrial Design Intern




Participate in product design development, conduct brand and market research, perform competitor analysis, contribute to design proposals, create 3D renderings, and assist with graphic design and 3D printing management.

 



Product Management Intern (PM)




Participate in product planning and project management, coordinate between R&D, design, and market needs, conduct industry and product analysis, contribute to product concepts, collaborate with engineering and industrial design teams, and support project coordination and scheduling.
