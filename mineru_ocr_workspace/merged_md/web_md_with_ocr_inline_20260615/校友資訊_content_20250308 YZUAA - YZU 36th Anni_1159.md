- **文章簡介**: YZU 36th Anniversary Celebration – Alumni Association Event Highlights

In March 2025, Yuan Ze University (YZU) celebrated its 36th anniversary with a lively campus-wide event that welcomed numerous alumni back to their alma mater. The YZU Alumni Association specially organized a Family Fun Bouncy Castle Activity and took the lead in inviting alumni from various departments to participate in the Alumni Banquet, hosted by the YZU Alumni Services Center, making this milestone celebration even more memorable.

Alumni Banquet – A Warm Gathering of Shared Memories

Thanks to the Alumni Association’s efforts, many alumni joined the banquet, reconnecting with old friends and sharing updates on their personal and professional journeys. The atmosphere was warm and joyous, filled with laughter and nostalgia, creating unforgettable moments of camaraderie.





Exciting Family Fun Trampoline Activities

In the afternoon, the Alumni Association hosted Family Fun Trampoline Activities in front of Building 7, where alumni and their families gathered for an energetic and joyful experience. The event was filled with laughter and excitement as children and adults alike enjoyed bouncing, playing, and making wonderful memories together. The activity saw enthusiastic participation from both local and international alumni, bringing a diverse and vibrant energy to the celebration.





Strengthening Connections & Looking Ahead

This event not only strengthened bonds among alumni but also created precious moments for families to enjoy together. The YZU Alumni Association is committed to continuing its collaboration with departmental alumni groups, organizing engaging events such as alumni reunions, family-friendly activities, sports challenges, and corporate visits. With a focus on fostering connections and opportunities, the association looks forward to building an even more dynamic and enriching alumni network.
