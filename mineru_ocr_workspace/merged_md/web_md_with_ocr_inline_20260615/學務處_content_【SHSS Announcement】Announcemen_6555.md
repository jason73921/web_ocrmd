- **文章簡介**: Subject: Announcement of Priority Housing Application for Domestic Students for the 115th Academic Year

Explanation:

1. Important Notes:

(1) Students applying for on-campus housing are required to stay for two semesters and pay the annual housing fee in two installments (except for students without official enrollment status).

Early termination requires prior approval. Without approval, the housing deposit and relevant housing fees will not be refunded. Exceptions may be granted for approved special circumstances (e.g., graduation, suspension, withdrawal, transfer, internship, exchange, or force majeure).

All dormitory rooms accommodate four students. Applicants are advised to carefully consider their adaptability to communal living and their physical and mental health conditions before applying.

(2) Applicants must prepare the Student Housing Agreement Declaration Form in advance. Approved applicants must submit the signed form to the Student Housing Service Section within the announced deadline; otherwise, the housing eligibility will be forfeited.

(3) Approved applicants must personally check in. Impersonation is strictly prohibited. Violators will have their housing assignment revoked and be subject to university regulations.

2. Eligibility:

Students enrolled in the 115th academic year who meet the requirements of Article 7 of the Yuan Ze University Student Dormitory Management Regulations may apply. Dormitory priority is granted in the following order:

(1)Students with pre-approved housing priority: dormitory committee members, network administrators, and university sports team members.

(2) Students verified by the Office of Student Affairs:


Those with mobility limitations or special medical conditions

Students living alone in Taiwan (including outlying islands)

Students from financially disadvantaged families

Students with special circumstances approved by the Office of Student Affairs

Children of active-duty military personnel


3. Application Period: March 9 to March 12. Late applications will not be accepted.

4. Application Procedure: Submit the completed Priority Housing Application Form, along with supporting documents, to the Student Housing Service Section.

(1) Students with physical disabilities or special medical conditions:

 Submit relevant medical certification documents.

(2) Students residing alone in Taiwan (including offshore islands):

 Submit a copy of the Household Registration Transcript for the entire household. The registered residence must have been established for at least six months.

(3) Students from financially disadvantaged families:

 Submit a Low-Income Household Certificate issued by the local township/city/district office. Certificates issued by village or neighborhood leaders will not be accepted.

(4) Students with special circumstances approved by the Office of Student Affairs:

Submit supporting documents relevant to the special circumstances.

5. Announcement of Review Results: March 18, 2026 (Wed.).

6. Submission of Dorm Deposit and Student Housing Agreement:

March 19 – 23,2026.

(Students are required to download the payment slip and complete the payment. Detailed instructions will be announced on March 18.)

7. On-Campus Housing Subsidy Program(Domestic Student ONLY):

To reduce students’ housing expenses and support their academic studies, the Ministry of Education will implement the On-Campus Housing Subsidy Program for University and College Students starting from February of the 113th Academic Year. ## 本人於住宿期間，願意履行並遵守下列住宿規定。

I hereby guarantee that I fully understand and agree to the commitments listed below. I also pledge to abide by all school regulations and dormitory rules throughout my stay in the dormitory for one academic year (two semesters). I take full responsibility for any violations that may occur.

## 一、承諾配合事項 Commitments

1.本人同意住宿學生宿舍兩學期且繳交兩學期住宿費，除特殊原因如畢業、休學、退學、轉學、實習、出國交換及不可抗力原因經核可者外，均不申請退宿、退費。

I agree to stay in the student dormitory for two semesters and pay the dormitory fee accordingly. Except for special circumstances such as graduation, suspension, withdrawal, transfer, internship, exchange abroad, or force majeure, I will not request early check-out or a refund.

2.本人已確實了解住宿費按年計算，分兩學期繳納，並已充分考慮自身對團體生活的適應能力及健康狀況是否適合住宿。在簽署住宿申請切結書後，除非經學校核可，兩學期內決不無故退宿。若期中違約自願放棄住宿，則同意繳納剩餘住宿費。

I understand that the accommodation fee is calculated on an annual basis and is payable in two semesters, and I have fully considered my ability to adapt to group living and whether my health condition is suitable for dormitory accommodation. Unless approved by the school, I will not withdraw from the dormitory within the agreed period. If I voluntarily terminate my stay mid-term, I agree to pay the remaining accommodation fees.

## 3.同意寒暑假住宿須另行申請及繳費（續住之研究生除外）。

I agree that staying during winter and summer breaks requires separate applications and payments (excluding Master and Ph.D. students).

4.同意2,000 元保證金將於辦理退宿驗收項目合格後無息歸還，驗收項目：住宿期間是否違規、床位是否清空整潔、退宿手續是否完成等。

I agree that the NT\$2,000 security deposit will be refunded without interest after successfully passing the check-out inspection, which includes but not limited to compliance with dormitory regulations, ensuring that my bed space is cleared and cleaned, and completing all check-out procedures.

## 二、配合事項 Cooperation

1.本人已看過且瞭解本校「學生宿舍管理規則」、「學生宿舍生活須知」以及宿舍相關規定，願意於住宿期間確實遵守各項規定，本人若有違反事項，願意坦然接受懲處。

I have read and understood the University's “Student Dormitory Management Rules”, “Dormitory Rules and Regulations”, and dormitory related regulations, and I agree to comply with all rules during my stay. I accept any penalties for violations.

2.配合公告進/退宿時間，宿舍清館時間以及辦理離/退宿程序。

I will adhere to the announced check-in/out schedules, dormitory closure times, and required procedures.

I agree to cooperate with any dormitory arrangements or relocations made by the university due to campus development needs or special circumstances and will complete the required relocation within the specified timeframe.
