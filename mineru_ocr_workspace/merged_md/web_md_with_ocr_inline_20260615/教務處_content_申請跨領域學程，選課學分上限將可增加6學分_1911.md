主旨：申請跨領域學程，選課學分上限將可增加6學分

說明：

一、114-1學期第一階段選課將於5/2開始，只要至個人Portal>學習檔案>跨領域學程>申請選修學程，並經各系學程承辦人員審核通過後，選課學分上限，將可增加6學分(原25學分加6學分，上限增加為31學分) 。

二、各系、院皆已開設「微學程」(8學分) ，亦很容易修習完成，即可領取「微學程」證書。

三、其他相關事項，請參閱：元智首頁/學校行政業務/教務處/學生專區/學程申請跨領域學程一覽表( Interdisciplinary Course Program)

課務組敬啟

 

Academic Affairs Office Announcement

Announcement date： 2025/05/28

Subject：By applying for interdisciplinary courses, the maximum credit limit for course selection will be increased by 6 credits.

Content：

1.The stage of course selection ( Phase Ⅰ ) for the 114-1 semester begins at 12 p.m. on Friday, May 2nd. To apply for elective programs, simply go to your personal Portal > Learning Profile > Interdisciplinary Programs> Applying for elective programs. Once approved by the coordinators of respective departments, the credit limit for course selection can be increased by 6 credits (from the original 25 credits to a maximum of 31 credits).

2.Each department and college has introduced "Micro Programs" (8 credits), which are easy to complete as well. Upon completion, you"ll receive a certificate for the "Micro Program."

3.For further details, please refer to: Yuan Ze University Homepage/Administration/Office of Academic Affairs/Student Resource/ Application Program/The List of Interdisciplinary Program Application (Interdisciplinary Course Program).
