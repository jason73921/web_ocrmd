Office of Academic Affairs

Subject: The 2021 Graduate Program Entrance Examination will take place on March 2. The Curriculum Adjustment of Building 1 is as followings;

Statement:

According to the resolution of the Admission Affairs Committee, Graduate Program Entrance Examination will be held on March 2, 2021 (Tuesday).

1.   The Classes, in Building 1 from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. on March 2, 2021, will be suspended. The classes after 6 p.m. in Building 1 will be held as usual.

2.   The Classes in other buildings on March 2, 2021 will be held as usual.

Curriculum Section, Office Academic Affairs
