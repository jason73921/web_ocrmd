- **文章簡介**: To accommodate students from central and southern Taiwan returning home, Kuo-Kuang Bus Zhongli Station is offering a special discount program for students traveling to Taichung and Kaohsiung. (Students heading to Nantou, Puli, Chiayi, Tainan, and Pingtung may transfer at Chaoma Station.)

Program Details:


Groups of 10: 1 person rides for free.

Groups of 20: 3 people ride for free.

School pickup services are available upon request.


Students in need of this service may contact Guoguang Bus Zhongli Station using the information below.
