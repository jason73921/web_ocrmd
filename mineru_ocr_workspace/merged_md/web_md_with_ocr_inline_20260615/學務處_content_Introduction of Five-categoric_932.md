Origin: Besides the academic grades, the activities passport of five-categorical Grades of Student Activity of basic education, the system of five areas of basic education, and the records of five areas of basic education will give you another transcript.  

Since 1997, “the Transcript of Five-categorical Grades of Student Activity f basic education of YZU university” is promoted, and function of the passport is just like “National Park Passport” in USA(To record traveling memory by stamping on the passport, and the action could let citizens be willing to travel National Park). In addition to, when student is still studying in the school, the record covers all academic, programs, services, performing arts and sports activities. On the academic side, the records include that students participate in school special campaign. For example, the records include paper award, design competition, scholarship, and other excellent performance. In activities, the records include that the growth of students participating in activities on campus or outside the school. In services, the records include that students participate in volunteer work. In performing arts, the records include that students’ accomplishment in talent and skill. In sports, the records include that students’ accomplishment of participating in any sports campaigns.
