- **文章簡介**: Q：When can newly-admitted students check their course schedule?

A：

1. The compulsory courses of newly-admitted students are arranged by Curriculum Section. Courses of 2022 Autumn Semester will be arranged on 23rd August, 2022. It can be checked in the ”Portal / Learning Profile / Course Schedule”.

2. Please select courses on line during the period of Stage III on-line course selection for other vacant slots besides the arranged compulsory courses.

3. Do not drop the arranged compulsory courses.

4. If the compulsory courses which are arranged by Curriculum Section students have applied for exemption of credits or had taken before, students should apply for dropping course themselves during the period of course selection.

5. When accessing the Course Selection System, enter yours account (student ID: s*******) and PIN number (Change your PIN number for the first-time users).

Q：How transfer students select courses?

A：

1.Please select courses on line during the period of Stage III on-line course selection.

2.Please drop course on-line for those courses that have applied for exemption of credits during the period of course selection.

3.If transfer students wish to select courses over course capacity, for professional courses please contact departmental offices. For other general courses please contact unit that opens the relevant courses.

 4. When accessing the Course Selection System, enter yours account (student ID: s*******) and PIN number (Change your PIN number for the first-time users).
