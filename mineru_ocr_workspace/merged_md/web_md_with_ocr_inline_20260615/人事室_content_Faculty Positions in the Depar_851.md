## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 851
- **文章標題**: Faculty Positions in the Department of International Bachelor Program in Informatics, YZU
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/faculty-positions-in-the-department-of-international-bachelor-program-in-informatics-yzu
- **文章簡介**: We are currently looking for new Faculty member to join the Department of International Bachelor Program in Informatics. Click here for more information

Position: tenure-track/ non-tenure track assistant professor or above
1. Start date: August 1, 2025
2. Openings: 1 position
3. Qualifications and expertise.
A) Ministry of Education in Taiwan recognized doctorate degrees in Computer Science/Engineering, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, or related fields from local or abroad.
B) Excellent research performance;
C) Enthusiasm about teaching and serving, team spirit, ability to cooperate in harmony with coworker.
D) Expertise in VLSI Design, EDA, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, Software Engineering, UAV system related fields.

E) We sincerely welcome applications from foreign teachers with the above expertise.
F) Ability to teach/instruct undergraduate and graduate level courses in English.
4. Application deadline: Please submit the application materials to indept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw before April 15, 2025, and please specify in the email subject: Application Form for Faculty - (Name).

5. Required documents:
A) “Basic Information Form for Recruited Teachers of the Department of International Bachelor Program in Informatics, Yuan Ze University”
B) Resume and autobiography;
C) Statement of research and teaching interests (including brief description of research interests, projects participated, teaching plans of courses, and future research directions);
D) A copy of the highest degree certificate (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit a graduation diploma verified by the ROC overseas official office; for those who have not obtained the graduation diploma can submit the instructor’s certificate indicating the expected date of graduation);
E) A copy of the highest degree academic transcript (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit the transcript verified by the ROC overseas official office);
F) A copy of the official teacher certificate of the current rank (exempt if not yet obtained the certificate);
G) A copy of publication(s) and list (including patents) within five years (must indicate the source);
H) Letter of recommendations (at least two and the referrers should email the letter to indept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw ).

6. Contact information:
Secretary: Mr. Ho
Phone: (+886)3-4638800 ext. 7721
Fax: (+886)3-4638907
E-mail: indept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw
Website: https://www.in.yzu.edu.tw/en/

Note:

1. Candidates who pass the primary selection will be notified for an interview in English and the whole process will be recorded.

2. Please provide the following documents for the interview:

A) A separate “Teacher Qualification Accreditation Form” (in addition to completing the online form on the Ministry of Education’s Academic Review Committee website, candidates must submit both the hardcopy and digital files)

B) Candidates with a foreign degree must provide a “List of study status” and “Immigration records” (please contact the Immigration Department). Please refer to the website of our personnel office:
https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/en-us/downloaded-forms
