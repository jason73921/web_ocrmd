Jeffrey Sebastian Reyes Melgar holds a bachelor’s degree in Information and Communication and a master’s in Mechanical Engineering. He currently leads digital innovation as Director of Digital Development at Shane Group and holds a patent in robotic design.

During the session, he shared key strategies for building a successful international career. His core message—“Be an impact player. No one owes you a job.”—challenged participants to take initiative, communicate effectively, and create real value. His insights provided a clear and inspiring roadmap for career growth across cultures and industries.
