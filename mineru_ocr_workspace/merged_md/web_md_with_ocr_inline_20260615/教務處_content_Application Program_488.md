## Program Application Steps

Online registration: Portal→ Learning files→ Interdisciplinary Course Program→ Registration of favorite program

All programs adopt a pre-registration system, that is, register first and then take courses. Students who want to take program shall apply for registration with the responsible unit of each program within the prescribed period of time for each course, and shall not take programs until they are approved.

Online registration: Portal→ Learning files→ Interdisciplinary Course Program→ Registration of favorite program.

![](images/c884bc6558173a1cc37b9db16940fcccb794213503f10d72a3508adc4390a2c6.jpg)  
Students are encouraged to plan your course selection in advance.

All students who have completed the required courses of each course will be awarded program certificates.

For details, please refer to the program list of each program.
