Diligence Scholarship



2025/09/08~09/26

Process according to the Department announced schedule



1.Applicants’ average grade meets the passing standard, and the minima of moral conduct grade of 80 in the last semester. (Yu-Ziang Scholarship winner not eligible)

2.One of the following conditions must be met.

(1) Low-income households, economically disadvantaged students

(2) Students who have experienced sudden family crises

(3) Students who have experienced disaster-affected households

(4) Students with disabilities or their children (with a family income of less than 900,000 New Taiwan Dollars in the previous year and total real estate value not exceeding 6,500,000 New Taiwan Dollars)

(5) Indigenous students (with a family income of less than 900,000 New Taiwan Dollars in the previous year)

(6) other students with special circumstances



Apply to the Department (The application materials are based on the announcements of each department)

1. Yuan Ze University Diligence Scholarship Application Form

2. Low-income family certification

3. Transcript

4. Data on Various Incomes and properties of the whole family

5. Other Relevant Verification Documents
