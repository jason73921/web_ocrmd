104 Job Bank is one of Taiwan’s largest and most trusted employment platforms, connecting job seekers with employers across diverse industries. To assist international professionals, it offers job listings and resources tailored for non-Chinese speakers.

The platform provides bilingual support and career resources to help foreign professionals build resumes, navigate the local hiring process, and grow their careers in Taiwan.

For more information, visit the official 104 website (Chinese) or the English site for expats. You can also click here to search for internships or part-time jobs to find specific opportunities.
