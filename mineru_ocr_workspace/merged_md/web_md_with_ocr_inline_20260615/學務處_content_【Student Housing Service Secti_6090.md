- **文章簡介**: Announcement Date: 2025/6/19

 

1.During summer vacation(6/29-8/29), dorm will provide hot water from 19:00 to 21:00 everyday.(there will be announcement for any adjustments). Students are advised to shower within this time period. If not, please go to the following locations to use the electric heater. Please remain and repect the residents’ privacy. Thank you.

 

　Dormitory electric water heater’s location

　　Male Dorm I, 1st floor, section B: 3 shower rooms

　　Male Dorm I, 2nd ~7th floor, section B: 1 shower room each floor

　　Female Dorm I, 1st Floor: 5 shower rooms, near R2105

　　Female Dorm I, 2nd ~9th floor, south area: 2 shower room each floor

 

2.When the water heaters are used continuously, it takes time for them to reheat. Please check the temperature gauge on the water heaters and evaluate the acceptable temperature before using to avoid insufficient hot water.

3.The water heaters have limited water storage capacity. If each person consumes too much water, subsequent people’s usage will be affected. Please save water and use it cautiously.

4.If the electric water heater is broken, please contact Student Housing Service Section or fill in the online reparation form in the portal immediately.
