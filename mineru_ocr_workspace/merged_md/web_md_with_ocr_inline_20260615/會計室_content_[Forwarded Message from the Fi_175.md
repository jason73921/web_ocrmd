- **文章簡介**: Explanation:
 When any unit holds activities in the name of YZU or uses university resources (such as classrooms, venues, or equipment) to provide services, any income collected must be deposited into the YZU bank account.

Please also issue an official receipt for each payment to ensure proper control of financial transactions. Units that do not follow these procedures will be responsible for any issues that arise during reimbursement or auditing.

Examples of such income includ:


Registration fees for conferences, workshops, or camps

Venue rental fees for classrooms or conference rooms

Equipment rental fees

Fines or penalties set by the unit

Other income that should be recorded as university revenue


Thank you for your kind cooperation.

 

Finance and Property Management Section
