Contact TAIWAN is an official talent-matching platform created by the Ministry of Economic Affairs and managed by TAITRA. It aims to connect international professionals with job opportunities at Taiwanese companies.

The platform not only shares updates on Taiwan’s hiring policies but also offers consulting services and organizes recruitment events both in Taiwan and overseas to support global talent attraction.

You can learn more on the Contact TAIWAN official website or follow their updates on the Facebook.
