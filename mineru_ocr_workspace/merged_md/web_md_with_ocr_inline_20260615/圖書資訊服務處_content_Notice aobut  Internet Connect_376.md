- **文章簡介**: Dear University members:

Today (6/15), we found that the Internet connection is unstable, because of the center of TANET"s equipment failure. In order to avoid this problem, please apply proxy setting in your browser. Thank you.

Office of Library and Information Services

To apply setting of proxy in your browser, we remind you of taking action as follows:


Open Internet Explorer

Click on Tools

Click on Internet Options

Click on Connections

Click on the LAN Settings button

Make sure that the checkbox next to Use automatic configuration script is checked

In the Address text field, type in http://proxy.yzu.edu.tw/proxy.pac

Click on OK

Click on OK
