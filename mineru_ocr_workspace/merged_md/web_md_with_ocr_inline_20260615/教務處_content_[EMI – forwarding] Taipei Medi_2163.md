The Bilingual Education Promotion Center of our university (hereinafter referred to as “the Center”) will organize a series of EMI faculty development activities during the Spring Semester 2026. In the spirit of resource sharing and collaborative learning, we sincerely invite faculty members from your institution to participate in these activities.




The program features a diverse range of topics. In addition to EMI-related courses, it also covers ESAP (English for Specific Academic Purposes). Topics include AI-assisted course design, high-impact EMI course design, integrating inquiry-based learning into EMI courses, and ESAP course design. For detailed registration information, please refer to Attachment 1: Event Information, as well as the posters for each session.
