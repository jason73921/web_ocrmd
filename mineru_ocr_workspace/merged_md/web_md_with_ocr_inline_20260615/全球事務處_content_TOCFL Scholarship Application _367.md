# 網站文章資料_TOCFL Scholarship Application Before May 15th_367

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 367
- **文章標題**: TOCFL Scholarship Application Before May 15th
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/gao/index.php/tw/tocfl-scholarship-application-before-may-15th
- **文章簡介**: Who can apply for the scholarship? 誰可以申請華語獎學金

 

1.      Foreign students who have passed the TOCFL before 3rd grade of the bachelor’s degree, 1st grade of Master’s degree and 2nd grade of Doctor’s degree can apply for the scholarship.

外籍學位生於大學三年級、碩士一年級以及博士二年級之前參加TOCFL成績符合補助標準者。

 

2.      The application will be accepted only if the certificate issuance date is between May 15 2023 and May 15 2024 (The certificate obtained before entering Yuan Ze University will NOT be accepted.)

成績證書發放日期須在2023.5.15至2024.5.15之間。 (於入學之前取得之證書不予受理)

 

3.      Each student can apply for TOCFL scholarship only for once.

每位學生僅能申請一次

 

How to apply? 如何申請

 

Submit the TOCFL certificate, score report and the student card to Global Affairs Office (R70207) BEFORE May 15.

於五月十五日前繳交TOCFL證書、成績單正本以及學生證至全球事務處(R70207)

 

How much can I get? 獎學金金額

 

·         A2  1000NT

·         B1  1500NT

·         B2  2000NT

·         C1  2500NT

·         C2  3000NT

 

Know more details on GAO website 了解更多資訊請見:
