NSTC General Research Project (Batch)
Deadline announced via NSTC notification (around late December). Apply online via NSTC Academic Research Service Portal.
For multi-year projects: submit progress report online by end of May each year. Submit final report and complete financial closing within 3 months after project ends.
Ms. Lin
(ext. 2288)



NSTC New Faculty Research Project
Eligible applicants (newly appointed or Ph.D. awarded within 3 years) may apply anytime. One application only. Apply online and submit C301 & C302 forms to the Office of R&D.
Submit final report and complete financial closing within 3 months after project ends.
Ms. Lin
(ext. 2288)



NSTC College Student Research Project
Deadline announced via email (around March). Apply online via NSTC Undergraduate/Master Student System.
Submit final report and complete financial closing within 1 month after project ends (around end of March).
Ms. Lin
 (ext. 2288)



NSTC Defense Technology Academic Cooperation and Other Call-for-Proposal Projects
Deadline announced by NSTC via email. Apply online via NSTC Academic Research Service Portal.
Submit final report and complete financial closing within 3 months after project ends.
Ms. Lin
(ext. 2288)

Bilateral Collaboration – Joint Research Projects
International collaborative research projects under bilateral agreements, typically submitted during NSTC"s annual general project call. Examples: Taiwan-Italy, Taiwan-Spain, Taiwan-Russia joint research programs.

 (ext. 2289)



EU Framework Program Projects (FP6-FP7)
Open for application at any time. Research teams must join EU-based consortiums to apply for FP programs. These projects involve complex procedures, rigorous review, and extended approval timelines.
Ms. Chang
 (ext. 2289)



General Grant Applications

Special Programs
