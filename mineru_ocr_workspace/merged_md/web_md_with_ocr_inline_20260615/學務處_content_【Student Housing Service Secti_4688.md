Construction Dates: 2024/04/26~04/30

Construction Time: 8:00~17:00

Location: Reading Rooms in Areas A and C on the 4th Floor of Male 1 Dorm

Notice:

1.The reading rooms will be temporarily unavailable during the construction period. Students are required to clear their personal belongings before 3:00 PM on April 25 (Thursday).

2.Students should be aware of construction workers moving in and out and must ensure that their room doors are locked when leaving.

3.Noise may be generated during the construction period, which may cause inconvenience. We ask for your understanding and patience.

4.Normal use of the reading rooms will be restored immediately after the completion of the construction.

5.We apologize for any inconvenience caused and thank you for your cooperation!

 

施作日期：2024/4/26~4/30

施工時間：8:00~17:00

施工地點：男一舍4樓A、C區閱讀室

注意事項：

1.施工期間閱讀室暫停使用，請同學於4/25(四)15:00前將個人物品清空。

2.請同學留意施工人員進出，離開寢室時，房門務必上鎖。

3.施工期間易有噪音產生，造成同學困擾，請多包涵。

4.工程完畢將立即恢復正常使用。

5.造成不便敬請見諒，謝謝合作！

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
