Description:



Our EMI Resource Center (hereinafter referred to as “the Center”) was officially established in 2024 in alignment with the “Bilingual Nation 2030” policy and the promotion of EMI courses. By integrating the teaching strengths of both technological and general universities, the Center provides diverse instructional resources. The Taiwan Tech Talk (TTT) National Bilingual Presentation Contest aims to motivate students through diverse activity formats and encourage them to express personal experiences in a foreign language, transforming language learning into a creative and meaningful practice.




Contest Information: (1) Eligibility: Undergraduate and graduate students currently enrolled in universities and colleges nationwide, including exchange students (2) Categories:   a. Performative Storytelling   b. Stand-up Comedy   c. Shout-out EMI TA
