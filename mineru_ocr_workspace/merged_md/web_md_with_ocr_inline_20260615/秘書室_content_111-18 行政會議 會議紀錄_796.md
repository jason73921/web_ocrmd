## 111-18 行政會議摘要 Executive Summary of Minutes

一、不調整本校112學年度學雜費收費標準 The amendment to “retention of tuition and miscellaneous fees of 2023 academic year” was approved.

二、通過本校「內部控制委員會組織辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “the regulations on organizing the YZU internal control committee” was approved.

三、通過本校「學生懷孕受教權維護及輔導協助要點」新訂審議案 The draft for “upholding the rights of pregnant students to education and provision of counseling service” was approved.

四、通過本校「組織規程」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “organization regulations of YZU” was approved.

五、通過本校「教職員員額編制表」修正審議案 The amendment to “table of personnel quota for faculty and staff” was approved.

六、修正通過本校「學生紀律規範與處理程序實施辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU Implementation Regulations of Student Disciplines Code and Processing Procedures” was approved.

七、修正通過本校「學生團體平安保險要點」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “recruitment details for group student insurance ” was approved.
