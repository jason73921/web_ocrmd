主旨：公告111學年度英語學士班學生轉系時程及相關事項。

說明：

        一、申請日期：111年5月16日(星期一)起至5月23日(星期一)止。

 

        二、適用對象：

             (一)英語學士班學生申請轉入所屬學院各學系。

             (二)同一學院學生申請轉入英語學士班。

                       ◎上述不含管理學院大學部及管理學院英語專班。

      三、英語學士班學生申請轉入所屬學院其他學系，申請以一次為限。

      四、轉系標準由各學系自訂，請參閱大學部轉系審查標準表或至所屬學院各學系洽詢。

      五、相關表單與流程：

           (一)表單

              ✽英語學士班轉系申請表

              ✽元智大學學生申請轉系自我規劃分析表

              ✽歷年成績單

          (二)流程

                學生填妥表單→轉出學系經辦人簽章→轉出學系主任簽章→送教務處註冊組彙整

 

                遠距教學期間申請人如無法親自送件，可掃瞄申請資料Ｅmail寄交原學系核章。

     六、相關事項及表單請參閱：元智大學/學校行政業務/教務處/學籍相關/英語學士班申請

Notice: Procedures for students of International Bachelor applying for Change of Major of 111 School Year (2022)

I.  Application period: Form Monday, May. 16th to Monday, May. 23th, 2022

II.  Applicable objects:


Students of International Bachelor applying for changing major into Departments of the same College.

Students of the same College applying for changing major into International Bachelor.


        * not applicable for English Bachelor of Business Administration College of Management

III. For students of International Bachelor applying for changing major into Departments of the same College, 

      no application shall be submitted more than once.

IV. Students applying for change of major shall satisfy rules and meet requirements of the accepting department/program.

     Please see the following link for reference: YZU Criteria for Reviewing Applications for Change of Major大學部轉系審查標準表

V. Application procedure:

      Fill-in the application form → get signature from the person in charge of change-major affairs of the current department

       → get approval from the chairman of the current department → submit to Registration Section of Office of Academic Affairs

VI. Please see the following link for further information about change of major:

   YZU → Administration → Office of Academic Affairs → Students’ Resource → Students of International Bachelor applying for change of major
