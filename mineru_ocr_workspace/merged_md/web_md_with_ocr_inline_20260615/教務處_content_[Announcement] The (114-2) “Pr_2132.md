## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 2132
- **文章標題**: [Announcement] The (114-2) “Professional Independent Learning Program” application is open until March 20, 2026.
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/announcement-the-114-2-professional-independent-learning-program-application-is-open-until-march-20-2026
- **文章簡介**: Application Method:


Download the application form from the Office of Academic Affairs – *Professional Independent Learning Program* website.


Application Process:

Apply individually or in teams of up to five. Invite a full-time faculty member as your advisor. * For competition projects, follow team size rules. 




Take the “Introduction to Self-Directed Learning” course offered by the College of General Studies and prepare a project proposal.




Submit the application form and required documents directly to the Office of Academic Affairs for review and approval.




Complete your project and submit the final report for credit recognition.* Credit recognition will follow List of Compulsory & Elective subjects and Minor





Application Deadline:


From now until **March 20, 2026 (Friday)**

 

For more details, please visit the **Office of Academic Affairs – Professional Independent Learning Program** webpage.
