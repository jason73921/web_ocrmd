## 113-15 行政會議摘要 Executive Summary of Minutes

一、通過本校「系所重新調整辦法」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “YZU regulations for readjusting departments/colleges” was approved.

二、通過本校「研究生助學金設置辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations for grants awarding for graduate students” was approved.

三、 通過本校「學生校外實習辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations governing practicum interns” was approved.

四、通過本校「半導體研究中心」設置討論案 The establishment of the “semiconductor research center” was approved.

五、通過本校「國際專修部學生修業辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU academic regulations of international foundation program” was approved.

六、通過本校「計畫暨預算審查委員會組織辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to the “YZU regulations for the organization of the university's project and budget review committee” was approved.

七、通過 本校「內部稽核實施辦法」條文修訂審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations governing implementation of internal auditing” was approved.

八、通過本校「發言人制度辦法」法規廢止審議案 The abolition of the “YZU spokesperson system regulations” was approved.

九、本校「教職員工出差旅費報支規定」修正審議案 The amendment to “revision of the regulations of YZU faculty and staffs’ reimbursement of Business Expenses” was approved.

十、本校「教師校外兼課及兼職辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations for faculty lecturing or holding concurrent positions at other institutions” was approved.

十一、 本校「環境保護暨職業安全衛生委員會設置辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “measures for the establishment of the environmental protection and occupational safety and health committee of YZU” was approved.
