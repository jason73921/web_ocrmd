# 圖書館寒假開館時間/借期彈性展延公告Winter 2024 Library Hours / Extension of Loan Periods

借期弹性展延：凡借阅（含原始借書、續借、召回）到期日介於113.1.15\~113.2.18，且符合下列彈性展延條件者，到期日一律自動展延至113.2.19

資料類型：限圖書、書後附件、跨校借書證

讀者身份：限本校教職員生

注意事項：預約取件期限不適用展延，請依正常借閱規則辦理。

寒假開館時間：請參閱以下行事曆。

All checked-out items with due dates between Jan. 15th and Feb. 18th,2024, and meeting the following conditions are subject to loan period extension.

Borrowers: YZU students, faculty, and staff.

Material types: books, accompanying materials, and interlibrary cards only \*The extended due date of the winter loan will be on Feb.19th,2024.

Note that borrowing non-affiliates and the materials not mentioned above should follow the regular loan policy. For more information, refer to the calendar below.
