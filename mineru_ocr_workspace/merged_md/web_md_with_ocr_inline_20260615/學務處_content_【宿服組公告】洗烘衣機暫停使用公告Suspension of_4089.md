Announcement

Due to the renovation work in the female 1st dormitory bathrooms during the summer break, the regular cleaning and disinfection ofwashing and drying machines has been postponed until next week. The washing and drying machines in the dormitorywill be temporarilyoutof service duringthe construction period.

1.Construction Date: 9/20/112

2.Construction Location: Bathrooms on floors l-9 andthe basement of Women's Dormitory Building 1.

3.The washing and drying machines in the women's dormitory will be temporarilyunavailable during the construction period. We apologizefor any inconvenience andappreciae your cooperation. Thank you!
