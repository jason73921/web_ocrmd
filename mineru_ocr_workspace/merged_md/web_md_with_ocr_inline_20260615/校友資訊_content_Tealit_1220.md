Tealit (Teaching English and Living in Taiwan) is an online information platform that provides resources for foreigners in Taiwan, especially those seeking English teaching opportunities. The platform includes job postings, housing information, language exchange listings, and practical guidance for everyday life in Taiwan.

It is commonly used by international job seekers exploring opportunities in Taiwan’s private education sector.
