Subject: Invitation to "The 9th Original Young Singing Competition - Moonlit Mihumisang" at Chia Nan University of Pharmacy and Science

Details:



We cordially invite everyone to participate in the "9th Original Young Singing Competition - Moonlit Mihumisang" organized by Chia Nan University of Pharmacy and Science.




The competition aims to foster friendship and cultural exchange among schools through singing, utilizing the invisible power of music to strengthen students" confidence and sense of belonging. It also seeks to promote indigenous cultural identity in various higher education institutions and enhance the cohesion of student organizations. This year"s theme is "Moonlit Mihumisang - Welcoming the First Encounter with You."




Event Information: (a) Date: December 6, 112 (Wednesday), 6:00 PM. (b) Venue: T Building Auditorium (No. 60, Section 1, Erren Road, Rende District, Tainan City).
