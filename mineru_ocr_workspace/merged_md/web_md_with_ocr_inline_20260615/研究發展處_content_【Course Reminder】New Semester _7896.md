# 網站文章資料_【Course Reminder】New Semester Notice: Academic Ethics Training Required for First-Time NSTC Project Participants_7896

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 7896
- **文章標題**: 【Course Reminder】New Semester Notice: Academic Ethics Training Required for First-Time NSTC Project Participants
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/rd/index.php/tw/?option=com_content&view=article&id=7896
- **文章簡介**: In accordance with NSTC and university regulations, all newly hired full-time or part-time research assistants participating in an NSTC research project for the first time must complete 6 hours of academic ethics training within 3 months from the start date of their appointment and submit the course completion certificate to the Office of Research and Development for record-keeping.


How to Take the Course:

Please complete the course via the Center for Taiwan Academic Research Ethics Education (AREE) online platform.

Master’s and Ph.D. students: Account will be applied for by the Office of Academic Affairs.

Undergraduate students: May register individually or contact the Office of R&D for assistance.




Important Notice (Effective from August 11, 2025):

“Required students” and “required faculty/research staff” must complete account verification before their first login.

Accounts will be locked after 1095 days of inactivity (previously 730 days).

For full details, please refer to the AREE official website: https://ethics.moe.edu.tw/news/detail/406/




If you have any questions, please contact the R&D Office staff. (Ms. Wu / monicakirei0122@saturn.yzu.edu.tw )
