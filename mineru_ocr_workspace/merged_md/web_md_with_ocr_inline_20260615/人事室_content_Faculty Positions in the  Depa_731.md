## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 731
- **文章標題**: Faculty Positions in the  Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science 
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/february-29-2024
- **文章簡介**: The Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science at Yuan Ze University invites applications for faculty positions at the Assistant, Associate or Full Professor level commensurate with qualifications.

Qualification of Candidates


D. degree in Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology, Material Science or closely related fields.

Areas of expertise: Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology and Materials Science or related fields.

Outstanding academic achievements, enthusiasm for teaching, service, teamwork, and English teaching ability.


Closing date: 29 February 2024 at midnight.

Candidates should send their applications, including


Teacher Qualification Resume Form and Future Plan.


 (download https://www.che.yzu.edu.tw/upload/files/Teacher%20Qualification%20Resume%20form.docx )


Curriculum vitae including a publication list.

Courses that can be taught and future research directions (refer to relevant course information on the department"s website).

Certified photocopy of education diploma ( foreign diploma should be verified in advance by the overseas office, Republic of China (Taiwan))

Transcript of graduate study (fresh Ph.D. only)

Copies of three most representative publications

At least two letters of recommendation

Any supporting materials


To the following address by February 29, 2024. Please also upload the electronic file of application information to https://forms.gle/BLDTxraJRDFhfZhM6  .
