- **文章簡介**: 1、Level Position : Assistant Professor or above.

2、Quota : 3.

3、Beginning date of employment : Aug. 1, 2025.

4、Qualifications : Obtained a PhD degree in electrical engineering from domestic or foreign institutions, with expertise in electrical, electronic, or information fields (outstanding researchers in related fields may also apply). Must possess a strong passion for teaching and service, teamwork spirit, ability to collaborate harmoniously with others, and proficiency in English for teaching.

5、Documents required for application include following items: (Deadline: Feb 28, 2025. Review of applications will begin on receiving and continue until the position is filled)


Personal resume, autobiography (including educational background and professional experience from university onwards, and areas of expertise).

A photocopy of the highest academic degree certificate. 【For those with foreign degrees, please submit a graduation certificate verified by an overseas representative office. Those who have not yet obtained their graduation certificate may submit a provisional degree certificate instead.】

Yuan Ze University Faculty Qualification Form https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/en-us/downloaded-forms.

A photocopy of the transcript for the highest academic degree..

Representative works within the past five years (please include the full text of journal articles) and a catalog (indicating the sources). Include past research and significant achievements.

Two letters of recommendation (preferably one from an academic advisor if possible). Recommendations can be sent electronically by the recommender via email.

You can find information on courses and future research directions at the following website: https://portalfun.yzu.edu.tw/cosSelect/Login.aspx?Version=EN This site provides details on required and elective courses by department.

Electronic full text of doctoral dissertation .

Any other supporting materials.
