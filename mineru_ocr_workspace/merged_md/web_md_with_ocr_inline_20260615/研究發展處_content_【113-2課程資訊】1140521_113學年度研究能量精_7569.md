#MyLifeInANutshell

研發處邀請本校化材系邱冠雄教授分享他的學術研究經驗，邱教授曾榮獲青年學者獎及全球前2%頂尖科學家等殊榮，他將以英文分享他的研究心得。研發處誠摯邀請新進教師及碩、博士生報名參加。

The Office of R&D invites Professor KHOO KUAN SHIONG from the Department of Chemical Engineering & Materials Science to share his research experience. Prof. Khoo has received numerous prestigious awards, including the Young Scholar Award and recognition as one of the world’s top 2% scientists. In this talk, he will be sharing his research insights in English. Newly-recruited faculty, as well as Master’s and Ph.D. students, are warmly encouraged to register and attend.
