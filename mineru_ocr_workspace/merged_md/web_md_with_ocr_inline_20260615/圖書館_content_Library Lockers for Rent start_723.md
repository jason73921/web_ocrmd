- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/tw/2017071802
- **文章簡介**: A limited number of student card-based lockers are available for rent in the Library. The rental fee is NT$450 per semester and the rental period starts from July 18, 2017 to Jan 15, 2018.

Beginning on July 18, applications for rental will be accepted at the Library Circulation Desk (ext. 2321) on a first come, first served basis. Any YZU students with valid library privileges and in need of storage for personal belongings are encouraged to act soon. Do present a valid student card at the Library Circulation Desk and pay any fee at the time of rental. Be noted that one locker can be shared by two students.

For relevant regulations, please refer to  https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/en-us/2016-03-15-03-49-54/facilities#Facilities05
