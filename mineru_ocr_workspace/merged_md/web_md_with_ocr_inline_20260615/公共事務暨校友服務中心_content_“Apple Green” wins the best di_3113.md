A total of 15 teams from USA, Taiwan, China, Japan and Malaysia get together in the Yuan Ze University for the final 48 Hours Contest of ASIAGRAPH Reallusion Award hold by YZU and Reallusion Inc.

Two teams instructed by Prof. Shih-Ming Chang, from YZU Dep. Information Communication, are selected in the final round and YZU is the only school who has two candidates in the world. In the final, the animation “Apple Green”, made by Yung-Chieh Yang, Po-Hsing Lee & Wei-Hong Cho, is awarded for the best director with the prize of NTD 90,000.
