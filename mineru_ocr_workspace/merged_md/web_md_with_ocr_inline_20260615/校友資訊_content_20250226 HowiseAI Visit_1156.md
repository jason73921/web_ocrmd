- **文章簡介**: YZU Alumni Association – February 2025 Corporate Visit

On February 26, 2025, the YZU Alumni Association organized a corporate visit to HowiseAI, a leading AI technology company located in the Aba Innovation Hub at Linkou Startup Terrace. The visit aimed to provide alumni with firsthand insights into AI innovation and its future development trends.

Professional Insights & AI Innovation Applications

The event was hosted by HowiseAI CEO, Ms. Ru-Ying Chang, a proud YZU alumna from the Department of Information Management. Through her engaging and in-depth presentation, participants gained a comprehensive understanding of how AI is transforming industries and driving innovative solutions.Ms. Chang introduced the latest AI research and real-world applications developed by HowiseAI, emphasizing AI’s critical role in industry upgrades and digital transformation.



Encouraging Industry-Academia Collaboration & Technology Exchange

Drawing from her entrepreneurial journey, Ms. Chang encouraged alumni to stay engaged with emerging technologies and actively seek opportunities for collaboration with startups. She highlighted how industry-academia partnerships can accelerate the practical application of AI technologies, fostering innovation and contributing to industrial advancement.

Event Highlights & Future Outlook

This corporate visit provided a valuable learning and networking opportunity for alumni, strengthening connections and laying the groundwork for future collaborations between academia and industry.The YZU Alumni Association remains committed to organizing more corporate visits and learning activities, offering alumni enriched platforms for engagement, and exploring limitless possibilities in technological innovation together.
