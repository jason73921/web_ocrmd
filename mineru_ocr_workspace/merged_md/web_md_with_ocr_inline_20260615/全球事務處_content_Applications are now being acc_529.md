- **文章簡介**: Applications are now being accepted for the Student Exchange Program for Semester 1151 (Fall 2026).


Schedule: Starting now until March 16, noon (12 PM)
|Please note that the deadlines vary among schools. Kindly refer to the following information and the list of the exchange school (https://reurl.cc/AbrQvQ).|
The Ohio State University, USA: January 28, 4 PM
Akita International University, Japan: February 10, 4 PM
Schools who have noted "Early Nomination" at Column B: February 24, 5 PM



Process:



Complete the online application: YZU Student Exchange Program Online Application for Semester 1151 (Fall 2026)

Send your Necessary Documents to Global Affairs Office (R70208) by the deadline.


Necessary Documents ( Printed Copies )


Application form for exchange program- YZU Outgoing Exchange Allocation Wish List (Link)

Transcript with Ranking (Chinese version)

Transcript (English version)

English Certificate of Proficiency within two years


Documents submitted after the due date will not be accepted.


The session on the Student Exchange Program for semester 1151 (Fall 2026) will be held on March 11, 2026, from 12:20 to 14:00. Please await registration notifications.

School information

List of the partner schools (Please use a computer for better viewing, or rotate your phone to landscape mode.)




Dual Degree Program

List of Dual Degree Programs

The dual-degree /joint-degree program will invite partner schools to hold the info sessions in the future. Please await registration notifications.




Application Procedure (Link)

Please be reminded that,

Exchange Students would not need to pay to the partner schools but need to pay the total tuition fee for our school.

If you are funded by the scholarship (offered by YZU, MOE Taiwan, MOFA Taiwan, ICDF Taiwan, Elite Scholarship Program, …etc.) should not participate in exchange programs without giving up your scholarships at the semester. Scholarships for the semester you return to YZU will be determined based on your grade from the previous semesters, including the credits transferred from the semester abroad.

You must follow the academic office"s schedule and pay your tuition fee on time or your eligibility for the exchange program will be canceled.




If there are any concerns from your side, please feel free to contact us.
