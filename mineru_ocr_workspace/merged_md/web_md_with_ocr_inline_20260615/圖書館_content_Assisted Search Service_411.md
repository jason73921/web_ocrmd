When unable to locate a book belonging to the Main Library with STATUS “on shelf”, patrons can fill a search request online for Missing Items through in-Library computers.

How to use the Online Found Service

Step 1: Click on 【Search Request】within the Catalog book record

Step 2: Enter Account Number and Password

Note:

After searching for the requested items four times, patrons will be notified of the outcome via email.

How to check the status of your request: enter online Account Services
