Warm greetings from Yuan Ze University!

If you want to join the Alumni Association of Yuan Ze University (hereinafter abbreviated as AAYZU), please fill in the application form, and return the payment certificate (ATM transfer) by email (alumni@saturn.yzu.edu.tw) or fax (+886-3-4637488), the alumni card will be sent to the mailing address you applied, thank you!

 

Eligibility for membership


All graduates from YZU are eligible to apply for membership.

All the faculty and staff of the faculties of the YZU or those who have studied at YZU may apply for membership.

Those who have made special contributions to the Association or YZU, and those approved by the management meeting are honorary members.

Members should fill in the membership application form when applying, and fulfill the obligations of the membership.


 

Membership fee standard


Perennial members: annual membership fee of 500 yuan (need to pay once a year)

Permanent membership: permanent membership fee of 5,000 yuan (lifetime)

The first time entrants need to pay a entrance fee of 200 yuan


 

Payment method


Financial card transfer payment

Far East Bank account number: bank code-805 account number 00400100005296


 

If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Tsai from Office of Public Affairs and Alumni Service Center, Yuan Ze University, thank you!


Contact Us
