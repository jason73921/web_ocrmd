床位轉換日期：2023/7/25~7/26

暫住床位分配：（連結）

注意事項：

一、為配合寢室門扇汰換工程，請男一舍同學提前將寢室物品全部清空，並於7/25-7/26轉換至暫住床位。

二、若因個人未依規定而造成受損，不負賠償責任。

三、請留宿同學留意施工人員進出，重要物品務必隨身帶走。

四、施工作業期間禁止出入，造成不便請見諒，謝謝您的合作。

五、返回原床位時間另行公告，暫住床位進／退宿請至男一舍櫃台辦理。

 

Time: 2023/7/25~7/26

Temporary Bed Allocation: <Link>

Notice:

1. For the dormitory door leaf replacement project, students in the male 1 dorm are requested to clear all dormitory items in advance, and move to temporary beds on 7/25-7/26.

2. Any damage due to an individual’s failure to comply with the regulations will not be compensated.

3. Students who stay during vacation, please pay attention to the construction staff going in and out. Please bring all personal valuables with you.

4. During construction, entry and exit are prohibited. Thank you for your cooperation.

5. The time to return to the original bed will be announced separately. For temporary bed entry/check-out, please go to the male 1 dorm counter.

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 

Student Housing Service Section
