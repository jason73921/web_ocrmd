Teachers


Sign in to Teams


1. How do I sign in to Teams?

Please use your Portal account to sign in to Teams, for example: portal@saturn.yzu.edu.tw




2. How do I prevent my account from being remembered on a public computer?

After signing out of Teams, remove the organization account. When signing in again, choose “No, sign in to this app only.”





Course Management


1. How do I view members?

Go to “Manage team” to view team members.




2. How do I add a channel?

Select “Add channel” within your team.

1. How do I sign in to Teams?

Create a Team


1. How do I create a team?

Click “Join or create a team,” select a class, and enter members to create the team.
