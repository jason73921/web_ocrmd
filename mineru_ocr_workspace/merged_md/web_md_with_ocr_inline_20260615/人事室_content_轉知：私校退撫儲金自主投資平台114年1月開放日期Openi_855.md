- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/zhuan-zhi-si-xiao-tui-fu-chu-jin-zi-zhu-tou-zi-ping-tai114nian1yue-kai-fang-ri-qiopening-date-for-the-private-school-retirement-fund-self-investment-platform-in-january-2025
- **文章簡介**: 主旨：有關私校退撫儲金自主投資平台將於114年1月11日至15日開放「教職員專戶」庫存部位調整，請查照。

說明：

一、為讓教職員於1月份人生週期年齡配置轉換之餘，亦能轉換庫存部位，本會於114年1月11日至15日開放自主投資平台「教職員專戶」庫存部位調整功能。

二、若教職員欲於114年1月份轉換庫存部位，請善加利用。

三、請協助公告並轉知所屬教職員。

To: Relevant Personnel

This is to inform you that the Private School Retirement Fund Self-Investment Platform will open from January 11 to January 15, 2025, for adjustments to the inventory positions in the "Faculty and Staff Special Accounts." Please take note of the following details:

Explanation:


In order to allow faculty and staff to make adjustments to their inventory positions during the conversion of their life cycle age allocation in January, the platform will open the "Faculty and Staff Special Account" inventory position adjustment feature from January 11 to January 15, 2025.

If faculty and staff wish to adjust their inventory positions in January, please make use of this opportunity.

Kindly assist in announcing this and informing all relevant faculty and staff members.
