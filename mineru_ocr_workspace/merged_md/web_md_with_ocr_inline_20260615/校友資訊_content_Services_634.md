 Library Service


To obtain a library privilege card, you need to submit your application along with a guarantee fee of NT$3,000 to the loan desk of the library. (Member of AAYZU can be exempt from paying the guarantee fee)

The library privilege card is valid for one year after the date of issuing the card; upon its expiration, you must have your card renewed to re-validate it.

Library privilege card holders are allowed to borrow a maximum of 10 books at a time. Books may be checked out for a 30-day loan period and may be renewed once if there is no reservation made for them.

The guarantee fee can be refundable when the library privilege card is discontinued and there is no violation of Article 8 and Article 9 of YZU Library Borrowing Guidelines.

Yuan Ze University Library Official Website


 

 Free Mail Service


The email accounts will be retained when the students graduate.

The email account is named as "s + student number + @mail.yzu.edu.tw.

If you forget your password, please call +886-03-4638800 ext. 2321 during office hours (Monday to Friday 08:00~16:30) to verify your identity, the staff of the Office of Library and Information Services will make the change for you.


 

 Venue Reservation


For venue reservation, please directly contact the Office of General Affairs.


 

 Wi-Fi Application


If you need to use the campus Wi-Fi, please fill in the "Alumni Wi-Fi Application Form" and send it to proffice@saturn.yzu.edu.tw.

This account is only for alumni to apply, please provide complete information to confirm your identity.

It takes about one week to verify Wi-Fi Application, we will get back to you by E-mail.

One person can only apply for one account. The valid period is limited to one season. After the use permission expires, a new application is required.
