- **文章簡介**: We sincerely invite all students to actively participate in the annual Bread Festival at Yuan Ze University on October 23rd (Wednesday). The theme this year is "A Heart That Isn"t Big Can Embrace All of You," symbolizing the emotions of wholeheartedness and inclusiveness towards one another. This is not only a major event on campus but also an important platform for promoting emotional exchanges among resident students and creating beautiful memories together.

 

On the day of the event, a wide variety of delicious breads will be available for everyone to purchase. Additionally, we will continue the traditional "Bread as a Message of Love" activity, where you can convey your feelings to that special someone through symbolic bread. Participants can write heartfelt cards expressing their blessings and care for those who matter while enjoying exciting performances by various clubs, creating a warm and affectionate atmosphere throughout the evening.

 

1) The Bread Festival may generate higher noise levels. We will do our best to control the volume to minimize any impact on you, but there may still be occasional inconveniences. We appreciate your understanding and tolerance!

Date：2024/10/23 (Wednesday)

Time：15:00~22:00

Place：Joy Square

 

2) The Bread Festival will allow visitors of the opposite gender to enter the dormitories, which may cause some inconvenience. We appreciate your understanding!

Date：2024/10/23 (Wednesday)

Time：19:00~20:00
