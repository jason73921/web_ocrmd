## 禁止吸煙 No Smoking

近日住宿生反應常有菸味，依《校園菸害防制法》規定，大專院校全面禁止吸菸 O

本校為無菸校園，請全體師生確實遵守相關規定，避免觸法，共同維護宿舍與校園環境。

如經查獲，將依宿舍管理規則第二十條第三款規定，將扣15點，請同學務必遵守規定，防止觸法。

Recently, residents have reported frequent cigarette odors in the dormitories.

In accordance with the Campus Tobacco Hazards Prevention Act, smoking is strictly prohibited at all colleges and universities.

Yuan Ze University is a smoke-free campus. All students and staff are required to comply with the relevant regulations to avoid legal violations and to help maintain a clean and healthy dormitory and campus environment.

If a violation is confirmed, 15 points will be deducted in accordance with Article 20, Paragraph 3 of the Dormitory Management Regulations. Students are urged to strictly follow the rules to avoid penalties.
