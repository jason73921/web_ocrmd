## Q: doES A NEw CLuB HAVe ITS OWn DedICated oFFICe Space?

A: Sorry! The allocation of club offices is based on the evaluation results of clubs. After allocating offices to clubs in the top categories, such as "Excellent" and "A," the remaining available offices will be shared with other clubs depending on the availability.

![](images/28c6ac04639449ec3f6aa0e8d2446490a905cda9750c6e1f82162c557766d21e.jpg)

## Q:ARe THeRe ANY PenaLTIeS IF A cLub ACCIDENTALLY DISSoLVES?

A: No! It is not easy to dissolve a club if it is managed properly. Yuan Ze University has a system that does not hold clubs accountable for their successes or failures. Therefore, if a club encounters operational difficulties and dissolves, the club's responsible person will not receive any disciplinary actions or penalties.

![](images/9dae6db11f14296f8564e6bf3f6754ebdb3d5d4188286df1c47346684096dc66.jpg)

## Q: can a New club ParTIciPaTe In The EVALUATION?

A: Yes! As long as it has the capabilities, all clubs have the opportunity to shine and be evaluated.

![](images/94ff424644aea55b9ca7d3026046a86793eb5ad88541e81edcc36d1253c68f8f.jpg)

## Q:DoeS THe EXTRACuRRICuLArACTIVITIES SECTION FORCeclubS To dISSoLve?

A: Clubs operate independently, and the Extracurricular Activities Sectiondoes not interfere unless the following circumstances occur, which may lead to reorganization or dissolution of the club:

1.Violation of laws, regulations, public order, or good morals.

2.Contradiction with the organization's purpose or activities not aligned with its objectives.

3. Engaging in improper activities under false pretenses.

4.Obtaining a "D" grade in club evaluations for two consecutive years.

5. Having no responsible person for two consecutive years.

## Establishment of a new club

## Sources of law

Yuan Ze University Guidelines for Extracurricular Activities

## Establishment Conditions

• The purpose of the club establishment must be appropriate.

• There are no other similar or identical clubs within the university.

• The number of initiators must be at least ten.

## Prepare the following application documents (please download from the website):

• Club Establishment Application Form

• Qualification Form for Club Leader

• Qualification Form for Club Mentor

• Club Constitution

![](images/bb6cc1b549cc229cce94932e0a90caae7c971aa8af04de02609254a21603a883.jpg)

• Club Activity Calendar

• List of Committee Members

![](images/ecfdacd4545cd4c406bab53c7b7a4c3d97345a15a5a5a217417e05efe4d490ab.jpg)

## Submit the hard copy of the application documents to the Extracurricular Activities Section before the deadline.

(The application deadline will be announced by the Extracurricular Activities Section.)

Submit the application case to the Student Activities Committee for review.

## Dissolution of Club

![](images/0fe6c26306a613c38ff1225ea152ff43cd6855f07c62d2d0fa089f168e48eb4d.jpg)

Convene a general meeting of club members to discuss and make a resolution, with the approval of the club's mentor.

Complete the "Student Club Report" form along with the minutes of the general meeting, and submit them to the Extracurricular Activities Section.

After the approval of the Section supervisor, submit the form for final approvall.

## Approved

• Arrange for the removal of personal belongings and property from the club office.

• Return the club's public property (equipment provided by the Extracurricular Activities Section) and club office keys to the Section.

## Not approved

The club will be included in the focus assistance list for the next semester (or year) by the Extracurricular Activities Section.
