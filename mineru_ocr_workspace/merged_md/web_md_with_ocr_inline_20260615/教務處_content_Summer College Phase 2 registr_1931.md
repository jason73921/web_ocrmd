- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/summer-college-phase-2-registration-starts-may-14-cross-university-credit-opportunities-this-summer
- **文章簡介**: Dear Students,

The 2025 Summer College offers 43 exciting credit-bearing courses from 27 universities across Taiwan. With class locations throughout different regions of Taiwan, you can attend courses near your hometown during summer break—and flexible options like live online classes are also available.

Insects and the Life of Mankind introduces the fascinating ways insects shape our world—from ecology and culture to crime scene investigation. You’ll raise insects, observe them outdoors, and visit bee and silkworm centers. It’s hands-on, eye-opening, and fun!

Dance Appreciation takes you into the world of movement and meaning. Through performances, studio visits, and real-life encounters with dance companies, you’ll explore how dance connects to history, culture, and society. A perfect start for anyone curious about the performing arts!

Three of the courses are conducted entirely in English: Application and Health Function of Spices and Herbs (distance learning, synchronous), Fundamentals of English Writing (in-person instruction), and General Psychology (in-person instruction).

The second phase of course selection will follow a first-come, first-served basis, and opens on Wednesday, May 14 at 10:00 AM, continuing until two weeks before each course begins.
  Why not take this opportunity to enroll in one or more general education courses and make your university journey even more enriching and colorful this summer?Here are some important details to note:


Tuition Refund Policy: The fee is NT$700 per credit. Students holding Low-Income, Lower-Middle-Income, or Financial Hardship Certificates and who pass their courses are eligible for a full refund of tuition fees (excluding transaction fees if not using our partner bank).

Curious about the course content? Join our pre-course seminar to find out more: https://www.n2.org.tw/enter/news/detail/280


To access further information and resources, please refer to the following links:

Sincerely,
