- **文章簡介**: To prevent the spread of the novel coronavirus and protect the health of the faculty, staff, and students, Yuan Ze University will delay the opening of the spring semester by two weeks to March 2nd and the end of semester will be postponed to July 3rd with total of 18 weeks.
