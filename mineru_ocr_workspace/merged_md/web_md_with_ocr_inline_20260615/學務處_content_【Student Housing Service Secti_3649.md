Time: 2023/6/19~8/31

Location: Female 1 Dormitory 1-9th Floors

Project:

　1)Female 1 dormitory 1-9th floors renovation of bathrooms and toilets.

　2)Female 1 dormitory 1-9th floors replacement of bedroom doors.

　3)Female 1 dormitory 5-8th floors replacement of bed sets.

　4)Female 1 dormitory 9th reconstruction of the kitchen.

Notice:

1.Female students who have applied to stay in dorms during summer vacation must move to female dorm 2 by 17:00 on 6/19 (Mon.). The bed will be announced later.

2.Please take away all your items in the bedroom, restroom, kitchen, and refrigerator on the 1-9th floors. Any damage due to an individual’s failure to comply with the regulations will not be compensated.

3.Students who stay during vacation, please pay attention to the construction staff going in and out. Please bring all personal valuables with you.

4.Entry and exit are prohibited during construction work. Thank you for your cooperation.

 

 

施工日期：2023/6/19~8/31

施工地點：女一舍1~9樓

施作項目：

　1)女一舍1~9樓整修浴室廁所

　2)女一舍1~9樓汰換寢室門扇

　3)女一舍5-8樓汰換床組(R2504~R2820)

　4)女一舍9樓廚房拆除重設

注意事項：

1.女生暑期申請留宿者於6/19(一)17:00前暫移宿至女二舍（床位另行公告）。

2.女一舍1-9樓曬衣間、廚房、冰箱及寢室物品須全部淨空，若因個人未依規定而造成受損，不負賠償責任。

3.請留宿同學留意施工人員進出，重要物品務必隨身帶走。

4.施工作業期間禁止出入，造成不便請見諒，謝謝您的合作。

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
