CALL FOR SCHOLARSHIPS 2023 - 2024: INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS FOR THE CERTIFICATE IN ENGLISH AS A SECOND LANGUAGE PROGRAM (CESL) AT THE UNIVERSITY OF BELIZE (UB)

## OVERVIEW

## The University of Belize ESL Scholarship Program Overview

The University of Belize, in partnership with the Ministry of Education and Cultural Science and Technology,hereby announces its annual scholarship offer for an intensive certificate program in English as a Second Language. This ten-month certificate program is offered at the prestigious Regional Language Center of the University of Belize from January to December.

The university offers this program on behalf of the Government of Belize, which remains the scholarship program sponsor. The government of Belize covers all tuition, program fees,and required teaching materials and provides a monthly stipend to defray housing expenses. The Regional Language Center (RLC) of the University of Belize has a long history of providing individualized services and a learning environment that maximizes the learner's chances for cultural immersion, language practice, and personal development.

## PURPOSE

## The ESL Program at the RLC

RLC is Belize's mechanism for bridging the language barriers in the region. The RLC offers many specialized foreign language courses in English and other languages to cater to the requirements of our national and international clients. The 10-month English as a Second Language Program is designed to advance the English competency of those who enter the program,regardless of their current proficiency. Interested individuals with basic to advanced competence in English may aply.

The ten-month CESL program focuses on strengthening learners'proficiency in reading,writing, listening,and speaking skills in English. AlI RLC language instructors emphasize practice and design coursework to encourage a practical application of knowledge; this includes a mandatory 60-hour language internship program for eligible students.

During the first week of classes,all new students will be required to attend an orientation session on the expectations and methodology of the program. Each student will be assigned an academic advisor to offer advice and orientation throughout their studies. At the end of the ten-month program, students will receive a certificate of completion from the University of Belize after completing the course.

## INTENDED BENEFICIARIES

This scholarship offer is only available to MEXICO, CUBA, HONDURAS, EL SALVADOR, COLOMBIA, PANAMA, VENEZUELA, and ROC TAIWAN citizens.

## Eligibility

Must be a México,Cuba, Honduras,EI Salvador, Colombia, Panama, Venezuela, or ROC Taiwan citizen.

•Applicants must be 18 years old upon application.

• Must commit to 10 months of living in Belize from January 2024 to December 2024.

Applicant must have a minimum of a High school certificate.

Commit to studying full-time in Belize without the possibility of employment or part-time studies.

## Entry Requirement

1. Complete a UB/RLC application form and an application fee of US \$15.00 (this fee may be waived for some applicants; please inquire at rlcinfo@ub.edu.bz before submitting your application).

1. Basic to Intermediate competence in English as a Second Language.

2. Able to commit to the program.

## Documentation requirements

1. Completed UB/RLC application form.

2. Updatedurculmvaranladtolisitiiotagg.

5. Copy of the passport and birth certificate.(translated to English)

6. 6 passport-size pictures (these can be taken in the country upon arrival)

## Program specific requirements

CESL Students will be required to have an English dictionary in hard copy or digital format, and it is recommended to have a translator /translation dictionary, recording devices, headphones, and a laptop.

## Stipend

Each scholarship recipient is paid a monthly stipend of \$600.00 in Belize dollars (US\$300.00) to help defray living expenses. This stipend is a gift from the Government of Belize and is disbursed by the University of Belize to the students on the first Wednesday of each month. The first stipend in January will be disbursed after registration.

Students who accept the scholarship must do so with the full responsibility of having additional monthly income to supplement their stipend and meet other living expenses.

The stipend is enough to cover your rent and utility services. Scholarship recipients must know that if they spend beyond the stipend,the university cannot add to the students'monthly stipends or waive or reduce university costs. This stipend is only for students from outside of Belize; recipients living in Belize are not eligible for the monthly stipend, only tuition and program fees.

貝里斯大學區域語文中心  
![](images/8c18b3f7335e4639a9279716a9756da6619d2ff1ecdb469e1b7a524a98172e39.jpg)

2023-2024年「英語課程獎學金計畫」

## 概述

貝里斯大學「英語課程獎學金計畫」概述

貝里斯大學(University of Belize， UB) 宣布該校與貝國「教育、文化、科學暨科技部」今年持續合作提供英語課程的獎學金計劃，並從明(2024)年1月到12月期間由貝里斯大學「區域語言中心」(Regional Language Center， RLC)提供為期 10 個月的課程。

此計畫是由貝里斯政府贊助的獎學金計劃，以支付學費、課程費用和所需的教材及每月津貼支付學員住宿費用。RLC長期以來持續提供優良且多元的學習環境，促進學生融入當地文化、語言練習和強化個人生涯發展。

## 計畫宗旨

RLC是當地知名優質語言中心，提供多元且專業的外語（包括英語及其他語言）課程及翻譯服務。此歷時 10個月的英語課程旨在強化學員的英語能力，因此只要有興趣，無論目前的英語能力是基礎還是進階等級，貝里斯大學都歡迎來申請。

該獎學金計畫的課程亦強化學員的英語閱讀、寫作、聽力及口語等，所有RLC教師都強調實踐所學，鼓勵學生實際使用課堂所學之知識，課程包括為達到特定程度之學生所設計達60小時的語言實習計劃。

所有新生該課程的首週將受邀參加介紹該課程的迎新會議，每位學生將獲分配一名學術顧問，並將在整個學習過程中接受建議和指導。順利結訓的學生將獲得貝里斯大學頒發的結業證書，以表彰他們在受訓期間所獲得的成果。

## 受獎生申請辦法：

此獎學金僅適用台灣、墨西哥、古巴、宏都拉斯、薩爾瓦多、哥倫比亞、巴拿馬、委內瑞拉等國國民。

## 申請資格

1．申請者須為台灣、墨西哥、古巴、宏都拉斯、薩爾瓦多、哥倫比亞、巴拿馬、委內瑞拉等國民。

2．申請者申請時須年滿18歲。

3．申請者須同意在2024年1月至2024年12月期間居住貝里斯。

4.申請人必須至少持有高中學歷。

5． 須承諾在貝里斯全職學習，申請者不可兼職或修讀兼讀制。

## 獲選條件

1．填妥UB/RLC申請表格，並繳交15美元的申請費（部分申請者將可獲免申請費用，敬請在提交申請前以電郵至rlcinfo@ub.edu.bz確認）。

2．具備英語基本及中階水平。

3.可全程參加該計畫。

## 申請所需文件

1．填妥UB/RLC申请表格（如附件）°

2． 個人履歷（如使用中文，請提供英文版本）。

3． 高中畢業證書或證明文件，請提供英文翻譯版本並經認證。

4.2封推薦信，可自學術機構、前雇主、現任雇主（可使用英文、西班牙文或中文撰寫）

5．本人護照資料影本。

6.6張護照尺寸的照片（可在貝里斯拍攝製作）°

## 課程相關需求

CESL學生必須具備實體或電子英語詞典，建議攜帶翻譯詞典、錄音設備、耳機和筆記型電腦。

## 獎學金

每位獎學金生每月將獲得由貝里斯政府贊助的600貝里斯幣（折合300美元），並由貝里斯大學在每月第一個星期三發放。課程首月的津貼將在註冊後發放。

受獎生須擔負額外的財務能力以因應當地其他生活所需。

此獎學金將足夠支付您的租金和水電費·若您自身支出超出了此獎學金額度，貝里斯大學將無法額外提供費用，也無法減免或減少學費·此獎學金只適用貝里斯以外的學生；具長期居留貝里斯資格的受獎生並不符領取獎學金的資格，只可獲助學費及課程費用。

## 交通、住宿和學生生活

有關住宿、當地生活、交通或其他任何問題詳細資訊，請參考以下該校學生中心網頁：https://studentservices.ub.edu.bz/，並以「聯絡我們」(ContactUs)表格與貝里斯大學聯繫。

## 生活費

學生住宿的租金範圍為350至500貝里斯幣。請受獎生聯繫學生事務辦公室(froches@ub.edu.bz)，該辦公室將協助您安排住宿，並提供其他資訊及解決問題。
