## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 622
- **文章標題**: Procedures for newly admitted students/transferring students applying for on-line Course Credit Waiver for the 2nd semester of 106 School Year
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/procedures-for-newly-admitted-students-transferring-students-applying-for-on-line-course-credit-waiver-for-the-2nd-semester-of-106-school-year
- **文章簡介**: 1.Application period: Feb.1st~Mar 2nd, 2018 (Students must apply for it during the period; the on-line system will be

    closed   afterwards.)

2.Notifications:

  (1)Verification of credit waiver:



Common core courses: verified by College of General Studies, International Language and Culture Center, Military Office, Physical Education Office, Office of Student Affairs




Required core courses: verified by Department of major




  (2)



Course credit waiver is applied to students admitted for 106 School Year,   including undergraduate students, graduate students and transferring students. Students who didn’t submit the application for credit waiver or who did submit the application but forgot to do so for certain courses during the 1st semester shall apply for it during the 2nd The application shall be only submitted once by the deadline.




Transferring students admitted for 106 School Year shall submit the application for credit waiver with a written application form.




 (3)Transferring students (2nd year student) are applied to the list of courses for 105 School Year.

      Transferring students (3rd year  student) are applied to the list of courses for 104 School Year.

      Newly admitted students (whose student numbers   beginning with 106) are applied to the list of courses

       for 106 School Year.

(4)The path for checking the list of required/elective courses:

     Office of Academic Affairs→ Students’ Resource → Course Selection → List of Required/Elective Courses

     https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-01-14-06-58-46/2016-03-25-13-49-59

(5)Application procedure:



YZU → Student Portal → Study File → Course Waiver
