日期:112年3月10日

主旨：公告112學年度學生轉系時程及相關事項。

說明：

      一、申請日期：112年3月13日(星期一)起至112年4月6日(星期四)止。

      二、保送升學之離島地區學生不得申請轉系，唯情況特殊報經原保送之地方政府同意者不再此限。

      三、大一學生填寫申請自由轉系第一志願且同類組者，於原系當學年上下二學期之學業平均成績皆需達到60分以上，

              否則取消該生自由轉系資格。

      四、依前項規定大一學生上學期學業平均成績未達60分者，第一志願請勿再填同類組學系，否則取消轉系資格。

      五、應填表單:

               學生轉系申請表　

            元智大學學生申請轉系自我規劃分析表

            另附個人歷年成績單

     六、轉系標準請參閱大學部轉系審查標準表或至各學系洽詢。

      七、其他相關事項及表單請參閱轉系網頁：元智大學/行政單位/教務處/學籍相關/轉系申請

Announcement

Office of Academic Affairs

March 10th, 2023

Subject: Application period and notification for change of major in the 112semester.

Content:

       1.Application period: 2023/3/13 (Mon.)-2023/4/6(Thu.)

       2.Students, who are from offshore islands and were admitted to the university through the

          guaranteed admission process, apply for change of major MUST have the permission from the

         original local government or the application will be dismissed.

      3.Freshmen apply for change of major in the same field MUST have the average score of 60 or above 

          in the first academic year, or the application will be dismissed.

      4 Freshmen whose average score in the first semester of the first academic year is lower than 60 are not allowed to apply

          for change of major in the same   field.

     5.Students applying for a change of major (department/college/program)

　　　 Self-planning table and transcript.　　　

     6.Please see the department’s websites for the regulation of change of major.

     7.For more details, please refer to the website: YZU homepage/Administrative Offices/

       Office of Academic Affairs/Student Statuses/Change of Major
