（107）元智教內字第0023號

日期：108年02月18日

主旨：108年3月4日(週一)因舉辦研究所招生考試，當天元智一館教室課程調整，請查照。

說明：依據107.08.06 107-10招生委員會議決議：108年03月04日（一）因舉辦研究所招生考試，當天課程調整如下：

一、一館教室：日間上課(第1節~第10節)的課程【停課】；夜間(第11節~第13節)課程【照常上課】。

二、其餘各館：所有課程皆【照常上課】

  

教務處／課務組 敬啟

  

Announcement

Office of Academic Affairs

Subject: The 2019 Graduate Program Entrance Examination will take place on March 4. The Curriculum Adjustment of Building 1 is as followings;

Statement:

According to the resolution of the Admission Affairs Committee, Graduate Program Entrance Examination will be held on March 4, 2019 (Monday).


The Classes, in Building 1 from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. on March 4, 2019, will be suspended. The classes after 6 p.m. in Building 1 will be held as usual.

 The Classes in other buildings on March 4, 2019 will be held as usual.


Curriculum Section, Office Academic Affairs
