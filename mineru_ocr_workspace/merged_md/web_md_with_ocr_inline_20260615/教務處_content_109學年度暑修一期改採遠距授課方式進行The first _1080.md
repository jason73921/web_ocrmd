109學年度暑修一期改採遠距授課方式進行

因應疫情三級警戒持續，「暑修一期」改採遠距授課方式進行，敬請同學依規定時間上網學習，如與校際選課(同步遠距)有任何節次衝堂，皆以零分列計。

教務處／課務組

 

Announcement

Office of Academic Affairs

Announcement Date：2021/06/24

The first term of the summer course will be conducted in distance learning in the 109th academic year.

In response to the level 3 epidemic alert, " the first term of summer course"" will be taught in distance learning.

Students are requested to study online at the specified time. If there is any course overlap with the inter-school elective (synchronous online learning), the score will be zero.

Office of Academic Affairs,Curriculum Section
