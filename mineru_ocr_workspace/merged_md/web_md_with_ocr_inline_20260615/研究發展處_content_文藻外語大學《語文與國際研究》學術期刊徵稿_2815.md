## Call for Manuscripts

Submissions Opening for the 2020 June issue (23rd Issue)

The LIS, Journal of Languages and International Studies submission portal opens on October 1, 2019 for papers aimed at publication in June 1, 2020.

The 2020 LIS June Issue encourages interdisciplinary research, with the primary emphasis being on addressing critical issues and current trends and research in the fields of Computer-assisted Language Learning (CALL) or Technology-enhanced Language Learning (TELL), and Intercultural Communication.

The journal also covers a variety of topics, including:

• Cross-cultural aspects of language learning and teaching

• Curriculum and/or program development for language learning and teaching

• Culturally relevant and culturally responsive teaching

• Differentiated instruction for those engaged in language learning

• Foreign language learning among young learners

• Foreign language learning and assessment

• Psychological issues in language learning

• Social and cultural issues in language learning and teaching

• Bilingual, EFL, and other foreign language learning programs

• International business related studies

Publication Timeline

Publication date: June 1, 2020

Submissions will open for this issue: October 1, 2019

Submissions will close for this issue: February 1, 2020
