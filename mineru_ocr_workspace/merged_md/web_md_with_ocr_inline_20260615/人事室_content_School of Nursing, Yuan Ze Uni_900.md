- **文章簡介**: Position Title: Full-Time Faculty (Assistant Professor or above)


Number of Positions: Two



Priority consideration will be given to candidates specializing in Obstetrics, Psychiatric Nursing, and international applicants.



Appointment Start Date: August 1, 2025

Qualifications and Areas of Expertise:



Applicants must hold a Ph.D. in Nursing or a closely related field such as Life Sciences, Epidemiology, Public Health, or Smart Healthcare from an accredited domestic or international institution.

Applicants should demonstrate academic expertise in Fundamental Nursing, Medical-Surgical Nursing, Maternal and Child Nursing, Community Health Nursing, Psychiatric Nursing, Nursing Administration, and Nursing Research. The ability to teach courses in Long-term Care, Smart Nursing, and Clinical Practicum is also required.

Candidates must exhibit a strong commitment to teaching and service, possess excellent teamwork and communication skills, and be willing to assume departmental administrative responsibilities and contribute to academic service.

Preference will be given to applicants with:

Documented teaching experience

A valid teaching certificate issued by Taiwan’s Ministry of Education

Proficiency in English for academic instruction

Potential for interdisciplinary research collaboration






Application Procedure:
 Interested applicants should submit the following documents by April 25, 2025. Application materials may be submitted by post (postmarked by the deadline) or electronically via email to sndept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw. All application materials are non-returnable.
 Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis. Exceptional candidates may be invited for early review, and the recruitment process will close once all positions are filled.


Required Documents:


Completed Faculty Application Form and Curriculum Vitae Summary
 Download Form Here

Full Curriculum Vitae and Personal Statement

A copy of the highest academic degree diploma

For foreign degrees, the diploma must be verified by a Taiwan overseas representative office or an accredited credential verification service




A copy of the official academic transcript of the highest degree

Verification is required for foreign transcripts as well




A copy of the valid Teaching Qualification Certificate issued by Taiwan’s Ministry of Education (if available)

A Registered Nurse License issued by the Ministry of Health and Welfare (MOHW), Taiwan

A list of academic publications from the past seven years, including 2 to 3 full-text copies of representative works

Two letters of recommendation

Course syllabi for courses the applicant is prepared to offer

Candidates selected for the preliminary review will be notified separately to submit a video of an English teaching demonstration and will be invited to an on-site interview for the final selection process.
