# 2020 外語與翻譯國際學術研討會

# 跨域之外語與翻譯探究：「變遷與重置」

# An Interdisciplinary Study of Foreign Languages and Translation: Transformation and Relocation

## 論文徵稿 Call for Papers

高雄科技大學自 2019 年 2 月已由三校（高雄應用科技大學、高雄第一科技大學、高雄海洋科技大學）整併，外語學院亦開始邁入新的里程碑；為提升本校能見度、整合更多教師的學術研究能量，並與外校教師、外界人士相互對話與交流，外語學院特舉辦「跨域之外語與翻譯探究：『變遷與重置』國際學術研討會」。歡迎產、官、學各界先進踴躍參加，共襄盛舉。

## 徵稿議題

在全球化多元文化時代，在地與全球的界線不再是明顯的二元對立，各種產業的板塊也不斷地位移、質變與重組，所以我們可在跨域的脈絡中重新探究外語與翻譯的教學研究、應用與定位，並從文化、科技、出版業等各種場域與面向來探討外語與翻譯的教學研究與應用。由此目的出發，本研討會希望提供一個知識交流與辯證的平台，藉由學者們提出各種跨域整合與對話的研究觀點與論述，可為外語與翻譯的研究、教學與應用，找尋新的定位與契機。

歡迎以「外語與翻譯」為主題之論文投稿。論文涵蓋但不侷限以下子題：

1. 外語教學之理論與實務跨域整合

2. 翻譯教學之理論與實務跨域整合

3. 外語應用與文化之跨域對話

4. 翻譯應用與文化之跨域對話

5. 外語教學與產業之跨域連結

6. 翻譯教學與產業之跨域連結

7. 外語教學與科技之跨域競合

8. 翻譯教學與科技之跨域競合

9. 外語研究與出版業之跨域協商

10. 翻譯研究與出版業之跨域協商

11. 其他相關跨領域之研究

International Conference on Foreign Languages and Translation (May 29, 2020, in NKUST)

# An Interdisciplinary Study of Foreign Languages and Translation: Transformation and Relocation

## Call for Papers

After the merger of three universities, National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology (NKUST) was established on February 2018, and College of Foreign Languages started to move into a new milestone. To engage our college teachers in academic dialogues with scholars, researchers, educators and practitioners from other universities and industries, we organize this conference on “Interdisciplinary Study of Foreign Languages and Translation: Transformation and Relocation.” We hope that this conference can open up an avenue to brainstorm brilliant ideas and generate excellent academic research output.

## CONFERENCE THEMES

In the era of globalization and multiculturalism, the division point between local and global communities is phasing out. The sectors of various industries are undertaking a transformation in operation and features. In light of this shifting landscape of industries, educational and academic communities are encouraged to reflect on the methods of reform, innovation, and re-explore how to relocate the positioning of teaching, research of foreign languages and translation. We thus provide a platform and invite all participants to re-examine the issues of research, teaching and application of foreign languages and translation, from the interdisciplinary perspectives of connections with industries, relevance to cultures, and collaboration with technologies.

Participants are invited to submit papers that fall into the area of “foreign languages and translation”. The topics of interest include but are not limited to the following:

1. Interdisciplinary integration of theory and practice in the teaching of foreign languages.

2. Interdisciplinary integration of theory and practice in the teaching of translation.

3. Interdisciplinary dialogues between culture and application of foreign languages.

4. Interdisciplinary dialogues between culture and application of translation.

5. Interdisciplinary connections between industry and teaching of foreign languages.

6. Interdisciplinary connections between industry and translation teaching.

7. Interdisciplinary collaboration between technology and foreign language teaching.

8. Interdisciplinary collaboration between technology and translation teaching.

9. Interdisciplinary relationship between the publishing industry and foreign language studies.

10. Interdisciplinary relationship between the publishing industry and translation studies.

11. Other research in relation to interdisciplinary study.
