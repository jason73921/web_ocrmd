- **文章簡介**: Presentation and Evaluation Conference for Industry-University Collaboration Projects, hosted by Department of Engineering and Technologies under Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), provides a platform for the enterprises and universities to exchange collaboration experiences and enhance academic research capacity and industry R&D efficiency. The conference also aims to encourage the academic institutes to develop both innovative and practical technology and knowledge application research.

YZU has outstanding performances on people’s livelihood and chemical engineering. With the research of improving PCB board gilding and metal recycling technology, Dean Lee Shuoren from College of Engineering receives the poster award in the competition of Pilot Project. Awarding the Best Conference Paper, Prof. Kuen-Song Lin ‘s research, from the Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science, is about new technology of biodiesel production from waste cooking oil & PAHs pollutant identification. And Prof. Chao-Ling Yao is awarded the Best Conference Paper in the competition of Application by the research about Serum-Free cryopreservation for Human Hematopoietic Stem Cells.
