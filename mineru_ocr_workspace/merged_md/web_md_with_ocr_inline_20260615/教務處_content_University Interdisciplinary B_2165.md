⭐ Craft Your Personalized Degree ⭐

 Interdisciplinary × Self-Designed × Personalized Learning Pathways

 

The Yuan Ze University Bachelor’s Degree Program offers a flexible academic framework that allows students to break free from the constraints of traditional departments.

Tailor your own interdisciplinary degree by combining cross-departmental courses, credit programs, self-directed learning, and internships based on your personal interests and career aspirations.

 

Upon completion, you will be awarded:

Bachelor of Interdisciplinary Studies

 

With your specialized field of expertise (e.g., AI, Semiconductors, Sustainability, Innovation, etc.) officially noted on your diploma.
