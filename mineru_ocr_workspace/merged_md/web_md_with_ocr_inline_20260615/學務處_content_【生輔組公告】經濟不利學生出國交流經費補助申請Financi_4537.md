一、本校依據行政院核定高等教育深耕計畫，為鼓勵經濟不利學生赴海外交流拓展視野，學習多元文化及培養全球移動能力，以有效提升高等教育促進社會流動功能。
二、申請日期：即日起至113年11月1日截止。 
三、申請資格：凡符合高等教育深耕計畫定義之經濟不利學者，可提出申請並檢具相關證明文件，依規定申請日期前送達學務處生輔組。 
四、其他相關補助申請表及作業要點，請詳參下列連結。 
 承辦人：生輔組 簡麗芬小姐/分機2240 



In accordance with the Higher Education Deep Plowing Plan approved by the Executive Yuan, our school aims to encourage economically disadvantaged students to broaden their horizons through overseas exchanges, learn diverse cultures, and cultivate global mobility skills to effectively enhance the role of higher education in promoting social mobility.




Application Period: From now until November 1, 113th year (date of closure).




Eligibility: Those who meet the definition of economically disadvantaged students under the Higher Education Deep Plowing Plan can apply by submitting relevant supporting documents. Applications must be submitted to the Student Affairs Office before the specified application deadline.




For other related subsidy application forms and operational guidelines, please refer to the following link.




Contact Person: Ms. Chien Li-Fen, Student Affairs Office, Extension 2240.
