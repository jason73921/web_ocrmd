【活動延期通知】原訂10/02(三) 12:00-14:00 辦理之2024國際就業論壇，因應颱風來襲，活動延期公告。

因應颱風來襲，當天活動可能受風雨影響，考量外賓講者、全校師生交通安全，故活動延期辦理。

2024國際就業論壇 延至 11/13(三) 中午12:00-14:00舉行，地點：有庠廳。

【活動報名及保證金提醒】


先前已完成報名及繳交保證金之同學，敬請預留11/13(三)中午時段，仍可保留餐點及座位席次。

已完成保證金繳交如無法配合11/13日期異動，請於10/30(三)前之上班時間，前來活動中心R8201，憑保證金收執聯辦理退還保證金事宜。


如有任何未盡事宜煩請不吝與諮就組聯繫。

 

[Event Postponement Notice] The 2024 International Employment Forum, originally scheduled for 10/02 (Wednesday) 12:00-14:00, has been postponed due to the incoming typhoon.In response to the approaching typhoon, the activities on that day may be affected by wind and rain. Considering the traffic safety of foreign guest speakers, teachers and students of the school, the activities have been postponed.The 2024 International Employment Forum has been postponed to 11/13 (Wednesday) from 12:00 to 14:00 noon, location: Youxiang Hall.

[Event registration and deposit reminder]1. Students who have previously completed the registration and paid the deposit are kindly requested to reserve the noon time slot on 11/13 (Wednesday). Meals and seats can still be reserved.2. If you have completed the deposit payment and are unable to comply with the change of the 11/13 date, please come to the activity center R8201 during working hours before 10/30 (Wednesday) to handle the refund of the deposit with the deposit receipt. If you have any unanswered questions, please feel free to contact the consultation team.
