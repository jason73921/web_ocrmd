一.宿舍每層樓皆備有打掃用具供住宿生自行取用，清潔後請將其物歸原位。

Cleaning tools are available on every floor of the dormitory for residents to use. After use, please return them to their original locations.

二.公用掃具為每學年末統一巡檢汰換更新，學期中如遇損壞則無更換遞補，請同學愛惜使用。

Common cleaning tools are inspected and replaced at the end of each academic year. If they become damaged during the semester, replacements will not be provided. We kindly ask students to use them with care.
