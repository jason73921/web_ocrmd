International Conference on

## Multidisciplinary Approaches towards Clean Environment

22-23 August 2024

![](images/e0f53d1cfd9126c84faa737dfda2fe06f797639622a02902ec040a8c68997569.jpg)

Organized by Directorate of Interdisciplinary Research Assam Don Bosco University

The conference covers all the major disciplines with an objective to promote “Viksit Bharat” by providing an ideal environment to develop new collaborations, discuss the applications, and disseminate the latest research and developments aligning to green environment in close collaboration with scientists, researchers, engineers, industrial companies, government agencies, non-government organisations and policy makers.

## OBJECTIVES OF THE CONFERENCE

To encourage cross disciplinary collaboration to facilitate the integration of diverse scientific and technological discipline.

To promote innovative solution from various fields aimed at reducing environmental pollution.

To enhance knowledge exchange and contribute to environmental sustainability.

To develop policies for sustainable environmental management.

## SUB-THEMES

Good health and well being (SDG 3)

Clean energy and water (SDG 6 & SDG 12)

● Sustainable cities and communities (SDG 11)

Climate action (SDG 13)

Green and circular economy (SDG 12)

Life on air, land, and water (SDG 15 & SDG 14)

## ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

Assam Don Bosco University (ADBU) is a non-profit, coeducational university located in Guwahati, Assam. It was founded in 2008 by the Salesians of Don Bosco (SDB). It is Assam’s first state university in the private sector. The university is recognized by the University Grant Commission (UGC) with 2f and 12B status and accredited with “A” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
