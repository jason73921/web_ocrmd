- **文章全文**: Banping mountain in New Taipei. Overall, the hike took around 2 hours and it was fairly easy. However, you might need basic rock climbing skills because you will have to rock climb with ropes at some points.
From the top of the mountain, you can see the Taipei view, the coastline in the north and the east side, and the green grass in the south part.

Not far from the hiking spot, if you still have extra energy you can continue going to dragon cave for swimming or snorkeling (P.S. the place is also known as a rock climbing spot facing the Pacific Ocean view).
If you love adventure, those two places are really worth visiting!
