Hsinchu County is a major hub for technology and a key incubator for industries in the Taoyuan-Hsinchu-Miaoli region. By fostering interdisciplinary digital skills, we empowers young professionals with exceptional workplace capabilities. This initiative creates a win-win situation for both enterprises and young talents in Hsinchu County, driving digital transformation and innovation.

 

To apply for an internship, please download and complete the internship application form below. If you wish to apply more than one company or position, please submit a separate form for each. If the company requires additional documents or if you would like to submit your resume, portfolio, or other files, please send them along with your application to the email address digitalyouth@scenariolab.com.tw. You will receive a confirmation within two business days after submitting your application. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please contact Ms. Chen at 0955689866 for verification.

 

 Official Website：https://sites.google.com/scenariolab.com.tw/digitalyouth/IS

First round： 3/24 - 4/23
✨Applications submitted in the first round will have the opportunity to participate in the in-person internship matching event on 4/26✨

Third round： 5/12 - 5/23

Participating Companies Providing Internship Openings: 

 

Coretronic Corporation 

Established on June 30, 1992, in the Hsinchu Science Park, Coretronic has positioned itself since its inception as an "innovative display system integration solution provider." It was Taiwan"s first manufacturer of LCD backlight modules and a pioneer in mass-producing the world"s smallest and lightest VGA single-panel LCD projectors and XGA DLP projectors. By integrating technologies in optics, mechanics, electronics, thermal management, materials, and precision mold processing, the company inaugurated a new era for Taiwan’s display systems and established a strong foundation for future corporate development.

 

BenQ Materials 

Founded in July 1998, BenQ Materials initially focused on the research and manufacturing of high-quality, high-capacity optical discs for storing knowledge and entertainment. Adhering to the philosophy that "Innovation is everywhere," the company continues to develop material science products based on independent R&D. It possesses two core material technologies—optical multi-layer film design and polymer synthesis—along with four major process technologies: roll-to-roll, precision engraving, precision coating, and injection/extrusion, providing customers with high-value, integrated solutions.

 

USI - Universal Scientific Industrial 

A global leader in electronic design and manufacturing, USI holds a leading position in the field of SiP (System-in-Package) modules. With 30 manufacturing and service sites across Asia, Europe, the Americas, and Africa, USI provides brand customers with comprehensive D(MS)² services, including electronic product design, manufacturing, miniaturization, industry software and hardware solutions, as well as material procurement, logistics, and maintenance services. USI is a member of ASE Technology Holding (TWSE: 3711, NYSE: ASX).

 

Landseed Health Ecosystem 

Dedicated to building a smart health platform centered on "preventative medicine," Landseed integrates resources from health management, medical care, wellness products, and digital platforms through precision technology and personalized services. The company aims to improve public health literacy and quality of life by combining service, education, and research to drive the development of a cross-disciplinary health ecosystem, with the goal of becoming a leading brand in Taiwan’s smart health ecosystem by 2030.

 

Brownie Medical 

A smart medical startup invested in and established by LeadRay Technology, Brownie Medical focuses on the R&D and clinical application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) medical imaging recognition technology. Its parent company, LeadRay Technology, is a leader in optical defect inspection within the semiconductor industry. With over a decade of core strength in high-speed image processing, deep learning, and computer vision—serving the world’s top 15 wafer foundries and OSAT (Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly and Test) providers—it has laid a powerful technical foundation for Brownie Medical.

 

Sinyi Realty 

Sinyi Realty is a pioneer in ESG and a practitioner of PropTech (Property Technology). We continuously optimize our talent cultivation programs and compensation/benefit systems, striving to ensure every partner can realize their unique value at Sinyi Realty. Your excellence does not need to be limited by seniority; we will continue to build a career stage where everyone can reach their maximum potential.

 

FOPi Bakery 

Founded in 2016, FOPi Bakery is the sole designated bread supplier for FamilyMart. As the core of the convenience store bread supply chain in Taiwan, we are continuously expanding our production scale and optimizing our talent layout. We are currently actively recruiting outstanding talent to join our ranks!

 

Wunsong Logistics 

Founded in May 1984, the company’s core services are customs brokerage and transportation. Having cultivated Taiwan"s logistics services for over 40 years, growing alongside Taiwan"s economic takeoff and the development of the ICT industry, it is the premier logistics partner for Taiwan"s high-tech enterprises.
