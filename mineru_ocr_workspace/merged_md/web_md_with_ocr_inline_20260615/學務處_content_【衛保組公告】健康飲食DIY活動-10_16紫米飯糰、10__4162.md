學務處衛保組將於活動中心一樓社創基地辦理健康飲食宣導活動，歡迎踴躍參加。




112.10.16(一)18:00-19:30雞肉紫米飯糰DIY
112.10.26(四)18:00-19:30彩蔬鮪魚優格穀堡DIY

參加者請配合全程配戴口罩，成品可外帶，不開放現場飲食！
活動對象為全校學生，僅接受PORTAL系統報名，各場次需分開報名，名額有限，歡迎踴躍參加。
線上報名後請於112.10.11(三)16:00前至衛保組繳交證金50元/場次，參與後全額退還。
PS.本活動不開放多元學習護照認證

   學務處衛保組敬啟

 

Healthy Eating DIY Workshops. Welcome to participate enthusiastically.

Participants are required to wear masks throughout the event, and the finished products can be taken home. On-site dining is not allowed.

Registration for the event is only accepted through the PORTAL system. Each session requires separate registration, and space is limited. We welcome enthusiastic participation.

After online registration, please submit a deposit of 50 NT dollars per session to the Health Promotion Division by 112.10.11 (Wednesday) at 16:00. The full deposit will be refunded after participation.
