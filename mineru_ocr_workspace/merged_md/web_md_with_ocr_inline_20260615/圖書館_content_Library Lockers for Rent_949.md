- **文章簡介**: A limited number of student card-based lockers are available to rent in the Library. The rental fee is NT$450 per semester and the rental period starts from Jan. 16, 2019, to Jul 15, 2019.


Applications for rental will be accepted at the Library Circulation Desk (ext. 2321) on a first come, first served basis.

Any YZU students with valid library privileges and in need of storage for personal belongings are encouraged to act soon.

Do present a valid student card at the Library Circulation Desk and pay any fee at the time of rental.

Be noted that up to 2 students may share the same locker.
