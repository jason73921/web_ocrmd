Subject: Male 2 Dorm Architecture Maintenance

Time: 2023/6/10(Sat.)~6/11(Sun.), 8:00~17:00

Description:

1.The construction work will occupy LeXue Square, as shown in the attached picture below.

2.During the operation, road traffic will be controlled, and pedestrians are requested to temporarily enter and exit through the basement or Male & Female 2 dorm 2nd floor. 

3.Normal use will resume immediately after repairs are completed.

4.Sorry for any inconvenience, and thank you for your cooperation!





 

主旨：男二舍裝置藝術整修工程

時間：2023/6/10(六)~6/11(日) 8:00-17:00

說明：

1.施工作業將占用樂學廣場，如附圖。

2.作業期間將管制道路通行，請行人暫由地下室、男女二舍2樓進出。

3.維修完畢將立即恢復正常使用。

4.若造成不便請見諒，謝謝合作！





 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
