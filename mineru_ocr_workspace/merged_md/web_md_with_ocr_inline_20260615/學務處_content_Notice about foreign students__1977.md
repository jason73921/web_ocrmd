# 境外生寒假住宿注意事項

## Notice about foreign students' winter vacation accommodation

Students who wish to stay in the dorm during winter vacation MuST apply within the announced time, report to the Student Housing Service Section and follow the application procedure below.

二、申請資格：僑生、陸生、外籍生、交換生、雙聯生寒假期間欲住宿者。（109-2續住之研究生除外） Application eligibility: Undergraduate students (including Mainland Chinese students, Overseas Chinese students, foreign students, exchange students, dual degree students) that wish to stay in the dorm for winter vacation. (except for graduate students who continue to live in dorm in 109-2 semester)

三、申請期間 Application period:2020Dec.7th\~Dec.30th

四、開放住宿期間 Winter vacation staying period: 2021 Jan. 18th\~Feb. 19th

五、申請流程 Application procedure for Winter Vacation accommodation:

六、住宿費：每日100元。例如住宿全期共計33天x100元=3，300元整。 Accommodation fee: 100 NTD per day. E.g., 33 days in total for the entire period x 100 NTD = 3,300 NTD.

十、寒假期間如有住宿需求，請完成以上申請手續，否則將無法使用網路與刷卡權限。 Any foreian students who wish to stay during this period must complete the aboye procedure, otherwise neither internet nor access cardwill be provided.

八、學校宿舍春節年假期間（2021.2.9\~17）將進行封舘，封館期間學校餐廳暫停營業。 The dorm will be closed in winter vacation during Chinese New Year from 2021.Feb.9th to 2021.Feb.17 th Cafeteria will not be opened during this period

九、封館期間學校軍訓室每日仍有執勤教官值班，如遇緊急狀況或身體不適，請撥打電話 03-4553698 The school military office is opened during the period. If you have an emergency or feel sick, please call 03- 4553698.

十、春節封館期間宿舍僅供應電熱水器之熱水，男生請至男一舍各樓層之B區衛浴間，女生請至每樓層房號尾數為 13 附近的衛浴間沐浴。 The dorm only provides hot water from the electric heater. Male students should take ashower inSection BofMale1 Dorm;Femalestudentsshouldtake a showerat the bathroom nearbyroom No. 13 of each floor.
