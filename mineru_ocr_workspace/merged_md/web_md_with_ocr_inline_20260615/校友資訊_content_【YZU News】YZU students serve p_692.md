Yuan Ze University’s office of Student Affairs organized a group of twenty students from school clubs, including New Century Leadership, Robot Researchers, Photo Horizon and Ukulele clubs, as well as foreign students from Colombia, St. Vincent, Thailand and Vietnam. They went to rural areas to promote AI information education and international education during National Day holiday. With the spirit of caring for the disadvantaged, they celebrated the birthday of the Republic of China with tribal elders and local residents.
