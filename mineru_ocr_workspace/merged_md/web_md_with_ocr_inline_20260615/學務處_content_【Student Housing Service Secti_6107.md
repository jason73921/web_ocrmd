# Typhoon Emergency Notice

Typhoon DANAS has now developed into a severe tropical storm. The C.W.A. has issued a land typhoon warning for parts of Taiwan. The typhoon is expected to slightly strengthen, and its storm radius may gradually expand. All dormitory residents are urged to closely monitor typhoon updates and pay attention to the following points:

## 1.Prepare typhoon supplies (at least for 3 days):

• Store drinking water and dry food (such as instant noodles and biscuits)

• Prepare a flashlight to prevent power outages

• Have personal medications ready

• Ensure your power banks are fully charged
