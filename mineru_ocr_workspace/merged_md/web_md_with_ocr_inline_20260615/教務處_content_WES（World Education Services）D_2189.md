Yes.




 

I would like to have my transcript or diploma evaluated through a third-party credential evaluation organization (WES). How should I proceed?


Please attach the electronic files you wish to send and include the email subject and content (usually in English) addressed to the recipient. After completing them, send the email to the Registrar Section at aaregi@saturn.yzu.edu.tw. The Registrar Section will then forward the documents to WES.
