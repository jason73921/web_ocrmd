同學您好:

寒假的腳步慢慢接近，在此預祝同學們都有一個美好的假期。

寒假期間，為使住宿同學持續落實自主防疫，學校將於學期結束前(1月6日)發給每一位住宿同學2劑快篩試劑。請同學於開學前自主檢測，若如為陽性，請勿入住宿。應立即完成自主填報，實施居家照護，並透過教務系統安排線上課程，避免宿舍群體感染事件發生。

快篩試劑將由樓長轉發至各寢室，請同學於開學入住宿舍前務必自行檢測，宿舍區的健康由您我一起來守護，謝謝配合，

學務處宿服組 關心您~

  

Hello students:

Winter vacation is approaching slowly, and wish all the students have a wonderful holiday. During the winter vacation, in order to enable the dormitory students to continue to implement Self-initiated Prevention, the campus will distribute 2 doses of COVID-19 rapid test to each dormitory student before the end of the semester (January 6). Students are requested to self-test before back to campus. If your COVID-19 rapid test is positive, please don’t enter the dormitory. Self-reporting should be completed immediately, home care should be implemented, and online courses should be arranged through the educational administration system to avoid group infection incidents in dormitories.

The COVID-19 rapid test will be forwarded by the building director to each room. Everyone requested to test themselves before moving into the dormitory. The health of the dormitory is guarded by you and me. Thank you for your cooperation.

Best Regards from Student Housing Service Section.
