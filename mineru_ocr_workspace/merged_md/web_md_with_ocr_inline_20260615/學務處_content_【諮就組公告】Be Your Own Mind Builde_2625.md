　　During this pandemic, we’re facing challenges that be stressful and overwhelming. It’s important to take care of yourself and you’ll be better equipped to live your best life. There’s some tips you can do：

（from Go through the storm of COVID-19 while working at home）


Policy-follower


　　Pay attention to official government announcements on epidemic prevention every day, and abide by the advice provided by health officials.


Enhance the self-efficacy


　　Give yourself more opportunities during the day to allow yourself to feel a sense of accomplishment.


Accept your emotion


　　Allow yourself to have negative emotions due to the many sudden changes during the epidemic.


Change the habits


　　Rearrange your lifestyle brought about by the epidemic, and use creativity to establish new behaviors and schedules for yourself, your relatives and friends.


Engage in life


　　Incorporate active interpersonal management in your life by intentionally arranging a meaningful interaction with people in order to maintain a diverse and fulfilling daily routine.
