- **文章簡介**: Shen Jhen (沈禎), Dean of Art and Design, displayed his artworks in the art gallery of Dr. Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall from April 14 to June 16. There were more than 60 paintings in the exhibition, including ink, oil, and watercolor paintings.

In this display -Joy, and Love of Art in Life”, Mr. Shen revealed his best works in his time. Those arts showed that he revealed himself to a world of pure art and with free-spirited. Shen is active in teaching and art painting, especially in promoting military art activities. He has been invited as the judge of Military Art Academy Award, regularly participating in events, and sent his artworks as gifts. This exhibition was a courtesy given by the Ministry of Defense for his contributions.
