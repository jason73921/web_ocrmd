Date & Time: Tuesday, April 14, 2026 | 14:10 – 16:00

Venue: Online via Webex

Topic: Innovative Strategies and Risk Identification in AI-Assisted Manuscript Writing

Speaker: Shu-Yi Huang, Assistant Research Fellow, Department of Medical Research, National Taiwan University Hospital

Session Overview:

This lecture explores the role and challenges of Artificial Intelligence in academic writing. It will introduce how generative AI tools can assist in data collection, synthesis, and language refinement while analyzing potential concerns such as lack of source transparency, model bias, content reliability, authorship responsibility, and citation accuracy.

The speaker will discuss how researchers can maintain professional judgment, alertness, and academic autonomy when using AI tools, ensuring research integrity while effectively leveraging technology to improve efficiency.
