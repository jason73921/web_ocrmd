- **文章簡介**: 2017 Taoyuan Land Art Festival has opened in Guanyin and Bade Districts from August 18 to September 3. This festival is supported by University Social Responsibility Project of Ministry of Education, and is presented by students from Yuan Ze University. They not only chose unique Ponds , Fushan Road and natural ecosystems as background of works, they also showed several cultural features in Taoyuan. The six works that YZU students present become the most attractive view in Bade.

The event in Bade is planed by teacher Pei Chuan Hsieh. She invited six international and local artists cooperating with communities and students from YZU and elementary schools to create six land artworks into people life.
