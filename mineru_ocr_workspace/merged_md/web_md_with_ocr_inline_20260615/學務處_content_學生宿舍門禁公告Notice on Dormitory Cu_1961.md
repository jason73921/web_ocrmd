- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: 公告日期：2020年10月20日(星期二)

 

有鑑於全校各單位及師生同仁皆配合及落實各項防疫措施，

而住宿同學因課業研習有晚歸之需求，

本校學生宿舍即日起門禁系統改為24小時刷卡進出。

提醒住宿同學，注意交通與人身安全，並請遵守各項防疫規定。

若無迫切需求，仍宜儘早回宿舍休息，早睡早起身體好。



 

So far, all the teachers, staff and students have strictly obeyed regulations on the pandemic prevention. Noticing that many students cannot go back to dormitory before 12 am due to studying or part-time jobs, we decided to cancel the 12am curfew. You are now able to access the dormitory 24/7 using your card.  

For students living in the dormitory, please pay attention to your own safety, conform with the dormitory regulations and regulations on the pandemic prevention. If you do not have any urgent issues, please go back to the dorm early to take a rest after a long day. Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise. 

 

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
