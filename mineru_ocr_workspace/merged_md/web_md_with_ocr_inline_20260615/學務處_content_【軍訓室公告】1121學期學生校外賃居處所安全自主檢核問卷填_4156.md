為強化學生校外賃居關懷安全訪視及緊急事件聯繫，感謝同學填寫【校外賃居處所安全自主檢核表】，10月2日已抽出30位同學獲得《路易莎元智店現金抵用卷100元》，附件冊列獲獎同學請於112年10月20日12時前至軍訓室(活動中心二樓)領取。

To strengthen the care and safety visits for students living off-campus and enhance emergency contact measures, we would like to express our gratitude to those students who filled out the "Off-Campus Housing Safety Self-Assessment Questionnaire." On October 2nd, 30 students were randomly selected to receive a NT$100 cash voucher for Louis" at the Yuanzhi Store. The list of winning students is attached.

Winners are kindly requested to collect their vouchers from the Military Training Office (2nd floor of the Activity Center) by 12:00 PM on October 20th, 112th academic year.

Once again, we appreciate your enthusiastic participation in helping ensure the safety of your off-campus housing.

謝謝您Thanks
