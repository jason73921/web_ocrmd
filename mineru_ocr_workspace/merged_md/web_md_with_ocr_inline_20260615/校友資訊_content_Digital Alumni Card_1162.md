【How to Use the Digital Alumni Card】

Step 1. 
Please log in to the portal using the account and password you set during your studies.If you have forgotten your password, please call 03-4638800#2321 during office hours (Monday to Friday, 08:00–16:30) for identity verification. If your identity is verified, the Information Services Office will reset your password, but it will only become effective the next day.

Step 2. 
Click on the left sidebar > Alumni 



Step 3. 
Click on "Click Here to Display Alumni Card" below your photo, and your Digital Alumni Card will be shown.



【FAQ】

Q：What should I do if I forget my password？
A：If you forget your password, please call 03-4638800#2321 during office hours (Monday to Friday, 08:00–16:30) for identity verification. If your identity is verified, the Information Services Office will reset your password, but it will only become effective the next day.

Q：Why is there no photo in the system？
A：If your photo is missing from the system, it may be due to incomplete records when you enrolled. Please contact the Alumni Center for assistance.

Q：Can I change my photo？
A：The photo update feature is currently under development. Please stay tuned!！
