Ask Your Mentor is a one-on-one online career consultation organized by Talent Taiwan for international students in Taiwan. Mentors from various industries provide guidance on career planning, Taiwanese workplace culture, and job application tips. 

🔍 What you can consult about:


Work regulations and application procedures in Taiwan

Industry trends and career direction

Resume and cover letter reviews

Mock interview preparation

Workplace culture insights

Personal career planning
