Description:



To enhance university instructors’ ability to teach in English and to promote inter-institutional resource sharing, our Bilingual Education Resource Center is organizing an EMI Faculty Training Program. A total of seven EMI professional development sessions will be held during the semester.




For detailed information regarding the schedule, topics, speakers, venues, and registration links of the EMI training sessions, please refer to Attachment 1: Training Course Poster.




Additional information about the program is available on our Bilingual Education Resource Center website under the “Faculty Development – Training Courses” section: https://best-tdc.nsysu.edu.tw/TrainingCertification.aspx




Participants who complete the requirements for each training stage will be issued a certificate of completion accordingly. Certificates can be downloaded from the Center’s website. Please refer to Attachment 2: Training Program Guidelines for detailed requirements of each stage.
