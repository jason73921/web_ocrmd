The Counseling and Career Development Division of Yuan Ze University"s Student Affairs Office currently employs several part-time counseling psychologists with diverse expertise. If instructors wish to organize various class discussions or group counseling sessions for their students, they can apply to our division. Possible topics include gender relations, interpersonal dynamics, career planning, emotional management, time management, effective learning strategies, psychological assessments, self-esteem enhancement, stress management, and more. Instructors are welcome to submit applications for these activities.
