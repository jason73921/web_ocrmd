## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 1971
- **文章標題**: Application for Dormitory Living Renewal or Withdrawal
- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: Renewal period: from the beginning of the second semester of 109 school year (109-2 semester) to the end of the first semester of 110 school year (110-1 semester).


Renewal eligibility:

Foreign students who enter school from the second semester of 108 school year (spring semester 2020).

Students with no dormitory violations and point deduction.

Students who have not paid or are in arrears with accommodation related fees are not allowed to apply.





Application time: From 10:00 am, 2020/11/16 until 10:00 am, 2020/12/31.


Submission details: Please submit the Yuan Ze University Accommodation Consent Form (see attachment 1) to the Student Housing Service Office (Male dorm, 1st floor, Area C) before 10:00 am, 2020/12/31.


Payment of accommodation-related fees: Renewal students does not need to repaid the deposit, and the accommodation fee will be incorporated into the registration form of the next semester. Please be sure to complete the payment before moving in at the beginning of 109-2 semester.


Bed arrangement: For renewal students, you will be assigned with your original bed. Under special circumstances, your bed shall be adjusted accordingly. If you want to choose the bed by yourself or change bed, please contact Miss Su at the Student Housing Service Section Office.


Deadline for check-out of this semester: 2021/1/17 for Undergraduate students, 2021/1/31 for Graduate students.


Entry dates for the next semester: from 2021/2/20 for Undergraduate students; from 2021/2/1 for Graduate students. Students who want to stay during the winter vacation, please apply separately (exempt for graduate students).


If you wish to give up accommodation for 109-2 semester after you have lived in the dorm for a full year, please fill in the withdrawal form (Attachment 2) and submit it to the Student Housing Service Office before 12/31, and complete the check-out before the check-out deadline for this semester.
