 How do I know the relevant regulations for master"s students to study for a doctoral degree? 


 

 Please refer to “ Yuan Ze University Regulations for Direct Admission into Doctoral Program ”. 




 

 When should I apply for master"s degree and doctor degree? 


 

 

 For the degree programs of each institute (department), students are divided into two semesters to study for a doctoral degree. The principle is to apply within the deadline specified in the school calendar. The review results of the degree programs of each institute (department) are approved by the executive meeting of the institute (department) where they are studying. , send it to the Academic Affairs Office for remittance. 




 

 What are the application requirements and procedures for directing Pursuit of the Ph.D. Degree and master degree? 


 

 

Undergraduate students with exemplary academic performance and potential for research who are in their final year of study as well as master’s students who have been enrolled in a graduate program for at least one semester may apply for direct admission into a doctoral program on condition that they have received the recommendation of two associate or full professors in their current department (institute, college, degree program), the consent of the department meeting(institute, college, degree program) to which they are applying, and the approval of the president of the university.
The standards for determining whether an applicant demonstrates exemplary academic performance as well as shows a potential for research shall be judged by the department (institute,college, degree program).




 

 Can I apply to the master degree after I have been promoted to a doctor degree from a master"s degree? 


 

 

 If a graduate student who is studying for a doctoral degree stops studying for some reason or fails to pass the doctoral candidate qualification assessment, he may return to the master"s degree program after being reviewed and approved by the institute (department) executive meeting and approved by the president.

# Yuan Ze University Regulations for Direct Admission into Doctoral Program

Passed by the 8th Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 1991, on June 10, 1992   
Amended by the 4th Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 1997, on November 19, 1997   
Amended by the 4th Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2000, on February 14, 2001   
Amended by the 2nd Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2002, on November 13, 2002   
Amended by the 2nd Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2006, on November 08, 2006   
Amended by the 2nd Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2008, on November 05, 2008   
Amended by the 3th Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2019, on December 05, 2019   
Amended by the 4th Academic Affairs Meeting, Academic Year 2021, on March 01, 2023

Article 1 These regulations are established in accordance with University Act and the Direct Admission into Ph.D. Program Guidelines for Students Graduating with a Bachelor’s Degree and Graduate Students in the Master’s Degree Program as established by Ministry of Education.

Article 2 Undergraduate students with exemplary academic performance and potential for research who are in their final year of study as well as master’s students who have been enrolled in a graduate program for at least one semester may apply for direct admission into a doctoral program on condition that they have received the recommendation of two assistant professors or above in their current department (institute, college, degree program), the consent of the department meeting (institute, college, degree program) to which they are applying, and the approval of the president of the university. The standards for determining whether an applicant demonstrates exemplary academic performance as well as shows a potential for research shall be judged by the department (institute, college, degree program). Undergraduate students in their final year of study whose application for direct admission into a doctoral program has been accepted must obtain their bachelor’s degree before enrolling in the doctoral program; those who fail to do so shall have their admission cancelled.

Article 3 Students applying for direct admission into a doctoral program shall turn in the following documentation together with the application form to the department (institute, college, degree program) to which they are applying. The application deadline for direct admission into a doctoral program shall be left to the individual departments to set and announce during each semester and no late applications shall be processed.

1. Application form for direct admission into a doctoral program.

2. Overall transcript of the bachelor or master program (including the accumulative ranking).

3. Two recommendation letters from an associate or full professor.

4. All other documents required by the department (institute, college, degree program) to which they are applying.

Graduate students shall not file the application during their first semester of study. Students approved for direct admission to a doctoral degree program shall, starting from the next semester after approval, follow the regulations for course enrollment, performance assessment, and the duration of study, in accordance with the procedures for incoming doctoral students of that academic year.

Article 4 The number of students accepted by department (institute, college, degree program) through the direct-admission process shall not exceed 40 percent of the total admission quotas for doctoral students permitted by the Ministry of Education for that academic year. However, this does not apply to cases where the quota for direct enrollment in a doctoral program is utilized within other departments, institutes, or degree programs of the same college.

The quota for direct admission to doctoral programs in the aforementioned departments, institutes, colleges, or degree programs may not be entirely allocated for direct doctoral admissions. However, the following exceptions apply:

1. The approved enrollment quota for the doctoral program in the department, institute, college, or degree program is one person;

2. A talent cultivation program or project approved by the Ministry of Education;

These quotas must be included in the university’s total admission quotas permitted by the Ministry of Education for that academic year.

If a student admitted for direct admission into a doctoral program during the current academic year is forced by circumstances to withdraw his application after it has been approved, the admission quota that becomes available may be applied by other graduates during the winter vacation.

Article 5 Students in the direct admission into a doctoral program who meet any of the following criteria may apply to study at the original department’s master program or transfer to a master program in a related department (institute, college, degree program) with the consent of the department meeting of both the doctoral program in which they are enrolled and the master’s program to which they seek to transfer, and the approval of the president of the university.

1. who discontinue the doctoral program study;

2. who do not pass the doctoral candidacy examination;

3. who do not pass the doctoral degree examination in accordance with the statements specified in Article 6 of these regulations;

The students may be conferred a master’s degree with the consent of the meeting from their original department (institute, college, degree program) in which they are enrolled and the master’s program to which they seek to transfer upon completion of all required courses, thesis and upon passing the master degree examination through the examination committee. The term of study in the doctoral program shall not be included in calculating the term of study in the master program.

Article 6 Students in the direct admission into a doctoral program who pass the doctoral candidacy examination but fail the doctoral degree examination shall be conferred a master’s degree if their doctoral dissertation is deemed by their doctoral degree examination committee to have met a standard appropriate for a master’s degree.

Article 7 These regulations will be adopted by the Academic Affairs Meeting, as shall amendments when they are made.

If any controversies or disputes occurred regarding clauses of the contract, it shall always refer to its Chinese version.
