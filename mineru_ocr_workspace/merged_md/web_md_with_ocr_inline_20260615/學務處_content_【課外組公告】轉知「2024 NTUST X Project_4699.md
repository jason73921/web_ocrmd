## 2024 NTUST X Project LET'S Go! Summer Program A part of the University Social Responsibility Initiatives

## What is Project LET’S Go! (PLG)?

PLG is a nonprofit organization that brings young talents from around the globe to work with under-resourced schools. During the program, our volunteer teachers design innovative curriculum that promotes art, music, science and critical thinking in an English language immersion setting. Also, we take part in some community activities to gain a deeper cultural understanding.

## Who are we recruiting?

We work closely with Taiwan Tech (NTUST) and are recruiting college and graduate students with greatest enthusiasms to serve as volunteer teachers. Our volunteer teachers are talented and versatile; they come from a wide range of language backgrounds and nationalities.
