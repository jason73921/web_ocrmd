 What are the conditions for early graduation? 


 

1. Please refer to the "  Yuan Ze University Regulations for Advanced Graduation on Academic Excellence  " document.

2. A student who completes all courses and the minimum credits required for graduation in his/her department and satisfies the qualifications described below may request to graduate one semester or one academic year ahead of the regular time of study:

1. In good academic standing with an average grade of 80 points and above for each semester.

2. With good conduct record receiving an average grade of 80 points and above for each semester.

3. With good grade of courses of Physical Education and Military Education receiving an average grade of 70 points and above for each semester.

4. Achieving academic excellence as ranked in the top 10% of the class,including transfer students,in the department for the first six semesters. Those born in 2005 or later applying for the Flexible Study Program for conscripts are not subject to this limitation.

3. Special cases for advanced graduation

1. Students who transfer into the second grade of the university with academic excellence in accordance with Article 2 of these regulations may apply for advanced graduation. The courses that transfer students have taken at their original schools and the credits have been exempted for the university’s required credits shall be with an average grade of 80 points and above of that semester to be qualified for advanced graduation.

2. Students who transfer into the third grade of the university shall not be eligible for advanced graduation due to their short term of study at the university.




 

 What is the application process for early graduation? 


  

An applicant may apply for advanced graduation to Office of Academic Affairs to get the approval of the corresponding chairman of the department as well as Academic Dean and the president.




 

 What documents should I bring to receive my graduation certificate? 




After completing the Portal"s departure procedures, bring your student ID to the Registration Office at the Academic Affairs Office during the announced collection time each semester to collect it.




 

 If I am unable to take the graduation certificate in person, can I entrust someone else to get it on my behalf? 


 

Someone else can be authorized to collect the diploma on your behalf. The authorized person must bring the graduate"s student ID or identification card, their own identification card, and an authorization letter(Yuan Ze University Authorization of Applying Documents).






What should I do if I cannot receive my diploma at the time announced by the school?




It can still be collected at the Registration Office counter during office hours.






Can graduate students receive their graduation certificate after handing in their thesis?




1. You need to confirm with the department staff that the thesis grade has been sent to the Academic Affairs Office.

2. After completing the Portal"s departure procedures, bring your student ID to the Registration Office at the Academic Affairs Office during office hours to collect it.




 

 When can I receive my graduation certificate after the summer class? 




Please confirm with the Registration Section of the Academic Affairs Office that your summer study scores have been sent to the registration team. After confirming that you can graduate, you can then go to the Registration Section of the Academic Affairs Office to collect them to avoid waiting for a long time.




 

 How to apply for a replacement diploma if it is lost? 


1. Apply at the Registration Office of the Academic Affairs Office during office hours, or use the mail application.

2. For the Chinese version of the diploma, only one replacement can be issued at a time, with a fee of 200 NTD.

3. For the English version of the diploma, multiple replacements can be issued at a time, with a fee of 100 NTD each.

## Article 1 These regulations are established in accordance with Article 46 of the university’s study regulations.

## Article 2 Qualifications for advanced graduation

A student who completes all courses and the minimum credits required for graduation in his/her department and satisfies the qualifications described below may request to graduate one semester or one academic year ahead of the regular time of study:

1. In good academic standing with an average grade of 80 points and above for each semester.

2. With good conduct record receiving an average grade of 80 points and above for each semester.

3. With good grade of courses of Physical Education and Military Education receiving an average grade of 70 points and above for each semester.

4. Achieving academic excellence as ranked in the top 10% of the class,including transfer students,in the department for the first six semesters. Those born in 2005 or later applying for the Flexible Study Program for conscripts are not subject to this limitation.

## Article 3 Application period for advanced graduation

A student who wishes to apply for advanced graduation shall complete course registration in the second semester of his/her third year of study or in the first semester of the fourth year; the courses and credits required for graduation shall be reviewed to see if the applicant satisfies the qualifications stipulated in Article 2 of these regulations.

## Article 4 Application procedure for advanced graduation

An applicant may apply for advanced graduation to Office of Academic Affairs to get the approval of the corresponding chairman of the department as well as Academic Dean and the president.

## Article 5 Special cases for advanced graduation

1. Students who transfer into the second grade of the university with academic excellence in accordance with Article 2 of these regulations may apply for advanced graduation. The courses that transfer students have taken at their original schools and the credits have been exempted for the university’s required credits shall be with an average grade of 80 points and above of that semester to be qualified for advanced graduation.

2. Students who transfer into the third grade of the university shall not be eligible for advanced graduation due to their short term of study at the university.

Article 6 Documents for application

All applicants shall submit one copy of their overall transcript; transfer students shall submit one copy of transcript of their original school as well.

Article 7 These regulations will be adopted by the Academic Affairs Meeting before being reported to the Ministry of Education for ratification and implementation, as shall amendments when they are made.

If any controversies or disputes occurred regarding clauses of the contract, it shall always refer to its Chinese version.
