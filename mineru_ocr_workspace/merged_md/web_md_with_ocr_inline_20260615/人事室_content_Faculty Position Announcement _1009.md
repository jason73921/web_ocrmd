- **文章簡介**: The Department of Information Communication at Yuan Ze University, Taiwan, invites applications for full-time faculty positions at the ranks of Professor, Associate Professor, or Assistant Professor. The expected starting date is August 1, 2026.

Position Details

For more information：  https://reurl.cc/oK2GRQ (另開新視窗) 

Areas of Specialization Include (but are not limited to)：


Multimedia Technology

Artificial Intelligence-Generated Content (AIGC)

Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)

Human-Machine Interface and Interactive Technology

Computer Graphics and Image Processing

Computer Vision

Digital Learning

Game Design

3D Modeling and Animation Design

Interactive Art Design

Metaverse Design


Qualifications


A Ph.D. degree in a relevant field recognized by Taiwan"s Ministry of Education.

Demonstrated enthusiasm for teaching, commitment to service, and ability to collaborate effectively in a collegial environment.

Proficiency in English for teaching purposes.


Required Application Materials


Cover Letter – Indicate your area of specialization and explain your motivation for applying to Yuan Ze University.

 Application Form for Faculty Position (Word format) (另開新視窗)  （Email 至：  itkammie@saturn.yzu.edu.tw (開啟郵件程式) ）

 YZU Faculty Qualification Form (另開新視窗) 

Curriculum Vitae – Include educational background, research interests, teaching experience, and a list of academic achievements.

The original Ph.D. diploma and Transcript – Foreign degrees must be authenticated by Taiwan"s embassies or missions.

Teaching Rank Accreditation Certificate (if applicable).

Catalog of academic publications – Include publication year, journal/conference name, and co-authors.

A copy of the Ph.D. dissertation.

Representative Publications – Full-text versions of key works published within the last five years.

Research Statement – Summarize contributions and outline future plans.

Teaching Statement – Include courses qualified to teach (see  undergraduate (另開新視窗)  /  graduate programs (另開新視窗) ）。

Two Recommendation Letters – Include contact information of recommenders.

Other Supporting Materials – Awards, patents, teaching evaluations, etc.


Applicants who have not yet completed all supporting documents may submit primary materials first (such as CV) to initiate the review process.

Submission Details

Deadline： April 10, 2026。Applications reviewed on a rolling basis。

※ Foreign applicants requiring a work permit must apply by March 6。

Email Submission：Combine all documents into a single PDF and send to：  itkammie@saturn.yzu.edu.tw (開啟郵件程式) 。
