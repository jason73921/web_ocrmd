因應嚴重特殊傳染性肺炎(新型冠狀病毒)疫情，敬請配合本校學生宿舍各項措施，謝謝！

 

1. 24:00-06:00各出入口實施門禁。

2. 出入口每日07:30-09:30、11:00-13:00、17:00-19:00配合量體溫。

3. 男一宿B1及1A側門、男二宿2F安全門關閉。

4. 交誼廳、閱讀室關閉。

5. 男女二宿空調關閉。

 

防疫非常時期，造成不便，敬請見諒～

 

 

In accordance with the epidemic prevention policy, these actions will be immediately put into effect:

1. 24:00 - 06:00 Entrance control is implemented at  each entrance.

2. Body temperature is measured daily at entrance at 07:30 - 09:30, 11:00 - 13:00, 17:00 - 19:00.

3. Side doors B1 and 1A in Male dorm 1 and safety door 2F in Male dorm 2 are closed.

4. Lounge and self-learning rooms are closed.

5. Air conditioners in Male dorm 2 and Female dorm 2 are turned off.

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
