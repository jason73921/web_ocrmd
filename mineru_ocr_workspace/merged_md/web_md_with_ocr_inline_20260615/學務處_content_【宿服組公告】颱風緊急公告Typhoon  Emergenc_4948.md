## 颱風緊急公告

目前凱米颱風已發展為中度颱風，中央氣象局預計將在今日下午（07/22）發佈陸上颱風警報請住宿生務必注意以下幾點：

1.自行準備防颱物資（至少可供3天）

•儲存飲用水及乾糧（泡麵，餅乾）

•準備手電筒，以防停水停電

•準備個人藥品

•充满行動電源

2.時刻注意颱風動態

•關注颱風的最新消息，如淹水、道路封鎖等新聞

3. 避免從事任何戶外活動

請避免任何非必要的外出。即使颱風風勢突然減弱，可能只是暂時現象。

4.檢查並固定好陽台和窗戶周邊物品

確保所有陽台和窗戶周邊的物品固定好，防止被風吹走

# Typhoon. Emergency Notice

Currently, Typhoon GAEMI has developed into a moderate typhoon. The Central Weather Administration is expected to issue a land warning today afternoon (07/22). Dormitory residents, please pay attention to the following:

## 1. Prepare typhoon supplies (at least for 3 days):

• Store drinking water and dry food (such as instant noodles and biscuits)

• Prepare a fl ashlight to prevent power outages

• Have personal medications ready

• Ensure your power banks are fully charged

## 2. Stay updated on typhoon developments:

• Keep an eye on the latest news about the typhoon, such as fl ooding and road closures

## 3. Avoid any outdoor activities:

• Please avoid any unnecessary outings. Even if the typhoon appears to weaken, it may only be temporary

## 4. Secure items around balconies and windows:

• Check and secure all items around balconies and windows to prevent them from being blown away

If you need any assistance, please contact Yuan Ze University's security hotline at 03-4553698 or the Yuan Ze Dormitory Office at 0903032011.
