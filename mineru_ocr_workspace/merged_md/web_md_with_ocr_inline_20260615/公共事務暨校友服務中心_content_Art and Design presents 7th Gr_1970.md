- **文章簡介**: “The Edge” opens at Warehouse 2 of Songshan Cultural and Creative Park starting from June 3 to 6. There are 33 artworks in the exhibition, including paintings, decoration arts, video works, product design and community artworks. Graduates display what they have learned for the past four years and have left good memories to the university.

Yang Han-Yu (楊涵友), the general coordinator for the event, says “The Edge” exposes a non-mainstream phenomenon in our modern society. The idea of decentralization dominated the culture trends after the mid-20 century. Decentralization has become a measure instead of fulfilling a purpose while we were in the time which the social transition was shifting between mainstream and non-mainstream. Perspectives coming from non-mainstream vision keep us standing in a clear and critical thinking, and the attitude with confidence and respect inspires our creativities in art expression.
