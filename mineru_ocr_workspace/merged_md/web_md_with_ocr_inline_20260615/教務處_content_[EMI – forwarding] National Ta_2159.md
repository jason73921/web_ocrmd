The aforementioned workshop series is jointly organized by our Bilingual Education Center and Fulbright Taiwan (Foundation for Scholarly Exchange). Through sessions led by U.S.-based professional teaching advisors, the workshops aim to enhance instructors’ EMI teaching competencies and support the ongoing development and practical effectiveness of EMI courses.




A total of four EMI faculty development workshops will be held during the Spring Semester 2026. Details are as follows: (1) March 17 (Tue), 2026 – Professional Development Workshop (2) April 7 (Tue), 2026 – Professional Development Workshop (3) May 12 (Tue), 2026 – Professional Development Workshop (4) June 2 (Tue), 2026 – Teaching Sharing Session




Time: 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
