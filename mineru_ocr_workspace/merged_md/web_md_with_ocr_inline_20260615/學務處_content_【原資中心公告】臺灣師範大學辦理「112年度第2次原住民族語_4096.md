Subject: Commissioned by the Indigenous Peoples Commission and the Indigenous Languages Research and Development Foundation, National Taiwan Normal University conducts the "112th Year Second Indigenous Language Proficiency Certification Test."

Directions:



The registration period for the test is from September 7th, 112th Year (Thursday) to October 6th, 112th Year (Friday). The test will be held on December 2nd, 112th Year (Saturday). The test levels include Elementary, Intermediate, Intermediate-High, and Advanced. Registration is open to individuals of all nationalities, ethnicities, ages, and educational backgrounds.




The brochure and related test information can be found and downloaded from the website of the "112th Year Second Indigenous Language Proficiency Certification Test" (https://exam.sce.ntnu.edu.tw/abst/). For inquiries, please contact the Candidate Service Line at (02) 7749-3664 or the toll-free number 0800-699-566. You can also stay updated by following the official LINE account for certification (@107abst).
