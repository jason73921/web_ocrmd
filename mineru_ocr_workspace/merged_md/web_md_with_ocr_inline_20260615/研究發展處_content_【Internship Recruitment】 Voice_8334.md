Our parent company, Voice of Victory Broadcasting Co., is actively dedicated to cultivating broadcasting talent. Both organizations are pleased to offer summer and semester internship opportunities to students.

 

Internship Details:

 





Location: Voice of Victory Broadcasting Co. (No. 22, Sec. 1, Jiankang Rd., West Central Dist., Tainan City 70052)




Internship Period:



Summer Internship: July 1, 2026, to August 31, 2026.




Priority will be given to those who can complete the full two-month internship. Please note that summer internships are not calculated based on individual hours or days.







Working Hours: Monday to Friday, 08:30–12:00 and 13:30–18:00 (8 hours per day).



Lunch Break: 12:00–13:30. Meals are not provided; interns are expected to dine out on their own.








P.S. If support is needed for events held on Saturdays or Sundays, we will consult with interns beforehand to check their availability. If interns assist with weekend events, they may take compensatory time off on weekdays.





Assignments: A reflective report of at least 500 words is required for activities performed.




Compensation: Please note that this internship is unpaid.





How to Apply:



Please visit our website and complete the "Student Internship Resume Form" online.




Deadline: Applications must be submitted by 12:00 AM on April 19, 2026.




Interview: The Planning Department will arrange interviews.



Tentative Interview Date: April 26, 2026, at 10:00 AM.




Location: No. 22, Sec. 1, Jiankang Rd., West Central Dist., Tainan City (Voice of Victory Broadcasting Co.).







Final Confirmation: Once the admission is confirmed, the student’s department office must send an official formal letter to the Foundation to finalize the internship application.
