- **文章簡介**: The first waiting list process for AY 115 student dormitories has officially concluded (Male students: up to waiting list number 357; Female students: up to waiting list number 204). No further waiting list admissions will be arranged at this stage.

If additional beds become available in the future, applications will be handled through a manual waiting list registration system. The application period and registration procedures will be announced separately.

Thank you for your patience and cooperation throughout the waiting list process. For any future housing updates or changes, please continue to follow the latest announcements from the Student Housing Service Section.
