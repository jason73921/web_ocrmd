# 圖書館租用型置物柜Lockers Available for Rent

![](images/b7e1746368defdd387cb406d244d2eba85fad6a4ca1db2bf9f9ba5a72a549094.jpg)

租賃對象：1)校內學生優先。

2)持有圖書館核發之借書證者(限額10名)

租用費用：以學期計價，每櫃收費450元。

租用期限：：上學期8/1\~1/15。

下學期 2/1\~7/15。(若遇閉館日，再依公告為主)

申請方式：

>持本人學生證或借書證，當場繳費後，並當場啟用。

>二人共用者，請務必同時到場

>櫃號為現場排序，不提供選位。

>提醒：借閱權限停權者(如：借書逾期或欠款)將無法辦理租用。

A limited number of student card-based lockers are available to rent in the Library. The rental fee is NT\$450 per semester. The rental period of first semester starts from Aug.1, to Jan.15, and the rental period of second semester starts from Feb.1, to Jul. 15.

> Applications forrental will be accepted at the Library Circulation Desk on a first come, first served basis.

> Any YZU students with valid library privileges and in need of storage for personal belongings are encouraged to act soon.

> Do present a valid student card at the Library Circulation Desk and pay any fee at the time of rental.

> Be noted that up to 2 students may share the same locker.

任何諮詢，請洽圖書館流通櫃台（分機2321）

For any inquiry, contact the Circulation Desk or extension number 2321.
