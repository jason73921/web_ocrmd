113-10 行政會議摘要 Executive Summary of Minutes

一、通過113學年度第二季預算調整申請複審案 The second review of “adjustment application for Q2 of the 2024 academic year.

二、通過本校「大學部二年制在職專班學則」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “undergraduate two-year in-service program regulations” was approved.

三、通過本校「二年制技術學系在職進修班運作辦法」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “regulations for the operation of the two-year inservice technical program” was approved.

四、通過本校「研究所在職進修專班運作辦法」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “regulations for the operation of the in-service graduate program” was approved.

五、通過本校「校務研究獎勵辦法」新訂審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations for institutional research incentives” was approved.

六、通過本校「教學單位自我評鑑作業規範」修正審議案 The amendment to "YZU academic unit self-evaluation guidelines” was approved.

七、通過本校「承攬商職業安全衛生管理辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations safety and health management for contractor labor” was approved.

八、通過本校「零用金管理辦法」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU regulations governing the management of petty cash” was approved.

九、通過本校「軍訓人員獎懲辦法」廢止審議案 The abolition of the “YZU university's military training personnel awards and penalties regulations” was approved.

十、備查113學年度關帳通知報告案 The report of the “closing fiscal year announcement of academic year 2014” was ratified and approved.

十一、備查本校「亞東醫師駐元智諮詢服務站」新設報告案 The report of “Far Eastern Memorial Hospital doctor consultation service station at YZU” was approved.
