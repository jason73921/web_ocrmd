- **文章簡介**: AI is convenient and powerful, but using it properly is the real skill!
 We can leverage AI to spark ideas, organize information, and draft content. However, the output still requires your judgment and review to ensure that emerging technologies truly support academic research. To maintain research quality, please keep the following principles in mind when using AI tools:

1. Disclose AI Usage

Honesty is the core value of academic integrity. Using AI tools is not prohibited, but you must clearly state how and when AI was used. This helps readers and reviewers understand the source of your work and demonstrates your commitment to research integrity.

2. Verify Information

Even AI can make mistakes! Treat yourself as the quality controller of your research. Always fact-check and confirm the accuracy of AI-generated content to prevent errors from becoming the root of misconduct and compromising research quality.

3. Do Not Copy and Paste

AI-generated content can be referenced, but never copy it verbatim. Add your own interpretation and insights to showcase your originality. If you use someone else’s ideas or statements, make sure to cite them properly to avoid plagiarism or copyright infringement.

4. Keep Complete Records

AI is not a substitute for human effort. Document your creative process, including prompts given to AI, edits made, screenshots or recordings of your work, and versions of your drafts. These records can serve as proof of transparency and integrity when needed.
