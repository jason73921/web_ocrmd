Applicants granted eligibility must reside in the dormitory for both the Fall and Spring semesters of Academic Year 115. Early termination will result in forfeiture of the dorm deposit and the Fall semester housing fee, and the Spring semester housing fee may still be payable in accordance with relevant regulations. Applicants should carefully assess their suitability for communal living and their health condition before applying, so as not to afect the housing rights of other resiets.

Applicants for dormitory residence should prepare the Student Housing Agreement in advance.

This schedule outlines the preliminary timeline and procedures for bed assignments. Adjustments may be made as necessary. All matters are subject to the official St announcements issued at the time of implementation.
