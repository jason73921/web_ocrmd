- **文章簡介**: The results of the 30th Male Student Dormitory Association President and Vice President election are as follows: Agree: 133 votes Disagree: 16 votes Invalid ballots: 18 votes

The results of the 30th Female Student Dormitory Association President and Vice President election are as follows: Agree: 148 votes Disagree: 4 votes Invalid ballots: 9 votes

This election was conducted in accordance with Article 5 of the Organizational Charter of the Yuan Ze University Student Dormitory Association, under the supervision of the Election Committee. The election threshold was set by the committee as “the number of Agree votes must exceed the number of Disagree votes.”

Based on the vote count results, all candidate pairs for the 30th Male and Female Student Dormitory  Association President and Vice President positions have met the election criteria and are duly elected.

The election results are hereby announced in accordance with the relevant regulations. The list of elected candidates will be handled and announced separately in accordance with the Student Dormitory Association regulations.
