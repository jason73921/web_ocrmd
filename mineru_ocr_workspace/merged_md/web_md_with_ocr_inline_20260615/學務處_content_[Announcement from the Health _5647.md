- **文章簡介**: Dear All,

The Ministry of Health and Welfare, Centers for Disease Control (Taiwan) and the Urbani Memorial Foundation have jointly produced three animated videos focusing on HIV prevention, testing, and treatment. Each video is approximately 4-5 minutes long and is available in multiple languages, including Chinese, English, Indonesian, Vietnamese, and Thai.

The video links can be accessed via the CDC"s Global Information Website (https://www.cdc.gov.tw/) under the following navigation path:
Infectious Diseases and Epidemic Prevention Topics > Infectious Disease Introduction > Category III Notifiable Infectious Diseases > HIV/AIDS > Awareness Materials > Multimedia > Videos.

Feel free to download and utilize these resources.
