Subject: Water supply will be suspended due to Boiler Replacement in Dorm 1

Date: 2026/1/12-2026/2/2

Description:

1.Water Supply Suspension (Jan 12 - 13): Due to rooftop water pipeline maintenance, water supply to Dorm 1 will be completely suspended. For washing and grooming needs, please bring student ID card proceed to the Fitness Center. The water supply will be restored immediately upon completion of the construction work.
Shower Availability: 9:00~10:00 、 19:00~21:00.

2.Hot Water Suspension (Jan 14 – Feb 2): Hot water will not be available in Dorm 1 during the construction. Students may use hot water from the electric water heaters which located in the following areas: 1F Area B: 3 shower rooms, 2F – 7F Area B: 1 shower room on each floor.
Note: Electric water heaters provide hot water 24 hours a day. However, continuous use will require time for the water to reheat. Students are advised to stagger their usage times and check the water temperature gauge on the heaters before use to avoid insufficient water temperature affecting the quality.

3.Hoisting Project Operation on Jan 16, 2026 (Fir.) 09:00-16:00.  The plaza in front of the Main Entrance of Area B on 1st floor of Dorm 1 will be restricted. Please refrain from using this entrance during the construction period.

4.During construction, please be mindful of workers moving in and out of the area. We apologize for any inconvenience caused and thank you for your understanding.
