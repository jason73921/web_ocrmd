說明：

一、 依本校109學年度行事曆，109年10月2日（週五）因中秋節假期調整放假，應於109年9月26日（週六）補課。

 



二、 109年9月26日（週六）當天補課教室安排原則如下：

 （一）1091學期原排定於星期五或星期六上課之課程，以原教室上課為原則；

 （二）如教室有衝堂者，除系上另行排定外，以星期六之課程優先安排於原教室。當天上課排定之教室，請參閱附件 (1090926補課教室)。

   

 教務處／課務組 敬啟

  

【Announcement by Office of Academic Affairs】

 

 Subject: Declaration on the Scheduling of Make-up Classes on SEP. 26th, 2020 (Sat).



1. According to the calendar of academic year 2020-2021, classes on OCT. 2nd, 2020 (Fri) which are adjusted as Moon Festival (bridge holiday) should be made up on SEP. 26th, 2020 (Sat).

2. The scheduling of make-up classes on SEP. 26th, 2020 (Sat) is based on the following principles:

    (1) Friday or Saturday classes as per the original schedule of 2020 Fall Semester maintain the original classroom. 

    (2) When classes are in overlapping time, Saturday classes is top priority on the original classroom unless there is another arrangement by the department. Please refer to the attachment (1090926 Make-up classes)for the classrooms scheduled on that day.
