## 1ST NAMR INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

The National Academy of Marine Research (NAMR) celebrates the fifth anniversary and aims to enhance academic and technical exchanges in the field of marine science and technology. We are pleased to announce that the lst VisionMarine Conference will take place fromApril 22 to24,2024 inKaohsiung,Taiwan.

This conference has invited a total of 21 international marine scholars fromthe United States,the United Kingdom, Germany, Canada, Malaysia, South Korea, Japan, Sweden, and the Philippines to give keynote speeches. This provides an opportunity for participants to grasp the latest trends in marine research. (For detailed information about the scholars, please refer to the conference website for the scholars' profiles.)
