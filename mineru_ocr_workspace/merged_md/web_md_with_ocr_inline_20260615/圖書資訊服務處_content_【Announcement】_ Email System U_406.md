- **文章簡介**: 【Faculty and Admin Staff】: Email System Upgrade Notice

We will conduct a university-wide Email System Upgrade operation from 1st of August 2019, for an approximate duration of two weeks. Although we expect a flawless operation, a temporary email service interruption may still occur. In any case, we apologize for any inconvenience. The details of this operation are as such:

Operation start date: 1st of August 2019 –

Scope of impact: university-wide email system servers

Contact information: Section of Network and Media (isnm@saturn.yzu.edu.tw, internal extension number: 2325).

If your computer pops out the following window during the upgrade operation, please press OK and close the Outlook application and then re-start the application.




【Student】: New Email Service Announcement – Official Launch of Office 365

Through continuous efforts to provide a more creative and smarter email service, the Office of Library and Information Services will conduct the Student Email System’s upgrade and transfer operation from 1st to 7th of August, 2019. Although we expect a smooth operation, a temporary email service interruption may still occur.

For more details regarding the settings for this new email service, please refer to the personal email account setup website. Note that the benefits of using Office 365 include:


A very large email storage size of 50G.

Provision of “anytime, anywhere access” (or web-enabled access) that is device-independent, regardless of any desktops or smartphones.

A comprehensive set of tools for collaboration and learning, including Microsoft Teams (a group collaboration platform), 1 TB of OneDrive personal storage, etc.


More details on Office 365 features will be posted on the Office of Library and Information Services website. If you encounter any problems related to Office 365 usage, please contact the Section of Network and Media at (03) 4638800 ext. 2325 or isnm@saturn.yzu.edu.tw.
