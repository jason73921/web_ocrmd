# CALL FOR PAPERS

# 2023 Feng Chia Conference on

Recent Advances in Sustainable Development, Green Finance, and Regional Cooperation

Organized by Department of Economics, Feng Chia University, Taichung, Taiwan

We are pleased to announce the international conference on economic development, international trade and finance to be hosted on June 20, 2023 at its beautiful campus in Taichung, Taiwan. We welcome both theoretical and empirical papers in the broadly defined areas below:

 Sustainable development, income distribution, and economic growth

 Climate change, carbon neutrality, and remedy policy

Circular economy and environmental policy

 ESG and green finance

 Digital Age, big data, and platform economy

 Pandemic, public health, tourism, and the economy

Regional cooperation and sustainable development

Globalization, localization, and trade

Global supply chain and sustainable growth

Exchange rate and international capital movement

 Dutch disease and The Japan’s Lost Decade

 Research, innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic growth

FDI, infrastructure, and growth

Inflation, growth, and monetary policy

 Asian economies: past, present, and future

## Keynote Speakers:

Professor Hamid Beladi, University of Texas, San Antonia, USA

Professor Eden Yu, Chu Hai College of Higher Education, Hong Kong

## Submission Guidelines:

1. All papers will be presented in English or Chinese.

2. Online submission with papers or extended abstracts should be made no later than April 30, 2023 to Ms. Tseng at mctseng@fcu.edu.tw. Preference will be given to completed papers or detailed drafts. The selected authors will be notified by May16, 2023.

3. Upon receiving acceptance, authors must confirm attendance no later than May 31, 2023.

## Registration:

 Conference refreshments and materials will be provided with no registration fee.

## Supporting journals:

Conference papers can be submitted to Journal of Asian Economics and The North American Journal of Economics & Finance under regular review process

## Collaborating institutions/organizations/journals:

Research Center for International Trade and Business, Chu Hai College of Higher Education, Hong Kong

International University (Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City), Vietnam

International Journal of Business and Economics, College of Business, Feng Chia University
