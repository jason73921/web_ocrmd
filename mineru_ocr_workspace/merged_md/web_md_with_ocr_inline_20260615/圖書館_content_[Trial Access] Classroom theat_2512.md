Overview:

Classroom Theater features a wide range of thematic films, including topics such as gender equality, life education, human rights, and special education. It offers a collection of at least 400 films, with new titles added regularly. The platform also provides downloadable worksheets to support teaching and learning, making it a valuable resource for both teachers and students. It supports multiple devices, including computers, tablets, and smartphones, allowing users to stream content on demand without the need for downloads. With internet access, users can conveniently watch legally licensed and educationally meaningful films anytime, anywhere.
