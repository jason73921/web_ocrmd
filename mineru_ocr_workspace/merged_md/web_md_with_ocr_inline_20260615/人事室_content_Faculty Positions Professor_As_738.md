## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 738
- **文章標題**: Faculty Positions Professor/Associate Professor/Assistant Professor  Faculty Positions in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management (IEM)
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/faculty-positions-professor-associate-professor-assistant-professor-faculty-positions-in-the-department-of-industrial-engineering-and-management-iem
- **文章簡介**: Founded in 1989, Department of Industrial Engineering and Management (IEM) at Yuan Ze University is one of the leading IEM departments in Taiwan. YZU IEM currently offers four degree programs: the undergraduate program (since 1989), graduate master program (since 1990), graduate doctorate program (since 1992) and the in-service master program (since 1999). In 2020, ensuring the department’s continuous excellence, the four degree programs of IEM department have been accredited successfully and received a 6-year accreditation from IEET. Candidates with enthusiasm for teaching, research, and service are welcome to join our team.


Position: tenure-track assistant professor or above

Start date:  August 1, 2024

Openings: 1 position

Qualifications and expertise:

Ministry of Education in Taiwan recognized doctorate degrees in Industrial Engineering, Management, Mathematics, or Informatics related fields from local or abroad;

Excellent research performance;

Enthusiasm about teaching and serving, team spirit, ability to cooperate in harmony with coworker.

Expertise in statistics and quality management, services science management, smart manufacturing, information technology , data science, production and supply chain management, or other IEM-related fields;

Ability to teach/instruct undergraduate and graduate level courses in both Chinese and English.

Application deadline: Please submit the application materials to lchsieh@saturn.yzu.edu.tw by February 20, 2024, and please specify in the email subject: Application Form for Faculty- OOO.


Required documents:

Fill in the word file "Basic Information Form for Recruited Teachers of the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, Yuan Ze University";

Personal autobiography;

Statement of research and teaching interests (including brief description of research interests, projects participated, teaching plans of courses, and future research directions);

A copy of the highest degree certificate (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit a graduation diploma verified by the ROC overseas official office; for those who have not obtained the graduation diploma can submit the instructor’s certificate indicating the expected date of graduation);

A copy of the highest degree academic transcript (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit the transcript verified by the ROC overseas official office);

A copy of the official teacher certificate of the current rank (exempt if not yet obtained the certificate);

A copy of publication(s) and list (including patents) within five years (must indicate the source);

Letter of recommendations (at least two and the referrers should email the letter to lchsieh@saturn.yzu.edu.tw ).

Candidates who pass the primary selection will be notified to come for an interview (the interview will proceed in English and be recorded).

For the interview:

Candidates will need to provide a separate "Teacher Qualification Accreditation Form" (in addition to completing the online form on the Ministry of Education"s Academic Review Committee website, candidates must submit both the hard and electronic files);


Candidates with a foreign degree must provide a "List of study status" and "Immigration records” (please contact the Immigration Department)". Please refer to the website of our personnel office: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/2016-03-17-06-14-27
