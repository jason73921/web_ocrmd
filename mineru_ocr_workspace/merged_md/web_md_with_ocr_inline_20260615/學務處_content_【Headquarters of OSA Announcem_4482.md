# 網站文章資料_【Headquarters of OSA Announcement】2024 Spring Break Consecutive Holidays-Public Transportation Preferential Measures_4482

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 4482
- **文章標題**: 【Headquarters of OSA Announcement】2024 Spring Break Consecutive Holidays-Public Transportation Preferential Measures
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/st/index.php/tw/?option=com_content&view=article&id=4482
- **文章簡介**: In response to the coming of the consecutive Spring Festival holidays in 2024, the Highway Bureau has formulated a series of public transportation plans and implemented a number of passenger fare and transfer preferential measures during this Spring Festival holiday to effectively alleviate the problem of transportation to various scenic spots. to ensure the safety and convenience of passengers.

 

The Highway Bureau of the Ministry of Transport has launched the following transportation preferential measures during the consecutive Spring Festival holidays: 88 national highway passenger routes enjoy weekday discounts or a 15% discount on the original fare. In addition, if you take national highway passenger transport, high-speed rail, and Taiwan Railway, you can transfer to local highway passenger transport within 10 hours. Enjoy 1 segment ticket or basic mileage for free. In addition to using electronic tickets to swipe your card directly to enjoy transfer discounts, passengers holding National Highway Passenger Transport, Taiwan Railway and High-Speed Rail paper tickets can also use the card check-in machines installed at Taiwan Railway, high-speed rail stations and national highway stations before exiting the station. Once the electronic ticket has been processed, you can enjoy the transfer discount by using the electronic ticket to take local passenger transportation within 10 hours.

 

In addition, the Highway Bureau of the Ministry of Transport has increased the preferential plan for the eastern part of the country to rent a foot on the ground and a free plan for four people traveling together. From February 8 to February 14, all passengers traveling on the Eastern National Highway can enjoy the rental of a foot on the ground. The discounts are as follows: Holders of Eastern National Highway Passenger Transport outbound tickets can enjoy car rental discounts from designated operators; Holders of Eastern National Highway Passenger Transport round-trip tickets + car rentals can enjoy ticket discounts, with a discount of 200 yuan for car rentals and a 100 yuan discount for renting motorcycles. For the other four people traveling together, one person is free. If you hold the Eastern Taiwan Travel Package or a legal B&B certificate, you can enjoy round-trip tickets to Beiyi and Beihua for four people traveling together and one person is free. The total return ticket will be refunded at NT$200. For discount details, please visit the Ministry of Transportation and Highways Bureau website and Taiwan Haoxing Travel Service official website.

 

Finally, it is recommended that passengers taking road transportation can check the passenger schedule before taking the bus or download the Highway Bureau"s "iBus_Road Passenger Transport" APP to check the shuttle bus time. It is easier to grasp the shuttle bus dynamics and ensure a smooth trip. The Highway Bureau of the Ministry of Transport cares about you.

For more details, please visit the Highways Bureau"s website.
