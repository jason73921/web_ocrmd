## Gender-Friendly Housing Application Process

1. Students who have obtained dormitory eligibility may submit a request to the Student Housing Service Section.

2. Complete the “Gender-Friendly Housing Application Form.”

3. The Student Housing Service Section will conduct an initial review and interview.

4. If necessary, students may be referred to the Counseling Center or gender equity education-related resources.

(Such referrals are intended to provide care and support for students and are not used as criteria for housing eligibility review.)

5. A comprehensive evaluation of housing needs and feasible accommodation arrangements will be conducted.

(Including considerations such as gender identity, safety, privacy, and dormitory management.)

6. Applicants will be notified of the application result, and follow-up housing arrangements will be assisted accordingly.

7. Continued care and follow-up support will be provided after check-in when necessary.
