- **文章全文**: Last weekend, YZU-GAO organized a 3-day and 2 nights trip to Tainan-Kenting-Pingtung for international students of YZU in association with LION travel company. This trip was organized so that students can learn about Taiwanese culture and heritage. More than 25 international students participated from different countries.

Among them, Dieuhang Nguyen a student from Vietnam currently pursuing her Ph.D. degree in Finance told,” Excited to go out and enjoy this trip after a long pandemic break following regulations.” The students enjoyed the water sports activities like snorkeling, and banana boats, visit many cultural places, and most importantly enjoyed authentic Taiwanese food.

Mr. Lee, the guide was very informative regarding the history of Taiwan and made the journey interesting and memorable for everyone. We hope to organize more of these tours in the future and look forward to your active participation.
