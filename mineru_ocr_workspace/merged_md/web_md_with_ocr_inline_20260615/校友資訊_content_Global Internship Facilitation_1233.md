The Global Internship Facilitation of Taiwan (GIFT) is a government-supported platform that helps international students and young professionals find internship and job opportunities across Taiwan. Whether you’re looking for a summer or winter internship, a semester internship, full-time positions, or part-time jobs, GIFT connects you with leading companies, startups, and research institutes to jumpstart your career in Taiwan.
