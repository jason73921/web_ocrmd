親愛的同學您們好：

為建立學生宿舍為友善環境，並營造住宿溫馨氛圍。

下班時間同學有需協助事項，請撥打0903-032011執勤專線（手機），

或撥宿舍分機2881（男一櫃檯）、2882（女一櫃檯），

我們將有專人協助處理。

學務處宿服組  關心您~

 

Hello Dear students:

In order to establish a friendly environment in the student dormitory and create a warm accommodation atmosphere.

 If students need assistance during off-hours, please call the duty hotline (mobile) 0903-032011.

Or dial the dormitory extension 2881 (male counter) or 2882 (female counter)

We will have someone special to assist you.

The Student Housing Service Section of the Student Affairs Office cares about you~
