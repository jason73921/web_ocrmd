Subject: Application Period and Regulations for Change of Major
 Academic Year 115

Content:


Application Period:
 March 17, 2026 (Mon.) – April 10, 2026 (Fri.)

Students from offshore islands who were admitted through the Guaranteed Admission Program must obtain permission from their original local government before applying for a change of major. Applications without such approval will be dismissed.

Freshmen applying for a change of major within the same academic field must have an average grade of 60 or above for the first academic year; otherwise, the application will be dismissed.

Freshmen whose average grade in the first semester of the first academic year is below 60 are not eligible to apply for a change of major within the same academic field.

Students approved for a change of major due to departmental requirements or voluntary demotion and classified as demoted students must, in accordance with regulations, complete their studies through at least the second semester of their senior year before graduation.
 Students who meet the criteria for excellent academic performance and are eligible for early graduation may apply to graduate one semester earlier, in accordance with the regulations governing early graduation.

Required Forms:
 ■ Student Application Form for Department Transfer
 ■ Yuan Ze University Student Self-Planning and Analysis Form for Department Transfer


In addition, applicants must attach an official academic transcript for all academic years, including class ranking.


Please refer to each department’s website for detailed regulations regarding the change of major.
