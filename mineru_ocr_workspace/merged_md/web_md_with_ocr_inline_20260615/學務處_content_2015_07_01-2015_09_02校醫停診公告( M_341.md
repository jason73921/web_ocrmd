全校教職員工生，大家好：

 

103學年度校醫看診於2015/07/01（三）開始停診，暑假期間若有需要醫師診療服務，請至鄰近醫療院所就診，不便之處敬請見諒。

本組仍於暑假期間提供衛生保健諮詢及意外事件處理服務，暑假(7/6-8/30)服務時間為週一至週四(9:00-16:30)。

 

Medical Service is not available for summer vacation. Campus medical service will not be available during summer vacation, closed on June 23rd. If medical care is needed during this period, please contact the nearest hospital or clinic. 

Healthcare consultations and accident assistance remains as usual. It opens from 9:00 to 16:30,Monday to Thursday, during the summer vacation.

 

祝福大家  暑假平安健康喜樂！！

 

 

學務處衛生保健組關心您
