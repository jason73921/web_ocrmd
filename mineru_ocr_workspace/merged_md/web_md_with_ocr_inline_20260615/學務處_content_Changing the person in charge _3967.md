Change the person in charge of the club

## The person in charge suddenly transferred or suspended school, how to change the club possession?

A：Ac the meeting (or proxy),and hand in the following documents to Extracurricular Activity Section,then change the club possession on Portal.

## Document

1. Report form for special events of student club Title: the reason of changing the person in charge the class and the term of office of new/ old person in charge

Ex:XX系2A000同學（學號）於110.06.30结束社團負責人任期XX系2B000同學（學號）（手機號碼）於110.07.01開始擔任社團負责人

2.The record in the meeting It needs to include the reason of changing the person in charge,how to decide the new person in charge, the class and the term of office of new/old person in charge in detal

3.Sign in form in the meeting (can use the presentform of the club)

good bye
