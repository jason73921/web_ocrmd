# 網站文章資料_Procedures for newly admitted students/transferring students applying for on-line Course Credit Transfer for the 2nd semester of 113 School Year_1847

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 1847
- **文章標題**: Procedures for newly admitted students/transferring students applying for on-line Course Credit Transfer for the 2nd semester of 113 School Year
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/aa-202502040930-02
- **文章簡介**: I. Application period: Feb.1st ~ Feb.20th, 2025 (Students must apply for it during the period; the on-line system will be closed afterwards.)

II. Notifications:

1.Verification of credit transfer:

(1)Common core courses: verified by College of General Studies, International Language and Culture Center, Physical Education Office, Office of Student Affairs

(2)Required core courses: verified by Department of major

2. (1)Course credit transfer is applied to students admitted for 113 School Year, including  

undergraduate students, graduate students and transferring students. Students who didn’t

submit the application for credit transfer or who did submit the application but forgot to do so

for certain courses during the 1st semester shall apply for it during the 2nd semester. The

application shall be only submitted once by the deadline.

(2)Transferring students admitted for 113 School Year shall submit the application for credit transfer with a written application form.

3.Transferring students (2nd year student) are applied to the list of courses for 112 School Year. Transferring students (3rd year student) are applied to the list of courses for 111 School Year. Newly admitted students (whose student numbers beginning with 113) are applied to the list of courses for 113 School Year.

4.Application procedure:

(1)YZU → Student Portal → Learning Profile → Credit Transfer

(2)The path for checking the list of required/elective courses: Office of Academic Affairs→ Students’ Resource →List of Compulsory & Elective subjects and Minor
