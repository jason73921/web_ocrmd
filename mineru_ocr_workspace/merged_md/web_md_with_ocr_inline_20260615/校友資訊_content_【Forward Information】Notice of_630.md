The candidates must meet the following criteria:

The Presidential Search Committee of Yuan Ze University, Taiwan, invites nominations and applications for the president’s position at this university starting from Feb. 1, 2022.

(1) Committed to the education ideals and the founding spirit of this university, demonstrating excellent leadership and being equipped with outstanding administrative experience.

(2) Possessing a distinguished record of academic achievement and reputation.

(3) Being a paragon with impeccable character and integrity.

(4) Meeting the qualifications stipulated by Act Governing the Appointment of Educators Article 10 (Please see below for detailed regulations).




A candidate should be nominated by one to five people who are full-time faculty members of the university, or full-time professors or researchers above the level of assistant professors at domestic or international universities (colleges) or other academic institutions, or alumni of this university; the nominees should acknowledge their acceptance of the nomination. A nomination will be considered invalid when the same nominator is found nominating two candidates.

The Presidential Search Committee will keep all candidates’ information and the entire search process confidential.

Further information, including the Regulation of Presidential Search of Yuan Ze University as well as its application formats, may be obtained from:
