一、如何申請在職證明書？多久可以拿到？是否須本人親自辦理？

承辦人：林雯靖（分機：2039）


於人事室網頁  表單下載  下載並列印在職證明申請表。

申請人可親自填寫申請，或以電子郵件申請；若不便，可委託他人代辦。

本室收到申請後三日內，以電子郵件通知領取。


English

Person in charge: Ms. Wen-Ching Lin (ext. 2039)


The Certificate of Employment Application Form can be downloaded from the  Download Forms  section.

Applicants may apply in person or via email. A representative may apply on behalf of the applicant if necessary.

The applicant will be notified when the certificate is ready (within 3 days).




二、辦理離校手續須攜帶哪些資料、證件？

承辦人：黃雅芬（分機：2224）

離職人員應於離職前一個月提出離職申請，並完成以下事項：


繳回教職員工識別證

結清公保、勞保及健保費用

薪資結算（適用月中離職者）

繳回停車證

辦理財產移交


完成流程後，將核發離職證明。

English

Person in charge: Ms. Ya-Fen Huang (ext. 2224)

Employees who plan to resign shall submit resignation one month in advance and complete the following procedures:


Return ID card

Terminate insurance (labor, health, etc.)

Salary settlement (if leaving mid-month)

Return parking permit

Complete property handover


The release certificate will be issued after completion.




三、如何取得離職證明？

承辦人：林雯靖（分機：2039）


需於離職前一個月提出辭呈並完成簽核。

離職前一週至  表單下載  列印離職手續清單。

教師若於聘期未滿離職，需繳回聘書辦理修正。

完成簽核後，離職證明將以電話通知或郵寄方式提供。


English

Person in charge: Ms. Wen-Ching Lin (ext. 2039)


Submit resignation one month in advance.

Download the exiting procedure list from  Download Forms .

Faculty leaving before contract end must return the contract.

The release certificate will be issued after approval.
