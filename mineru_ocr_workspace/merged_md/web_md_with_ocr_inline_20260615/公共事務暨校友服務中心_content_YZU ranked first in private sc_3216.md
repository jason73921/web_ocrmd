- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/pr/index.php/tw/20181006
- **文章簡介**: According to a survey about EMBA preferred by 3,000 managers of major enterprises in 2019 released by Cheers Magazine, Yuan Ze University has been the top one in private universities for three consecutive years since 2017, and go up to 7th place among all schools.

Recently, YZU enriches his faculty and school resources to attract more students. Chih-Hung Hsieh, Director of EMBA program, said that the university facilitates collaboration with local industry around Taoyuan area and provides the best in-service training courses. In the survey, YZU also receives the third place on the categories “the working location close to study” & “recommendation by supervisor and colleague” that shows the EMBA program has earned alumni and industry’s recognition.
