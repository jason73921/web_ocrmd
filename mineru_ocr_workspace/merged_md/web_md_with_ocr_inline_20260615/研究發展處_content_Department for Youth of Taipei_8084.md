Description (Details):



To assist the city"s youth in entering the global workplace, enhance their experience and adaptability in diverse international industries, and showcase the potential and innovative energy of Taipei"s youth, this Bureau is launching the "Taipei City Youth Overseas Internship Program" for the first time in 2025. This initiative combines government leadership, international collaboration, and industry connections, working with international organizations to offer internship opportunities across various fields, thereby fostering cross-cultural cooperation and practical professional experience among the youth.




The first cohort of participants in the aforementioned program interned in destinations such as the United States, Singapore, and Thailand. This Outcome Sharing Session will feature the youth personally reviewing the challenges and growth experienced in their overseas workplaces, and openly presenting their gains and insights regarding cross-cultural adaptation and project participation. It is hoped that this sharing will amplify the momentum for youth global career exploration.




Information regarding the Outcome Sharing Session is as follows:



(1) Date & Time: Saturday, November 22, 2025, from 1:30 PM to 4:30 PM.




(2) Venue: International Conference Hall, 6F, Department of Youth Affairs, Taipei City Government (6F, No. 17, Sec. 1, Ren"ai Rd., Zhongzheng Dist., Taipei City).




(3) Target Audience: All youth interested in international career development, overseas internships, and cross-cultural exchange are welcome to register and participate.




(4) Registration Method: Online registration link: https://forms.gle/Jeb8Fu3iG2qKPmWL8. Registration will close when capacity is reached.
