Origin:

In March 2018, President Chih-Yang Wu invited a few distinguished alumni for a casual dinner gathering. Following this, more alumni took turns hosting similar meetups. To strengthen the bond among elite alumni, a meeting was held in July 2019 to discuss future development and draft an annual plan.

 

Mission:

The Club currently consists of around 30 members, with gatherings planned every 3–4 months. These meetings include themed sharing sessions, workshops, corporate visits, and cultural exchanges. The aim is to serve as a platform for academic-industry collaboration and interaction among Yuan Ze’s elite alumni. It is hoped that, in addition to their professional achievements, alumni will join hands in giving back to their alma mater, thereby promoting industrial advancement and collaborative exchange.
