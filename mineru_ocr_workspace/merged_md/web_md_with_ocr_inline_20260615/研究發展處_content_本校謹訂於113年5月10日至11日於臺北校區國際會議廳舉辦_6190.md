# 2024 餐旅觀光休閒國際學術研討會─ 科技、創新、永續

![](images/70fc05f268d6df683f8ccc9b75d12174ef0a8a2e17563aa4bfb5f4a566b01bb1.jpg)

2024 年 5 月 10 日－11 日

實踐大學國際會議廳(臺北市)

研討會網頁: https://ichtlusc.usc.edu.tw/

![](images/6ec0afa8a84c4a0f93fd66d0b33eeb3606b2bce73488662da8436faf1475a03a.jpg)

研討會主題

餐旅觀光休閒：科技、創新、永續

重要日期

摘要截稿日：2024年 3月 15日（星期五）

摘要接受通知日：2024年 3月 29日（星期五）

全文截稿日：2024年 4月 19日（星期五）

報名日期：2024 年 1 月 22 日（星期一）至 4 月 19 日（星期五）

1 月 22 日起可投稿，隨到隨審，歡迎投稿者請盡早投稿，可早日獲悉審稿結果

## 研討會內涵

1. 國際知名學者/SSCI主編專題演講

Professor Kaye Chon, Dean and Chair Professor of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Travel & Tourism Marketing, and the Asia Pacific Journal of Tourism Research

Professor Marianna Sigala, Digital Transformation ourism at Sheffield Hallam University, UK.

Editor-in-chief of the Journal of Hospitality & Tourism Management

Professor Stanislav Ivanov, Tourism Economics and Vice-Rector (Research) at Varna University of Management. Editor-in-chief ROBONOMICS: the Journal of the Automated Economy, and Co-Editor-in-Chief of European Journal of Tourism Research.

2. 學業界專家論壇

3. 口頭與海報論文發表

## 徵稿領域

餐旅觀光休閒行銷與管理

創新創業管理

餐旅觀光休閒之科技應用

廚藝與美食

餐旅觀光休閒教育與訓練

餐旅觀光休閒民生當代議題與永續管理

⚫ 餐旅觀光休閒與樂活產業相關議題（家政/家庭、生活應用科學、民生、食品營養、幼保、老人、美容等議題）

## 般投稿指引

## ⚫ 兩種投稿類型

1.摘要（1500字為限，含參考文獻）

2.全文（5000字為限，含參考文獻）

## 摘要格式 務必全英撰寫

⚫ 標題頁：英文撰寫─作者訊息（姓名；單位職稱，如○○大學○○系副教授、碩士生等；可聯絡之電話號碼、e-mail信箱）。

⚫ 英文摘要：1500字摘要為限

（單行間距打字版，版面邊界採「標準」版（上下各2.54cm，左右3.18cm）。內文包括題目、緒論、研究目的、研究方法、重要發現、結論等，以及列出所用之重要參考文獻（參考文獻採 APA 第 6 或第 7 版格式）。標題置中：14 號字 Times New Roman，各段落起始內縮 5 格空白鍵，內文主體：12 號字 Times New Roman)。

請註明投稿「口頭發表」或「海報發表」。惟實際發表形式，將會依據論文品質決定為「口頭發表」或「海報發表」，在摘要審查結果告知。

## 全文格式 可採英文或中文全文發表

## 一、英文全文：

經摘要審查通過後，建請繼續以全文英文（或英文長摘要）投稿（英文全文稿word檔）。

格式：單行間距，以5000字為限（含參考文獻，採APA第6或第7版格式）。標題：14號字 Times New Roman，各段落起始內縮5格空白鍵，內文主體：12號字 TimesNew Roman。版面邊界採「標準」版（上下各2.54cm，左右3.18cm）。

## 二、中文全文：

格式：單行間距，以 5000字為限 （含參考文獻，採APA第6或第7 版格式）。

標題：14號字，內文主體：12號字，字體：中文－標楷體；英文數字－Times New

Roman，各段落起始內縮半形4格空白鍵。版面邊界採「標準」版 （上下各2.54cm，左右 3.18cm）。

全文檔也可以用簡報檔案替代，海報發表者可以直接提供海報的版面內容。

擬參加最佳論文獎遴選者，請提供英文全文檔供評選用。

三、被接受發表之論文，至少須有一位作者報名參加本研討會活動發表，論文才會編入研討會彙編。

摘要與全文格式範例請參照研討會網頁撰寫投稿文章（https://ichtlusc.usc.edu.tw），投稿請將文檔寄至大會email：ichtlusc@g2.usc.edu.tw，由於大會須編輯論文集，故請務必提供可編輯檔案類型文件（word佳）。

最佳論文獎

本研討會將採匿名審查，選出最佳論文獎，並將在開幕式頒予獎狀。

論文出版計畫

研討會全文論文彙編將申請 ISBN，請發表者協助填寫著作授權同意書，論文集將於會後存放於國家圖書館

# 2024 International Conference on Hospitality, Tourism and Leisure: Technology, Innovation, and Sustainability (2024 ICHTL)

![](images/37485eb8ef4cfec9e4102df0c44f40cee82d3fd9f5b82cc6017a89b9468f3bd3.jpg)

10-11 May 2024, Taipei, Taiwan

Organized by Shih Chien University, Taipei, Taiwan Conference Website: https://ichtlusc.usc.edu.tw/

![](images/d6acbb6bfb9bec8594d816e6d7df3a9cd241ba8d417299087f7dc8ff59a3f812.jpg)

## THEME

Hospitality, Tourism and Leisure: Technology, Innovation, and Sustainability

## IMPORTANT DATES

Abstract Submission Deadline: 15 Mar, 2024

Decision Notification: 29 Mar, 2024

Full Paper Submission Deadline: 19 Apr, 2024

Submission will be available from 22 Jan, 2024 on.

Registration Dates: 22 Jan - 19 Apr, 2024

## CONFERENCE INCLUDE

1. Famous professor or SSCI editor-in-chief keynote speeches

Professor Kaye Chon, Dean and Chair Professor of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Travel & Tourism Marketing, and the Asia Pacific Journal of Tourism Research

Professor Marianna Sigala, Digital Transformation & Tourism at Sheffield Hallam University, UK.

Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Hospitality & Tourism Management

Professor Stanislav Ivanov, Tourism Economics and Vice-Rector (Research) at Varna University of Management. Editor-in-chief ROBONOMICS: the Journal of the Automated Economy, and Co-Editor-in-Chief of European Journal of Tourism Research.

2. Industry experts and academic professor panel discussion

3. Oral and poster paper presentations

## AREA OF INTEREST

Hospitality, tourism, and leisure marketing and management

Innovation management or entrepreneurship in H&T

Technology application in tourism, leisure, and hospitality

Culinary arts and gastronomy

Education and training in tourism, leisure and hospitality

Contemporary issues on hospitality, tourism, leisure, and sustainability management

Other topics relating to tourism, leisure, hospitality, and LOHAS industry

## General Paper Submission Guidelines

Two types of submission are accepted:

Abstract (maximum 1,500 words including references section) or

Full paper (maximum 5,000 words including references section)

## ABSTRACT

Cover Page: author information (names, affiliations, current positions in their organizations, such as: Assistant Professor, Doctoral Student, etc., phone numbers and e-mail addresses).

Abstract: maximum 1,500 words including references section. (Typed single-spaced, title at the top center (Times New Roman 14-point); introduction, research purposes, method, findings, implications or conclusion and reference, 5-spaced paragraph indentation, and body content: Times New Roman 12-point).

Submission may be accepted either for Stand up or Poster based on the quality of paper or author’s preference.

## FULL PAPER

Full paper submission is allowed for the final selection process. Indicate the title, the author’s (authors’) name, affiliation, and e-mail of the full paper. For full paper submission; maximum 5,000 words including references section, single-spaced pages. Instead of submitting a full paper, you can submit a PowerPoint. For poster presenters who can submit poster file only. Submission of completed full papers, PowerPoint or poster will be acknowledged in the Conference Proceedings.

Once the paper is accepted, at least one of the authors must register for and present the paper at the conference.

Full paper submissions are required for those who want their paper to be considered as a candidate for the best paper awards. Best paper awards will be competitively selected by the paper review committee.

Please use the template to work on your abstract and full paper at the Conference Website: https://ichtlusc.usc.edu.tw

To submit your abstract or full paper, please send it to the designated email address for the conference: ichtlusc@g2.usc.edu.tw

## BEST PAPER AWARD

The best paper will be selected by anonymous review. The best paper award will be issued at the opening ceremony.

## PUBLICATION PLAN

Final copies of accepted papers will be professionally published as edited conference proceedings with ISBN for the conference.
