- **文章標題**: Faculty Positions in the Department of Art & Design，Yuan Ze University，Taiwan
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/faculty-positions-in-the-department-of-art-design-yuan-ze-university-taiwan
- **文章簡介**: 1. Position: assistant professor or above

2. Start date: Feb 1, 2025

3. Openings: 1 positions

4. Qualifications and expertise:


Ministry of Education in Taiwan recognized doctorate degrees in Art, and Design related fields from local or abroad;

Excellent research performance;

Enthusiasm about teaching and serving, team spirit, ability to cooperate in harmony with coworker.

Expertise in



Expertise in art and design creation, cross-disciplinary design, art history and other related fields.

Preference for those with experience and achievements in teaching and research, and over 3 years of teaching experience in art and design-related departments.

Ability to independently create and research, with priority given to those capable of proposing research projects.

Teaching content should focus on design creation, with a passion and patience for teaching, team spirit, and a strong commitment to the department.

Ability to lead students in professional internships, graduation projects, and participation in art and design competitions is preferred.

Ability to teach courses related to Cross-disciplinary art and design creation



We sincerely welcome applications from foreign teachers with the above expertise.

Ability to teach/instruct undergraduate and graduate level courses in both Chinese and English.


5. Application deadline: Please submit the application by Sep. 25, 2024,

6. Required documents:


Fill in the word file "Basic Information Form for Recruited Teachers of the Department of Art and Design, Yuan Ze University";

Personal autobiography;

Statement of research and teaching interests (including brief description of research interests, projects participated, teaching plans of courses, and future research directions);

A copy of the highest degree certificate (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit a graduation diploma verified by the ROC overseas official office; for those who have not obtained the graduation diploma can submit the instructor’s certificate indicating the expected date of graduation);

A copy of the highest degree academic transcript (For those who hold a foreign degree, please submit the transcript verified by the ROC overseas official office);

A copy of the official teacher certificate of the current rank (exempt if not yet obtained the certificate);

A copy of publication(s) and list (including patents) within five years (must indicate the source);

Letter of recommendations (at least two and the referrers should email the letter to tuliling@saturn.yzu.edu.tw or Add: No. 135, Yuandong Rd., Zhongli Dist., Taoyuan City 320315 , Taiwan, Yuan Ze University Department of Art & Design )
