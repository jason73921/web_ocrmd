# 未形 Future of Work

## ► 主題 Theme

未形

科技的發展總是被認爲能夠精準地勾勒未來，而跨域共創的思考方式，則牽動著人類的生活模式以及促使產業不斷更新樣貌以回應這個變幻的年代；面對眼前混沌的未知，從教育到產業，人們要如何從曖昧性中精準窺見趨勢，互相生生不已，建立時代的新價值。

## Future of Work

The Future of Technology and its development is always expected to embody and exemplify the future.

In order to react to the unpredictable changes of this age, the core essence of design must be cross‐disciplinary creative thinking. Thinking that impels us forward by accelerating industrial growth as well as pushing it into new sustainable directions.

As our world is facing an ever more chaotic and unknown future, it is our role to be aware of cutting edge technology, to investigate its worth in education and help to create, nurture and establish emerging industries
