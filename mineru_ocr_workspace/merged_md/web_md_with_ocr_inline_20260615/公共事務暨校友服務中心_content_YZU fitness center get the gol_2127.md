- **文章簡介**: In order to enhance the quality of fitness center and protect consumer’s right, The Department of Public Health in Taoyuan city held the project about golden award of water quality at the first time. YZU fitness center participated this project and has got the golden certification out of 28 competitors with effective period of 2 years to July 31, 2019.

Ching-Hui Lin, the director of Physical Education Office, said that it is very strict to do the certification on the quality of water. It can strengthen the autonomous management of Safety and Health on water quality and help consumers to choose a good and safe place. The Department of Public Health in Taoyuan City especially invited experts to evaluate fitness centers if they meet several items, such as SOP on the control of water and water quality testing and checking as well as the regulation on the management of public sanitation in the business sector.
