# 圖書館寒假開館時間/借期彈性展延公告Winter2021 Library Hours / Extension of Loan Periods

A 借期弹性展延：凡借閱（含原始借書、續借、召回）到期日介於110.1.18\~110.2.21，且符合下列彈性展延條件者，到期日一律自動展延至110.2.22。

資料類型：限圖書、書後附件、跨校借書證

讀者身份：限本校教職員生

注意事項：預約取件期限不適用展延，請依正常借閱規則辦理。

## 寒假開館時間：請參閱以下行事曆

All checked-out items with the due dates between Jan. 18th and Feb.21th,2021 and meeting the following conditions are subject to loan period extension.

Borrowers: YZU students, faculty, and staff.

Material types: books, accompanying materials, and interlibrary cards only \* The extended due date of winter loan will be on Feb.22th,2021.

Note that borrowing of non-affiliates and the materials not mentioned above should follow the regular loan policy. For more information,refer to the calendar below.
