- **文章簡介**: Notice: Procedures for students of International Bachelor applying for Change of Major of 115School Year (2026)

I. Application period: Form Monday, May. 4h to friday, May. 8th, 2026

II. Applicable objects:

(1)   Students of International Bachelor applying for changing major into Departments of the same College.

(2)   Students of the same College applying for changing major into International Bachelor.

* not applicable for English Bachelor of Business Administration College of Management

III. For students of International Bachelor applying for changing major into Departments of the same College, no application shall be submitted more than once.

IV. Change of major (due to departmental requirements or voluntary demotion) approved as demoted students ,must according to regulations, complete their studies until at least the second semester of their senior year before graduation. Additionally, students who meet the criteria for excellent academic performance and are eligible for early graduation may apply to graduate one semester ahead, as stipulated by the regulations on early graduation implementation.

V. Students applying for change of major shall satisfy rules and meet requirements of the accepting department/program. Please see the following link for reference: YZU Criteria for Reviewing Applications for Change of Major大學部轉系審查標準表

VI. Application procedure:

Fill-in the application form→ get signature from the person in charge of change-major affairs of the current department → get approval from the chairman of the current department → submit to Registration Section of Office of Academic Affairs

VII. Please see the following link for further information about change of major:

YZU → Administration → Office of Academic Affairs → Students’ Resource → Students of International Bachelor applying for change of major
