# 網站文章資料_The application for EMI incentives for 1142 semester is now open!_2171

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 2171
- **文章標題**: The application for EMI incentives for 1142 semester is now open!
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/202603241507-03
- **文章簡介**: Dear Students,

The application for EMI incentives for 1142 semester is now open! We invite students who meet the following criteria to apply.📢📢📢 the details are as follows.

✔ Purpose：

In order to encourage YZU students to actively participate in the Bilingual Learning Program for College Students (EMI Program), and to enhance Taiwanese students" English- learning motivation and language proficiency in English-taught courses. "The regulation for student incentives to Bilingual Learning Program at Yuan Ze University " is formulated.

✔ Eligibility：

🔹The scholarships shown as below are only for the domestic students who are currently enrolled in undergraduate or graduate degree programs at YZU. 

 

✔ Incentives：

These incentives are mainly divided into two categories, incentives for English proficiency tests and incentives for students taking the EMI Courses. Please read the regulation for more details before applying.

✔How to apply：

🔹To download the application form and regulation, please click 👉the link: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/emi2023/2023-12-29-03/1539-regulations-for-student-incentives-2025-12-26

🔹Students from the College of Engineering, College of Humanities and Social Sciences, and College of Medicine and Nursing, please submit the hard copies of the application form and required documents in person to R1711, Teaching Excellence Center. Application documents include the application form, a copy of your student ID card, and academic transcript/English Proficiency Test score report/SPT test score certificate. 💡Please note that the application form has changed, so do remember to download the latest version from the link above.

🔹Students from the College of Management, College of Electrical and Communication Engineering, and College of Informatics, please kindly consult your college’s EMI center for further information. Regulation of EMI incentives may be different by each college, please contact your college for details. 

✔Application Period：

Starting from today to April 30, 2026, 5:00 PM 🕛Applications will not be accepted after the deadline or the financial allocation is completely used up. Eligible students are invited to apply as soon as possible.
