Subject: Yu-Shan University Original Resource Center to Host "《祭義 · 承載vina"ʉ》 New Executive Officers Inauguration Traditional Ceremony and Intercollegiate Indigenous Student Club Social Gathering" on November 15, 2023

Description:



The Yu-Shan University Original Resource Center is pleased to invite all students to participate in the "《祭義 · 承載vina"ʉ》 New Executive Officers Inauguration Traditional Ceremony and Intercollegiate Indigenous Student Club Social Gathering" scheduled for November 15, 2023, at 6:00 PM. This event aims to strengthen the community connections among various Indigenous student organizations, resource centers, and student clubs from different schools. By conducting the traditional ceremony for the new executive officers and organizing this social gathering, we intend to promote cultural identity among Indigenous students and enhance their cohesion. Additionally, we aim to foster collaboration between on-campus and off-campus Indigenous administrative and student club units.




Event Details: (a) Date and Time: November 15, 2023 (Wednesday) at 18:00. (b) Venue: Yu-Shan University (Main Campus) in front of the Gymnasium (No. 1, Section 1, Xuecheng Road, Dashu District, Kaohsiung City). (c) Contact Person: Piya Zeng-Jun, Public Relations Officer for the club (Mobile: 0968-349-661).




To register for this event, please use the online registration form at https://lima.pse.is/2023-11-15.




In accordance with the guidelines from the Executive Yuan Environmental Protection Administration and our university"s policy on reducing the use of disposable tableware and packaged drinking water, this event will not provide or use single-use products. We kindly request all attendees to bring reusable and environmentally friendly utensils.




Thank you for your attention, and we look forward to your active participation in this meaningful event.
