- **文章簡介**: Position Title: Full-Time Faculty (Assistant Professor or above)

Number of Positions: One


Priority given to candidates specializing in psychiatric nursing, and international applicants with a Registered Nurse License issued by the Ministry of Health and Welfare (MOHW), Taiwan.


Appointment Start Date: February 1, 2026

Qualifications and Expertise:


Applicants must hold a Ph.D. degree in Nursing or a related field such as Life Sciences, Epidemiology, Public Health, or Smart Health, obtained from a domestic or international institution.

Expertise in areas including Psychiatric Nursing, Fundamental Nursing, Medical-Surgical Nursing, Community Nursing, Nursing Administration, and Nursing Research is required.

Must have at least one year of clinical experience in a teaching hospital and be capable of teaching courses such as Long-Term Care, Smart Nursing, and Nursing Practicum.

Candidates should demonstrate strong commitment to teaching and service, possess team spirit, and be willing to undertake administrative responsibilities within the department.

Preference will be given to candidates with teaching experience, Ministry of Education teaching certification, English teaching ability, and interdisciplinary research potential.


Required Documents:

Interested applicants should submit the following documents via email to sndept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw by October 31, 2025.

Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis. Outstanding candidates may be interviewed early, and recruitment will close once the position is filled.
