- **文章簡介**: We are pleased to invite you to the upcoming International Joint Webinar on Aging and Gerontechnology, co-hosted by Yuan Ze University (YZU) and Atma Jaya Catholic University of Indonesia (AJCUI).
This webinar will delve into the latest innovations in gerontechnology and examine how these advancements can enhance the quality of life for elderly populations worldwide. We will feature presentations from distinguished international experts, including Prof. Yeh-Liang Hsu, Director of the Gerontechnology Research Center at YZU, Prof. Chor-Kheng Lin from the Art and Design Department at YZU, and dr. Kevin Kristian from the Public Health and Nutrition Department at AJCUI. Together, they will provide valuable insights into the challenges and solutions for promoting healthy aging in today’s technological era.
