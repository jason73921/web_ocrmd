- **文章簡介**: In the pursuit of academic excellence, the College of Management at Yuan Ze University is currently accepting applications for positions as assistant/associate/full professors in the areas listed as below. 

Major/Area: Financial Institutions, Investment, Corporate Finance, ESG, Quantitative Investing or other financial fields

We are inviting applications for positions that involve contributing to research and conducting teaching activities in the field, spanning undergraduate, postgraduate (including MBA, MS, and Ph.D.), and executive levels. The college is actively advancing its internationalization and diversification of faculty and staff. We extend a particularly warm welcome to international applicants.

Job Qualifications: Candidate with a domestically or internationally recognized Ph.D. in a management-related field, accredited by the Ministry of Education, and possessing English teaching capabilities. Strong publication records or research potential are desirable.

Closing date: 7 April  2025

Application Documents:
 Candidates should submit the following materials:


Curriculum Vitae and Personal Statement (including academic and professional background, expertise, and research interests).

Copy of the Highest Degree Certificate (For those holding foreign degrees, please submit a verified graduation certificate from the overseas representative office. Graduating students should include a statement from the academic advisor, indicating the expected graduation date).

Copy of the Transcript for graduate study (fresh Ph.D. only).

Copy of Teaching Certificate (if available, otherwise not required).

A publication list and a summary of the research and significant achievements.

The most representative publications within the last five years and references to academic publications.

Two letters of recommendation.

Any other supporting materials.


Submission Details:
 Applicants should prepare the above documents and submit both hard and electronic copies (consolidated into one PDF file, or provide a download link) by 7  April , 2025.
