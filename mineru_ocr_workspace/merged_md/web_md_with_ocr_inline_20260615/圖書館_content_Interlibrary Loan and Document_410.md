- **文章簡介**: An interlibrary loan is a service whereby library patrons of one library are able to borrow or receive photocopies of library materials such as journals/periodicals, books, theses/dissertations, patents and so on from its Consortium Library.





Services



Nationwide Document Delivery Servic 

(NDDS)



Natural Science Library Service Project: Chemistry Division- Interlibrary Loan Service






Membership



NDDS is an online system which provides interlibrary loan and photocopy services to all participating libraries in Taiwan.



This service is subsidized by the Department of Natural Sciences and Sustainable Development Library Service Project – chemistry domain, conducted by the Ministry of Science and Technology.
