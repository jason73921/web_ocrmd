Title: Welcome, Graduate Students: Building Academic Publishing Literacy




Description:
 As academic communication continues to evolve, understanding the landscape of academic publishing has become an essential skill for researchers. This video introduces three common types of academic publishing and highlights key points to be aware of: Predatory Journals, Preprints, and Mega Journals. Researchers are encouraged to stay informed about publishing trends and emerging issues.
