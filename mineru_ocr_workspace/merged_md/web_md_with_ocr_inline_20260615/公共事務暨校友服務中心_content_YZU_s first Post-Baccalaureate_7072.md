- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University"s first Post-Baccalaureate Program on Net Zero Emissions and Sustainable Development will open for enrollment from October 16 to November 16. This program is for enrolling 50 students, with one year of study and a second year of internship, assisting with obtaining relevant ISO certifications such as ISO 14064-1, ISO 14064-2, ISO 14067. Upon graduation, the students will earn a degree from Yuan Ze University"s College of Engineering.

As the global climate change problem becomes more serious, the issue of carbon emissions in industrial areas has aroused widespread concern. YZU has taken the initiative to propose a comprehensive plan for the sustainable development of industrial fields, using the Jhongli Industrial Park as an example. The project combines academic expertise to help solve the challenges of carbon emissions by industry.
