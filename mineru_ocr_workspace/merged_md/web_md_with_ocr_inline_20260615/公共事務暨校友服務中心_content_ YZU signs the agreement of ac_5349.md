- **文章簡介**: As the local epidemic slows down, Yuan Ze University expect to accelerate the international cooperation. A few days ago, YZU held a ceremony for signing agreement of academic cooperation with Mahidol University in Thailand to promote bilateral academic exchange.

Prof. Chih-Yang Lin, the dean of Global Affairs Office, said that the purpose of signing the agreement is to establish an exchange mechanism for both-side students, teachers and faculty. Under the framework of the agreement, teachers and students are encouraged to conduct online or offline exchanges and cooperation during the winter, summer or academic period. He more expects that the two universities can maximize their academic output, outside the framework, through abundant teaching resources, reserving energy for research, R&D and innovation, and creating brilliant achievements.
