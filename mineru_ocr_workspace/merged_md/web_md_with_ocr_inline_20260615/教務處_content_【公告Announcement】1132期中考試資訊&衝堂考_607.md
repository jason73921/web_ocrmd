考試資訊 (含考試日期、時間及教室)

考試週之前，授課老師仍可能會調整考試安排，請務必密切留意系所公告，並隨時查看PORTAL→考試課表。

授課教師亦可自個人PORTAL查詢授課科目之排考資料：PORTAL→教學檔案→考試課表。

考試科目如有衝堂者(含系內或跨系之衝堂)，請務必於受理時間內前往登記，以免影響應考權益。






衝堂考登記

受理登記日期：3/18(二)～3/20(四)(逾期恕不受理)

受理登記時間：09:00~16:30

登記地點：

跨系衝堂登記：一館二樓R1210教務處課務組

學系內衝堂登記：各系辦公室







注意事項：

衝堂登記前，請先查明課號、班別、課名，以利申請作業。

跨系衝堂考試登記前，請先確認衝堂科目是否有非筆試或照常上課的考試 (例如上機考/聽力測驗/口試/繪圖/口頭報告)。





 

教務處課務組

 


2025 Spring Semester Midterm Exam Announcement & Exam Conflict Request


Exam Information (including the exam date, time and classroom)

Before the exam week, instructors may change the date/time of the exam, please pay attention to the announcement by courses offering departments, and check your exam timetable at the portal at any time (PORTAL→ Exam Timetable).

 Instructors can check the exam schedule information of teaching subjects from Portal. 


If students have two or more exams scheduled at the same period, please be sure to request a rescheduled exam within the following time. Please refer to the following information:






Exam Conflict Request

Period:  3/18 (TUE) ~ 3/20(THU) (OVERDUE WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED)

Office Hours: 09:00~16:30

Where to Request:

Interdepartmental Exams Conflicts: at the Curriculum Section, Office of Academic Affairs (Room 1210, Building 1)

Exams Conflicts within Own Department: please contact your own department office to submit an exam conflict adjustment request.







Requirements: Course Code, Class, and Course Name.


Please confirm first whether it is a non-written test (such as computer-based test/listening test/oral test/painting test/oral report) or class as usual.
