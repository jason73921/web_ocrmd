Subject: International Students who will live in the dormitory continuously in 2017 Fall semester need to pay NT$2,000 deposit. 

Description:

 

1. NT$2,000 deposit need to be paid during May 2 to May 5 from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm in Office of General Affairs. Your room will be canceled if you don’t pay the deposit.

 

2. The deposit won’t be refunded and will be deducted from your 2017 Spring semester dormitory fee.

 

If you have any problems, please contact Ms. Su of Student Housing Service Section.
