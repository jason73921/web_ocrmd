- **文章簡介**: 一、本校為鼓勵學生參加校外學術競賽，藉以發揮自我潛能，提昇學生宏觀特質。補助參加競賽活動相關之報名費、交通費、材料費及膳食費，以茲鼓勵。

Our school encourages students to participate in off-campus academic competitions to unleash their potential and enhance their overall qualities. We provide subsidies for registration fees, transportation expenses, materials costs, and meal expenses related to participating in competition events to encourage this.

二、申請資格/日期：凡本校在校學生，即日起至112年11月24日（星期五）止。

Eligibility/Date of Application: All current students of our school, from now until November 24, 112th year (Friday).

三、申請程序：申請人繳交申請表、校外競賽報名表、競賽辦法、競賽作品影本或作品成果照片及其他佐證資料，經系所初審及學院複審後送達生輔組。 

Application Process: Applicants are required to submit the application form, external competition registration form, competition rules, photocopies of competition works or photographs of the outcomes, and other supporting documents. After the initial review by the department and a secondary review by the college, the documents should be submitted to the life guidance section.

四、其他相關補助申請表及作業要點，請點閱此連結。

For other related grant application forms and operation details, please click on this link.
