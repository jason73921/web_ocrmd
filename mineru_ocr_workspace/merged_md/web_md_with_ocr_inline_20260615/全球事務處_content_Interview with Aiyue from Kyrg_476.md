- **文章簡介**: This week, we had the pleasure of interviewing Aiyue, a student from Kyrgyzstan currently studying EBBA at Yuan Ze University. We asked her about her experience at the International Cultural Show, which was held on Wednesday, May 22.
- **文章全文**: Aiyue’s Experience:
Aiyue shared that she really enjoyed the International Cultural Show and found it fascinating. She mentioned, “There were a lot of songs and dances, and I think it’s a good idea to hold such an event because it helps us learn about the cultures of different countries.”

Memorable Performances:
Among the various performances from Pakistan, Vietnam, Eswatini, Japan, Thailand, and Indonesia, Aiyue found the Thai performance to be the most memorable. She expressed her admiration for the vibrant and energetic display of Thai culture.

Future Participation:
Aiyue is enthusiastic about participating in similar events in the future. She mentioned that she would be willing to join with her Kyrgyz friends to perform, adding to the rich tapestry of cultural exchanges at YZU.
