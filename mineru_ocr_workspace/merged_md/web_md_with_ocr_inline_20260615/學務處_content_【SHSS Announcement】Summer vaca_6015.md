# 網站文章資料_【SHSS Announcement】Summer vacation accommodation application for international students _6015

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 6015
- **文章標題**: 【SHSS Announcement】Summer vacation accommodation application for international students 
- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: Students who did not successfully apply for housing for the 114 academic year or do not plan to continue their stay must complete the check-out process within the designated period. If you require accommodation between June 23 and September 5, please follow the application procedures outlined below.
未成功申請114學年床位或不續住的同學，請務必於規定時間內完成退宿手續。若需於6月23日至9月5日期間繼續住宿者，請依下列流程申請。


Check out Time for this semester本學期退宿時間:




Undergraduate students大學部學生 - Before JUNE 22nd2025 17:00

Master & Ph.D. students碩士及博士生 - Before JULY 31st2025 17:00




Students who want to stay in the dorm during summer vacationMUST apply for summer vacation accommodation in accordance with the regulations.
(Master‘s and Ph.D. students are not required to apply or pay additional fees.)
若需於暑假期間留宿，必須依規定提出申請。(碩博士生寒暑假無需申請及額外繳費)

Application eligibility: International Students 
申請資格：境外生

Application period申請期間: 2025 April 28th～2025 May 29th

Staying period住宿期間: 2025 June 23rd～September 5th　
(Application for non-continuous periods will not be accepted 不接受申請非連續期間住宿)

Application process申請流程: Portal → Apply for stay during vacation → Print out application form → Signed by GAO/dept./teacher/instructor → Send to Student Housing Service Section for approval → Pay the fee at Office of General Affairs (Building 1) → Deliver the stamped form to Student Housing Service Section

Accommodation fee: $120 NTD per day 
住宿費用：每日新台幣120元整

Any students who want to stay during this period must complete the procedure above, otherwise accommodation, internet and access card will not be provided.
凡欲於此期間住宿之學生，必須完成以上所有手續，否則將無法提供住宿、網路及門禁卡服務。

If you have any questions, please contact the Student Housing Service Section (Male 1 Dorm, 1st Floor, Section C)
如有任何疑問，請洽詢宿服組（男一舍C區1樓）
