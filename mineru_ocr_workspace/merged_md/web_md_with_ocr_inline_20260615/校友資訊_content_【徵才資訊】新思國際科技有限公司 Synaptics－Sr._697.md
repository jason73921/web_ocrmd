【公司介紹】

Synaptics (Nasdaq: SYNA) is changing the way humans engage with connected devices and data, engineering exceptional experiences throughout the home, at work, in the car and on the go. Synaptics is the partner of choice for the world’s most innovative intelligent system providers who are integrating multiple experiential technologies into platforms that make our digital lives more productive, insightful, secure and enjoyable. These customers are combining Synaptics’ differentiated technologies in touch, display and biometrics with a new generation of advanced connectivity and AI-enhanced video, vision, audio, speech and security processing.

 


 

【職缺名稱】Sr. Manufacturing Engineer

 

【關於該職缺】

Millions of people experience Synaptics every day. Our technology impacts how people see, hear, touch, and engage with a wide range of IoT applications -- at home, at work, in the car or on the go.

We solve complex challenges alongside the most influential companies in the industry, using the most advanced algorithms in areas such as machine learning, biometrics and video processing, combined with world class software and silicon development.

Responsibilities

As a senior manufacturing engineer, you will be responsible for driving towards world class factory operations with multiple factories. This role will cover sustaining operations, new product introductions and qualifications of materials, processes and new factories. You will drive for manufacturing excellence with the use of DOEs, identifying and/or developing new processes and working closely with both our suppliers and our design engineering teams.

Activities Include


Leads Synaptics’ efforts to develop product manufacturing plan to effectively manufacture PC module products from NPI through mass production phases

Ensures manufacturing is building product in conformance to current engineering documentation

Reviews the design of components and parts for design-for-manufacturability and design-for-test

Working with EMS suppliers - Monitors costs, manpower efficiency, machine capability, space utilization, etc. and recommend as appropriate revisions in manufacturing methods

Leads continuous improvement efforts in the areas of Quality, Cost, Delivery, Safety, and ProductivityIdentify, process map, analyze, and execute impact projects using LEAN or Six Sigma tools that result in significant process improvements.Leads the integration of new designs into manufacturing

Participates and contributes productively as part of Synaptics’ design, manufacturing, and customer service/support efforts

Directs cross team activities to address manufacturing challenges

Supports Business Unit, Operations, Sales, and QATechnical lead inside the outsourced manufacturing environment. Trains and works with CMs, customer & supplier to resolve quality, root cause analysis, short term and long term solutions

Drives for ongoing quality and yield improvement program at the contract manufacturing sites


Qualifications


Bachelor’s or master’s degree in industrial/manufacturing engineering

8+ years’ experience in PCB SMT manufacturing, advanced automation assembly, and test

Strong cross functional leadership able to influence across organizations

Experience with quality standards, FMEA, Lean, Six Sigma processes

Ability to multi-task effectively, self-motivated, positive attitude and a cooperative, team-based approach to solving problems

Travel to supplier sites for new product introduction support and critical manufacturing situations

Experience and knowledge in capacitive touch, fingerprint, glass and metal frames is an advantage

Fluent in Mandarin and English
