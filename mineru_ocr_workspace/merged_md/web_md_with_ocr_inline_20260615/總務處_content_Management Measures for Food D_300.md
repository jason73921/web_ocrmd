Delivery Hours:


Weekdays: 9:30 AM - 2:30 PM, 5:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Weekends/Holidays: 9:30 AM - 9:30 PM


Delivery/Pick-up Zone: Entrances/exits of each building on the side of the campus loop road. Delivery personnel are not allowed to enter the buildings.

Prohibited Areas for Delivery Vehicles: The road between Buildings 1 and 3, the roundabout in front of Buildings 5 and 6, and the red brick road between Buildings 2 and 7.
