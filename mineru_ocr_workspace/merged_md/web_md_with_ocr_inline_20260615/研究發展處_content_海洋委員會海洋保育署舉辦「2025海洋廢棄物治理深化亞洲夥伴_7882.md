# 2025 年海洋廢棄物治理深化亞洲夥伴孕育工作坊

# 2025 Asian Partnership Workshop on Advancing Marine Debris Management

海洋委員會海洋保育署將於 2025 年 9 月 29 日至 30 日舉辦亞洲夥伴合作工作坊，邀請亞洲國家專家學者、YIC青年創新挑戰得主及我國產官學研代表，共同探討海洋廢棄物治理之創新解方，並建構區域合作網絡。透過經驗交流與人力資源整合，推動亞洲海洋環境治理能量，攜手維護全球藍色海洋。

The Ocean Conservation Administration will host the 2025 Asian Partnership Workshop on Advancing Marine Debris Management from September 29 to 30, 2025, bringing together experts and scholars from Asian countries, YIC Youth Innovation Challenge awardees, and Taiwan’s industry, government, academic, and research representatives. The workshop will explore innovative solutions for marine debris management and foster a regional cooperation network. Through experience sharing and human resource integration, the event aims to strengthen Asia’s marine environmental governance and work collectively to protect our global blue ocean.
