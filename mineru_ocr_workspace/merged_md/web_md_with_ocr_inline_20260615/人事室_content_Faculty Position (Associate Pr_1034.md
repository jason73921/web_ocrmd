Position Overview

The Doctoral Program in Cultural Industries and Cultural Policies at Yuan Ze University (YZU) invites applications for one full-time faculty position at the rank of Associate Professor or Full Professor. We seek an outstanding scholar with a strong commitment to teaching, research, and academic service in the interdisciplinary fields of cultural industries and cultural policies.

The appointment is expected to commence on August 1, 2026.

Qualifications


Hold a Ph.D. degree in cultural industries, cultural policies, or a closely related field, recognized by the Ministry of Education (Taiwan).

Demonstrate a strong record of scholarly publication, preferably in international peer-reviewed journals.

Exhibit excellence in teaching and a commitment to student mentorship.

Show clear potential for interdisciplinary research collaboration.

Possess strong communication skills and a willingness to contribute to program development and academic service.


Preferred Areas of Expertise


Cultural industry management and marketing

Cultural heritage and preservation policy

Digital technology and cultural industries

Other areas relevant to cultural policies and cultural industries


Ability to teach in English is highly desirable.

Responsibilities


Teach graduate-level courses in the doctoral program (in Chinese and/or English).

Supervise graduate students and contribute to curriculum development.

Maintain an active and productive research agenda.

Participate in institutional service and interdisciplinary collaboration.


Compensation and Benefits
Salary and benefits are provided in accordance with YZU’s regulations for full-time faculty members, and are competitive within Taiwan’s higher education system.

Application Materials


Completed YZU Faculty Qualification Review CV Form  https://reurl.cc/5gY5A7 (另開新視窗) 

Curriculum Vitae (academic background, research projects, teaching experience).

Autobiographical statement.

Copy of Ph.D. diploma (foreign degrees must be authenticated).

Copy of Ph.D. transcript (foreign degrees must be authenticated).

Copy of teaching certificate.

Academic publications (representative works from the past five years, additional publications, full list).

Teaching statement and course proposals (Program curriculum reference:  https://www.hs.yzu.edu.tw/Uploads/files/20250508100038.pdf (另開新視窗) )

Statement of research agenda (future research directions).

Two letters of recommendation.
