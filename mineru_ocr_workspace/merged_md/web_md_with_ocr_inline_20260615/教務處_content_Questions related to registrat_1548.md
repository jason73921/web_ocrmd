Course selection issues

 Where can we check schoolwide courses?

Refer to the link: https://isdna1.yzu.edu.tw/Cnstdsel/default.aspx

 Unable to enter the course selection system?

1. The system does not open during non-course selection periods.

2. The password for the first time using the course selection system is your ID card number. In order to maintain the security of course selection, students must change their "password" before entering the course selection system.

3. If you have any questions about the account and password of the course selection system, please directly contact the information service counter (extension 2321).

 Drop the courses designated by the department and choose the same required courses in other classes.

1. The purpose of designating compulsory courses into classes is to protect students’ right to take compulsory courses.

2. Please also note that if you drop the designated course on your own and are unable to reselect the course, the Academic Affairs Office will not accept the application for re-selection.

3. If you need to change to a new class, it is recommended to ask the new class's teacher and the secretary of the course department, and then submit an application for transfer within the specified period. Since Chinese course is a designated course, teachers may have different teaching content for students in different departments.

 Is it possible to select courses from other departments?

1. Course exclusion restrictions are set by the course department. Please contact the course department first. If the course department applies to cancel the restriction, the Academic Affairs Office will accept the change of course selection restrictions.

 The selected course disappeared during the next stage of course selection.

1. If there is a similar situation where courses disappear, please check the course change announcement at each stage on the Yuan Ze homepage. For courses with time changes (including compulsory courses), the system will delete the old time-period courses and actively add new time-period courses. If the new period courses are added and cause course conflicts, the academic affairs will send a notification letter and students must choose courses and delete a redundant course.

2. Since the change in period is requested by each course department, if you have any questions about the content or reasons for the changed courses, please directly ask each course department.

 Unable to select minor program courses via online course selection?

If students have special reasons (generally due to course selection qualification issues, such as associate students being excluded, etc.) and cannot directly select courses through the online selection system even though the course does not reach the upper limit of enrollment, they can fill out the " YZU Course Sign-up/Drop Form" and get teacher’s signature. After the course department receives the opinions, the application will be reviewed and processed. However, if the course has reached the upper limit of enrollment, it cannot be processed.

 How to deal with course selection issues?

1. However, any change of class during the internship class must be signed and approved by the teacher in charge, and signed by the person in charge of the course and the supervisor. Unless you need to fill in the ''YZU Course Sign-up/Drop Form'' for special reasons, the acceptance conditions must be met and the number of enrollees for the course in the course selection system does not reach the upper limit, the course department will make additional selections.

2. Only the following five reasons can be used to fill in the ''YZU Course Sign-up/Drop Form'' to assist in the selection.

Transferred students or late enrollments this semester

Schedule conflict due to course rescheduling during the third stage

Course cancellation

Disqualification due to departmental rule

Illnesses who can't add courses by themselves during the course selection period need to provide a medical certificate.

 Can’t drop the courses via online course selection on the third phase?

If the number of students is less than or equal to the "minimum number of people who can drop from the computer", you will not be able to drop on your own and must obtain the consent of the course department.

 How do students who defer their studies choose courses and apply for a refund? answer:

1. The course selection period and method for deferred students are similar to those for ordinary students. Using the second-stage course selection credits of suspended students as the basis for student payment in the next semester. Students who take a leave of absence must pay full tuition and fees for elective courses ranging from 10 credits (inclusive) to 9 credits. In order to pay for credit, students need to carefully evaluate their secondary course options. If the credits selected in the second phase exceed 10 credits, the amount on the registration form must be paid to complete the registration. If they do not want to select as many credits in Phase 3, then adjust the course and cancel the course before the Phase 3 course selection deadline. If you select multiple credits, please pay in full upon notification by the Financial Management Team.

2. The school will uniformly refund the difference to the student's bank account based on the credits selected by the student. Please refer to the "Course Selection Instructions" for the credit refund time.

 How to apply for inter-school courses?

1. All universities and independent colleges can choose inter-school courses (you must ask the department whether the course is recognized).

2. Ask other schools about inter-school course selection and payment deadlines.

3. Log in to Yuan Ze Home Page/Personal Portal/Learning Files/Inter-School Course Selection to apply for inter-school course selection.

4. After the review by the course department or department head is completed, the system will automatically send a letter to the student's mailbox, asking students to print the approved written application form. The curriculum section has electronically agreed that there is no need to go to the school to get a seal. The student can take the form and submit it to other schools. Go through the course selection procedures, and after completing the external school procedures, return the form to the curriculum section to confirm the additional selection. if your application does not meet the regulations, such as class cancellation, etc., the order will be disapproved. It must comply with the regulations of the course department, and the seal of the curriculum section will take effect.

5. After going to other schools to complete the payment registration and other procedures, bring the second copy of the Academic Affairs Office of the school back to the school for reference (the second copy can be sent back to the curriculum section by mail within the time limit.)

 How to confirm personal course selection information?

answer:

1. When selecting courses, students should check the course selection list online at any time, and confirm the online course selection list through "Personal Portal/Teaching Affairs/Course Selection System/Course Selection List/Click the "Course Selection Confirmation Button". If it is overdue, the course selection information stored in "Personal Portal/Teaching Affairs/Course Selection System/Course Selection List" shall prevail.

2. Inter-school course selection courses taken at other schools will be included in the school's course selection system. Students are required to properly keep the "Student Copy of Inter-school Course Selection Slips" for at least one year to provide a basis for future verification.

 What are the qualifications for Course Overload and how to apply for it?

Students with an average score of 80 or more during the semester or taking interdisciplinary course programs, a minor, or a double major may take additional courses for not more than 6 credits in the following semester with the approval of the department/college/program chairperson. The course selection system will automatically increase the maximum credit limit of qualified students by 6 credits, 31 credits in total without the need to apply.

How to apply for Course Reduction? Could I apply reduction for course withdrawal during the semester?

1. Students shall apply online during the application period and be verified by the office of the department. Starting from the 2024 Fall Semester, there is no need to submit the Parental Consent Form for course reduction.

Each department can modify its course reduction regulations to meet the needs of the department, and can also inform student's parents of the credits reduced in the current semester.

2. After the course reduction deadline, students shall not apply reduction for course withdrawal. (Except for the serious issues)
