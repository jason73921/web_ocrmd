- **文章簡介**: 1、簡易廚房開放時間為每日07:00-20:30，其餘時間禁止使用。

2、簡易廚房只能使用學校提供爐具，禁止使用個人爐具，若因使用個人爐具而造成災害者，應自行擔負相關刑事責任與民事賠償。

3、使用時請開啟抽油煙機，避免油煙漫延影響環境及健康。

4、廚房空間有限，私人物品不得占用，如有則送交宿服組，兩週內未領回，視同廢棄物處理，不得異議。

5、使用後請自行清潔，保持廚房環境衛生。如未確實清潔，廚房將關閉二至三日進行整理。

 

1.The kitchen is open from 07:00 AM to 08:30 PM. Usage is prohibited at all other times.

2.Students can only use school-provided stoves; personal stoves are strictly prohibited. Any accidents caused by the use of personal stoves are liable for both criminal liability and civil compensation.

3.To prevent the spreading of cooking fumes, please make sure that you turn on the range hood when cooking.

4.As kitchen space is limited, personal items should not occupy it. Items left in the kitchen will sent to the Student Housing Service Section. Items not retrieved within two weeks will considered abandoned and disposed of without objection.

5.Please clean up the kitchen area after use to maintain cleanliness and hygiene. If students fail to clean the kitchen properly, it will be temporarily closed for two to three days for maintenance.
