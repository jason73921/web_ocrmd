- **文章簡介**: Announcement：114年1月起基本工資、保費等相關規定/ Regulations on Minimum Wage, Insurance Premiums, and Other Related Matters Starting from January 2025


依勞動部公告，自114年1月1日起，每月基本工資由27,470元調整至28,590元。每小時基本工資由183元調整至190元。

自114年1月1日起，公保、勞保、健保之保險費率及收繳措施如下:
勞保：勞保費率則從11%調升至11.5% (勞工保險級距分級調整成12級，第一級也提高至28,590元)，就業保險費率1%，職業災害保險費率為0.11%。

公保：保險費率由10.16%調整為8.89%(此費率僅適用112.06.30以前具公保年資者)；未具112.6.30以前公保年資者適用費率為10.32%。

健保：投保金額最高一級由219,500元調整為313,000元，增加10個等級。




　　　　A.一般保險費：維持費率5.17％。

　　　　B.補充保險費：維持費率2.11％。


依財政部公告114年薪資所得扣繳稅額表起扣標準不變(一樣為$88,501元)。


Announcement: Regulations on Minimum Wage, Insurance Premiums, and Other Related Provisions Starting from January 2025


Minimum Wage Adjustment: According to the Ministry of Labor"s announcement, starting from January 1, 2025, the monthly minimum wage will be adjusted from NT$27,470 to NT$28,590. The hourly minimum wage will be adjusted from NT$183 to NT$190.

Insurance Premium Rates and Collection Measures: Starting from January 1, 2025, the premium rates and collection measures for labor insurance, public insurance, and health insurance are as follows:
Labor Insurance: The labor insurance rate will increase from 11% to 11.5% (labor insurance is now adjusted into 12 categories, with the first category also increased to NT$28,590). The employment insurance rate will be 1%, and the occupational hazard insurance rate will be 0.11%.

Public Insurance: The insurance rate will be adjusted from 10.16% to 8.89% (this rate applies only to those with public insurance service before June 30, 2023). Those without public insurance service before June 30, 2023, will be subject to a rate of 10.32%.

Health Insurance: The maximum insurance amount for the highest category will be adjusted from NT$219,500 to NT$313,000, with an increase of 10 additional categories. 
A. General Insurance Premium: The rate will remain at 5.17%. 
B. Supplementary Insurance Premium: The rate will remain at 2.11%.



Income Tax Withholding for Salary: According to the Ministry of Finance"s announcement, the tax deduction threshold for salary income will remain unchanged at NT$88,501.
