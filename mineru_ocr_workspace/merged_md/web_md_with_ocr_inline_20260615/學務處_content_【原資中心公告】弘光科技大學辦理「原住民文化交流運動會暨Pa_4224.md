弘光科技大學原資中心辦理原住民文化交流運動會暨 Patiyaman 部落市集

Subject: "HGM University Resource Center Presents the Indigenous Culture Exchange Sports Event and Patiyaman Tribal Market" - Join Us!

Description:



In order to promote nationwide sports and strengthen the bonds among our indigenous communities, enhance mutual support, and nurture cultural heritage and self-identity, we are excited to announce our upcoming event. Besides the united sports competition, we have also organized a multicultural tribal market that offers a diverse array of cultural experiences.




Event Date: October 22, 112 (Sunday) from 09:00 to 20:00.




Event Location: Outdoor Sports Field/Red Soil Plaza, HGM University, Shalu District, Taichung City.




HGM University"s Resource Center is thrilled to host the Indigenous Culture Exchange Sports Event and Patiyaman Tribal Market. We welcome everyone to participate enthusiastically!
