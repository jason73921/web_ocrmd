EP32—“ A Prerequisite for Every Faculty Member — Academic Ethics in Teacher Promotion ”

[Highlights of this episode]


Criteria and Review Process: Understanding the requirements and procedures for university faculty promotion.

The "Faculty Qualification Review Curriculum Vitae": What information is included, and what are the critical points to watch out for?

Handling Alleged Misconduct: How do universities or the Ministry of Education manage cases of suspected academic ethics violations during the promotion process?

Common Ethical Pitfalls: Frequent academic integrity issues encountered during faculty promotion.

Opportunities for Correction: Is there a chance for faculty members to amend content or replace publications if a suspected violation arises?

Consequences of Violations: What are the potential impacts on a teacher"s career if academic misconduct is confirmed?

Recommendations for Candidates: Essential advice for faculty members preparing for promotion.

The AI Factor: How the emergence of Generative AI is influencing the faculty promotion landscape.
