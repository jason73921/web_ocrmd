Further information regarding the subsequent dormitory waiting list process is provided as follows:

The detailed procedures for the second waiting list are expected to be announced in early July.
The subsequent waiting list process will be arranged based on the progress of new student housing applications and is tentatively scheduled for late July (after the completion of new student applications).
Students who have not yet been admitted are advised to stay attentive to future announcements.
