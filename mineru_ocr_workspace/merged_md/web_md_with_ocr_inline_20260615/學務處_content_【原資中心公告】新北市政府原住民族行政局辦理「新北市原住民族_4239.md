Subject: New Taipei City Government Indigenous Peoples Affairs Bureau to Hold "New Taipei City Indigenous Economic Development Seminar and Industry Excellence Awards Ceremony" on October 22, 112 (Saturday).

Description:



The seminar will feature special guests including Ms. Yagu Yurow, CEO of "Awaken Cultural Community Co., Ltd." from Yilan County, Mr. Lin Ting-wei, the head of "Linshan Farm Selection Co., Ltd." from Taichung City, and Ms. Chu Chien-yu, the head of "Mountain Never Withers" from New Taipei City. They will share their entrepreneurial journeys and the experiences they"ve gained in brand management.




Participants in the entire event will not only gain valuable insights from these successful entrepreneurs but will also have the chance to win one iPad (9th generation, 256GB) through a lucky draw. We welcome you to enthusiastically register and participate.
