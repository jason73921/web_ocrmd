- **文章簡介**: Position: Assistant Professor (or higher) for Full-time or Project-based positions.

Number of Openings: Two(2)

Required Qualifications: Applicants must hold a Ph.D. degree in a field related to Chinese Literature, recognized by the Ministry of Education.

Specialization Areas:


Classical Literature: Specializing in Fu poetry, with capabilities in digital applications and English instruction.

Modern Literature: Candidates with interdisciplinary teaching experience and English teaching ability are preferred.


Appointment Date: August 1, 2025

Required Documents:

YZUCL Faculty Qualification Form (Download Here) and mail it to juchun@saturn.yzu.edu.tw ,The e-mail subject line should include "[Application for 114 Full-time/Project-based Faculty] [Name]".

Application Form for New Faculty (Download Here).

Resume and biography, including educational background, area of expertise, research direction and teaching experience.

A scanned copy of the original D. diploma. (For foreign degrees, include a graduation certificate authenticated by the appropriate Taiwan"s Embassies and Missions.)

A scanned copy of the original D. transcript. (For foreign degrees, include a transcript authenticated by the appropriate Taiwan"s Embassies and Missions.)

A scanned copy of the original Academic Teaching Rank Accreditation Certificate issued by Taiwan"s Ministry of Education (if available).

Catalog of academic publications (indicating publication details) and research accomplishments.

Full-text electronic versions of representative works and conference papers published within the last five years.

Two recommendation letters, which can be sent directly by referees to juchun@saturn.yzu.edu.tw.

Teaching plans for courses (please see the website for undergraduate courses and master’s courses in this department)

Any additional supporting materials, such as awards or patents relevant to your field.





7. Additional Information::


Applications must be sent by February 7, 2025, to:
 Department of Chinese Linguistics and Literature, Yuan Ze University
 No. 135, Yuan-Tung Road, Zhongli District, Taoyuan City, 32003, Taiwan.

Please mark the envelope with "[Application for 114 Full-time/Project-based Faculty] [Your Name]". Submissions via fax or email are not accepted.

Shortlisted candidates will be notified for interviews. Travel expenses are the applicant"s responsibility. Unsuccessful applicants will not be notified.
