- Date: January 17, 2024 (Wednesday), from 11:00 to 14:00
- Location: Global Lounge, 1F, Building 7

Event Description:
1. Prosperity Bag Collection and Chinese Calligraphy Workshop
- Reservations must be made via the →portal ← system. If you have successfully reserved your prosperity bag, please come to Building 7’s Global Lounge between 11:00 and 12:30 on the event day to collect your prosperity bag and join the Chinese Calligraphy Workshop (duration around 5-10 min).

- Limited slots available. Please ensure early registration through the portal system. Only 180 slots are opened for the prosperity bag, so register early to secure a prosperity bag (reserved for you until 12:30).

- For those international students and faculty who haven’t registered but wish to participate, there will be 20 slots reserved at the event for on-site registration to collect the prosperity bag and participate in the Chinese Calligraphy Workshop.

2. Important Note
For those who have reserved a prosperity bag but fail to collect it by 12:30, it will be considered forfeited, and these bags will be made available to on-site participants without prior registration until they are all claimed.

We look forward to celebrating this special Lunar New Year with you. Thank you for your participation!
