If you have successfully installed the wireless adapter with driver in your computer, then follow the instructions below for configuring your computer to access campus-wide wireless network:


Click on the Start menu and navigate to "Control Panel".

    Double click on the "Network Connections" icon.

    Right-click on the "Wireless Network Connection" icon then left-click on "Properties".

    Double click on the "Internet Protocol (TCP/IP)".

    Select "Obtain IP address automatically" and "Obtain DNS server address automatically".

    Click OK to close all window.

    Right-click the network connection icon in the notification area, and then click View Available Wireless Networks.

    Choose SSID named "YZUWLAN" and click "Connect".

    Open a web page and enter your ID and password.
