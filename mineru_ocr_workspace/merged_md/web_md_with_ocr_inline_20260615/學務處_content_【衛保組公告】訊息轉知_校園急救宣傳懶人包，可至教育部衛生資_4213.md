- **文章簡介**: 為提升大專校院教職員工生急救知能，教育部業編製完成「校園急救宣傳懶人包」及相關校園急救訓練影片案，轉知教職員工生，並鼓勵至教育部學校衛生資訊網下載運用

教育部為協助各大專校院提升緊急傷病處理及急救知能，特委請臺北醫學大學編製旨揭衛教素材，說明如下：
(一)校園急救訓練影片：包含「急救前注意事項與基本生命徵象評估」（5分鐘）、「認識基本生命急救術」（15分鐘）、「基本生病急救術與哈姆立克法」（30分鐘），計3單元影片。
(二)校園急救宣傳懶人包：內容包含急救、CPR、AED之口訣及異物梗塞處理原則等。

相關電子檔及影片業置於教育部學校衛生資訊網（https://cpd.moe.gov.tw/）/資料下載/其他項目，鼓勵教職員工生下載運用。

 

校園急救宣傳懶人包及校園急救訓練影片


To enhance the first-aid knowledge and skills of faculty, staff, and students in tertiary institutions, the Ministry of Education has prepared a "Campus First Aid Quick Guide" and related campus first aid training videos. We would like to inform faculty, staff, and students about this initiative and encourage them to download and utilize these resources from the Ministry of Education"s School Health Information website.

To assist tertiary institutions in improving their emergency injury and illness handling and first-aid capabilities, the Ministry of Education has commissioned Taipei Medical University to create the following health education materials:



Campus First Aid Training Videos: These videos cover topics including "Precautions and Basic Vital Sign Assessment in First Aid" (5 minutes), "Understanding Basic Life Support" (15 minutes), and "Basic First Aid and the Heimlich Maneuver" (30 minutes), comprising a total of three units of videos.




Campus First Aid Quick Guide: This quick guide includes mnemonic devices for first aid, CPR, AED usage, and principles for dealing with foreign body obstructions.




Related electronic files and videos are available on the Ministry of Education"s School Health Information website (https://cpd.moe.gov.tw/) under the "Data Download" section. We encourage faculty, staff, and students to download and utilize these resources.
