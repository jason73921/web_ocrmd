The result of undergraduates submission of online selection for 2023summer courses （check here）.

1. This result provides for units who in charge of these courses for reference.

2. If the signed up student number is 15 or above, the units have the right to decide open the course or not. The units are also in charge of arranging instructors and the course schedules.

3. The formal announcement of course selection for 2021 summer courses will be posted at the end of May.

4. Credit fees will not be returned to the students who apply for drop courses after the formal announcement has been posted. Please think carefully while selecting summer courses.
