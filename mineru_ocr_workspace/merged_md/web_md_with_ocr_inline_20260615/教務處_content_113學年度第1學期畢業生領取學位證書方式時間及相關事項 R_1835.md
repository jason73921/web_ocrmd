ANNOUNCEMENT

Subject：Regarding the time and method of collecting certificates for graduates in Semester 1, 2025

Content：

1. Please be aware of:

1.1 Complete the procedure for student status termination online: Individuals must access "Graduation Clearance" in PORTAL. Please refer to the Instructions for Initiating the Graduation Clearance Process for Graduates.

1.2 Please bring your student ID card to collect. If authorizing someone else, the authorizing person"s ID card must also be checked.

1.3 Students who are pursuing a minor, double major, or education program but do not meet the required credits must submit an abandonment statement at least one month prior to receiving the certificate. Failure to do so will result in ineligibility to graduate and the withholding of the certificate.

2. How to collect and other related matters:

2.1 For postgraduate students:

◆ Postgraduate students who have passed the oral defense must upload their thesis online by Feb. 3rd 2025, failure to do so will delay graduation until the next semester. Also, individuals must submit at least 2 copies of thesis for graduation (watermark required, and the exact number of copy depends on the department), 1 original copy of the "National Central Library Authorization" and the "Yuan Ze University Authorization" (please make sure to sign in blue ink), along with the verification letter from the oral examination committee to the office of individual department, and have the department staff to log in the score of the thesis. Please visit: https://cloud.ncl.edu.tw/yzu/.

◆ Once the department staff has submitted the thesis score, postgraduate students qualified to graduation can collect the certificate at the Service Desk of the Registrar"s Office 2 days later (For example, the score was submitted on July 8th, the certificate can be collected on July 10th).

2.2 For undergraduate students:

◆Check if the graduation requirements are met: Confirm that the grades have been submitted to the Office of Academic Affairs. Do not proceed with the graduation clearance procedure if the grades have not been submitted or are temporarily saved.

◆ To be collected for all: Qualified graduates collect the certificate on Feb. 7th, 2025, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM, in Room 1209, 2nd floor, Building 1.

◆ If individuals fail to collect within within the specified time frame, please complete the " Graduation Clearance " online through the PORTAL system and bring student ID card and collect the certificate every Monday to Friday, 1:30 PM - 04:00 PM , in Room 1209, 2nd floor, Building 1.

3. Call Registration Section at 03-4638800 for more information:

Ext.2252   Service Desk

Ext.3212   College of Electrical and Communication Engineering, College of Humanities and Social Science , Graduate Institute of Medicine
