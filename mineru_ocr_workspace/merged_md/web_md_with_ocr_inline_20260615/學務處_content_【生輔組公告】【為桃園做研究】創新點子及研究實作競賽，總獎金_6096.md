“Do Research For Taoyuan”

114年度桃園市政府大專校院學生

創新點子及研究實作競賽

2025 Taoyuan City Government Innovation Ideas and Research Implementation Competition for University and College Students

 


徵件時間：114年7月1日至10月20日


Submission Period: July 1 to October 20, 2025


參賽資格：於申請時，需具備國內外大專校院（含專科學校、碩博士）在學身分。


Eligibility: Applicants must be currently enrolled students at domestic or international colleges and universities (including junior colleges, master"s, and doctoral programs) at the time of application.


本活動分為「創新點子組」及「研究實作組」

創新點子組：10組，獎勵金2~３萬元/案

研究實作組：10組，獎勵金12萬元整/案


The competition is divided into two categories: the "Innovation Ideas Track" and the "Research Implementation Track."


Innovation Ideas Track: 10 teams will be selected, each awarded a grant ranging from NT$20,000 to NT$30,000.

Research Implementation Track: 10 teams will be selected, each awarded a grant of NT$120,000.
