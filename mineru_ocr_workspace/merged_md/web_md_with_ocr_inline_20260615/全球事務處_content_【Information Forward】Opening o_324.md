The 2024 Turkish Scholarship for Bachelor"s, Master"s, Ph.D. programs, and short-term scholarships is open for online applications for students from all countries starting from January 10, 2024, until February 20, 2024. For application procedures and other details, please refer to the official website of the Turkish Scholarship: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/about/mediaandpresentation/brochure, and apply online through the aforementioned website.
