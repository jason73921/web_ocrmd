- **文章簡介**: 1. Activity description:
In order to serve Japanese students in Taiwan and provide employment-related guidance and information, the Japan Taiwan Education Center has cooperated with Poseidon Management Consulting Company. We will organize employment counseling lectures and invite professional lecturers to provide Japanese students with the latest methods and instructions for finding a job in Taiwan. We hope that interested Japanese students studying abroad enthusiastically participated.
