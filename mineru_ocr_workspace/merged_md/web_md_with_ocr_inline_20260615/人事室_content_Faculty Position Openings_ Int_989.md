- **文章簡介**: Position: Full-time faculty member (Assistant Professor or above)
Number of Positions:  1
Start Date: Expected to begin on August 1, 115
Application Deadline: January 30, 115

Qualifications: Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Electronics, or related fields from domestic or international institutions, Outstanding research performance, Strong enthusiasm for teaching and service, Team spirit and ability to work harmoniously with others. Ability to teach in English

Application Method:
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis until the position is filled. Interested candidates should prepare the following documents and provide a cloud link (with download permissions enabled) via email to the contact person, Ms. Hsu Yun-Chu (chubbyhsu@saturn.yzu.edu.tw), by January 30, 115.

Email Subject: “Application for Full-time Faculty Position in International Bachelor Program in Informatics– Applicant’s Name”
Application materials are not accepted by fax or hard copy.

Document Submission Instructions:


Items 1–9 should be combined into a single PDF file

Item 10 (Ph.D. dissertation) should be saved as a separate PDF file


Required Documents:


Faculty Qualification Review Resume Form  Download from the university’s HR website (click here)

Application Form for Full-time Faculty Download from the university’s HR website (click here)

Personal resume and autobiography (including detailed academic and professional experience above undergraduate level and areas of expertise)

Copy of highest degree diploma [For foreign degrees, please submit a diploma verified by the overseas representative office. If the degree has not yet been obtained, a certificate from the advisor indicating the expected graduation date is acceptable.]

Copy of transcript for highest degree [For foreign degrees, please submit a transcript verified by the overseas representative office.]

Copy of Ministry of Education teacher certificate (if available; not required if not applicable)

List of courses that can be taught and future research directions (Required and elective course list, click here to view)

Electronic full text and catalog of representative works and journal/conference papers published in the past five years (must indicate sources), as well as research experience and major achievements

Other documents helpful for review (such as awards or patents in professional fields)

Electronic full text of Ph.D. dissertation (not required if Ministry of Education teacher certificate has been issued)

Two letters of recommendation (recommendation letters may be sent directly by referees via email to chubbyhsu@saturn.yzu.edu.tw)


Note:
Applicants with foreign degrees must also provide the “Overview of Teacher Qualification Review for Foreign Degrees” and “Entry/Exit Records” Download from the university’s HR website (click here).
