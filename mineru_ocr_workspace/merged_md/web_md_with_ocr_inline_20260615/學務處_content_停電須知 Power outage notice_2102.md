停電時請勿驚慌，並謹記以下事項：

★避免搭乘電梯。如受困電梯內，拿起緊急電話即可接通男女櫃台求援。請勿撬砸電梯門或將身體伸出電梯外，以防電梯突然啟動造成傷害。

★拔掉電器插頭，預防電器因復電後瞬間高壓而損壞。

★停電時，熱水、電梯、冷氣、電扇、飲水機、洗衣機……等暫停使用。

★避免走動，以免發生危險。

★若有任何問題，請聯繫男女櫃台輔導員03-4638800#2881、2882、0903-032011。

 

 

Don’t panic during a power outage, and remember the following:

★Avoid taking the elevator. If you are trapped in the elevator, you can pick up the emergency phone to connect to the Male & Female counters for help. Do not pry the elevator door or extend your body out of the elevator to prevent the elevator from starting suddenly and causing injury.

★Please unplug all of your electric appliances, prevent electrical appliances from being damaged due to instantaneous high voltage after the power is restored.

★In the event of a power failure, hot water, elevators, air conditioners, electric fans, water dispensers, washing machines, etc. will be temporarily suspended.

★For your own safety , please avoid walking around

★If you have any questions, please contact the male and female counter counselor 03-4638800#2881, 2882
