## 【討論案五】

提案單位：全球事務處

提案名稱：本校與諾丁漢大學寧波分校簽定學生交流專案同意書審議案

## 提案說明：

一、寧波諾丁漢大學（The UniversityofNottingham Ningbo China）是一所位於浙江省的英國諾丁漢大學的分校之一，也是中國教育史上第一個外方大學·鑒於中國政策影響(外方大學必須與中國機構合作)，諾丁漢大學選擇浙江萬裡教育集團作為中方合作對象，其校區正式啟用於2004年並開始招收大學學生，執行校長是英國諾丁漢大學的副校長，其參與世界學術排名並享有同等之資源。寧波諾丁漢大學分為五個學院，並提供範圍廣泛的各種科學和人文科學的約45種學位，針對中國國內學生採用高考制度作為招生依據，只有高考分數超過一本線且英文成績優秀的學生能有報名入學的資格且仍需經過面試·針對國際學生的入學資格則和諾丁漢大學體系相同，相求同等之成績·所有在諾丁漢大學體系任職的教育人員皆由英國校區統一進行資格審查及招聘，海外校區的核心師資成員則直接由英國校區指派。諾丁漢大學體系作為英國與世界的一流大學，長年名列世界百大，在2013年QS世界大學排名全球前一百名中排行第72，2013年上海交通大學世界大學學術排名中全球前一百名排行第83。

二、本校於2017年與該校簽定學術交流協議書，為進一步實質合作,提出學生交流合作專案，每年交換人数以6名為原則。

# 元智大學－寧波諾丁漢大學學生交流專案同意書

甲方：元智大學

乙方：寧波諾丁漢大學

元智大學與寧波諾丁漢大學本著優勢互補、共同發展、平等互惠的原則，經雙方協商，就兩校學生交流事宜達成如下協議：

## 一、合作形式

1．雙方同意根據學生意願，每學年互派大學生、研究生（以下稱「交流學生）到對方學校學習一個學期或一學年，學習結束後返回各自學校繼續完成學業。

2．交流學生以修讀雙方均已開設的對口學系或科目為限。

3．交流學生之名額，雙方同意一對一的原則，等額互換，每學年總共六人，可以每學期派遣三人，也可以一個學期派遣六人。雙方交流學生總數，以五年內達到平衡為原則。

4．甲乙雙方須對交流學生之學習表現進行考核，考核結果並及時回饋對方，依自訂相關標準認定該學分，計入學生的學籍檔案。學習結束，甲乙雙方須為交流學生出具學習證明。

## 二、甄選範圍

1．雙方從各自學校就讀的全日制學生中，根據學習成績、個性、參加本交流計畫的合適性等情況，甄選到對方學校學習的學生。

2．雙方各自有權拒絕錄取由對方推薦的學生。在此情況下，推薦方可再次補充推薦。

## 三、學生管理

1．雙方學生管理部門負責交流學生在各自學校學習、生活的管理工作。除雙方的交流學生不參與交流院校的評優、評獎活動外，其它管理辦法將參照各自學校的管理規定執行。

2．甲乙雙方有關部門或單位儘量安排交流學生與本校生進行各項學術交流活動，並關心其生活起居，使交流計书獲得最大成效。

3．雙方選定交流學生後，合作學校應給予來校就讀的交流學生各方面的協助與輔導，包括協助安排住宿等，使交流學生能儘快適應新環境。

## 四、費用

1．交流學生按各自所屬學校收費標準繳納學費，雙方學校不再收取交流學生的研修費。

2．交流學生應自行負擔食宿費旅費、書籍費交通費、出入境手續費保險費及其它個人支出費用。

## 五、組織實施

1．本同意書內容在雙方主管校長領導下，指定各自學校相對應的行政職能部門實施，並歸該單位管理。

2．交流學生必須遵守當地法律及對方學校之校規。

3．雙方按情況為有辦理出入境手續需要的學生提供協助。交流學生要自行負責取得相關入境許可及遵從有關移民法規等條文。學校方面會盡力配合，但不負責保證能給予入境許可等文件。

4．交流學生應於指定期間內，向對方學校繳交校方規定之必要文件。

5．本同意書由雙方代表簽字之日後立即生效，有效期五年，同意書期滿時，本同意書將自動更新。同意書中未盡事宜由雙方協商後解決。如任何一方擬終止本同意書，應在預定終止日期之前至少六個月，以書面形式提出中止同意書的要求。在本同意書終止之前開始或已經存在的交流學習活動不受同意書終止的影響。有關的學生仍然可以按照原定計畫完成在對方學校的學習。

# UNIVERSITY OF NOTTINGHAM NINGBO CHINA

## PURPOSE

The purpose of this Memorandum of Understanding is to establish an affiliation Yuan Ze University (hereinafter referred to as "YZU") and University of Nottingham Ningbo China (hereinafter referred to as "UNNC") to implement a University-wide student exchange program.

## DEFINITIONS

In this agreement unless the content will otherwise imply, "exchange" shall mean a one-for-one exchange of students from each university, "exchange students" shall mean students participating in the exchange implemented herein,"home institution" shall mean the university at which the student intends to graduate and "host institution" shall mean the university which has agreed to receive the exchange students from the home institution.

## NUMBERS

Each year, the Institutions may exchange up to 3 full-time undergraduate students for one academic year of study or research or up to 6 students for one semester each of study or research per year at the host Institution. This number may be varied by mutual agreement.

## SELECTION OF PARTICIPANTS

This Memorandum of Understanding will be implemented according to UNNC's policy on international co-operation and YZU's Equal Opportunity Policy. These policies state that students should be selected on the basis of merit without regard to race, national or ethnic origin, colour, religion, age, sex, marital status or physical handicap. All participants will be treated in the same non-discriminatory manner in carrying out the provisions of the Agreement, subject to the provisions of the policies and requirements of

each of the institutions.

The home institution will screen its applicants for the exchange. Normally at least four months before the start of the academic year/semester, each institution will send to the other, the applications for exchange,that will include details of the students' academic record,personal statement, academic reference and proposed study plan. The host institution will reserve the right to make final judgments on admission of students nominated for the exchange. The following guidelines apply to all exchange students:

a) The exchange students may apply to any academic program participating in the Student Exchange Programme at the host institution, as full time, non-degree status students at a level, undergraduate or graduate, determined by the host institution.

b) Exchange students must complete at least one year of tertiary studies prior to commencing studies at the host institution.

c) The host institution may impose prerequisites and reserves the right to exclude students from restricted enrolment programs.

d) Upon acceptance of a graduate research student the host institution will assist the student to find a supervisor, however the host university will not be required to guarantee that a suitable supervisor will be available in all cases.

e) Any academic credit earned at the host institution will be transferred back to the home institution in accordance with procedures determined by the latter.

f)Upon completion of the year or semester at the host institution, the exchange students must return to the home institution unless an extension of stay has been approved by both co-operating universities.

g) The exchange students must abide by all the rules and regulations of the host institution.

## RESPONSIBILITIES OF YUANZEUNIVERSITY

According to the conditions outlined above YZU will agree to accept the prescribed number of exchange students, to enroll them as full time, non-degree students for the two regular semesters of the academic year or for one semester only.YzU will assist the UNNC exchange students, as far as possible, in arranging accommodation; and will provide appropriate assistance in matters of health, language and local custom that may arise. YZU shall provide UNNC with sufficient information to enable the latter to determine the academic credit to be granted to their exchange student for modules completed at YzU. This information should include details of:

a. Modular structure and module credit ratings and levels (e.g. Second Year Undergraduate, First Year Postgraduate)-

## b. Module grading system

This documentation is required by UNNC to enable it to meet the requirements imposed by the UK Quality Assurance Agency.

At the end of each academic semester, YZU will send to UNNC an official transcript of credits for each exchange student from UNNC studying at YzU.Co-ordination of this exchange program at YZU will be the responsibility of the designated Exchange Liaison Officer.
