Students who want to stay in the dorm during summer vacation MUST apply for summer vacation accommodation in accordance with the regulations.

Application eligibility: International Students

Master, Ph.D. students & Japan Future Enrichment Program Freshmen who need to stay in dorm need to register through the form (https://reurl.cc/NQGkbx)  before 5/31.  No extra fee is required for summer vacation accommodation.

Application period: 2024 May 6th～2024 May 31st

Staying period: 2024 June 23rd～September 7th　(Application for non-continuous periods will not be accepted)

Application process: Portal → Apply for stay during vacation → Print out application form → Signed by GAO/dept./teacher/instructor → Send to Student Housing Service Section for approval → Pay the fee at Office of General Affairs (Building 1) → Deliver the stamped form to Student Housing Service  Section

Accommodation fee:
Male & Female 1st Dormitory: $120 NTD per day
Male & Female 2nd Dormitory: $200 NTD per day

Any students who want to stay during this period must complete the procedure above, otherwise accommodation, internet and access card will not be provided.

If you have any questions, please contact the Student Housing Service Section (Male 1 Dorm, 1st Floor, Section C).
