活動簡介/ Introduction：


113學年度教師研究績效填報作業即將開始，為協助教師順利完成填報，特舉辦「教師研究績效填報說明會」。本次說明會將重點說明相關規範依據、填報作業時程、系統操作方式，以及佐證資料的檢附規定，歡迎有需要的老師踴躍參加。

The faculty research performance reporting for Academic Year 113 is about to begin.  To help you complete the submission smoothly, we invite you to attend the Faculty Research Performance Info Session. The session will cover relevant regulations, the reporting schedule, system operation procedures, and documentation requirements. All interested faculty members are welcome to join.
