- **文章簡介**: Turnitin will host a series of online webinars from March to June, featuring global research on AI in education, practical strategies to reduce educators’ workload, and approaches to balancing AI use with authentic learning.

Four sessions are specially recommended for participants in Taiwan. All webinars are conducted in English.

As some live sessions take place late at night or early morning (Taiwan time), registered participants will receive access to the recordings after each webinar.

Faculty and staff are warmly invited to attend and share this information with colleagues.



1. What the research reveals about students as AI allies

Join us as we discover how AI, when used responsibly, can act as an essential ally for both students and teachers. We will take a deep dive into recent research to show how making the writing journey visible helps move AI in education from a place of fear to one of partnership.

Speaker: Patti West-Smith, Senior Director of Customer Engagement

Time (Taiwan Time, Please choose one session.):


April 1 (Wednesday), 12:00 a.m.

April 1 (Wednesday), 5:00 a.m.


Registration: Recordings will be available to registered participants.

 

2. Does detection matter? Integrating AI with authentic learning

Explore how AI can support authentic learning, critical thinking, and writing development without compromising academic integrity with Turnitin’s Chief Product Officer, Annie Chechitelli. This session will share global insights on AI’s role in education and offer strategies for educator-guided AI use. You will gain insights from global research on AI in education and learn how students, educators, and administrators are navigating an increasingly AI-driven world. You will walk away with an understanding of how to responsibly incorporate generative AI into the classroom while prioritizing transparency, fairness, and developing authentic writing skills.

Speaker: Annie Chechitelli, Chief Product Officer.

Time (Taiwan Time)& Registration:


Session 1: April 23, 2026 (Thu.), 10:00 PM–10:45 PM

Session 2: April 24, 2026 (Fri.), 05:00 AM– 06:00 AM

Please choose one session.




3. Why Detection Alone Fails: A Holistic Way to Build AI Literacy

Discover how to turn the challenge of AI into an opportunity for authentic learning with Turnitin’s integrated learning solution. See how process-level insights and integrity tools help reduce workload, deliver meaningful feedback, and empower students to write responsibly in an AI-enabled world.

Speakers: 


Namrata Nanda (Chief Product Marketing Manager)

Leah Stockstill (Senior Product Marketing Manager)


Time (Taiwan Time)& Registration:

4. Make the Invisible Visible: Bringing Transparency to the Drafting Process

Learn how to shift from AI detection to authentic student growth with trackable drafts that make the writing process visible. Discover how Turnitin Clarity supports a process-driven approach that rebuilds trust and strengthens critical thinking.

Speakers: 


Audrey Campbell (Educator Engagement Manager)

Leah Stockstill (Senior Product Marketing Manager)
