Yuan Ze University, established in 1989, has developed close collaborations with Fujian Province, including the Fujian Province Teacher Training Program and the signing of multiple joint talent development agreements with universities.

To strengthen alumni relations, with the support of President Ching-Jong Liao, the "Yuan Ze University Mainland China Alumni Association" was officially established on June 17, 2023, in Xiamen, Fujian. Professor Ran-Xuan Ke of Xiamen Jimei University has been elected as the president of the Mainland China Alumni Association, to bring together alumni and foster a strong sense of unity.
