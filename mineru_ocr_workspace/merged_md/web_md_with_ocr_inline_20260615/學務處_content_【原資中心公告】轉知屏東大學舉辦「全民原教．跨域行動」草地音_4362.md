Subject: Grassland Concert - "Cross-domain Action for Indigenous Education" at National Pingtung University on December 14, 2023

Description:



This event is organized by the Indigenous Student Resource Center and the Bachelor"s Degree Program in Cultural Development, both hosted by National Pingtung University. It aims to connect indigenous students from higher education institutions in the southern region, including student clubs and youth associations from indigenous communities. The event will feature music performances and the sharing of subtle campus discrimination experiences, showcasing the contemporary challenges and diversity faced by indigenous youth. The goal is to enhance communication and connections among indigenous students from various schools and create a friendly campus environment.




Event Details: (a) Date and Time: December 14, 2023 (Thursday) 17:00-20:00 (b) Venue: Grassland beside the Central Walkway, Pingtung Campus, National Pingtung University (No. 51, Minsheng East Road, Pingtung City) (c) Target Audience: Students and faculty from universities and colleges nationwide.
