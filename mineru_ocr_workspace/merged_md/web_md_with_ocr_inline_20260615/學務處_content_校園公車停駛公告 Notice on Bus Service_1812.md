因應嚴重特殊傳染性肺炎防疫措施，

155、156號公車將暫停駛入校園，

造成不便請見諒。

 

For prevention of the severely infectious pneumonia, buses No.155 and No.156 will not enter the campus, temperature will be measured at the school gate from 2020/03/02. Sorry for any inconvenience.
