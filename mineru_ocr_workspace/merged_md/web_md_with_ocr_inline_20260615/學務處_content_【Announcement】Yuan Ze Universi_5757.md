Award Categories
(1) Silver Award: Each recipient will be awarded a certificate and registered in the Multi-Achievement Certificate System.
(2) Gold Award: Each recipient will be awarded a certificate and registered in the Multi-Achievement Certificate System, with a monetary reward.
Note: Gold awards earned in the 113-1 semester will be combined with those from the 113-2 semester, along with Achievement Awards, and will be issued at the end of the 113-2 semester.

Certificate Pickup: Details will be provided separately.
Award List: Please refer to the attachment.

Note: Please pay attention to notifications via the school’s portal mail regarding certificate pickup times. Congratulations to all the awardees, and we look forward to your continued efforts and achievements!
