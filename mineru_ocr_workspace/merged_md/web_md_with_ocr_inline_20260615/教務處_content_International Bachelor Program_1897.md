- **文章全文**: International Bachelor Programs application conditions and limitation 






1.  Students who meet any of the following conditions are not eligible to apply for transfer (to another department, program, college, or school): 

(1) Students who have not completed at least one academic year of study. 

(2) Students who have already studied up to the fourth year (senior year). 

(3) Students currently on a leave of absence. 

2. Transfer-related Regulations: 

  (1) Students enrolled in the internal Bachelor’s Program may apply to transfer to other departments within the same college. Each student may apply only once. 

  (2) Students from the same college may apply to transfer into the internal Bachelor’s Program. 

  (3) The above regulations do not apply to students in the English Program of the College of Management. 

  (4) The admission standards and final decisions are determined by the respective departments within the college.     






Application Process 






 

1.  Within the application period specified on the academic calendar, download both the "Transfer Application Form for the English-Taught Bachelor’s Program" and the "YZU Student Transfer Self-Evaluation and Planning Form" from the transfer website.

2. Attach a transcript of academic records (must include grades and class rank from the most recent semester). 

3. Submit the signed application (approved by the original department’s administrative staff and department chair; no signature is needed from the department being transferred into) to the Registration Section of the Office of Academic Affairs. The Registration Section will forward the application to the intended department for review. 

4. Once a transfer has been approved, it cannot be changed or withdrawn. 






Important Notes for Students Who Have Been Approved for Transfer: 






1. Course selection must follow the required courses of the new department. 

2. Submit a paper-based credit transfer application form. Only professional courses require a credit transfer process. General education courses such as military training, physical education, and liberal arts do not require credit transfer applications. 

3.  Credit transfer information page https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-03-22-06-44-19/193-2016-03-22-08-09-27。

# Yuan Ze University Regulations for Students of International Bachelor Programs Applying for Change of Major

Passed by the 1st Academic Affairs Meeting of the 2017 School Year, September 27, 2017   
Amended by the 7th Academic Affairs Meeting of the 2019 School Year, June 17, 2020   
Amended by the 1st Academic Affairs Meeting of the 2023 School Year, September 27, 2023

Article 1 “Yuan Ze University Regulations for Students of International Bachelor Programs Applying for Change of Major” is provided to offer students of International Bachelor Programs a flexible and wide range of learning channels.

Article 2 Students of International Bachelor Programs who are not interested in the current major to which they are enrolled may apply for a change of major after they have completed their first academic year of studies. Each student can only apply one time. Each department may establish its own criteria for reviewing applications.

Article 3 Students of the same College who want to apply for transferring into International Bachelor Programs shall meet all the criteria set forth by the program. The International Bachelor Program will select the best students for admission.

Article 4 Students who apply for a change of major into or out of International Bachelor Programs shall file an application during the $2 ^ { \mathrm { n d } }$ semester of each academic year.

Article 5 Application procedures:

1. Students applying t for a change of major into or out of International Bachelor Programs shall fill out an application form, which must be signed and approved by the applicant’s parents or guardians (except for students who meet the legal age requirement of adulthood under the Civil Code). The application form shall be submitted to Registration Division of Office of Academic Affairs along with the applicant’s personal development plan and transcript of the previous semesters. These documents will then be sent to the corresponding department for reviewing and approval.

2. Based on the reviewing results of the corresponding department, students may be required for a grade repetition due to their ability levels; those who don’t accept the requirement will be deemed as to waive the application.

3. The review results shall be approved by the meetings of accepting departments and sent to Office of Academic Affairs to process the change of an applicant’s student status which shall be based on the applicant’s order of department preference. The change of an applicant’s student status will be effective beginning from the next semester after the application.

Article 6 Matters not specified herein shall be governed by “Yuan Ze University Regulations for Change of Major”.

Article 7 These regulations shall be implemented following the approval of the Academic Affairs Meeting, modifications shall follow the same procedure.
