Do you know that monosodium glutamate (MSG) is actually L-glutamate? Or that sorbitol, an artificial sweetener, can be derived from blueberries?
Food additives are everywhere—but are they always safe and healthy? Join this fun and educational quiz hosted by Web of Science and Journal Citation Reports (JCR) to learn about research on food safety, personality traits, and more. Answer correctly for a chance to win great prizes!

How to Join:


Access the JCR database subscribed by Yuan Ze University.

Click the quiz link on the event site.

Submit all correct answers and complete your information to enter the prize draw!
