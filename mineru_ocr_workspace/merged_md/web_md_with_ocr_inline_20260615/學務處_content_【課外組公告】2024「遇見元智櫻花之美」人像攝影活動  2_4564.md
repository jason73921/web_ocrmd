- **文章簡介**: 元智大學「有庠花園」園區內種植超過30棵櫻花樹，從開學前夕就陸續綻放。

為了讓社區民眾也能飽覽朵朵盛開的櫻花勝景，元智大學邀請課外組輔導之攝影社同學，運用其人像攝影專業，為社區民眾拍攝專業的櫻花人像攝影。

活動日期：113年2月26日（星期一）11:30-13:00
活動地點：元智大學「有庠花園」。

活動方式：時段內由元智大學課外活動組安排攝影社同學為蒞臨本校賞櫻之社區民眾拍攝人像照片，拍攝照片將上傳雲端，並將提供下載連結，供民眾下載留念。

活動聯絡人：課外活動組 簡老師，03-4638800分機2912

 

Yuan Ze University"s "You Xiang Garden" campus is adorned with over 30  cherry blossom trees, which have been gradually blooming since the eve of the new semester.

To allow members of the community to fully appreciate the blossoming cherry blossoms, Yuan Ze University has invited students from the extracurricular photography club to utilize their expertise in portrait photography, capturing professional cherry blossom portraits for the local residents.

Event Date: February 26, 2024 (Monday), 11:30-13:00 Event Location: Yuan Ze University "You Xiang Garden".

Event Format: During the designated time, students from Yuan Ze University"s extracurricular activities group will arrange for photography club students to take portrait photos of community members who come to enjoy the cherry blossoms on campus. The photos will be uploaded to the cloud, and download links will be provided for people to download as keepsakes.

Contact Person for the Event: Teacher Jian from the Extracurricular Activities Group, 03-4638800 ext. 2912
