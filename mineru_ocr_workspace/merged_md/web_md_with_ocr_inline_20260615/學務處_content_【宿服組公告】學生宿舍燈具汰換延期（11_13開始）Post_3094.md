因期中考週將至，燈具汰換工程暫停施作，延至2022/11/13再開始汰換，請同學安心準備考試。

 

日期：2022/11/13起　8:00～17:00

地點：學生宿舍寢室及公共區域

注意事項：

1.施工期間將進出寢室內部，個人貴重物品請隨身攜帶或放進衣櫃鎖起。

2.請各位同學將地面物品清空（床組中間走道、窗戶下方），避免影響工程施作。

3.請同學留意施工人員進出，離開寢室時，房門務必上鎖。

4.造成不便請見諒，謝謝您的合作。

 



 

Due to the upcoming mid-term exam week, the lamp replacement project has been postponed until 2022/11/13. Please prepare for the exam with peace of mind. Give your best shot and Good Luck！

 

Date：From 2022/11/13　8:00～17:00​

Place：Student bedrooms and Public areas​

Note:​

1.Repairman will enter the student bedrooms during the constructions period so please bring all your personal valuable stuffs with you or lock them in the closet.

2.Please clean all stuffs on the floor (the aisle in the middle and under the windows) to avoid affecting the construction.​

3.Please pay attention to the workers during the construction, remember to lock the door when leaving.​

4.Sorry for this inconvenience, thank you for your cooperation.​

 

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
