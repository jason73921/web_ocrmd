近期發現數起宿舍區電力系統遭惡意破壞事件，請大家特別留意門禁管制及廠商洽公修繕動線及施工情形。

如發現蓄意破壞屬實，將報警處理，以維護宿舍同學人身安全。

若發現異常，請立即通報男女櫃台處理0903-032011、03-4638800#2881、2882。

Recently, there have been several incidents of malicious damage to the power system in the dormitory area. Please pay special attention to the access control, the location of the repairman, and the construction situation.

If deliberate sabotage is found to be true, the police will be dealt with to protect the personal safety of the dormitory students.

If any abnormality is found, please immediately report to the male and female dorm counters for processing 0903-032011, 03-4638800#2881, 2882.
