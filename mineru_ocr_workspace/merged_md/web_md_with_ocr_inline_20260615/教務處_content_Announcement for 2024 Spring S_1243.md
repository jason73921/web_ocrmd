Office of Academic Affairs

Subject： 2024 Spring Semester Reduced Course Load Application will start at 12:00 noon on February 1st, 2024 (Thu.) and end at 12:00 noon on February 29th, 2024 (Thu.). Individuals will be able to apply online, applicants shall submit a parental consent form to the office of department before its deadline and have the course selected before the online course selection stage III ends.

Content：

1. Reduced Course Load procedure：

Portal →Study File →Course Reduction →Fill in your reason for application →Submit（Parental Consent Form）will need to be verified by the office of department →Once it’s been approved, students can reduce the course online during the course selection period.

2. Online Course Reduction starts at 12:00 noon on February 1st, 2024 (Thu.) and end at 12:00 noon on February 29th, 2024 (Thu.). Students shall apply online and submit the Parental Consent Form to the office of the department as soon as possible. And complete the course selection before the deadline for online course selection stage III.

3. According to the university rules and regulations, undergraduate students who apply for the Course Reduction shall have a special reason and take at least one course each semester (not including PE and Service Learning), except for exchange students. Violators shall suspend from school. Students who extend their studies can select the courses for wish and do not need to apply for the course reduction. The students who take no courses still need to register.

4. Individuals who wish to drop course in the mid semester but will then have credits less than the minimum of credits required that semester shall apply for Reduced Course Load at this time.

Once it’s over the course reduction deadline, shall not apply again due to course withdrawal.
