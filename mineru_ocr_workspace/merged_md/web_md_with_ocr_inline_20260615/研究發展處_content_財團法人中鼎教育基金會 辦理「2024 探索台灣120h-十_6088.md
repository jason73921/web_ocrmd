## 壹、活動簡介 Introduction

探索台灣 120h 活動已經成功地邁入第十屆了，活動以增進國內大專青年瞭解聯合國 17 項永

續發展目標在國內的實踐為重心，今年我們特別定名為「十現永續」。獲選學生以組隊方式進

## 展目標（SDGs）相關。

"Taiwan in My Eyes" has successfully entered its tenth occurrence. This event is focused on enhancing the college students understanding of the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and their implementation within Taiwan. This year, we have named the program "Fantastic Ten". The selected students will engage in a five-day continuous activity in teams, during which the organizing committee will lead the students to five designated visitation points (including benchmark companies, government organizations, and national parks) for visits to explore sustainability aspects. During the rest of the activity period, students are free to arrange their exploration of sustainability aspects in Taiwan. They must complete visits to at least six self-selected sites within the five-day activity period, and these self-selected sites must be related to the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) released by the United Nations.

businesses.
