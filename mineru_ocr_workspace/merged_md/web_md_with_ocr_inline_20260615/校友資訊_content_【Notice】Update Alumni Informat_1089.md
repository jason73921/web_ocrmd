Since the founding of Yuan Ze University, we have cultivated countless outstanding talents. Our alumni continue to excel in their respective professional fields, contributing greatly to society. In this digital age, the influence of online platforms is ever-growing. Wikipedia, as one of the world’s largest open databases, plays a significant role in enhancing the visibility and reputation of academic institutions. To further elevate Yuan Ze University"s global presence and academic standing, we sincerely invite you to take a moment to update your educational background, career achievements, and any YZU-related information on Wikipedia. Additionally, we encourage you to connect with Yuan Ze University on LinkedIn. This will help foster stronger connections and communication among alumni, creating a more robust Yuan Ze alumni network.

Every small action you take will lay a solid foundation for the future development of Yuan Ze University, helping our alma mater climb even higher in global university rankings. We deeply appreciate your support and participation, and we look forward to working together with you to create an even brighter future for Yuan Ze.

 

Wishing you all the best,

Yzu Alumni Service Center
