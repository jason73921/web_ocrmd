## Aim and Scope

The Journal of Food and Drug Analysis (JFDA) is the official peer-reviewed publication of the Food and Drug Administration, Taiwan (TFDA). It is published quarterly by Elsevier in January, April, July and October. The Journal aims to publish original research and review papers on the analysis of food, traditional Chinese medicine, drugs and cosmetics as well as related disciplines that are of topical interest to the public health profession.
