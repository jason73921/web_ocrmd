As 2024 is about to enter its final month, Yuan Ze University’s annual Christmas Lighting Concert is ready to light up the campus! The Yuan Ze Christmas Carnival series presents "Dreamy Christmas: A Feast of Light and Shadow" lighting event. 

Join us to light up Yuan Ze and share the warmth and joy of the Christmas Carnival.

傳遞給每個參與的你我一份最溫暖和誠摯的祝福。
