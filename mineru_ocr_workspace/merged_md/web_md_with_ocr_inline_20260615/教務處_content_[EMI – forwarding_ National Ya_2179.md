Description:

1.       The Advance HE EMI Training for Professional Academics is jointly delivered by certified trainers from Advance HE (UK) and our university’s international higher education training team. The program provides higher education teaching training that integrates international perspectives with Taiwan-based practical experience. Participants who complete the program will be awarded a certificate issued by Advance HE (UK).

2.       Course Information: (1) Target Participants: Faculty members, doctoral students, and doctoral-level professionals from public and private universities and colleges in Taiwan who wish to enhance their EMI teaching strategies and professional teaching development (2) Course Duration: A total of 24 hours (including synchronous online sessions and in-person EMI teaching demonstrations and lesson observations) (3) Course Objectives: To support instructors in improving their teaching techniques and strategies for delivering subject content in English (EMI), and to help them stay updated on the latest theories and practices in higher education teaching (4) Registration Period and Method: From April 13 to May 29, 2026. Please register and complete payment via our Continuing Education website under the “EMI Course Program for International Higher Education”: https://cec.nycu.edu.tw/Course/CourseInfo?CId=93171 Early bird discounts are available for registrations completed before May 15. Group discounts are also available for groups of three or more participants. (5) Additional Information: Please refer to the attached poster. For further details, visit the registration website above or the Center’s website: https://reurl.cc/M4eA9k
