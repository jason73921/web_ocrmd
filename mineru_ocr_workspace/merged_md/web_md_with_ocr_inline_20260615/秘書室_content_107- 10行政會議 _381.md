※決定：

2. 學務處昨天（12/11）傍晚臨時收到遠東資源開發公司丁經理的來信通知，遠紡宿舍將一次性整體修繕，租約到明年三月為止，之後暫不續約。這段期間，該區域的住宿生將搬移到校內宿舍居住。由於校內宿舍有預先保留床位給境外生 174 床，短期內床位供應無虞。另一方面，過去因為保留床位所致，住宿率不佳而造成虧損；例如從 105 學年度就虧損 204萬，到 106 學年度虧 213 萬、到 107 學年度虧 313 萬，因為研究生的住宿率不到一半。在 105 學年度保留給境外生是 1000 床，但是入住率是67.35%；106 學年度我們把它降到 830 床，入住率大概是 79.75%；107學年度保留700床，但是入住只有 524床，入住率是 74.85%。承上結果，我們大概虧了 324 萬。倘若我們暫時不承租的話，一年約可省掉 330 萬的費用(合約租金+保全人事費+基本維護清潔費)。本校將藉此機會重新思考與遠紡宿舍新訂合約，以及冷氣處置和電梯及保全等定期合約做適當調整。

公務機關或非公務機關依第十五條或第十九條規定向當事人蒐集個人資料

時，應明確告知當事人下列事項：

一、公務機關或非公務機關名稱。

二、蒐集之目的。

三、個人資料之類別。

四、個人資料利用之期間、地區、對象及方式。

五、當事人依第三條規定得行使之權利及方式。

六、當事人得自由選擇提供個人資料時，不提供將對其權益之影響。有下列情形之一者，得免為前項之告知：

一、依法律規定得免告知。

二、個人資料之蒐集係公務機關執行法定職務或非公務機關履行法定義務所必要。

三、告知將妨害公務機關執行法定職務。

四、告知將妨害公共利益。

五、當事人明知應告知之內容。

六、個人資料之蒐集非基於營利之目的，且對當事人顯無不利之影響

## 第十九條

非公務機關對個人資料之蒐集或處理，除第六條第一項所規定資料外，

應有特定目的，並符合下列情形之一者：

一、法律明文規定。

二、與當事人有契約或類似契約之關係，且已採取適當之安全措施。

三、當事人自行公開或其他已合法公開之個人資料。

四、學術研究機構基於公共利益為統計或學術研究而有必要，且資料經過提供者處理後或經蒐集者依其揭露方式無從識別特定之當事人。

五、經當事人同意。

六、為增進公共利益所必要。

七、個人資料取自於一般可得之來源。但當事人對該資料之禁止處理或利用，顯有更值得保護之重大利益者，不在此限。

八、對當事人權益無侵害。

蒐集或處理者知悉或經當事人通知依前項第七款但書規定禁止對該資料之處理或利用時，

應主動或依當事人之請求，刪除、停止處理或利用該個人資料

## 第二十條

非公務機關對個人資料之利用，除第六條第一項所規定資料外，應於蒐集之特定目的必要範圍內為之。但有下列情形之一者，得為特定目的外之利用：

一、法律明文規定。

二、為增進公共利益所必要。

三、為免除當事人之生命、身體、自由或財產上之危險。

四、為防止他人權益之重大危害。

五、公務機關或學術研究機構基於公共利益為統計或學術研究而有必要，且資料經過提供者處理後或經蒐集者依其揭露方式無從識別特定之當事人。

六、經當事人同意。

七、有利於當事人權益。

非公務機關依前項規定利用個人資料行銷者，當事人表示拒絕接受行銷時，應即停止利用其個人資料行銷。非公務機關於首次行銷時，應提供當事人表示拒絕接受行銷之方式，並支付所需費用。

# AGREEMENT REGARDING STUDY ABROAD PROGRAMS BETWEEN YUAN ZE UNIVERSITY AND HANOI TECHNICAL COLLEGE

This Agreement is made by and between Yuan Ze University, Taoyuan, Taiwan, R.O.C., (hereinafter “YZU”) and Hanoi Technical College, Viet Nam, (hereinafter “HTC”), pursuant to the Cooperation Agreement executed between the two parties.

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish the terms and conditions under which the study abroad programs between YZU and HTC shall take place.

Both parties hereby agree that:

## 1. Program Management

The study abroad program shall be available to students of HTC and shall be administered by YZU and HTC respectively via the Global Affairs Office of YZU and International Collaboration Office of HTC.

## 2. Study Abroad Student Numbers and Balance

HTC may send up to seven (7) full-time equivalent study abroad students per year during any year of the Agreement. Special permission is given by mutual agreement between the two institutions.

## 3. Study Abroad Period

The normal period of study abroad for each student shall be one (1) year with the consent of HTC.

## 4. Admission Requirements

Study abroad candidates must have completed at least six (6) months of study at HTC prior to the study abroad period, with an overall GPA of 2.4 or higher out of a 4.0 grade scale. Each party will notify the other as quickly as possible if any changes are made to student study abroad admission requirements. While it is understood that candidates nominated by HTC will normally be accepted by YZU, YZU reserves the right to review the applications of nominees and make final decisions concerning their admission.

## 5. Language Requirements

Nominees must obtain either a TOEFL score of 500 or a TOCFL score of level 3, or theirs equivalent and present proof of the score when applying. YZU will notify the other as quickly as possible if any changes are made to language requirements.

## 6. Application Documents

HTC will forward the appropriate application documents to YZU, and YZU will inform HTC of its final admission decision within thirty (30) days after the application deadline.

## 7. Visa

YZU will issue appropriate documents to accepted students for visa purposes in accordance with their respective applicable laws and regulations. It is the responsibility of the individual study abroad student to obtain a visa in a timely manner.

## 8. Courses

Study abroad students will be able to take courses regularly offered at YZU and will

have all the rights and privileges enjoyed by other students at that university.

## 9. Credit Transfer

Any academic credit received during the course of the program may be transferred to HTC in accordance with the appropriate regulations of YZU. YZU will inform HTC of study abroad students’ grade information in a timely manner upon the request of the study abroad student or HTC.

## 10. Tuition and Fees

Study abroad students will pay normal tuition and fees to YZU in accordance with the policies of YZU.

## 11. Miscellaneous Fees

Study abroad students will be responsible for making their international travel arrangements and shall bear all associated costs, including but not limited to room, board, study materials, local transportation, student health insurance and medical care, personal expenses, and passport/visa application fees. Regarding payment of these expenses, HTC together with YZU, shall assume responsibility for providing guidance and dealing with situations that may arise so as not to cause undue inconvenience to third parties when students fall into arrears.

## 12. Liability

During their time at YZU, study abroad students will be individually liable for any and all incidents, accidents and damage to third parties resulting from the students’ actions where these actions occur in situations beyond the control of YZU and HTC. Both parties will not hold each other liable for such incidents.

## 13. Insurance

It is mandatory for study abroad students from both institutes to purchase overseas travel and health insurance before departure.

## 14. Accommodation

YZU will make every effort to find suitable accommodation for study abroad students.   
The cost of the accommodation shall be borne by the individual student.

## 15. Student Support

YZU will make every effort to facilitate integration of study abroad students into the student life of YZU. YZU will strive to protect the privacy of the student as well as their personal safety in addition to offering orientation sessions and a sufficient support network for everyday life.

## 16. Emergency Procedures

Both YZU and HTC shall provide the other with emergency contact information and will be responsible for carrying out emergency notification in the event of student illness, injury, or other emergencies.

## 17. Appropriate Use of Personal Information

When obtaining personal information from the study abroad student, it is necessary for both parties to inform the student ahead of time of the purposes for its use and to keep its use to a minimum. Personal information must be managed carefully to prevent its spread to a third party or use for purposes unauthorized by the student.

## 18. Rules and Regulations

Study abroad students shall be subject to the rules and regulations of YZU. Both parties reserves the right to dismiss any study abroad student at any time for conduct in violation of their established rules and regulations. However, the dismissal of a study

abroad student shall not adversely affect the agreement and the arrangements regarding other students.

## 19. Length of Stay

Upon completion of the program at YZU, study abroad students are required to return to HTC. No extension of stay will be authorized unless otherwise specifically agreed upon by both parties.

## 20. Negotiations

Both parties will strive to resolve any misunderstandings out of or in relation to this agreement that arise in a sincere and respectful manner.

## 21. Agreement Period

This Agreement shall remain in effect from the date of execution for an initial period of five (5) years. Unless either party notifies the other in writing to terminate the Agreement at least six (6) months prior to the date of expiration, the Agreement will be automatically renewed and extended for another five (5) years from the date of expiration. In the case of termination, both parties shall make necessary arrangements to ensure the appropriate completion of ongoing study abroad programs. This Agreement may be amended by mutual written consent the two of parties through consultation.

## 我們面對的危機

• 人文社會科學教育的本質：1.重視人文的基本價值；2.關懷社會現象；3.豐富人類的精神生活。

1.以科技發展與經濟競爭為主軸的國家發展策略：一向重視人文精神與社會關懷的科系比較無法凸顯其價值，故在家長與社會各界眼中成為可有可無的教學部門。

• 2.目前大學教育績效重視學生的就業傾向，以教育投資報酬率為考量：人文社會教育由於以追求人類精神文明與社會價值，不容易展現其投資報酬價值，故教育成果不容易受到社會肯定。

## 元智藝設結合中語 AR教學瞭解桃園「塘」詩

元智大學藝術與設計系副教授林楚卿(左起)、中語系助理教授黃智明、人文社會學院院長丘昌泰，發表開發的桃園「塘」詩AR互動教學系統。

 元智大學藝術與設計系副教授林楚卿及中語系助理教授黃智明，設計與文學跨域合作，共同開發桃園「塘」詩AR互動教學系統。

 該系統希望藉由大數據及AR技術，開發場域體驗的文學教學工具，結合地景文化、文學詩詞的互動教學媒材，讓學生實地以AR實虛共構的空間，來體驗詩詞意境，學習文學變得更有趣活潑，甚至深入領悟詩人在詩詞中之意象。

 林楚卿表示，希望將開發的AR互動教學工具，應用於中語系黃教授的詩詞教學課堂中。基於黃智明教授對台灣埤塘相關的詩詞研究與整理，林楚卿設計團隊將所有詩詞內容，進行意象分類，再找出詩詞中所述埤塘相對應的地理位置，而對於每一種意象類型，再透過大數據分析，產生詞雲分析，細分不同類型詩詞常用的景象或物件。

## 林楚卿：科技可以很藝術；電腦輔助設計與製造課程

• 此課程為藝術與設計系大二下學期的選修課，三學分。屬於創客（Maker）實作教學課程，課程目標為訓練學生應用數位製造（digital fabrication）技術來製作設計與藝術的創作實品，除了培育設計師具備Maker所需跨領域工程之技能，重點在於訓練設計背景學生有創意的應用數位製造技術於設計與藝術創作中，達到「科技可以很藝術」之教學目標。課程中學生將理解目前「自造者運動」（maker movement）的發展，以Maker三大精神：學習（learn），製造（make）及共享（share），來學習數位製造原理及其應用。

• 以設計導向講解數位製造的應用，每兩週結合翻轉教室方式（基礎的技術教學利用線上影片教學，實體課程著重於進階技術教學及作品討論），讓學生學習不同的數位製造技能，包含雷射切割、3D列印、CNC銑床、3D掃描。學生兩人一組，每週都必須實作設計作品，給予主題以產品設計及空間元素為主，ex雷射切割光影燈罩、3D列印有機型態面板設計、CNC紋理牆面及接頭設計、3D掃描場景/頭像設計
