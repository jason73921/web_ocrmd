一、管制施工時間：115年2月6日（星期五）7:30 至 14:00。

二、學生機車停車場訂於2月6日進行灌漿作業，將佔用管制操場旁環校道路通行，詳如附圖。

五、施工期間如造成不便，敬請見諒。

 

Office of General Affairs Announcement

Subject: Traffic Control Notice for Campus Perimeter Road (Adjacent to the Athletic Field).

Description:


Construction & Control Period: Friday, February 6, 2026, from 7:30 AM to 2:00 PM.

Reason for Control: Concrete pouring for the Student Motorcycle Parking Lot is scheduled for February 6. This work will occupy the campus perimeter road next to the athletic field (please refer to the attached map for details).

Traffic Restrictions: Road access will be restricted during the operation, and cars will not be able to pass through. If you are heading to the living quarters (Life Zone), please park your vehicle in the designated parking spaces along the perimeter road.

Alternative Access: During the control period, the side gate on Dongtai Street (Bade District) will be open. Vehicles heading to the living quarters may use the Dongtai Street gate to enter and exit.


         Note: If you entered the campus via Yuandong Road, do not exit through Dongtai Street. This will result in an incomplete exit record, which may cause significantly higher parking fees.


Apology for Inconvenience: We apologize for any inconvenience caused during the construction period. Thank you for your cooperation.
