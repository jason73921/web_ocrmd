Talent Taiwan is a national service center that supports international professionals living and working in Taiwan. Building on the success of the Gold Card program, which has issued over 8,000 cards since 2018, the center now serves a broader range of foreign talent.

It provides one stop support with trusted information on visas, jobs, housing, banking, and more to help individuals and their families settle into life in Taiwan. You can explore these services directly through the Talent Taiwan website.

Stay connected and follow Talent Taiwan on LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook.📬 Subscribe to the Monthly Newsletter – Work-Related Opportunities for updates and useful resources.
