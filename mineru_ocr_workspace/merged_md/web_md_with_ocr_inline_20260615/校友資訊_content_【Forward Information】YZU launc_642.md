## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 642
- **文章標題**: 【Forward Information】YZU launches two-week online teaching for the opening of new semester
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20210902-en
- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University announced to hold two-week online teaching from Sep.22 to Oct.5, avoiding the risk of cluster infections caused by moving around during the Mid-Autumn Festival.

The English summer camp for freshmen will be canceled this year, and new student orientation will be promoted through online videos.

In accordance with the regulations of CECC, the courses with the limits on the number of indoor gatherings will resume physical teaching. The policy will be reviewed on a rolling basis.

  

For more information, please visit http://yzunews.yzu.edu.tw/back-to-school-within-distance-teaching-on-two-week-yzu/.
