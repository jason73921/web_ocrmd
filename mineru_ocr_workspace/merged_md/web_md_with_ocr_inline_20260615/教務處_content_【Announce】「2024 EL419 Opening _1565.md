To align with the Ministry of Education"s Bilingual Nation Initiative, an advanced English course (EL419) will be offered. This event serves as the commencement ceremony for the course, announcing the set learning objectives and the reward measures for achieving them. Additionally, distinguished instructors will be invited to share more effective English learning methods and examination strategies.

All first-year students who have chosen EL419 are encouraged to promptly register for participation via the Portal system. Lunch will be provided on the day of the event. We warmly welcome all students to join us in this auspicious occasion.
