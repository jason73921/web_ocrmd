Yuan Ze University was founded in 1978 and is about to enter its 34th year. It has graduated alumni all over the world, with a total of more than 58,000 people. In order to expand alumni services and unite alumni energy, "Yuan Ze University Alumni Association" was officially established on December 15.

Yuan Ze University Alumni Association is composed of group members, represented by departmental alumni associations and regional alumni associations. In the future, overseas alumni associations will also be established, and the alumni organization is expected to grow and thrive.
