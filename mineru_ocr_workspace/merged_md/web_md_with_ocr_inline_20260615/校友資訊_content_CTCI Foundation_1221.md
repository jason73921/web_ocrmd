- **文章簡介**: About CTCI Education Foundation

The CTCI Education Foundation is committed to promoting sustainable development and international talent cultivation. It supports young professionals by offering programs such as internship matching, career guidance, and industry-academia collaboration initiatives.

Through its talent platform, the foundation connects foreign professionals with opportunities in Taiwan’s green and tech industries, helping them integrate into Taiwan’s workforce and society.

You can explore their programs and resources at the CTCI Education Foundation website.
