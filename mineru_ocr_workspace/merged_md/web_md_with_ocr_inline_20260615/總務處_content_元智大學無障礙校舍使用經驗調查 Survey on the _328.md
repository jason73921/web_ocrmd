各位師長、同仁與同學，大家好!
為配合【115年度教育部辦理大專校院優良校園無障礙建築物評選及獎勵實施計畫】打造更安全、友善、包容的校園，
我們邀請您分享在校內建築與空間的使用經驗，無論是日常行走、上下樓、推車搬運，或陪同他人，都歡迎提供觀察與建議。

元智大學無障礙校舍使用經驗調查  （請點選連結）

您的回饋將協助學校檢視無障礙設施、優化空間，並作為改善與資源投入的重要依據。
本調查僅用於環境改善與研究，請放心填寫。部分題目可上傳照片，請避免拍攝可辨識個人身分的影像，以維護隱私。

您的每一個意見，都是推動友善校園的重要力量！感謝您的參與。

總務處敬啟


Dear Faculty Members, Staff, and Students,

To support the 2026 Ministry of Education Program for the Evaluation and Award of Outstanding Accessible Campus Buildings, Yuan Ze University is committed to creating a safer, more friendly, and inclusive campus environment. We sincerely invite you to share your experiences using campus buildings and spaces—whether related to daily walking routes, stairways, moving carts, or accompanying others—and to provide your observations and suggestions.

Survey on the Use of Accessible Campus Facilities at Yuan Ze University 

Your feedback will assist the University in reviewing existing accessibility facilities, improving campus spaces, and serving as an important reference for future improvements and resource allocation.

This survey is intended solely for environmental improvement and research purposes by the General Affairs Office and is not related to any form of personal evaluation. Please feel free to respond with confidence. Some questions allow photo uploads; to protect privacy, please avoid submitting images that may reveal identifiable individuals.

Every comment you provide is a valuable contribution toward building a more accessible and inclusive campus. Thank you very much for your participation.

Sincerely,

General Affairs Office
