- **文章簡介**: 🚩 Yuan Ze University Alumni Association - Visit to Xin An Long-Term Care Facility on October 26, 2024

For its October visit, the Yuan Ze University Alumni Association had the pleasure of visiting Xin An Residential Long-Term Care Facility in Taoyuan, led by Alumni Association Board Member Mr. Huang Yung-Chen, who also serves as the Chairman of the facility and its affiliated home care agency. Xin An operates with a mission of "Treating the Elderly with Warmth and Kindness," providing safe and comfortable age-friendly facilities equipped with advanced smart technology, all aimed at delivering top-quality care services. Their corporate philosophy emphasizes an “Innovative and Pragmatic” approach, with goals to “Exceed Expectations,” a principle of “Unity and Dedication,” and a mission to “Dare to Innovate.”
