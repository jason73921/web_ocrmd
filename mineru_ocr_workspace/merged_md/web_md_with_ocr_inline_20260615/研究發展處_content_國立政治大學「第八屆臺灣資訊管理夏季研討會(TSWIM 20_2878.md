## CALL FOR PAPERS

## 2020 Taiwan Summer Workshop on Information Management (TSWIM)

We invite the submission of full completed research papers, research-in-progress papers, and extended abstracts to the 8th Taiwan Summer Workshop on Information Management (TSWIM 2020), which will be held in National Chengchi University on June 13-14, 2020. TSWIM 2020 brings together senor editors at major IS journals, scholars and PhD students to exchange knowledge related to TSWIM 2020 theme, "Making the use of IS/IT meaningful."
