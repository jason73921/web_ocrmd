- **文章簡介**: There is no doubt that the Lunar New Year is the most important festival in traditional Taiwanese society, which symbolizes family reunions and harmony.

During the Lunar New Year, Chinese people follow the tradition of having a "Reunion Dinner", also known as "family reunion."

All family members, young and old, gather to enjoy a meal together. Many people that far away from home will rush home to welcome the new year with their loved ones.

This year, Office of Student Affairs is organizing a pre-Chinese New Year reunion dinner for international students who cannot return home.

We are open to 30 participants, welcoming those staying during the winter break to come together, experience traditional Taiwanese New Year culture, and enjoy a festive meal.

Registration: Please sign up on the Google Form. Limited to 30 participants.

Time: February 5, 2024, 12:00 PM

Location: Xingxiang Food Court
