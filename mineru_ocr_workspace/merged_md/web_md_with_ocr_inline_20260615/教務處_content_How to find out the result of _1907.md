- **文章簡介**: Dear faculty,

The Beginning-of-2025 Academic Year Spring Semester study evaluation has ended.

Go to Portal--> Teaching File --> New Questionaire and you can find out the result.
It is suggested to have mutual communication between students and you based on the result so as to teach more effectively and bring a positive outcome.

 

The End-of-2025 Academic Year Spring Semester study evaluation will be done during 2025/05/15~2025/05/29.
（Graduating Students Online Learning Survey Period: from May 1, 2025 to May 7, 2025.）

 

Best Regards,


Teaching Services Section Office of Academic Affairs
