Principles for Restricting the Use of Products That Endanger National Information and Communication Security


According to the Executive Yuan’s official letter No. 1121000202 dated June 20, 2023, the principles for government agencies using ICT products are reiterated:



(1) As stipulated in the Executive Yuan Secretary-General’s letter No. 1090201804A dated December 18, 2020, the use and procurement of Mainland brand ICT products (including software, hardware, and services) are strictly prohibited.

(2) If there is a business need and no alternative solution is available, and the use of products that may endanger national information and communication security is necessary, the agency must:



Provide a detailed justification.

Obtain step-by-step approval from the agency’s Information Security Officer and the superior agency’s Information Security Officer.

Submit the case to the competent authority of the Information and Communication Security Management Act (Ministry of Digital Affairs) for final approval.

Before the product is phased out, the agency must implement the following enhanced cybersecurity measures.
