- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20210917-en-1
- **文章簡介**: With the support and call from the Ministry of Science and Technology, Academia Sinica, Ministry of Education, and Ministry of Health and Welfare, the “2021 Future Tech Award” has received more than 500 applications and 100 are selected in the final to display in the online exhibition of FUTEX during Oct.14~23. Two Yuan Ze University professors won the prizes, including Prof. Cheng-En Ho of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science standing out with “the technology in the development and application of 5G high-frequency radiating components”, as well as Prof. Tien-Lung Chiu of Electrical Engineering Department with “Ultra-Efficient Triplet -Triplet Annihilation Up-conversion in Light-Organic Components”. YZU becomes the top private university winning the most awards.
