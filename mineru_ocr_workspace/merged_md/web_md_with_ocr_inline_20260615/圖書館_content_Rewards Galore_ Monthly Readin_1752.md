- **文章簡介**: Discover a World of Treasures in Books: Delightful Surprises Await You at the Library! Join us to experience the latest reading trends, where your love for books and the library will shine through with an enchanting aura of confidence.

If you are a faculty member or student of Yuan Ze University, you are eligible to participate in the monthly reading points accumulation event by simply borrowing books and swiping your card at the library during the event period.


Event Period：From September 1, 2023 to December 31, 2023

How to score points：

Borrowing one book gives you 1 point. Borrowing two books gives you 2 points, and so on, with no limit on the number of books you can borrow. (Renewing a book does not count for points.)

Swiping your card to enter the library gives you 1 point, with no limit on the number of times you can enter. (Leaving the library does not count for points.)






Get even more rewards for your points!





Top Achievers Bonus: Top achievers of the month will be rewarded with an extra 30 points for both book borrowing and card swipe activity.

Runner-Up Bonus: The month"s second highest book borrowers and card swipeers will receive an additional 20 bonus points.

Third Place Bonus: The month"s second highest book borrowers and card swipeers will receive an additional 10 bonus points.






How to Track Your Competition Progress







On the 15th of each month, the Library will announce the names and scores of the top 30 participants for that month. This will give everyone the chance to make a final push for victory. Stay tuned to the official Yuan Ze University Library Facebook fan page for updates.






Event Guidelines



The top achiever in monthly points accumulation will receive the Brand PLUS Gift of the month.

The runner-up and third-place achievers in monthly points accumulation will receive the Brand Gift of the month.

The participant with the highest total points accumulated from September to December will win the ultimate YZU Super Fan Gift.







 


Announcement：The list of winners for each month will be posted on the Library"s website and Facebook fan page on the 10th of the following month.



Notes：




The prizes for this promotion are physical items, and winners may not request changes, substitutions, transfers, or cash equivalents for the prizes awarded.

The Library reserves the right to make final changes, alterations, interpretations or cancellations to this promotion. Any relevant updates will be announced on the website and no separate notification will be given.
