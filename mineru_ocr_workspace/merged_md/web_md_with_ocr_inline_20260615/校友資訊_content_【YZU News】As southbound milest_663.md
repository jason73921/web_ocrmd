- **文章簡介**: Under the global COVID-19 epidemic, Taiwan has seen success in containing the virus, but it has also made it more difficult for foreign students to enter the country. However, Yuan Ze University regarded the crisis as a turning point and established Outreach Center at Kasetsart University in Thailand on February 18. Forty-eight guests including Lee Ying-yuan, representative of Taiwan to Thailand, Kampanat Pensupar, Vice President of Kasetsart University and YZU Vice President Ching-Ting Lee were invited to participate in the ceremony on line. It has become YZU southbound milestone.
