- **文章簡介**: National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST) and leading Japanese semiconductor company DISCO are jointly organizing the Winter 2026 Student Internship Program in Japan. Students from all universities are welcome to apply.

 



Number of Interns: Four students will be selected to intern in Tokyo.




Internship Period: January to February 2026.




Location: The internship will take place at DISCO"s offices in both Taipei and Tokyo.




Application Requirements: Applicants must be undergraduate or graduate students currently enrolled in an engineering, electrical engineering, or computer science-related program. They must also have a Japanese language proficiency level of N2 or higher, be proactive, be recommended by their university, and pass an interview with DISCO.




Financial Support: Selected interns will receive a round-trip economy class ticket to Japan and a partial living allowance in Japan from NTUST. DISCO will cover accommodation costs in Japan and provide a salary for the internship period in Taipei.




Note:


 If you are interested in applying, please first attend the online information session on September 5, 2025, at 2:30 PM. The link is: https://ppt.cc/fqZi9x.

 If you have any questions, please contact the Office of International Affairs at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology at (02) 2730-1118.
