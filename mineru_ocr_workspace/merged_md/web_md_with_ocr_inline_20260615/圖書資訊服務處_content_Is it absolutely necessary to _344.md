Please update your IE to version 8 for better performance.

Open IE, Tool→Internet Options→Connections tab→LAN settings.

Select "Use automatic configuration script" and fill out the address: "http://proxy.yzu.edu.tw/proxy.pac" as illustrated in the figure. Click “OK”.
