傳出６月１日起，桃園地區實施「供５停２」限水，

The water conditions are severe, drought does not rain, and water supply continues to be tight.
 
 It is said that starting from June 1st, Taoyuan area will water supply to be cut 2 days per week water restrictions,
 
 The reservoir is facing severe water conditions. In order to ensure the maximum utilization of water resources,
 
 During the epidemic prevention period, please wash your hands frequently with a small amount of water and turn off the faucet after use.
 
 Cherish water resources, please save water!
