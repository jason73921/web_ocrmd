# Yuan Ze University Scholarship Review Committee Regulations

November 13 1991, Ratified by the 1st Educational Committee Meeting of 1991 Academic Year.

August 14 1996, Amended at the 1st Student Affairs Guidance Committee Meeting of 1996 Academic Year.   
November 18 2002, Amended by the 1st Student Affairs Guidance Committee Meeting of 2002 Academic Year.   
April 9 2003, Amended by the 3rd Student Affair Committee Meeting of 2002 Academic Year.   
May 10 2018, Amended by the 5th Student Affair Committee Meeting of 2017 Academic Year.   
December 24 2025, Approved by the 1st Student Affairs Meeting of 2025 Academic Year.

Article 1 To enhance the review of student scholarships and aid, and to implement the objectives of various scholarships and grants, a "Scholarship Review Committee" is hereby established (hereinafter referred to as the Committee).

Article 2 The Committee members shall include the Dean of Student Affairs, the Vice Dean of Student Affairs (Chief Educator), the Dean of Academic Affairs, the Head of the Cashier Section, the Head of the Life Guidance Section, three faculty(advisor) representatives from each college, and three student representatives. The term of the members is one academic year, and rotating members may be appointed to handle time-sensitive applications.

Article 3 The Committee shall have one chairperson, who shall be the Dean of Student Affairs, overseeing all matters of the Committee. The execution of duties shall be managed by the Life Guidance Section.

Article 4 The responsibilities of the Committee are as follows:

1. Review applications and disbursements of scholarships, financial aid grants, and emergency relief funds.

2. Evaluation and review of special circumstances applications for student loans and scholarships.

3. Review scholarship regulations.

Article 5 Scholarships and grants donated by external individuals, organizations, or groups shall be coordinated by the Committee unless specific provisions in the donation statutes dictate otherwise.

Article 6 The Committee shall convene one to two times per semester as needed, called and chaired by the chairperson.

Article 7 The Committee's reviews will be conducted in accordance with the regulations of each scholarship and grant. In cases with multiple applicants, priority will be given to those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds and those who have not received other scholarships or grants.
