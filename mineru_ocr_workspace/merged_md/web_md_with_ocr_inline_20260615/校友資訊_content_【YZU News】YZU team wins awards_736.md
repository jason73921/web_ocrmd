Professor Shih-Ming Chang, from the Department of Information Communication of Yuan Ze University, led twenty-four students participated in “2021 VTuber Hackathon Contest” which is under the guidance of the Industrial Development Bureau of the Ministry of Economic Affairs, and co-organized by Digital Economy Industry Promotion Office, Taiwan VTuber Association and Japan Linked Brain Inc. YZU team received three awards: the Sanchuang Life Award, the Taiwan Virtual Celebrity Association Cash Reward of Student Group, and the Affinity Special Reward of General Group.
