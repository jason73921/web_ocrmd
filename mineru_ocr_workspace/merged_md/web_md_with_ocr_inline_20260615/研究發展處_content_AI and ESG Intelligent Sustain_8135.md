- **文章簡介**: Conference Summary

As the world approaches the critical moment of achieving Net-Zero Emissions and Sustainable Development, the intelligent integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) is spearheading a new wave of technological innovation and industrial transformation.

This "AI and ESG Intelligent Sustainability Cross-Disciplinary Integration Business Matching Conference" will consolidate and present cutting-edge technological outcomes from eight participating universities. Through this cross-institutional innovation showcase, the conference aims to demonstrate the academic sector"s R&D capacity and commercialization potential in the domain of Intelligent Sustainability.

To expedite Technology Transfer and Industrial Adoption, Chang Gung University (CGU) and Ming Chi University of Technology (MCUT) are co-hosting this event. We invite experts from both the academic and industrial spheres to congregate, providing a platform for direct exchange among corporate executives, scientific research institutions, and academic units.

We anticipate that this matching conference will facilitate concrete technology licensing and business opportunity alignment, accelerating the industrialization process of Intelligent Sustainability Solutions. Our goal is to establish a new paradigm of co-creation and mutual prosperity among the Industry, Government, and Academia (IGA) sectors, jointly building a greener future.
