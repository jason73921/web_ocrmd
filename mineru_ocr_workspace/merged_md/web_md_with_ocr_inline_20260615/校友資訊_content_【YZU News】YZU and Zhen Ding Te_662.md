- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20210416
- **文章簡介**: To enhance Taiwan’s PCB manufacturing technology and talent cultivation advantages, Yuan Zhe University and Zhen Ding Technology, Taiwan’s leading PCB vendor, jointly established the ” Zhen Ding Technology – Yuan Ze University Big Data Research Center”. On April 8, both sides signed the ” Agreement of Research Center Cooperation” and held the opening ceremony. Zhen Ding Technology Group will invest at least NT$30 million in 5 years, which will not only promote bilateral industry-university cooperation, but also bring the synergies to improve Taiwan’s PCB industry toward a higher level of smart manufacturing.
