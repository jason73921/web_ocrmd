- **文章簡介**: For me, a girl coming from Peru, studying in Taiwan is undoubtedly privileged and sometimes it still feels like a dream.
- **文章全文**: Taiwan is a beautiful country with breathtaking landscapes and wonderful, kind-hearted, and trustworthy people. I love Taiwan.

Here, it is so easy and convenient to go out and be in contact with nature. I chose YZU because the campus is so green and eco-friendly. You can always see different animals walking around and enjoying their natural habitat, hear the birds singing, and admire beautiful sunrises or the moon at night.

Besides that, the campus also has a swimming pool and a gym, which helps a lot when finding a work-life balance. I"m so happy to be here in Taiwan, I"ve had and I"m having the best time of my life and I"m, sure still have a lot of wonderful and new experiences to come and I"m really looking forwards to it.

Mayte Souza

Undergraduate Student

International Program for Informatics in Bachelor 

Yuan Ze University
