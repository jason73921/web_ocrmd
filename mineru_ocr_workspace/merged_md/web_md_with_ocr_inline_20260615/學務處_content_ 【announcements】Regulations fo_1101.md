- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: Description:

1 . Important Notice: Once completing the roommate selection procedures, students must check the status in the portal to ensure the grouping is complete. If students find out that the grouping has failed after the period for roommate selection, students are responsible for the outcome as no action will be taken to address the error.

2 .  Roommate Selection Date: May 16th to 23rd, 2017.

3 . Requirements: Undergraduates who have paid  NT$2,000 deposit (Foreign students who are still living in the dormitory in the next semester with stipend on accommodation fee and location students who are under special care are the exempted from the requirements.) 

4 . Procedures: Logon to yzu portal → Accommodation Application →Roommate Selction →follow the instructions given.

5 . 2~4 students are needed for roommate selection. Students are allowed to choose their own bed-spot, and SHSS will allocate the room. 

6 . Local students who wish to select foreign student (exchange student not included) as their roommate must register at SHSS first (Only 5 students staying in male and female dormitory each can apply, first-come, first-serve). Local students who do not register at SHSS will still be considered as invalid. 

7 . Students who do not form their own roommate will be allocated by SHSS, and any objections will not be entertained. 

8 . Student’s bed-space will be announced on June 9th ,2017 by SHSS. Student cannot request for changing bed-space once the announcement is made.



Office of Students Affairs of  Student Housing Service Section (SHSS)
