（106）元智教內字第0029號

日期：107.04.25

主旨：106學年度暑期課程預估開課，學生上網登記時間及方式。

說明：


暑期課程預估開課採上網登記，登記系統路徑：元智首頁／選課系統：


      https://isdna1.yzu.edu.tw/Cnstdsel/default.aspx


開放登記時間：107.04.30（星期一）AM9：00 ~107.05.04（星期五）PM4：00。

登記方式：請用你的學號（學號前+s，例：s901234）及電子郵件密碼來登入「暑期開課預估登記系統」。系統登入帳號及密碼相關問題請洽資服處。

注意事項：

本次登記僅供預估人數之用，正式開放選課科目與選課繳費日期，教務處將另行公告（約於5月底，請同學注意公告）。

同學可依自己的需求登記欲修科目，為使估算正確，每人登記以十八學分為上限。

預估課程以學生本系科目為主，包括通識科目與未修科目等。預估選課不限登記曾修不及格的科目。

暑修資格：本校在學學生可暑修（休學者除外），優先順序：應屆畢業生、不及格、轉學生、輔系、雙主修、停修者等。

開課標準：各科目以選修人數滿十八人始得開課為原則。

於公告確定開課日後，已註冊繳費者，一律不得申請退費。

本系統僅開放予大學部學生使用。


課務組/業務承辦人：楊惠敏(分機：2941）

Announcement of submission of online selection for summer courses and the period and procedures of sign-up (2018)

Announcement date: 2018/04/25

Subject: Submission of online selection for summer courses and the period and procedures of sign-up (2018)

Content：

1.Submission of online selection for summer courses must be signed up through on-line system. Please follow the path: YZU homepage/Course Selection System.

https://isdna1.yzu.edu.tw/Cnstdsel/default.aspx

2.Sign-up period: 2018/04/30(Mon.) 9 a.m.-2018/05/04 (Fri.) 4 p.m.

3.Sign-up: Use student ID number and email password to log in the “summer course online registration system” (Put an “s” in front of your student ID, example: s901234) and e-mail password. Please contact Office of Library and Information Services for farther questions.

4.Notification:

(1)This sign-up is only for estimation of the number for summer courses. The official date of course selection and the deadline of payment will be announced by the Office of Academic Affairs. (It will be announced at the end of May. Please refer to the announcement.)

(2)Students are free to register for any courses depending on personal needs. For having accurate estimation, each student can sign-up for no more than 18 credits.

(3)During this estimation period, credits from any courses will be calculated.

(4)Sign-up qualification: All YZU enrolled students (excluded students who apply for suspension). Order of priority: upcoming graduates, students who fail the course, transfer students, students who take minor/double major, and finally students who apply for withdrawing course.

(5)The standard of running a course: each course should at least have 18 students.

(6)While the course list is announced, students who have finished payment cannot apply for payment refund.

5.This system is only open to undergraduate students.

Curriculum Section, Office of Academic Affairs
