- **文章全文**: What is plagiarism? Under what circumstances do quoting someone else"s research content constitute copyright infringement? Is the code written in a collaborative project with classmates for a thesis considered mine, my classmates", or the teacher"s?Are there any issues with using Chat GPT to generate book reviews or research reports? Copyright is closely related to our lives and even serves as an important foundation for entrepreneurship. Therefore, it is necessary for us to understand the subject matter and scope of copyright protection. When I have a copyright, what can I do?

The seminar "Common Copyright Issues on Campus and the Impact of AI Applications" will use case studies to help participants understand the essence and rights of copyright and explore the influence of Chat GPT and the trend of AI applications on copyright protection. The topics covered include:


Overview of intellectual property rights

Types of works protected by copyright

Copyright rights and case studies

Impact of emerging technology applications on copyright
