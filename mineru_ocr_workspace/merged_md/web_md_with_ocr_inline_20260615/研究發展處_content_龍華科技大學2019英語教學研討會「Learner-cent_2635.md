# 2019 LHU DAFL Conference

Call for Papers

# “Learner-centred Language Teaching and Learning”

22nd November 2019 Taoyuan, Taiwan

This conference will be held on 22nd November 2019 at Lunghwa University of Science and Technology, Taoyuan, Taiwan. The conference is organised with the purpose of bringing together researchers, teachers, and scholar students to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, and research results about all aspects of language teaching and learning, curriculum design, information and education technology. The conference is intended to be a forum to expose and share current and future research work in the above areas, as well as the relationships among them.

We are now accepting abstract submissions and all submissions will be refereed. All the accepted abstracts and the associated presentation materials will be included in the official conference proceedings.

We look forward to seeing you in Taoyuan! The Organising Committee
