Confirm that all course grades have been submitted to the Office of Academic Affairs.



Time: January 29, 2026 (Thursday), 10:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.






Please be sure to read the instructions for initiating the graduation and departure process.



Confirm that all required procedures have been completed.



Location: Office of the Registrar, Room 1209R, 2nd Floor, Building 1






Notes:


When collecting the degree certificate, students must present their student ID card.
 If authorizing another person to collect it on their behalf, the authorized person must present the applicant’s identification document for verification.

Students who have applied for a minor or double major but have not completed the required credits must submit an application to withdraw from the program no later than one month prior to collecting the degree certificate.

Students who do not collect their degree certificate during the above-mentioned time period may do so starting from January 29, 2026, Monday to Friday, 1:30 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. (excluding public holidays), by presenting their student ID card at the Office of the Registrar counter (Room 1209R, 2nd Floor, Building 1).
