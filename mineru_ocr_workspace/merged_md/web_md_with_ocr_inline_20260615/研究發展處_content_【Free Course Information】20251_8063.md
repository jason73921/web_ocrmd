Topic： Academic Ethics Issues to Be Aware of When Using Generative AI Tools

Date： Tuesday, November 11, 2025, 13:00–15:00

Location： Microsoft Teams Online Meeting Room

Speaker：Assistant Professor Hsuan-An Pan, Department of Adult and Continuing Education, National Chung Cheng University

Description：This lecture will approach the topic from the perspectives of academic ethics and research integrity, engaging faculty and students in a discussion on ethical issues arising from the use of generative AI tools. Topics include potential ethical concerns associated with generative AI, examples of how AI can assist in research processes as well as violate academic ethics, and key competencies and recommendations for using generative AI in academic research.

Capacity:：100 participants. Registration will close once full. Confirmation emails will be sent two days before the event.
