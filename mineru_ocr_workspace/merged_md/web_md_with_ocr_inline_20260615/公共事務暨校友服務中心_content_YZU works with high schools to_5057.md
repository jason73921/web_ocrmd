- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University"s Department of Industrial Engineering and Management hold technology workshop of second semester with high schools from March 3 to May 26. The workshop is also one of the projects as the flexible study time of high school and the collaboration with university promoting by the K-12 Education Administration in Ministry of Education. This semester, Professor Chin Wei Chou, the director Shih-Yao Chou of National Yilan High School, Teacher Xiang Yu Lai, and the director Xiang Li of National Lan-Yang Girls" Senior High School jointly arranged the remoting teaching of 3D Design Course. The course also invited graduate students from Human Factors and Design Laboratory and university students of Dep. Industrial Engineering and Management to serve as teaching assistants.
