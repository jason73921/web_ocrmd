Subject: Post-earthquake restoration of student dormitories

Time: From 2024/4/10 8:00~17:00

Description:

1.Due to the earthquake on 113/4/3, the cement on walls and ceilings is cracked or collapsed in many places. In order to maintain the safety of the residents, renovation experts will come to repair it.

2.Students living in dormitories please check the status of your room. If any damage is found, please fill in the Portal Customer Services before April 11.

3.There might be loud noise during the construction process.

4.Normal use will be resumed immediately after the maintenance.

5.We apologize for any inconvenience!

 

主旨：學生宿舍地震後修復工程

施工日期：2024/4/10起 8:00~17:00

說明：

1.因113/4/3地震造成各處牆面及天花板水泥裂開、崩落，為維護居住安全，將由專業廠商進行修復。

2.請同學檢查寢室狀況，如發現災損情形，請於4/11前填報Portal顧客服務系統。

3.施工期間易有噪音產生。

4.維修完畢將立即恢復正常使用。

5.造成不便，敬請見諒！

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
