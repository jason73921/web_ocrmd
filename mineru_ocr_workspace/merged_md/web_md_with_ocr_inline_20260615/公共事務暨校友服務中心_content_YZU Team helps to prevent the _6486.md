- **文章簡介**: The College of Management of Yuan Ze University grouped a team for the first time to participate in the Global Youth Action of the Youth Development Administration（YDA）of the Ministry of Education. YZU team, guided by teacher Yu-te Liu, won the Best Action Excellence Award with a prize of NTD 140,000 and an additional action reward of NTD 300,000 for outstanding performance. The team "Seeking appropriate amount of food" is formed by Yi-fan Chen, Xin-Yu Chang, and Yi-Xuan Kuo. With the goal of "SDGs 12 Responsible Consumption and Production", the team has prevented too much leftover food from the life circle of Yuan Ze University and helped students choose the appropriate amount of food for themselves to reduce the waste.
