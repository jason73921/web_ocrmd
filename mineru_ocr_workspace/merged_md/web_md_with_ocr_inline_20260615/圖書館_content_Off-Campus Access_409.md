- **文章簡介**: Off-Campus Access to Online Resources
To assist faculty, staff and students by making it possible to search YZU Library E-Resources without having to connect to the YZU campus network.
[Note that on-campus access does not require any user authentication.]
Please download and install the YZU SSL VPN first.


GPP (PC version)
Please download and install the connection software first. For details, please refer to the GPP

IPsec VPN (Mobile version)
Mobile devices such as mobile phones and tablets, For details, please refer to the IPsec VPN


Please note:


Due to the campus licensing restraints, use of library e-resources is restricted to current YZU students, faculty and staff for the purpose of academic research and teaching.

Please adhere to copyright regulations when using library resources. Usage of these resources is governed by copyright law and individual license agreements. Systematic downloading, photocopying, online-distributing, or retaining substantial portions of information is prohibited. Violators will face legal consequences with the library access rights revoked.

In case of problems occurred with Off-Campus Access, you may report your problems to the Library References (ext. 2322) or email at library@saturn.yzu.edu.tw.

(The AutoRPA off-campus access ends in December 2021.)
