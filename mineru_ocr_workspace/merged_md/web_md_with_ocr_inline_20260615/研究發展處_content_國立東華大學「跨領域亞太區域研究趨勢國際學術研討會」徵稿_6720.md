# 國際學術研討會

## 徵 稿 啟 事

## 一、簡介

亞太區域研究博士班於 2019 年于東華大學設立，來自世界各國的研究生與教師，在此進行全面性的綜合教學研究。2024年，本博士班規劃辦理「跨領域亞太區域研究趨勢與展望」國際學術研討會。本次國際研討會旨為亞太區域人文及社會科學領域提供更多深入研究發展的機會，立足多元的學識養成背景，探討在區域內各國之間經濟發展、環境狀況、移民勞動、社會及文化變遷等範圍內的各種新興課題，乃至促進跨國、國文化的比較分析，從而提升推動亞太區域國家的跨學科交流。

與此同時，本次國際研討會的舉辦，將邀請來自世界各地研究亞太相關議題的學者參與。立基於花蓮的太平洋左岸地位，搭配其所具備的豐富原住民族、多元族群社會特色，建立獨特的學術交流平台，以促進花蓮參與亞太區域發展議題角色的討論和分析，為往後各項跨國區域合作累積能量和資源。

# International Conference on Cross-Disciplinary Research: Industry, Tourism, and Regional Studies in Asia-Pacific Region

## Introduction

The Ph.D. program in Asia-Pacific Regional Studies was initiated in 2019, involving Ph.D. students and professors from all over the world to create a comprehensive and integrative research institutes in Eastern Taiwan. In 2024, the program plans to host the “International Conference on Cross-Disciplinary Research”. This conference aims to provide opportunities to foster in-depth research development in the humanities and social sciences related to the Asia-Pacific region. Drawing from diverse academic backgrounds in social sciences, researchers will dig into various emerging issues such as economic development, environmental situations, migrant labors, and social as well as cultural changes among countries in the region. Moreover, the conference seeks to facilitate crossnational and cross-cultural comparative analyses and promote cross-disciplinary exchanges among countries in the Asia-Pacific region.

Date: November 21-22 (Thursday-Friday), 2024.

Venue: Humanities and Social Sciences Building, National Dong Hwa University, Hualien, Taiwan

Organizers: The Ph.D. Program in Asia-Pacific Regional Studies, College of Humanities and Social Sciences, National Dong Hwa University
