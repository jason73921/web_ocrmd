- **文章簡介**: To promote talent exchange and research collaboration between Taiwan and Canada, the National Applied Research Laboratories (NARLabs) and Mitacs Canada are jointly organizing the 2026 Globalink Research Internship (GRI). Applications will open in August 2025. Selected candidates will have the opportunity to undertake a 12-week research internship in Canada between May and October 2026.



Application Period: Early August to September 2025 (subject to the official announcement on the Mitacs website)




Application Method: Submit all required documents via the Mitacs website




Fields of Study: Open to all disciplines




Internship Duration: Approximately 3 months (12 weeks) between May and October 2026 at a Canadian research institution




Eligibility: Applicants must be Republic of China (Taiwan) nationals currently enrolled as undergraduate students in their third year or above (including students moving from second to third year) at a university or college in Taiwan. Applicants must maintain full-time student status during both the application and internship periods.




Application Requirements:



Academic transcript with a CGPA of 3.3 or above




Proof of English proficiency:



TOEFL iBT ≥ 79 or ITP ≥ 550




IELTS (Academic) ≥ 6.0




TOEIC ≥ 785







Letter of recommendation from a professor




Applicants who have previously received this internship award are not eligible to reapply






Funding: Up to CAD 12,000, covering living expenses, accommodation, insurance, training, and administrative costs. Taxes will be deducted according to the policies of the host institution.




Additional Support from NSTC (Taiwan’s National Science and Technology Council): A supplementary internship scholarship of NTD 30,000 per person




For more details, please visit the official website of the Taiwan-Canada Bilateral Talent Exchange Program hosted by NARLabs: https://www.niar.org.tw/xmdoc/cont?xsmsid=0I148623268432243722&sid=0O015409714272524767&r=2019066453
