- **文章簡介**: The ride was a crazy experience with my friends from YZU. I didn’t expect that riding a motorcycle in Taiwan could be this fun.
- **文章全文**: It left a good impression in my memory worth remembering. We started our journey from Neili and passed through Yingge, Bitan, and the mountain trails of Beiyi Road near Hsuehshan Tunnel. The scenic beauty of the entire trip was extraordinary. Our final destination, was a visit to Lanyang Museum and Waiao Beach in Touchen, Yilan. For those who love to ride a motorcycle and go on road trips, I would definitely recommend this route when you are in Taiwan. This motorcycle trip for sure will help release your stress from studies and work!!

Dipta Divakara Pius Purwadaria, Information Communications, Master’s degree
