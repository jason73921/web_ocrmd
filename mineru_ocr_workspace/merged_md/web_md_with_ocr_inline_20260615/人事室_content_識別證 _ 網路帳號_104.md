一、如何申請學術網路電子郵遞帳號？遺失帳號及密碼如何處理？

承辦人：林雯靖（分機：2039）

申請者請填寫  元智大學電子郵遞帳號申請表（另開新視窗） 。 如遺失帳號或密碼，可聯絡人事室承辦人查詢。

English

Contact Person: Ms. Wen-Ching Lin (Ext. 2039)

Applicants are required to complete the  Yuan Ze University Academic Email Account Application Form (opens in a new window) . In case of a forgotten account or password, please contact the responsible staff at the Personnel Office.




二、遺失職員證，如何申請補發？

承辦人：林雯靖（分機：2039）

請先至總務處服務台繳納工本費新台幣 200 元，並繳交二吋證件照一張至人事室申請補發。處理時間約 7 個工作天。

English

Contact Person: Ms. Wen-Ching Lin (Ext. 2039)

Please proceed to the Service Desk of the Office of General Affairs to pay a processing fee of NT$200. Then submit one 2-inch ID photo and the completed application form to the Personnel Office to request a replacement. The processing time is approximately 7 working days.
