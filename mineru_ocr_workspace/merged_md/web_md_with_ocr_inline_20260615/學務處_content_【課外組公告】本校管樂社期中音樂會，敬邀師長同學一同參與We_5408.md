本次音樂會將呈現管樂社成員們的努力與成果，透過一首首動人的樂章，希望為您帶來一個美好的夜晚。我們真心期待與各位師長、同學在音樂會現場相聚，共同度過這段難忘的時光。
您的到來將為我們的演出增添光彩，也讓我們更加感受到來自全校的鼓勵與支持。歡迎大家踴躍參加！
感謝您們的支持，期待在音樂會現場見到您！

元智大學管樂社 敬上


Dear professors and all YZU students:
Greetings!
We are members of YZU wind band. We sincerely invite you all to participate in our Midterm Concert and enjoy this enthusiastic , touching, and funny activity together.
Theme: 113th Academic Year Midterm Concert  
Date: October 23, 2024 (Wed)
Time: Admission at 19:30p.m, begin at 20:00p.m.  
Venue:Yu-Ziang Hall (有庠廳）, Building 6
This concert will showcase the hard work and achievements of our club members. Through a series of beautiful melodies, we will hope to bring you a wonderful and memorable night.
Your presence will not only add more brilliance and energy to our performance but will also let us feel the encouragement and support from the entire university. We welcome everyone to join us!
We look forward to seeing you at the concert and experiencing this splendid musical activity together!
Sincerely,  
YZU Windband
