Have you ever been curious about the mysterious quantum world?

Are you interested in how quantum technology is transforming the future of our lives and industries?

To deepen students’ understanding and practical application of quantum mechanics, the Office of Academic Affairs is organizing a two-part workshop series.

These sessions will explore the intersection of quantum science and innovative technologies—from theory to real-world practice—offering a comprehensive and interdisciplinary learning experience.
