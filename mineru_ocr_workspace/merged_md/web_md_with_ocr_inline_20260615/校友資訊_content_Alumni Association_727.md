Alumni Association of Yuan Ze University

In order to connect with alumni, the "Alumni Association of Yuan Ze University" was established in 1999 to assist alumni to grow and create the peak of their careers, promote YZU with the success of alumni.

 

Department Alumni Association

Alumni are the most precious asset of the alma mater, the most important social resource, and the greatest support for the development of the university. In order to provide a platform for alumni, teachers, and students in the school to interact, various departments have established departmental alumni associations.

 

Regional Alumni Associations

Yuan Ze alumni are located all over the world. Through the enthusiastic participation of alumni in local organizations, a global alumni network has been established. These associations aim to deepen local connections, strengthen ties with the alma mater, and contribute to social progress.

 

Mainland China Alumni Association

To strengthen alumni relations, the "Yuan Ze University Mainland China Alumni Association" was officially established in Xiamen, Fujian on June 17, 2023. Professor Ke Ranxuan of Jimei University, Xiamen, was elected as the president of the association. The goal is to unite alumni strength and foster a sense of community among alumni in Mainland China.

 

Overseas Alumni Association

With alumni around the world and an international learning environment that attracts many foreign students to Taiwan, Yuan Ze University is actively planning to establish overseas alumni associations to maintain friendships and global alumni connections.

 

Business Administration Elite Association

In order to provide a platform for management elites to communicate, the "Business Administration Elite Association" was established in July 2000. Members of the association include enterprises, finance, military, public education - people from all (different) walks of life.

 

EMBA Alumni Association

Yuan Ze University’s EMBA has cultivated many outstanding alumni. Therefore, the "EMBA Alumni Association" was established to connect the friendship between alumni and students in each session.

 

Yuan Ze University Elite Alumni Club

In March 2018, President Chih-Yang Wu invited several outstanding alumni for a dinner gathering. Subsequently, more alumni took turns hosting similar events. To strengthen the bond among elite alumni, a meeting was held in July 2019 to discuss future plans and draft an annual agenda.Currently, the club has about 30 members and holds gatherings every 3–4 months. Activities include keynote sharing, seminars, company visits, and cultural exchanges. The club serves as a platform for academic-industry collaboration and alumni engagement, encouraging members to give back to their alma mater while advancing their own careers and promoting industry progress and cooperation.

 

Yuan Ze University Doctor of Management Association, Republic of China

This association is a legally established, non-profit social organization dedicated to the public good. Its purpose is to promote applied research in management studies, encourage collaboration and exchange among industry, government, academia, and research institutions, support the development of its alma mater, foster alumni relationships, enhance interaction, offer mutual support, mentor the next generation, serve society, and benefit the public.
