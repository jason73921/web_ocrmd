## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 1845
- **文章標題**: Turnitin password setting update and AI detection for Teacher Edition closed on 31 December
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/tw/20231201-2
- **文章簡介**: The Turnitin Originality Checking System has undergone two information changes and updates:

1. Update password setting:

When users forget their password (password reset), the process of entering the last name has been removed and only the email address is required;

In addition, the length of the password combination has been changed to 12 digits, including a number, upper and lower case letters and special symbols.

 

2. AI detection for Teacher Edition will be disabled on 12/31

The English AI Writing Detection trial in Turnitin Teacher"s Edition will end on 12/31/2023, when Teacher"s Edition users will no longer be able to see the English AI Writing Detection feature on the report screen, and will need to subscribe to this feature if they need to use it after 2024. (No additional purchase required for Yuan Ze University)
