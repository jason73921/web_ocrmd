The wait is over! Starting from Academic Year 115 (2026), Yuan Ze University is officially launching the " University Bachelor " (校學士)!

✨ What is a University Bachelor ?

In short: Break free from departmental restrictions and curate your own curriculum to build a personalized degree.

The program utilizes Modular Learning + Cross-disciplinary Programs + Self-directed Learning + Dual-Mentor System. It allows you to design the learning path that best fits your interests, goals, and career aspirations.

⭐ Four Core Pillars (Choose your path!)

By completing a set percentage of courses in one of these high-demand fields, your diploma will officially feature your specialized track, making your resume stand out!



🎯 Artificial Intelligence (AI)




🎯 Semiconductors




🎯 Innovation & Creation (Entrepreneurship, Product/Service Development)




🎯 Sustainability (Green Energy, ESG, Social Practice)




⚡ Why join? The "3-Second" Summary:



✔ Ultra-flexible Course Selection: No longer tied to a single department.




✔ Seamless Cross-college Access: System-guaranteed opportunities to learn across fields.




✔ Dual-Mentor System: One mentor for professional expertise, one for career planning.




✔ Credits for Real-world Experience: Self-directed learning, internships, and senior projects all count toward your degree.




✔ Enhanced Degree: Your diploma highlights your specialized expertise for future employers.




👥 Who is this for?



Students eager for cross-disciplinary learning.




Those looking to pivot into AI, Semiconductors, Sustainability, or Innovation.




Students who want to switch tracks or didn"t find their "fit" in their original major.




Anyone who wants to bridge the gap between different fields of knowledge.




Proactive learners wanting to get a head start on their career.





📖 How is the degree structured? (Simplified)

You still need 128 credits to graduate, but the allocation is much more flexible:



University/College Core Requirements: 31 credits




Free Electives (Including original major): 30–40 credits




Program Credits (Cross-disciplinary + Specialization): 40–50 credits




Self-directed/Internships/Projects/Study Abroad: 15–25 credits





Note: All courses are drawn from existing programs, ensuring smooth registration and course availability.



📅 When does it start?

📌 Fully launching in Academic Year 115 (2026)!



Incoming Freshmen: Automatically eligible.




Current Students: May apply to join according to university regulations.




Modular Course Tracks: To be released progressively.




Dual-Mentor System: Officially goes live.
