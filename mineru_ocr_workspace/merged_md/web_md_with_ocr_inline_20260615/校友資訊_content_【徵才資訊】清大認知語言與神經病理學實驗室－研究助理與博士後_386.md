[徵才] 清華大學楊梵孛教授 認知語言與神經病理學實驗室 徵博士後研究員

應徵資格：擁有電機、資工、醫學工程、生科或心理相關科系博士學位，並符合下列的條件：

(1) 熟悉 Matlab 者將優先考慮

(2) 需有發表過學術論文

(3) 具 machine learning 及 signal processing 經驗尤佳

(4) 對醫學影像處理、臨床語言認知研究及核磁共振(fMRI)實驗有興趣

工作內容：為執行甫通過之國家衛生院多年期計畫，博士後研究員必須協助發展以及整合醫學影像分析技術，研究阿茲海默、認知障礙和老化人口之語言和記憶障礙，具體內容如下：

(1) 進行多種模式大腦之影像處理(Analysis of multimodal imaging data)

(2) 在新竹及台北執行各種行為及磁振造影實驗

(3) 協助研究計畫撰寫、發表學術論文

工作薪資：比照國科會待遇

有意願者，請將：

個人CV、自我介紹及興趣（請包含應徵理由）、發表過論文與兩份推薦信。

寄至 mstsai@mx.nthu.edu.tw，並註明連絡信箱與電話，

聯 絡 人 蔡小姐

聯絡電話 03-5742706

【其他備註】

1.實驗室簡介：

本實驗室研究方向為利用多種模式核磁共振影像處理（fMRI, DTI, fcMRI）及MEG 探討認知語言與大腦的關係，以及了解疾病、老化或腦損等因素，

如何影響人類大腦認知網絡。以醫學影像結果建立能解釋神經病理之認知科學理論，並供神經內外科和復建科設計療癒方法之參考。研究主題亦涵蓋健康人雙語能力及老化失智人口記憶力，

我們的研究對象包括正常人、腦傷病患、中風病患、阿茲海默症病人、帕金森氏症病人、小兒麻痺症患者、精神分裂症患者等。

詳細研究內容請於實驗室網頁查詢：

https://sites.google.com/site/neuroling504/home。

2.擁有國內外跨領域合作研究對象以及臨床資源，博士後研究員有機會與國外一流學府共同研究或相互拜訪。

合作對象：

國外：University of California San Francisco (radiology and biomedical engineering)、UC Berkley 、 San Francisco VA Medical Center,

University of Texas, Dallas 、University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center

日本國立長壽醫療研究中心、新加坡南洋理工大學

國內：台大醫院、 長庚醫院、台大醫院新竹分院

校內：清大資工系

本實驗室國內MRI以及豐富病患資源來自台大醫院總院與新竹分院、以及林口長庚醫院，博士後研究員必需習得跨院和跨領域的研究和溝通能力。

3. 楊教授畢業於加州大學柏克萊分校專長為計算語言學，於加州大學舊金山醫學院從事博士後研究臨床神經科學，作風美式，學術認真，研究論文多篇發表於知名期刊; 研究室成員領域多元，包含外語系所、語言所、生科院、資工所、臨床心理系，可以習得跨領域研究觀點與方法。

Job Title: Postdoctoral Position in Language and Cognitive Neurology Lab Organization: National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan

Position: A postdoctoral position is available in the Language and Cognitive Neurology Laboratory (directed by Fan-Pei Gloria Yang, Ph.D.). Applicants should possess a Ph.D. degree in medicine, engineering, life science, psychology, or a related field.

Expertise and qualifications: To implement the approved 3-year NHRI grant,   
the postdoctoral researcher has to assist in development and integration of   
biomedical engineering techniques for application of research on the linguistic   
and memory deficits in AD, MCI and aging populations:   
(1) Experience in Matlab, programming, machine learning, and signal   
processing is a plus   
(2) Academic publication experience is highly valued   
(3) Interested in medical image processing, language and cognition, and   
clinical neuroscience Our lab investigates general cognitive functions and their relationships with language. Research topics include reasoning, metaphor, imagery, aphasia and developmental language disorders. We mainly use imaging and   
neurophysiological techniques such as fMRI, EEG, DTI and MEG to conduct our research. The populations of interest include healthy adults and children, patients with stroke, cerebral palsy, traumatic brain injury and mild cognitive impairment, Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, schizophrenics, etc. Our lab has been in frequent cooperation with top research institutes including University of California, San Francisco, University of California, Berkley, National Taiwan University Hospital and Chang Gung Memorial Hospital. For further information, please visit our website:   
https://sites.google.com/site/neuroling504/home

The successful candidate will be expected to process multimodal brain imaging data, conduct behavioral and MRI experiments at different locations, and assist in applying funding, managing research projects, as well as writing manuscripts. Compensation is equivalent to National Science Council postdoctoral fellowship salary.

How to apply: Interested individuals should send their current CV, statement of research, reprint of general papers, two recommendation letters, and any other relevant documents to Dr. Yang at fpyang@mx.nthu.edu.tw. Only selected candidates will be contacted for interviews.
