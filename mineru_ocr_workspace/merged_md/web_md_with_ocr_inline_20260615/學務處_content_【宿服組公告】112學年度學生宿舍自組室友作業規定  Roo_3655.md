主旨：公告112學年度學生宿舍自組室友作業規定。 

說明：

一、重要提醒：

1. 系統自組前請相互討論床位選擇，選填床位重複，自組將不成功。

2. 完成自組室友作業後，請務必在系統開放期限內再上portal相互確認成功與否。

3. 宿服組6月15日公告分配床位，床位排定即產生住宿相關費用，如欲放棄請記得填寫放棄床位切結書並繳交至宿服組辦公室，期限延至６月５日止，逾期不再受理。

4. 優先住宿申請下舖需求者，男生請選填Ａ床，女生請選填Ｂ床。

二、自組室友期間：112年6月1日至6月5日。

三、自組室友資格：已繳交住宿同意書者。

四、自組室友方式：進 個人portal  ➔  住宿申請  ➔  自組室友  ➔  依指示完成作業。

五、境外生與本國生自組住宿棟別將在男女一舍，請至宿服組登記。

六、自組室友必須由2～4人組成，床位由學生自選，寢室房間一律由宿服組統一分配。

七、未自組室友之同學由宿服組統一分配，學生不得異議。　

 

 

Roommate Selection Procedures of 112 (2023-2024) Academic Year


Important Notice


Once completing the roommate selection procedures, students must check the status in the portal to ensure the grouping is complete.

There will be dormitory fee once the bed is arranged; if you wish to give up the bed please go Student Housing Service Section before June 5th so that it will not affect your right.




Roommate Selection Date: June 1st - 5th, 2023

Procedures: Login to yzu portal ➔ Accommodation Application ➔ Roommate Selection ➔ follow the instructions given.

2~4 students are needed for roommate selection. Students are allowed to choose their own bed-space, and Student Housing Service Section will allocate the room.

International and domestic students will be assigned to buildings in the 1st male and female dormitories. Please go to the Student Housing Service Section to register.

For students those who do not choose their roommate, your roommate will be assigned randomly. (No objections allowed)

Student’s bed-space will be announced on June 15th, 2023 by Student Housing Service Section.
