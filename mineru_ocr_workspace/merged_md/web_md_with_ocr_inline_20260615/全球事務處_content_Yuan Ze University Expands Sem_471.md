- **文章簡介**: From January 12 to 18, 2025, Yuan Ze University Vice President Dr. Tze Chi Hsu visited Vietnam to promote semiconductor collaborations with Vietnamese universities and attended the opening ceremony of the Da Nang International Financial Center.

On January 16, Dr. Hsu, representing Yuan Ze University, exchanged international cooperation agreements after a speech by Vietnam’s Vice Prime Minister. He visited partner universities, including Thai Nguyen University, Phenikaa University, Hue University, Da Nang University and the Da Nang University of Economics, discussing potential collaborations in dual-degree programs and research projects.

This visit not only strengthens Yuan Ze University"s academic reputation but also supports the growth of Vietnam’s semiconductor talents education and international financial development. Yuan Ze University remains committed to fostering global partnerships for technological and economic advancement in the region.
