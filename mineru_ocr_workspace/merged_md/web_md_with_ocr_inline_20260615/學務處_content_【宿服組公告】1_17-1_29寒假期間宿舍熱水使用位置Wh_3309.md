各位住宿生好/Dear dormitory residents

寒假期間（2023/1/17～2023/1/29）請至各樓層電熱水器位置使用熱水。

During the winter vacation (2023/1/17～2023/1/29), please go to the shower room with the electric water heater on each floor to use hot water.

 



 

☆熱水器注意事項Precautions for water heater usage

 

1.熱水器連續使用下，需要時間重新製熱，同學使用前可先查看熱水器上的溫度表，評估自己可接受的溫度後再使用，避免水溫不足影響品質。

When the water heaters are used continuously, it takes time for them to reheat. Please check the temperature gauge on the water heaters and evaluate the acceptable temperature before using to avoid insufficient hot water.

 

2.熱水器儲水容量有限，若一次性用水量過大，將影響後續人員之使用，請節約用水，謹慎使用。

The water heaters have limited water storage capacity. If each person consumes too much water, subsequent people’s usage will be affected. Please save water and use it cautiously.

 

 

學生事務處宿舍服務組 Student Housing Service Section
