## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章標題**: 【Announcement】The information about YZU APP
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/is/index.php/tw/112052401
- **文章簡介**: Thank you for your usage of the YZU APP. 
We have noticed that some users on Android versions 12 and above have reported issues with the APP crashing, causing inconvenience. We sincerely apologize for this.
We have been actively tracking and analyzing this issue, and we would like to provide some solutions in this announcement to help you address the crashing issue on the latest versions:
- **文章全文**: Solution 1:
Clear Cache: In some cases, cached data can cause the APP to crash. You can try clearing the APP"s cache by going to "Settings" > "APPs" > "Our APP" > "Storage" on your device, and then clicking on "Clear Cache" (the exact steps may vary depending on your device manufacturer).

Solution 2:
Reinstall the APP: If clearing the cache doesn"t work, please uninstall the APP and then download and reinstall the latest version of our APP from the following link: https://u.yzu.edu.tw/nx7XL0Pr5 .

If you need, you can visit Office 5201 in Building 5, our staff will be available to assist you.

Notice: To use Version 2.2.00 of the APP to ensure a stable experience.

We apologize for any inconvenience caused.
For further information, please contact the Office of Library and Information Services at extension 3110. Thank you.
