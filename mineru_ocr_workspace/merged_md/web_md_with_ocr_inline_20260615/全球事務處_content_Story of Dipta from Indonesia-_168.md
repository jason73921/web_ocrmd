- **文章全文**: This place will kill all of your stress for sure. Taipei zoo is a must-place to go while you staying in Taiwan, not only because of the natural environment of the zoo designed like native habitat but also because we can still interact closer with the animals inside the zoo. You can find special animals like Giant Panda, Red Panda, and some local animals inside this place. Though it really took some time to explore and made me stop several times, I really enjoyed my time there.
