The annual Bread Festival is here !! ヾ(≧▽≦*)o

This event is on 10/23 (Wednesday) ⏰ 18:30~21:30

Whether you want to express your love or feel for someone, this is it, this is your chance! Let us help you express your feelings.

This year"s limited bread flavors include 🍞:

- Klim Bread: "You make my heart rise like Klim bread!"

- Black Eyed Beans Bread: "Our love is un-bean-lievable!"

- Matcha Red Bean Mochi Bread: "I’m matcha in love with you!"

- Conch Shell Bread: "You’re the shell of my heart!"

- Sweet Buns: "You’re the sweetest thing in my life!"

- Donuts: "I donut know what I’d do without you!"

Each type of bread is a limited edition exclusive to the Yuan-Ze Bread Festival. Meticulously customized, every bite will leave you craving more!

If you’re too shy to deliver the bread yourself, no worries! We offer a delivery service to help you share this sweetness. (¬‿¬)

You can also choose to pair it with our specially designed love cards to write down those words hidden in your heart. Once you"re done, drop it into the love box at the Male and Female Dormitory Reception, and we will deliver your heartfelt message to that special someone. 📮

Booth Locations:

- In front of Building 3

- Dormitory Family Mart

- next to the Second Floor Dormitory Canteen

If it rains, the booths will be moved to the Dormitory Family Mart, or next to the Second Floor Dormitory Canteen.

Love Card Submission Time & Location: 10/7 - 10/15, Male and Female Dormitory Reception
Tasting Times: 10/7 - 10/9
- 11:30 AM ~ 1:30 PM
- 4:30 PM ~ 6:30 PM
Pre-order Period: 10/7 - 10/15
- For every pre-order over 150 NT dollars, receive a beautiful commemorative gift: a limited edition canvas bag (only 300 available, while supplies last).

Additionally, for every bread purchased, we will donate 5 NT dollars to World Vision to support the "Child Sponsorship Program."
While conveying your feelings, you’re also sending hope to children around the world. Why not do both?

On the day of the event, there will also be an evening party, and we will randomly select 10 lucky winners from the attendees to receive our mystery gifts. (｡･∀･)ﾉﾞ
This opportunity allows you to express your feelings and spread love. Take that brave step!

Payment Methods:

- From 10/7 to 10/11, please make payments at the "Bread Tasting Booth."

- On 10/14 and 10/15, please make payments at the "Cafeteria, 2nd Floor."

Orders will be cancelled if payment is not made by the deadline. Commemorative gifts will be distributed based on payment timing.
