Description:



To support universities in enhancing the competencies required for teaching assistants in EMI courses, our EMI Resource Center will organize the aforementioned workshop. Guided by the principle of resource sharing, the workshop aims to strengthen the support provided to instructors and improve the effectiveness of EMI teaching.




Workshop Information: (1) Topic: Bridging the Gap: The EMI TA as a Mediator Between Professors and Students (2) Speaker: Assistant Professor Ching-Ying Lin, Department of Applied English, National Pingtung University (3) Format: Online session




Registration Information: (1) Registration Period: From now until March 19 (Thu), 2026, 12:00 PM (2) Eligibility: University and college students nationwide (3) Registration Link: https://emitlc.nchu.edu.tw/workshop/91




Please refer to the attached poster for further details.
