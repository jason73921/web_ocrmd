本校與義守大學合作辦理全英語國際創業營「Catch On, Start Up! 2022 Entrepreneurship Summer Camp」，敬請惠予公告周知，並鼓勵學生踴躍報名，請查照。

說明：

一、本校為教育部創新創業推動基地，歷年創新創業團隊及新創企業之培育成果豐碩，為促進在台學生國際交流及創造跨國創業機會，特與義守大學合作主辦旨揭創業營，並邀得經濟部中小企業處「亞灣新創園」及台灣服務科學學會協辦，活動海報及英文版資訊如附件。

二、本創業營開放全國大專校院外籍生及本國生參加，並邀請 多位創業領域講師及業師指導，與加速器及投資人面對面交流，歡迎對創業有興趣、已創業或準備創業的學生參加。

三、創業營相關資訊：

(一)日期：2022年8月29日（一）10時至8月31日（三）17 時。

(二)地點：本校第一校區（創新創業教育中心創夢工場）、 義守大學創客園區及南臺灣國際級創業聚落「亞灣新創園」。

(三)費用與補助：免費。提供創業營錄取者全程食宿，外縣 市另補助交通費。

(四)結業證書：全程參與者，授予結業證書。

(五)本創業營採全英語、實體進行，參加者須配合相關防疫措施，若受疫情影響，活動有所修改或取消，將另行公告通知。

四、報名資訊：

(一)報名期間：即日起至8月21日（日）止。

(二)報名連結：https://forms.gle/XzZSRibNr57nmueXA

五、聯繫窗口：

(一)本校創新創業教育中心盧小微副理，email： alison@nkust.edu.tw

(二)義守大學創新育成中心陳憶欣組員，email: cinxy369@isu.edu.tw

## Catch On, Start Up! 2022 Entrepreneurship Summer Camp Your Best Guide to Start up in Taiwan

## Objectives:

The number of interpersonal communications and encounters will significantly increase compared to the past two years as more countries lift lockdowns and fully reopen borders. This presents an opportunity to start up new businesses or make other changes to the world. During this 3-day camp, participants from different cultures and backgrounds will be encouraged to collaborate on unique entrepreneurial opportunities that are available in the current situation. They will receive a series of structural training activities to enhance their knowledge and skills to pitch and launch their own business, and the most important of all, to contribute to the creation of global startups in the future. It is particularly worth mentioning that the Startup Terrace Kaohsiung's involvement will also liven up the camp, raise diverse practical issues such as policy, governance, venture capital, etc., and help the camp participants to access government resources and advice, and a global talent pool.

Organizer: Ministry of Education

Implementer: Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship Education (CIEE) of National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology, The Innovation and Incubation Center of I-Shou University

Cooperation: Startup Terrace Kaohsiung. Southern Consortium, Service Science Society of Taiwan.

Date：August 29 to 31, 2022

Location：Kaohsiung City (Fi-Lab, First Campus, National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology. ISU Innovation Hub, I-Shou University. Startup Terrace Kaohsiung.)

Online Registration (from now to August 21, 2022): https://forms.gle/XzZSRibNr57nmueXA

Eligibility: For domestic and international college or university students in Taiwan (Bachelor, Master, PhD) who are interested in innovative entrepreneurship.

Admission Quota: Estimated 36 students (about 18 international students and 18 domestic students) will be admitted. 15 students are on the waiting list for admission additionally.

Participation Fee: Free. Full subsidy for accommodation and meals during the camp is offered (personal expense is not included), and the transportation allowance is provided to the students whose schools are not located in Kaohsiung City (The public transportation in Taiwan accepted only).
