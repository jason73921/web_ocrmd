- **文章簡介**: Dear Esteemed Partners,

Greetings from Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR), Malaysia.

We will be hosting the "International Young Leaders Conference" (IYLC) themed Mental Health and Community Resilience – a program that fosters multi-stakeholder idea sharing and crosscutting partnerships for the implementation of SDG 3 (Good Health and Wellbeing), SDG 10 (Reduced Inequality) and 17 (Partnership for the Goals) as well as to harness the role of higher learning institutions in the implementation of the SDG 13 and 10 especially community resilience in times of crises. This conference is also to provide a platform for students and youths to discuss and share ideas of enhancing community resilience in cities as well as adaptation solutions towards crises.

We would like to extend the invitation to your university to participate where you may recommend up to TEN (10) students in the relevant fields who may be interested to participate in this conference.

Programme: International Young Leaders Conference 2023 + Inbound Study Tour

Theme: Mental Health & Community Resilience

Date: 23 November 2023 to 30 November 2023 (8 Days 7 Nights - Inbound Study Tour + Conference)

Venue: Multipurpose Hall, KB Block, Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman Sungai Long Campus, Malaysia

Participant fee: USD 680

Targeted Participants: Students, Staff, Lecturers, Helping Professionals, NGOs in the Relevant Fields

Registration Link: https://forms.office.com/r/zxd83UWQkM

Registration Deadline: Friday, 17 November 2023

For more information or should you have any queries, please feel free to contact us via email ylc.sl@utar.edu.my.

Attached links are the invitation letter, programme flow and registration form for your perusal.

See you in Malaysia!

Warm Regards,
International Young Leaders Conference Committee 2023
Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman, Sg Long Campus
