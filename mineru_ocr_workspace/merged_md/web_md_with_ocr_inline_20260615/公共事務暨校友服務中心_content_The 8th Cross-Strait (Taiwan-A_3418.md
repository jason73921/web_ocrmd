- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University, Technology Industry Association of Taiwan and Anhui Association for Science and Technology co-hold the 8th Cross-Strait (Taiwan-Anhui ) Forum on Technology in Yuan Ze University on Nov.29. Over 150 cross-strait scholars and industrial experts participate in the main forum and four sub-forums which topics are AI & IOT, Science Education, Green Energy and Health Care, respectively.

Yaw-Geng Chau, Dean of YZU Electrical and Communication Engineering, said that it’s the first time to hold a forum for cross-strait communication on technological and academic fields after nine-in-one elections and it aims to provide academic-industrial exchanging platform on the four sub-forums mentioned earlier.
