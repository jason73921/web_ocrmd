- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University’s “Global Cultural Initiative” project promotes international education in Taoyuan City’s primary and junior high schools to facilitate citizen’s diplomacy. First working with Embassy of the Republic of Nicaragua in Taiwan in 2021, Ambassador Mirna Mariela Rivera Andino visited the University and signed a Memorandum of Understanding with President Jyh-Yang Wu. They promise to work together for Taiwan’s multicultural understanding and international education.
