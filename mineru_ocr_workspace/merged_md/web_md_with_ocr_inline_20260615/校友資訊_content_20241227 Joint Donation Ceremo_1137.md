- **文章簡介**: With the Lunar New Year approaching, the Yuan Ze University Alumni Association, in collaboration with Yuan Ze University’s Global Culture Navigation program and the Taoyuan City Vegetable Merchants Association, has launched the "Care for Underprivileged Groups and Aid for Impoverished Children" initiative.

On Friday, December 27, a joint donation ceremony was held at Heng An Nursing Home in Sanxia District, New Taipei City. During the event, essential supplies such as rice and vegetables were personally donated to children with rare diseases. The initiative aims to inspire others to join in this charitable effort, providing warmth and support to children with rare diseases and underprivileged groups, helping them celebrate a joyful and fulfilling Lunar New Year.
