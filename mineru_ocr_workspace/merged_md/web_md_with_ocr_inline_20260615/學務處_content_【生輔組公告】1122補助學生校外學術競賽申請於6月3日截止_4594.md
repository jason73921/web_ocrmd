## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 4594
- **文章標題**: 【生輔組公告】1122補助學生校外學術競賽申請於6月3日截止, The student off-campus academic competition grant application deadline is Jun 3th.
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/st/index.php/tw/?option=com_content&view=article&id=4594
- **文章簡介**: 主旨：學生參加校外學術競賽補助申請

一、申請時間：凡本校在校學生，即日起至113年6月3日（星期一）止。

二、申請程序：提交申請表、競賽報名表、辦法、作品影本或作品成果照片，經系所初審及學院複審通過後，送達學務處生輔組。 

三、補助項目：報名費、交通費、材料費、膳食費及住宿費。

四、補助金額：國內競賽５仟元至２萬元、國外競賽１萬5仟元至4萬元不等(採先申請先補助且實報實銷)。

五、申請表及相關資訊，請點此連結。

   承辦人：生輔組 簡麗芬小姐/分機2240

 

 1. Application Period: For all current students of our school, from now until Jun 3th 113 (Monday).

 2. Application Procedure: Submit the application form, competition registration form, guidelines, copies of works, or photographs of work results. After approval through initial departmental review and college-level review, deliver the documents to the Student Affairs Office.

 3. Grant Categories: Registration fees, transportation expenses, material costs, meal allowances, and accommodation expenses.

 4. For other related grant application forms and operational guidelines, please click this link.

     Contact Person: Ms. Li-fen Chien,Life Guidance Section ,Extension 2240.
