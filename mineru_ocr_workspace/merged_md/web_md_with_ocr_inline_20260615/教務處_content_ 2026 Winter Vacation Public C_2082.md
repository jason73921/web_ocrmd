Reservation period:

Winter vacation:  January 11~February 23, 2026

February 12~February 20, 2026is the Lunar New Year holiday, all classrooms are closed for borrowing.




Online reservation on Portal:

 December 18, 2025 from 12:00 noon, for faculties and staffs.

December 24, 2025 from 12:00 noon, for students and clubs.




During the winter vacation, R1115 is still reserved as a self-study classroom and cannot be borrowed. Self-study classroom opening hours: 08:10-21:30 every day; except for the Lunar New Year holiday (February 12~February 20, 2026), other holidays are open as usual.




Notes:

Classroom power supply and e-desk are turned on by the automatic scheduling system. Please complete the online reservation application process in advance to avoid being unable to use them immediately due to temporary borrowing.

Classrooms can be borrowed for up to 3 days a week. Long-term borrowing is not allowed.

If the borrowed classroom is locked, please contact the staffs of the General Affairs Office during working hours (relevant information: General Affairs Office website →Contact Us); at night or on holidays, please contact the Campus Security Squad (#2270, #2271).

In order to make full use of school facilities, please borrow classrooms according to actual needs; if there is any change in the borrowing date (time), please cancel as soon as possible or change the application online.





Curriculum Section, Office of Academic Affairs
