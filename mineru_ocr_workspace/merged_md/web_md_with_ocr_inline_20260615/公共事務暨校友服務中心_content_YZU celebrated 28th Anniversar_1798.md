 ICAN, meaning “I Can Do it,” is supported by Taipei Autism Welfare Foundation, selling steamed bread made by employees with autism. YZU hosts the Dough Rolling Contest in the event and invites all guests to work with ICAN staffs. YZU also coordinates the steamed bread charity sale for the children in need and social welfare organizations.
