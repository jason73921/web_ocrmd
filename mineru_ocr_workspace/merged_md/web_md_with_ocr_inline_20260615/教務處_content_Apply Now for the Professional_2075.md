- **文章簡介**: 📢 Attention, students! 

The Office of Academic Affairs is launching the ✨Professional Independent Learning Program✨. Please refer to the attached documents for the required forms. We welcome all students to apply! 

👉 Have questions? Email us at csyxxx@saturn.yzu.edu.tw or visit us at R1208 — we’ll be happy to assist you! 

Build your own learning blueprint in just 3 steps! 

🔸Take the “Introduction to Self-Directed Learning” course: 

Learn how to set learning goals and write a proposal to build a solid foundation for independent learning. 

🔸 Discuss with your advisor and carry out your plan: 

With your advisor’s support, you will gain learning outcomes through execution, reflection, and adjustment. 

🔸 Submit your results for review and earn credits: 

After completing your plan and passing the review, you may earn up to 3 elective credits, according to university regulations. 

Program Tracks 

🔹 Research Track: Conduct in-depth exploration in your discipline or apply professional knowledge to real-world issues or settings. 

🔹 Competition Track: Participate in academic or interdisciplinary competitions to strengthen expertise and innovation skills. 

🔹 Certification Track: Plan your path to earn professional certifications that boost your learning and career. 

📌 Reminders 

🔺 Credits earned through the Professional Independent Learning Program may count toward graduation requirements. The method and number of credits recognized depend on each department’s curriculum guidelines. 

🔺 Results shown on the transcript will be recorded as Pass/Fail, not as numerical grades. 

🔺 As this is the initial trial phase, please submit your proposal to the Office of Academic Affairs (R1208) first. After approval, it will be forwarded to your department for review. 

🔺 For more details, please visit: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/2016-01-14-06-58-46/2050-2025-11-04-12-02-08 

🫵 Your learning, defined by YOU! Take the first step toward your personalized learning journey today! 🚀
