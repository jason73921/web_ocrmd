- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/library/index.php/tw/is-202604161048
- **文章簡介**: The Library, in collaboration with the National Central Library, has launched the Thesis Quality Enhancement System to help graduating master’s and doctoral students check thesis similarity and improve academic quality. The system uses the National Central Library’s thesis database and is recommended to be used alongside Turnitin.

The service period is April 27 to August 31, 2026, and each student is allowed up to five submissions. Students can access the system through the thesis submission platform and upload their thesis in PDF format for analysis.

This system is for self-check purposes only. Final evaluation standards remain subject to each department’s regulations. Training sessions and user resources are also available.

1. Eligibility and Period


Eligibility: Graduating master’s and doctoral students in the current semester (Academic Year 2025, Semester 2)

Period: April 27 to August 31, 2026


2. How to Use

Students should log in to the University’s thesis submission system using their personal Portal account, select “Thesis Detection,” and upload the full thesis in PDF format. Reports can be viewed or downloaded online after processing.

3. Training Sessions


Student Sessions: April 29 (Wednesday) and May 13 (Wednesday), 15:00 (Registration deadline: April 27)



System Website: https://wass.ncl.edu.tw
