Location: Female 1st Dorm

Notice:

1.In order to maintain the quality of the elevators in the Female 1st Dorm, two elevators will be replaced this year.

2.During construction, only one elevator will be operational, and elevators on floors 2-4 will be temporarily unavailable.

3.Please be attentive to the construction personnel entering and exiting. Ensure that your room doors are locked when you leave.

4.Construction may generate some noise during this period, causing inconvenience to residents. We appreciate your understanding.

5.Normal usage will be restored immediately upon project completion.

6.We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your cooperation. Thank you!
