- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University once again demonstrated outstanding achievements in the 114 Academic Year Bilingual Learning Program for College Students (EMI Program), standing out in the highly competitive national selection process and showcasing the university’s strong commitment to bilingual education and internationalization. This year marked a new milestone, as three colleges received recognition from the Ministry of Education, highlighting Yuan Ze University’s sustained and growing impact on cultivating professionals with strong English proficiency and disciplinary expertise.

Among them, the College of Management was selected as a Benchmark Program in the field of Social Sciences. Through its internationally oriented curriculum design, close collaboration with industry partners, and effective cross-disciplinary talent development, the college emerged as a model of excellence within the field, demonstrating its solid foundation and achievements in nurturing globally competitive management professionals.

Meanwhile, both the College of Electrical and Communication Engineering and the College of Informatics were selected as Key Cultivation Programs in the field of Engineering and Applied Sciences. Over the years, the two colleges have integrated AI technologies, smart manufacturing, electrical engineering, and information and communication technologies with English-medium instruction and international collaboration models. Their long-term efforts to cultivate highly interdisciplinary and globally competitive technology professionals earned strong recognition from the program reviewers.

Yuan Ze University stated that these achievements symbolized the gradual fruition of its strategic efforts in bilingual and international education. Looking ahead, the university planned to continue strengthening curriculum development, promoting teaching innovation, expanding international partnerships, and enhancing faculty development. By creating a learning environment that integrated English proficiency, professional competence, and global mobility, Yuan Ze University aimed to cultivate more outstanding talents capable of thriving on the global stage and contributing to the advancement of higher education in Taiwan.
