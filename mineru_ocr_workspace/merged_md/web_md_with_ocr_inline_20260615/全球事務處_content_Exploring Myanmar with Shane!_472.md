In our enlightening interview with Shane, a freshman student from Myanmar currently studying EBBA, we delved into the fascinating trivia of Myanmar’s culture and geography.
- **文章全文**: Let’s uncover the highlights of our conversation:

Mountains of Myanmar: Shane bravely tackled our first question about Myanmar’s highest mountain. While he initially guessed Mount Popa, the correct answer is Hkakabo Razi, showcasing the diverse landscapes of Myanmar’s mountainous regions.

Lakes of Myanmar: Moving on to our second question, Shane faced a challenge identifying Myanmar’s largest lake. Although he guessed Inle Lake, the correct answer is Indawgyi Lake, highlighting the vast natural treasures scattered across Myanmar’s landscapes.

National Flower: Shane redeemed himself with our final question, correctly identifying the national flower of Myanmar as Paduak, demonstrating his knowledge of Myanmar’s rich botanical heritage.
