- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/ho/index.php/tw/febaruary-1-2024
- **文章簡介**: Qualifications:


Native-speaker or near-native-speaker proficiency in English

Master degree of TESOL or a related field

3 years of teaching experience at the university level


Responsibilities:


Teach 16 hours per week during regular semesters

Teach and prepare teaching materials for Freshmen English Summer Camp

Participate and support teaching-related activities in the center.


Applications files should include:


Curriculum vitae

Autobiography

Thesis or dissertation

Two reference letters, written within the last twelve months, one of which preferably by a direct supervisor in the current or previous appointment.

Certificate of Lectureship issued by the MOE in Taiwan (if applicable).

A copy of diploma of highest degree and transcripts

Candidate holding a graduation certificate from foreign country should have the certificate certified or attested by the Taiwan’s official or semi-official office in foreign nations.

A copy of passport.

Two inches photos for two.

DVD as teaching demo and other relevant teaching materials in previous positions.


Mail to:  International Language and Cultural Center,

Yuan Ze University, 135 Yuan Tung Road, Chungli 320.

Application deadline: 2023.11.20

The position is expected to begin on Febaruary 1, 2024.

Phone:886-3-4511725
