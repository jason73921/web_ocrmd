- **文章簡介**: Founded by the Far Eastern Group, Yuan Ze University focuses on the "five forces" -digitalization, cross-domain power, creativity, global mobility, and public participation- to develop students’ employment competition by promoting the internships in Far Eastern Group and the exchanges with top 100 prestigious universities.

A New Normal is coming in the post-epidemic era. Many economic activities and school curricula have been digital transformation, which requires innovative and interdisciplinary thinking. Yuan Ze University takes "cross-disciplinary digital learning" as the core curriculum to cultivate students’ creative thinking, and the abilities of solving social and industrial problems through practice education.
