- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/aa/index.php/tw/apply-for-change-of-major
- **文章簡介**: Application conditions and limitation






Any student determined to be in one of the following circumstances may not apply for a change of major, department, track or program of study:

1. has not completed two complete semesters.

2. is currently a fourth-year undergraduate student.

3. has withdrawn from the University.

4. Individuals who enrolled by school recommendation or screening test and enrollment application will not be restricted if they have submitted department transfer application from the second semester after registration and approved by the academic affairs council.

5. Without permission from the original local government, off-shore island students who enrolled by recommendation are not allowed to apply for department transfer.

Transfers to another department the limit:

For freshmen wishing to transfer to the department in the same field shall have the average score among all subjects in that academic year reaches 60 at least, or he/she will immediately be unqualified.

Two-year On-job Undergraduate Program student is stop apply for a change of major.






Application flow






1. Students who wish to file for a change of major, department, track or program of study shall do so in accordance with the time-table set forth in the University’s academic calendar.

2. Submits the overall transcript (including the grades and ranks of last semester.

3.  After the approval of the department , director, college dean, submit to the Office of Academic Affairs.

4. Students granted approval to change their major, department; track or program of study may not file for a change in or withdrawal of their application.






The approval transfer students related matters






1. Choose the courses according to the new department.

2. Credits obtained from courses taken at the student’s previous major, department, track or program of study may be waived, guidelines for the selection of common required courses cannot handle.

3. Course Credit Waiver
