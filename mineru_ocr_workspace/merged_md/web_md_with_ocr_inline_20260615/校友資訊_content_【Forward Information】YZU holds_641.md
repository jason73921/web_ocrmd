- **文章標題**: 【Forward Information】YZU holds pre-recorded commencement online
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20210625-en
- **文章簡介**: In accordance with the CECC’s anti-pandemic policy, Yuan Ze University held this year’s commencement online by” pre-recorded video” released at 7 pm on June 25.

Chairman Douglas Hsu encouraged the graduates to strengthen three important abilities- Reimagine, Rethink and Reinvent- that help to overcome all difficulties and face the challenges calmly in the future. YZU President Jyh-Yang Wu encouraged students in three points. First, face the problems honestly; make strategies pragmatically, and then implement them reliably. Second, respect others, behave yourself steadily and do things determinedly. Third, be responsible and do not only think to take credit for your work. In addition, President Wu also expected students to keep the habit of lifelong learning and have the courage to chase dreams.

This year, YZU alumni Jackie Cheng, founder of Whoscall, was also invited to encourage graduates with his own experience.
