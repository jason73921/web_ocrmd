- **文章簡介**: As a member of TANet (Taiwan Academic Network) Roaming center, all YZU personnel are allowed to access the wireless network of TANet’s Roaming members by using YZU’s email account.

For example: staff: account@saturn.yzu.edu.tw, student: account@mail.yzu.edu.tw, and password. 

Personnel from TANet Roaming members are also allowed to access YZU’s wireless network with a valid email account in his/her own school.No application is required.

The list of members of TANet Roaming Center can be found in: https://roamingcenter.tanet.edu.tw/
