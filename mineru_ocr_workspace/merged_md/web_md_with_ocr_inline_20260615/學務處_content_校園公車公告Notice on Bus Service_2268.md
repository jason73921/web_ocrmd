Starting from February 22, 2022, buses No.155 and 156 will resume the usual routine on the campus.

 

2.為配合防疫，搭車入校請於下車處掃描QR-CODE，進入各館請配合各館防疫規定。

To cooperate with the epidemic prevention, please scan the QR-CODE at the bus stop when you enter the school by bus, also follow each building’s epidemic prevention regulations when entering the building.
