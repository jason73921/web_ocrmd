Yuan Ze University Counseling and Career Development Section is the primary psychological service facility on campus. The Counseling and Career Development Section is located on the third floor within the Division of Student Affairs building.

The Counseling and Career Development Section provides individual counseling and psychotherapy, group counseling, psychological assessment, crisis intervention, consultation and outreach programs. The Counseling and Career Development Section welcomes students from all backgrounds to come in.
