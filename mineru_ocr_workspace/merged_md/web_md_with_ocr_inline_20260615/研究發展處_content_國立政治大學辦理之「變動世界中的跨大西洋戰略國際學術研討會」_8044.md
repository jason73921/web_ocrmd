- **文章簡介**: 主旨：

檢送本校國際事務學院辦理之「變動世界中的跨大西洋戰略國際學術研討會」議程及海報，請貴單位公告周知，並鼓勵師長同學踴躍報名參加，請查照。

說明：

一、在大國競爭與安全挑戰快速演變的背景下，美國外交政策的轉向正重塑跨大西洋關係，促使歐洲重新思考其全球角色與防務戰略。中國作為系統性挑戰者的崛起，更凸顯民主國家在貿易、科技與價值外交上的協調需求。

二、本次會議匯聚學者與政策專家，探討美國外交政策演變、 歐洲回應及跨大西洋合作前景，特別聚焦與中國的戰略競爭、政策協調與地緣政治影響。同時，會議亦關注新興科技對防務韌性的衝擊，討論人工智慧、無人機與網路能力 如何重塑安全戰略，並探究臺灣在區域防務架構中的角色，共同思考美歐及其夥伴如何應對二十一世紀的不確定性。

三、本會議主辦單位為本校國際事務學院，協辦單位為中華民國外交部、中興大學國際政治研究所、本校國際事務學院國家安全與大陸研究所碩士在職專班、歐盟研究學分學程及研究發展處。

四、檢附會議議程（暫定版，將滾動更新）、海報供參考轉發。

# Conference Program (Tentative)

Transatlantic Strategies in a Changing World: U.S. Foreign Policy, China Challenge, and Defense Resilience

November 25th, 2025

In an era marked by intensifying great-power competition and rapidly evolving security challenges, the United States and its allies face a critical juncture. Shifts in U.S. foreign policy have reshaped the transatlantic relationship, compelling European partners to reassess their own strategies toward global order, defense commitments, and economic resilience. At the same time, the rise of China as a systemic challenger has introduced new layers of complexity to international politics, requiring greater coordination among democracies on trade, technology, and values-based diplomacy.

This conference brings together scholars and policy practitioners to explore the evolving dynamics of U.S. foreign policy, the European response, and the prospects for transatlantic cooperation in addressing shared challenges. Particular attention will be paid to the strategic competition with China, with panels examining policy coordination, technological rivalry, and the geopolitical consequences for allies and partners worldwide.

Beyond the transatlantic dimension, the conference also highlights the importance of defense resilience in an age of emerging technologies. The roundtable will address how AI, drones, and cyber capabilities are reshaping security strategies, with a special focus on Taiwan’s role in the broader defense architecture. By engaging diverse perspectives, the conference aims to foster dialogue on how the United States, the European Union, and their partners can collectively navigate the uncertainties of the 21st century.
