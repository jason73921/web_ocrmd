## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章標題**: 【Sanitary & Health Care Section】The 2024 Influenza Vaccination Program will expand its eligible recipients starting January 1, 2025.
- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: 一、Starting January 1, 2025, the vaccination program will be expanded to include all unvaccinated individuals nationwide aged over 6 months.

        This includes citizens of the Republic of China (Taiwan) as well as foreign nationals holding residence permits.Moreover, a subsidy of NT$100 per session

        will be provided for the administration of vaccinations to all eligible recipients, including students, at contracted medical institutions.

二、Regarding foreign students who have obtained a residence permit but are waiting for their residence certificate, during the expanded vaccination period

       of the 2024 Influenza Vaccination Program, they may use a temporary paper residence certificate or the receipt of application for a residence certificate as

      proof of residence to receive the 2024 publicly funded influenza vaccine.

      However, for those not covered by National Health Insurance (NHI), the administration fee for vaccination should be borne by the institution,

      paid out-of-pocket by the individual, or absorbed by the vaccination facility.
