Announcement of the First-Stage Admission List and Important Notes for the 2026 Academic Year Department Transfer Application

Important Notes:


For first-year students applying for a department transfer whose first choice is within the same academic group, the average academic grades for both the first and second semesters of the current academic year in their original department must each reach at least 60. Otherwise, their eligibility for transfer will be canceled.

Students whose final grades for the second semester of the 2025 Academic Year (2025–2026 Academic Year, Semester 2) do not meet the standards set by each department will have their eligibility for transfer canceled. Further notification will be provided at that time.
