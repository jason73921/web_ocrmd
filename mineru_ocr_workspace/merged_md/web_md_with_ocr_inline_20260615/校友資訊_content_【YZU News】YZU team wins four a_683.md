- **文章簡介**: The student team, Wen-Hao Wang, Yi-Cheng Chen, Yu-Cheng Chen and De Yua Zheng, from Information Management Department of Yuan Ze University participated in 2021 GSS Summer Chatbot Hackathon, and became the only team who won four awards – the first prize, the jury prize, the most popular prize and the utilitarian prize. Another YZU student team, Chih-Ting Lu,Min-Ting Tu, Shih-Ting Chen, Xin-Cheng Lu and Qian-Wen Tseng, also won the second award.
