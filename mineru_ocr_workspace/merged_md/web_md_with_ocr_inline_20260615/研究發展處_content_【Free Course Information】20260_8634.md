Topic: How to Develop a Research Questionnaire?

Speaker: Prof. Li-Ju Chen (Professor, Graduate Institute of Early Intervention, Chang Gung University)

Course Description:


A research tool is the core of any study; it directly determines the accuracy of the research findings. As one of the most common research instruments, the questionnaire plays a vital role in data collection.This course is designed to enhance participants" proficiency in questionnaire development, covering the standard development process and the establishment of reliability and validity. Furthermore, the session will emphasize the importance of maintaining a high standard of research integrity during the development process—a fundamental quality for any scholarly researcher.
