- **文章簡介**: 各位同學，大家好：

112學年校園商家滿意度調查已於112年1月12日圓滿結束，感謝各位的參與，

本次參與問卷調查活動送出100元學餐現金抵用券得主名單依電腦亂數隨機產生，

50位幸運得主名單如下，我們恭喜以下幸運得主!!

學務處宿服組 敬啟 (03)4638800 分機2869



 

 

Dear students,

The 112 Academic Year Campus Merchant Satisfaction Survey concluded successfully on January 12, 112.

We appreciate everyone"s participation in the survey.

The list of 50 lucky winners of the 100 NT Dollar campus dining cash vouchers was randomly generated by the computer. We congratulate the following 50 lucky winners:

Winners should collect their prizes by 5:00 PM on March 15, 2024 from the Student Housing Service Section of Male 1st Dorm.

Thank you!

Student Housing Service Section, Student Affairs Office(03)463-8800 ext. 2869
