🎃 Date: October 27, 2023

🌙 Time: 6:30pm to 9:30pm

🏰 Venue: Global Lounge

🎟 Registration Fee: 150 NTD

👉 Register Link: International Halloween Party

 

We"ve brewed up a cauldron of excitement for you, featuring:

🎮 Ice-breaking Games: Forge new connections with fellow students from around the world through exciting games that will break the ice faster than a chilly Halloween night.

👻 Costume Contest: Don your most creative, spooky, or hilarious costume and compete for the title of Halloween Royalty! Fabulous prizes await the winners!

🗺 Treasure Hunt: Embark on a thrilling treasure hunt that will take you on a journey through the Global Lounge. Solve puzzles, follow clues, and discover hidden treasures.

🎁 Rewards: Did we mention there are rewards? Prizes await the winners of our costume contest and treasure hunt, so come prepared to win big!

 

Ready to join the party? Simply register by paying 150 NTD to the Global Affairs Office from October 9 to October 13. Hurry, as spots are limited, and this event is sure to sell out fast!

Don"t miss this chance to experience Halloween like never before, with a global twist. Put on your spookiest costume, sharpen your treasure-hunting skills, and get ready to have a hauntingly good time with students from all corners of the world.

Share the Halloween spirit with your friends and classmates - the more, the merrier! We look forward to seeing you there for a night of scares and shared international memories.

If you have any questions or need more information, feel free to reach out to us.

Let"s make this Halloween unforgettable!
