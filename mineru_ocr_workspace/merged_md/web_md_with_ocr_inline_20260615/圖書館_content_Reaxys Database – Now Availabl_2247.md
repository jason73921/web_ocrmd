- **文章簡介**: We’re excited to announce that the Reaxys Academic Edition (RAE) is now available again nationwide! You"re welcome to make full use of this powerful tool.

Access Period:
From June 1, 2025 to May 31, 2026

The new RAE is built on the previous Reaxys Basic Edition and now includes two powerful new features:


AI-Based Retrosynthesis –
Draw your own molecule and get a predicted complete synthesis pathway within 10 minutes. It also provides information from literature about similar reactions, including reagents, yields, and reaction details.

Bioactivity Visualization –
Explore biological applications of your compound library, identify potential drug targets, and analyze bioactivity data through easy-to-understand heatmaps, based on literature and patent information.


To protect your intellectual property and ensure database security, RAE uses a dual login method: IP authentication + personal account (must be registered with a Yuan Ze University email).
Please log in at: www.reaxys.com

How to register a personal account:


First-time users:
While on campus or within the YZU’s IP range or YZU SSL VPN, go to www.reaxys.com and click Register. Sign up using your Yuan Ze email address.

Already have a Reaxys account?

If your current account is not linked to a Yuan Ze email, please register again following the first-time user steps.

If your account already uses a Yuan Ze email, you can continue using it directly.
