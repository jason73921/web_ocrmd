說明：

一、 依本校108學年度行事曆，108年10月11日（週五）因雙十節假期調整放假，應於108年10月5日（週六）補課。

二、 108年10月5日（六）當天補課教室安排原則如下：

（一）1081學期原排定於星期五或星期六上課之課程，以原教室上課為原則；

（二）如教室有衝堂者，除系上另行排定外，以星期六之課程優先安排於原教室。當天上課排定之教室，請參閱附件。

  教務處／課務組 敬啟



 

 

Subject: Declaration on the Scheduling of Make-up Classes on OCT. 5th, 2019 (Sat).

1. According to the calendar of academic year 2019-2020, classes on OCT. 11th , 2019 (Fri.) which are adjusted as Double-Tenth Day (bridge holiday) should be made up on 5th, 2019 (Sat).

2.The scheduling of make-up classes on OCT. 5th, 2019 is based on the following principles:


Friday or Saturday classes as per the original schedule of 1st Semester 2019-2020 maintain the original classroom.

When classes are in overlapping time, Saturday classes is top priority on the original classroom unless there is another arrangement by the department. Please refer to the attachment for the classrooms scheduled on that day.


Office of Academic Affairs, Curriculum Section
