# Yuan Ze University College of Medicine and Nuring Specific Rules Governing the Review and Selection of Student Academic Awards and Diverse Skills Awards

December. 11. 2025, Approved by the 4th College Affairs Meeting of the 114th Academic Year

April. 29. 2026, filed for reference at the 4th Student Affairs Meeting of the 114th Academic Year

Article 1 The Yuan Ze University College of Medicine and Healthcare (hereinafter referred to as “the College”) hereby establishes these Regulations in accordance with the “Yuan Ze University Student Reward Regulations” to encourage students of the College to achieve excellence in academic performance and diverse professional skills during their studies.

Article 2 Applicants or nominees for each award category shall meet the following eligibility requirements:

## 1. Academic Gold Award

Students who have received awards ranking within the top three in international academic competitions, received first prize or equivalent distinction in national academic competitions, or whose academic or research achievements have been published in or accepted by renowned domestic or international journals recognized by the College as Class A journals shall be eligible to apply. The same paper may only be used for application once.

## 2. Academic Silver Award

Students who have received awards ranking within the top three in on-campus or off-campus academic competitions, or whose academic or research achievements have been published in or accepted by renowned domestic or international journals recognized by the College as Class B journals shall be eligible to apply. The same paper may only be used for application once.

## 3. Academic Achievement Award

Students who have previously received the Academic Silver Award twice or the Academic Gold Award once, and thereafter again meet the eligibility requirements for the Academic Gold Award, shall be eligible to apply.

## 4. Diverse Skills Gold Award

Students who have passed a national higher-level examination (or an equivalent examination), obtained a Class A national technician certification recognized by the College, or achieved outstanding performance in language proficiency examinations in accordance with the “Yuan Ze University Student Reward Regulations” shall be eligible to apply.

## 5. Diverse Skills Silver Award

Students who have passed a national ordinary examination (or an equivalent examination), obtained a Class B national technician certification recognized by the College, or achieved outstanding performance in language proficiency examinations in accordance with the “Yuan Ze University Student Reward Regulations” shall be eligible to apply.

## 6. Diverse Skills Achievement Award

Students who have previously received the Diverse Skills Silver Award twice or the Diverse Skills Gold Award once, and thereafter again meet the eligibility requirements for the Diverse Skills Gold Award, shall be eligible to apply.

Article 3 Students meeting the eligibility requirements may submit applications on their own behalf or be recommended by their respective departments or graduate institutes. Applicants shall submit relevant supporting documents, including academic or research achievements, copies of papers published in or accepted by renowned domestic or international journals, and certificates or records of awards received in academic competitions, to their affiliated departments or graduate institutes for review and recommendation. Following review and selection by the College, the applications shall be submitted to the Student Affairs Meeting of the Office of Student Affairs for approval and public announcement of the award recipients.

Article 4 Any matters not specified herein shall be handled in accordance with the “Yuan Ze University Student Reward Regulations.”

Article 5 These Regulations shall take effect upon approval by the College Affairs Meeting and filing for reference at the Student Affairs Meeting. The same procedure shall apply to any amendments.

In case of any discrepancy between the Chinese and English versions, the Chinese version shall prevail.
