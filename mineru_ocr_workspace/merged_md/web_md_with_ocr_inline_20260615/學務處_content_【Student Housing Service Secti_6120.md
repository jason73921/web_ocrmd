- **文章簡介**: Date: Starting from 7/17 (Thursday)

Location: Male & Female Dorms Non-Occupancy Beds

 

Starting from 7/17 (Thursday), Student Housing Service will begin cleaning non-occupancy beds in the male and female dorms. Therefore, students who live in rooms with empty beds are requested to clean up their belongings and not occupy the vacant space, including the bed, desk, and the area next to the bed. 

Any items left in these areas will be discarded without any liability.

 

Thank you for your corporation.

Student Housing Service Section
