The Alumni Association of Yuan Ze University (AAYZU) was established on June 13, 1999, with assistance from the Career and Alumni Services Section of the Office of Student Affairs. Its primary mission is to support alumni in achieving personal and professional success, and through their achievements, to promote the development of Yuan Ze University as a model university in Asia.

 

Alumni Association Constitution

To foster alumni connections, provide sustainable services, promote the founding spirit of Yuan Ze University, and assist in the university"s development, the "Yuan Ze University Alumni Association" was established in accordance with the Civil Associations Act and related regulations.
