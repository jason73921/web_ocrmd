- **文章簡介**: Taiwan offers great opportunities for international graduates to begin their careers and enjoy life after school. This guide will help you take the next steps after graduation. You will learn how to stay legally, find a job, and live comfortably in Taiwan. 









  
Learn about the steps to stay in Taiwan legally for work, such as switching from a student ARC to a job-seeking ARC or applying for a work permit.


 
  
Explore official job search platforms and use career counseling services offered by universities or government programs to plan your career path. 


 
  
Get practical information on housing, healthcare, banking, and everyday life to help you adjust smoothly as a foreign professional in Taiwan.
