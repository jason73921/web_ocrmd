- **文章簡介**: June is a month as gorgeous as Phoenix flower blossomed, and it also is a graduation season in Taiwan. Finally, till June 10th, Taiwan is starting to slowly loosen restrictions put in place to prevent the spread of the disease, with no new domestic cases for over 59 days, celebrations have been a mixture of excitement and caution.

On June 13rd, 2020 Yuan Ze University International Graduation Ceremony had organized by Global Affairs Office (GAO) and Global Students Association (GSA) in Building 7. Furthermore, GAO invited Mrs. Zethu Dlamani (as Madam Ambassador of Eswatini), Ms. Lindiwe Kunene (as Acting Ambassador of Eswatini) and Acting Ambassador of St. Lucia attended to the graduation ceremony with best wishes for a happy graduation.
