- **文章簡介**: 🚩 Yuan Ze University Alumni Association 2024.12.13 Taoyuan Aerotropolis Visit

Last week, the Yuan Ze University Alumni Association led a delegation to visit the Taoyuan Aerotropolis Company. General Manager Hong Hsiu-Kuan personally received the group and provided a detailed overview of the planning and vision for Taoyuan Aerotropolis.

The development plan leverages the existing industrial resources of the surrounding areas while integrating Taoyuan"s advantageous industries. The focus is on a "3L1H" industrial model: low pollution, low energy consumption, low water usage, and high added value. The primary target industries for investment attraction include:


Cloud computing

Smart vehicle industries

International logistics and distribution industries

Biotechnology

Aviation support industries

Green energy industries


By linking the airport with surrounding infrastructure, the Aerotropolis aims to advance and elevate industrial development in Taoyuan.

Furthermore, Yuan Ze University is a partner institution of the Taoyuan Aerotropolis through its industry-academia collaboration agreement. The university supports resident companies in recruiting top-tier talent, enhancing their competitiveness, and achieving the ultimate goal of "rooting in Taiwan while aiming at the global market." This partnership exemplifies the long-term objectives of industry-academia cooperation.
