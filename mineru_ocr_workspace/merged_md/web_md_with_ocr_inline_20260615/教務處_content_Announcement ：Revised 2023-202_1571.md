2023-2024 Academic Calendar

Amendment description:

1. The “YZU Open Day” event will be held on April 13, 2024, classes and work will be kept as normal on that day, one day-off for this event is adjusted on July 1, 2024.

2.   “The deadline for submission of grades for all students” is updated from June 30, 2024 to July 1, 2024. 

3.   “Tomb Sweeping Day” holidays ( Children"s Day on April 4, 2024 and Tomb Sweeping Day on April 5, 2024)” are revised to “Children"s Day & Tomb Sweeping Day” holidays (Children"s Day & Tomb Sweeping Day on Apr 4, 2024, extra holiday on Apr 5, 2024.)”. 

Regards,

Office of Academic Affairs
