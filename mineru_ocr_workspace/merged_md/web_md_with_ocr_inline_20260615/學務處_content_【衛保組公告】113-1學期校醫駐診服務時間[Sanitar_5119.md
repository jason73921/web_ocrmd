Dear Faculty, Staff, and Students:


The campus doctor consultation schedule for this semester (113-1) is listed below. If you need medical consultation or services, please visit the Sanitary & Health Care Section on the 1st floor of Male Dorm 2 during the designated hours.

This semester (113-1), we have added a new service on Wednesdays (11:00 AM - 2:00 PM), provided by Professor Lin Pei-Fen from the Department of Nursing, offering health consultations and health education.

The campus doctor consultation schedule is listed below. For any health issues outside of these hours, please visit nearby medical facilities for treatment. Prescription medications must be issued by a doctor during consultation hours and dispensed by a pharmacist. Please note that we cannot provide prescription medications outside of the campus doctor consultation hours. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation!
