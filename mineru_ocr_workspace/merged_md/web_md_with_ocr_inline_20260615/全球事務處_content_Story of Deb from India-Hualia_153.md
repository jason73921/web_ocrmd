- **文章簡介**: During spring break, my friends and I took a trip to Hualien and Yilan. As I like to enjoy exploring nature"s wonder,
- **文章全文**: I very much enjoyed this trip as we traversed through mountains and beaches. The mesmerizing scenic beauty where the sky met the sea through mountains was marvelous. The mountain trails and the road leading up to it were the highlights of the entire journey. If you enjoy being with nature away from the city life along with friends and making lifelong memories, I highly recommended visiting Yilan and Hualien when you are in Taiwan.
