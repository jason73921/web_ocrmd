- **文章簡介**: 價值－Value
元智大學崇尚倫理、尊重多元、追求創新，秉持「誠‧勤‧樸‧慎」之校訓，實踐「卓越‧務實‧宏觀‧圓融」之教育理念。
Yuan Ze University emphasizes ethics, appreciates diversity and pursues innovation. We are committed to the school motto“sincerity, diligence, thrift and prudence,”and strive to achieve the educational ideals of“excellence, thoroughness, vision and harmony.”

使命－Mission
前瞻時代需要，培養學生多元能力，成為社會與產業菁英人才，及終身樂學的世界公民。
Envisioning the demands of the changing world, Yuan Ze University cultivates students into social and professional elites with diversified talents, and into global citizens pursuing life-long learning.
建立學術基礎，致力於應用導向研究，為增進人類文明、福祉與永續發展做出具體貢獻。
Based on a solid academic foundation, Yuan Ze University devotes itself to application-oriented research and makes substantial contributions to the enrichment of human civilization, well-being and sustainability.
建構全校成員得以充分發揮潛能、學習、成長，並引以為傲之優質大學。
Yuan Ze University builds its campus to support all members to fully realize their potentials by encouraging continuous learning and personal growth, and into a prestigious university which all members take great pride in.

願景：20年後的元智－Vision 20: What YZU will be in 20 years
元智大學將成為具國際聲望的雙語大學及應用導向研究型大學，擁有創新、多元、和諧的校園氣氛，提供高品質、國際化的教育。
Yuan Ze University aspires to become a bilingual and application-oriented research university with international reputation. Yuan Ze University will continue to have an innovative, diversified, and harmonious campus atmosphere, and excels in high-quality, internationalized education.

目標與策略－Goals & Strategies

－ 培養學生成為社會與產業菁英人才；
     Talents Cultivation
－ 建構元智大學成為具國際聲望的雙語大學；
     Bilingual University
－ 致力於應用導向研究，引領產業提升與社會進步；
     Application-Oriented Research
－ 建構創新、多元、和諧、永續的優質校園環境。
     Prestigious Campus


根據以上之總目標，「Vision 20」之策略主軸有4，即「優秀人才培育」、「雙語大學建構」、「應用導向研究」及「優質校園文化」。而根據這4項策略主軸，發展出相互關聯的10項策略：
By YZU"s 20th anniversary in 2008, YZU has inaugurated its long-term development plan called "Vision 20," that clearly defines the core value, mission, and vision of the university, and adheres to the four developmental goals of "Talents Cultivation," "Bilingual University," "Application-Oriented Research," and a "Prestigious Campus." We have established 10 main strategies to be realized as action plans (shown as figure below).

－ 建構重視倫理、多元、創新的人才培育特色；
     Cultivation of ethical, diversified, and innovative talents
－ 精進全校雙語教學與學習；
     Enhancement of bilingual teaching and learning
－ 培養學生成為產業菁英人才；
     Cultivation of students for industry"s favor
－ 營造不斷創新、進步的學習型組織；
     Construction of an innovative and progressive learning organization
－ 擴大優秀外籍學生之招收與外籍教師之延攬；
      Expansion of the recruitment of outstanding international students and faculty
－ 深化國際學術交流與合作；
     Consolidation of international exchange and collaboration
－ 建立完善之雙語大學校園環境；
     Construction of a fully bilingual university campus
－ 開展具元智特色之應用導向研究；
     Emphasis on application-oriented research
－ 發展和產業與社會密切結合之研究環境；
     Development of a research environment closely correlated with industry and society
－ 建置人文、永續的校園環境。
    Establishment of a humanistic and sustainable campus

後續將就此10項策略訂定具體行動方案及指標，作為校務發展及檢核之依據，而學校資源與經費亦將配合各行動方案作重點分配，以確實有效達成預定之指標。
According to the 10 strategies, YZU has established main strategies and indices as the basis of university development. The resources and budget will be in collaboration with various action programs focus on allocation in order to reach the predetermined target effectively.
