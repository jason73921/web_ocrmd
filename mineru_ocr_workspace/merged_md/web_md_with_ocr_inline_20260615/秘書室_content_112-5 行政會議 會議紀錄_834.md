## 112-5 行政會議摘要 Executive Summary of Minutes

一、通過本校「112學年度行事曆」修正審議案 The amendment to “YZU calendar for academic year 2023-2024” was approved.

二、通過本校「研究計畫管理費提列標準」條文修正審議案 The amendment to “the YZU provisions regarding the overhead charge for research projects” was approved.

The report of “projects” was ratified and approved.
