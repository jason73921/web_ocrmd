To enhance the understanding of personal data protection regulations and administrative audit practices among private schools, and to assist in comprehending the legal framework and practical operations regarding the collection, use, and notification obligations of personal data, the aforementioned seminar meetings are being organized.

This seminar will consist of two sessions. (1) 2026 Administrative Audit for Personal Data Protection Legal Seminar (1): * Time: Monday, May 18, 2026, from 1:30 PM to 4:30 PM. * Theme: Trends in Personal Data Regulations and Practical Updates.
