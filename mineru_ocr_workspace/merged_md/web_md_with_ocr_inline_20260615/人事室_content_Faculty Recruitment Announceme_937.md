- **文章簡介**: Summary:

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Yuan Ze University, Taiwan, invites applications for tenure-track faculty positions at all academic ranks and in all related areas of computer science.

Candidates should have a Ph.D. degree, a strong commitment to excellent research and teaching, and outstanding research records or potential. The department offers BS/MS/Ph.D. programs and provides an excellent research and teaching environment.

Position: Tenure-track faculty positions at Assistant, Associate, or Full Professor ranks.

Qualifications: Candidates must hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science/Engineering, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, or related disciplines from an accredited institution. Demonstrated excellence or potential in research and teaching is required.

Required Application Documents: Submit the following documents via email or postal mail by August 15, 2025. Applicants are kindly reminded to ensure all required documents are included, as incomplete applications cannot be processed.


Cover Letter

Curriculum Vitae (including education, experience, and expertise)

Photocopy of Doctoral Diploma (authenticated for international degrees)

Photocopy of Academic Transcripts (highest degree)

Photocopy of Teaching Qualification Certificate (if available)

D. Dissertation

Research Publications List (in recent 7 years, including citations and key achievements)

At least two Signed Recommendation Letters (original signatures required; sealed envelopes for postal submissions)

Statement of Research Interests and Teaching Plans (reference courses at https://u.yzu.edu.tw/dwaYNPs9p)

Faculty Qualification Review Form (download: https://u.yzu.edu.tw/9xaRhmZFc)

Applicant Information Form (Word format, download: https://u.yzu.edu.tw/JVByfksnM; email to csdept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw)

Additional Supporting Documents (e.g., awards, patents)

International Degree Verification Form and Entry-Exit Record (for international degrees, download: English https://u.yzu.edu.tw/mbngKSGjq, Chinese https://u.yzu.edu.tw/k0WtbSqZ3)

Certification of International Academic Records (certified by Republic of China embassy/consulate, https://www.boca.gov.tw/cp-203-444-9f06d-2.html )


Contact: Faculty Recruiting Committee, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Yuan Ze University, 135 Yuan-Tung Road, Chung-Li District, 32003, Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
 Email: csdept@saturn.yzu.edu.tw
 Website: https://www.cse.yzu.edu.tw/en
