The holiday season is also smash-and-grab season. Having a device stolen from your vehicle is a headache and can result in a potential security threat. 

Our gift to you is a gentle reminder not to store laptops and other electronic devices in your vehicle. Research shows that even if you conceal your computer in a trunk, grinches use Bluetooth scanners to search for connected phones and laptops.

So here are a few tips to keep your devices safe:


Put away charging cables. 

Shopping? Leave your laptop at home or bring your devices with you. 

Ensure your car is locked.
