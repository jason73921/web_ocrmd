Description:



Our EMI Resource Center (hereinafter referred to as “the Center”) will organize the Advanced Online EMI Teaching Assistant (EMI TA) Training Program during the Spring Semester 2026. This program aims to enhance the competencies of EMI teaching assistants across universities and colleges, and to promote resource sharing and collaborative development through the EMI Resource Center.




Course Schedule: April 20, April 21, and April 24, 2026 13:30–15:30 (2 hours per session)




Course Information: (1) Participants who complete all 6 hours of training will be awarded an Advanced EMI Teaching Assistant Certificate jointly issued by the EMI Resource Center of National Taiwan University of Science and Technology and the Foundation for Scholarly Exchange (FSE). (2) The program will be conducted online via Microsoft Teams. Registered participants will receive the meeting link via email. For more details, please visit the Center’s website: https://ntusttlc.ntust.edu.tw/




Registration Information: (1) Eligibility: Teaching assistants from universities and colleges nationwide (2) Registration Period: Open from now until full capacity is reached. Each session has limited availability. Successful applicants will be notified via email. (3) Registration Link: https://forms.gle/4XyxPpQ8yFP5Brq27




For inquiries, please contact the Center via email: (1) Ms. Chen: chiehanchen@mail.ntust.edu.tw (2) Ms. Hsieh: pilun88@mail.ntust.edu.tw (3) Ms. Huang: tyhuang1026@mail.ntust.edu.tw
