- **文章簡介**: On May 3, 2025, Yuan Ze University Alumni Association, in collaboration with the College of Management and its alumni association, co-hosted a heartfelt and inspiring homecoming event for alumni filled with wisdom and warmth.

The event featured Mr. Chia-Te Wu, a distinguished alumnus from the first graduating class of the Department of Business Administration and a current board member of the Alumni Association. He returned to his alma mater to launch his sixth book, The Philosophy of Happiness in Kindness, and deliver a keynote speech on the same theme.

The event drew a large and enthusiastic crowd of alumni, faculty, and students. Every seat was taken, and the atmosphere was vibrant and lively.

Adding a special touch to the occasion, Mr. Wu invited renowned author Eric (Ai-Rui-Ke) to join as a co-speaker. Together, they shared deep and resonant insights on “Happiness, Kindness, and Life Choices,” sparking thoughtful reflections and emotional connections among the audience.

In his speech, Mr. Wu emphasized:


“Helping others is a habit. Acts of kindness, like planting a tree without intention, will eventually grow and offer shade, returning blessings to your life.”



He encouraged everyone to become “a person with stories,” connecting with others through kindness and passion, enriching their own lives while illuminating the journeys of those around them.

Mr. Wu also quoted the renowned literary scholar Ch’i Pang-Yuan:


“I’m only 80 years old—there’s still my own life to live.”This served as a reminder that at every stage of life, we should strive to live with depth and breadth.



Author Eric then shared his thoughts on “inner achievement.”He highlighted that true happiness does not come from external accomplishments, but rather from living a life that feels meaningful to oneself. Whether it’s accompanying others, living out personal values, or offering sincere help, every ordinary choice is a significant step toward genuine happiness.

We sincerely thank Mr. Chia-Te Wu and author Eric for their powerful and heartfelt sharing. This warm, thought-provoking, and inspiring afternoon became a truly enriching learning experience for all who attended. The event not only deepened alumni bonds but also prompted everyone to reflect on the essence of happiness and the direction of their lives.

Looking ahead, the Yuan Ze University Alumni Association will continue to create meaningful platforms for connection, allowing stories and inspiration to flow between alumni and students, leaving a lasting legacy.
