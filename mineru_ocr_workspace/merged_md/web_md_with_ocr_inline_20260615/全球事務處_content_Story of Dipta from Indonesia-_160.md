- **文章簡介**: Exploring Taichung was another story that I choose as part of my journey here in Taiwan. Like other cities, I went there only for food and scenic view.
- **文章全文**: Once reaching Taichung the journey began from Gao Mei Wetlands, the infamous spot for its sunset and birdwatching. Make sure to go there when the weather is sunny, that way you can walk around up to the river’s mouth. It was really nice to walk and enjoy sitting around the banks to watch the sunset behind the windmills. Next, you must definitely try the cuisine in Taichung. You can head towards Feng Chia Night Market to try some of the delicacies, but this time I chose to go to 好旺飯包 and try one of their specialties 辣子雞 (Spicy Chicken). To close this short journey, I then went to Miyahara and grabbed some of the famous ice creams. Taichung is one of those places to go to release your stress and get back on track once again. I suggest you all travel with your friends or acquaintance. And lastly, do visit the City Centre as well, as there are beautiful buildings and scenic city life

Dipta Divakara Pius Purwadaria，Department of Information Communication
