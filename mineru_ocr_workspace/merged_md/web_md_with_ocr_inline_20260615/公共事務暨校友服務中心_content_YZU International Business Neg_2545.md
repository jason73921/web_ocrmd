- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/admin/pr/index.php/tw/20180119
- **文章簡介**: YZU International Business Negotiations course designed a special AR/VR auction on Jan. 13. They presented Grand Garden with ten artworks created by students of Art and Design Department; and implemented the theory & skill of commercial negotiation in the AR/VR auction. In the meantime, YZU signed MOU with Lenovo Global Technology (Taiwan), which will sponsor several VGA & RAM about NTD 2,000,000. They will also jointly organize AI application seminar this April.

Assistant Professor Yu Ching-Ying (尤淨纓) said the students who major in this course are junior and senior. Because they don’t have working experiences yet, they mock roles in different background and practice the skill after checking personality trait in the early semester. The course provides students the theory of distributive /single-text/multilateral negotiations in the beginning and then designs the situation of Grand Garden for them to practice negotiation skills.
