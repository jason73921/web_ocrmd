- **文章簡介**: YZU Departments of Art and Design hold graduation show called ‘Chaos, Stay awake” in Songshan Cultural and Creative Park during June 1st to 4th. There are 44 artworks categorized by painting, product design, architecture space, art video and installation art. Creating artworks is like to go on the road trip; the artists, under the pressure of unknown situation and desolate mind, have to remind themselves to persevere with works.

The Chairperson Kuan-hua Chen said that the Departments of Art and Design is well known by its multiple learning extending to interior designer, product design, painting, video art and　interactive installment. Even though grouping into art and design, students study and practice more extensively in four years. Their graduation works reflect the knowledge of aesthetics and materials, as well as, the wealth of experience and skills.
