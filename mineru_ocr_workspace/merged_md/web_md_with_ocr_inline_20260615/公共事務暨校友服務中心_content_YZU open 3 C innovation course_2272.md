- **文章簡介**: To adapt to the trend for the future industry and the need of multi-disciplinary, Yuan Ze University offered the courses with design thinking to inspire students’ creation. YZU opened several 3C courses this semester, including the course of creative thinking by Prof. Chin-Mei Chou, from Department of Industrial Engineering and Management; practice session for 3C by Prof. Chor-Kheng Lim of Department of Art and Design; and entrepreneurship practice by Prof. Ching-Ying Yu and Prof. Tseng, Yung-Ching of College of Management.

Fang Mei Tseng, Dean of Office of Academic Affairs, said that YZU would like to develop students to live and learn in creative thinking; and cultivate them to have ability of solving problem. Each of 3C courses takes one credit, such like design thinking, fundamental digital makers and start-up practice courses taught by different colleges.
