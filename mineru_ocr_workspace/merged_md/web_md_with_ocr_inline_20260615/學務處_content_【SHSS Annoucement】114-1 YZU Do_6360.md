- **文章簡介**: The most heartwarming dorm tradition, YZU Dorm Love & Charity Night, is coming soon!
This annual event celebrates friendship, love, and gratitude — sharing warmth through bread andkindness.
Part of the proceeds will be donated to World Vision Taiwan’s Child Sponsorship Program,spreading love beyond our campus.

■ Date & Time: November 20 (Thu) · 18:30–21:30
■ Venue: Gymnasium, Yuan Ze University

Event Highlights:

• Limited-edition bread tasting & sales
• Message card exchange
• Club performances & fun activities
• Lucky draws and a limited-edition cup sleeve (for pre-orders over NT$150)

Bread Flavors:

• Truffle Chocolate — Cocoa Truffle Dream 
• Chocolate QQ Balls(three pieces)– Don’t Go Away, My Sweet Little Ball! QQ Is My Heartbeat. 
• Butter Rosong – Can’t Stop Sticking to You, My Butter Love. 
• Golden Cheddar Toast – Say “Chedda” Yes to Forever!
• Cheese Soft Roll – Truly, I Fell for You Long Ago.
• Matcha Red Bean – A Love I Can’t Erase.

Message Card Drop-off

Time: Nov 4 – Nov 11
Location: Dorm 1 & Dorm 2 Front Desk
Pre-order over NT$150 to receive a limited commemorative cup sleeve (Only 250 available — first come, first served!)


Tasting & Pre-order Information


Tasting Period: November 4 (Tue.) - November 6 (Thu.)
Tasting Hours: 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM / 4:30 PM - 6:30 PM
Venue: Family Mart, Infront Building 2, Dorm 3 Lexue Food Court (Infront Building 2 canceled on rainy days)
Pre-order Period: November 4 (Tue.) - November 10 (Mon.)
Pre-order Location: Dorm 3 Lexue Food Court
Student Pre-order Form: https://reurl.cc/Vmayo5


Payment:


Pay at the bread tasting booth during 11/4 ~ 11/6
Pay at the Dorm 3 Lexue Food Court on 11/7, 11/10
Payment Method: Cash only(Orders unpaid by the deadline will be canceled. Souvenir distributed by payment order.)


Bread Pickup


• Self-pickup: Nov 19, 16:30–19:00 @ Gymnasium
• Event day: Nov 20 at bread booth
• Dorm delivery: Nov 18–19, 21:00–23:00 (delivered by floor leaders)


For every bread purchased, NT$5 will be donated to World.
Exclusive Link for Professors and Staff: https://forms.gle/1bWV1Ufr49czfN7dA
We warmly invite everyone in the YZU community to join this meaningful event and share the joy of love and giving.


Warm regards,
YZU Student Dormitory Association
