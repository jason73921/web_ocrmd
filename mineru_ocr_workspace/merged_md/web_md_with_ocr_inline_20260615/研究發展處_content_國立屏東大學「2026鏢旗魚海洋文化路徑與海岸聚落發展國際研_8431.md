## 一、研討會宗旨

「聯合國海洋科學促進永續發展十年（2021–2030）」 指出，要真正達成永續的海洋管理，科學必須走入社會，並以文化、教育與知識共享等多元面向共同推動。其中，特別提出文化連結、傳播、教育及知識系統整合四項核心行動，作為支持永續海洋的重要基礎。本次研討會將回應前述四項精神，研擬以「文化路徑連結的鏢旗魚知識系統」為主軸，期望打造跨國、跨學科、跨族群的交流平台，串連多元觀點並促進海洋知識協作。藉此回應聯合國海洋十年的行動目標，進一步強化社會對海洋永續的共同理解與推動基礎。

本研討會以鏢旗魚文化（Billfish Harpooning Culture）為核心，徵求相關學術研究與實務經驗分享之論文，徵稿主題說明如下：

## 鏢旗魚文化資產 Billfish Harpooning Cultural Heritage

著重於鏢旗魚文化資產的歷史脈絡、文化意涵與世代傳承。鏢旗魚不僅是一種傳統捕撈技術，更承載著沿海漁村的生活智慧與海洋信仰。透過探討其發展歷程與傳承制度之文化，深入理解人與海洋之間的互動關係。在現代社會變遷下，透過鏢旗魚文化資產的保存、記錄與活化方式，促進文化價值的延續。

## 旗魚生態與海岸聚落發展 Billfish Ecology and Coastal Settlement Development

以旗魚生態與海洋環境之關係，探討海岸聚落在漁業發展中的形成與變遷。透過分析漁村產業、地方創生與社群發展，呈現人與海洋資源互動的多元面貌。透過漁業資源管理、環境變遷對旗魚族群與漁業活動的影響，進一步思考鏢旗魚文化在生態保育與永續利用之間的平衡與未來發展。

## 鏢旗魚文化觀光與文化路徑 Promotion and Cultural Route Planning

聚焦於鏢旗魚文化觀光的推廣與文化路徑規劃，探討如何透過文化傳播、教育推廣與海洋文化旅遊，促進地方文化的保存與發展。藉由規劃文化觀光路線、設計文化體驗與文創活動，串聯海岸聚落與漁村產業，展現鏢旗魚文化的歷史價值與生活意涵。同時關注無形文化資產的活用、社區營造與地方創生，期望在文化保存與觀光發展之間建立兼具教育與永續的文化實踐。

## 一、研究背景

文化創意產業為國家重點發展方向，本系即為全台第一所成立的專門學系，具有引領台灣文創的地位與使命。本系均有優良的師資以及設備，均衡發展，師生屢獲國家級獎項，即為明證。本系成立於 1995 年，原系名為「台灣文化產業經營學系」，為全國第一個以文化創意產業領域為名之新設科系。

## 二、 研究動機

2017 年與2022 年分別成立「文化資產保存與活用中心」與「地方創生與城鄉治理中心」，中心創立旨為促進專業跨域與產學合作，關注於有形與無形文化資產保存再利用與地方創生議題。此外，本系教師運用專業，將產業實務資源帶入教學現場，讓學生就學期間，即可利用課程與產學計畫的參與學習到實務經驗。

The United Nations Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (2021– 2030) emphasizes that achieving sustainable ocean governance requires scientific knowledge to engage actively with society through culture, education, and knowledge sharing. ## 2. Conference Themes and Topics

## Theme 1

## Billfish Harpooning Cultural Heritage

This theme focuses on the historical context, cultural significance, and intergenerational transmission of billfish harpooning culture.

Billfish harpooning is not merely a traditional fishing technique; it also embodies the life wisdom, maritime beliefs, and cultural traditions of coastal fishing communities. By examining its historical development and systems of knowledge transmission, scholars can gain deeper insights into the interaction between humans and the marine environment.

In the context of modern social change, the preservation, documentation, and revitalization of billfish harpooning culture can help sustain its cultural values and ensure its continuity for future generations.

## Theme 2

## Billfish Ecology and Coastal Settlement Development

This theme explores the relationship between billfish ecology and marine coastal communities, as well as the formation and transformation of coastal settlements associated with fisheries development.

Through the study of fishing village industries, regional revitalization, and community development, this theme aims to reveal the diverse interactions between people and marine resources.

It also addresses fisheries resource management and the impacts of environmental change on billfish populations and fishing activities, encouraging discussion on the balance between ecological conservation and sustainable utilization of billfish resources.

## Theme 3

## Billfish Cultural Tourism and Cultural Route Planning

This theme focuses on the promotion of billfish cultural tourism and the planning of cultural routes.

It explores how cultural communication, educational initiatives, and marine cultural tourism can contribute to the preservation and development of local culture.

Through the planning of cultural tourism routes, the design of cultural experiences, and the development of creative cultural activities, the conference seeks to connect coasta communities and fishing industries while highlighting the historical and cultural significance of billfish harpooning culture.

This theme also addresses the utilization of intangible cultural heritage, community development, and regional revitalization, aiming to establish sustainable cultural practices that balance cultural preservation with tourism development.
