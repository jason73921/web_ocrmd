- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20211223-2
- **文章簡介**: The Chinese Business Ethics Education Association has held ethics competitions for 11 years and the theme this year is “Environmental Sustainability”. The final of competition, held in Yuan Ze University, selected 27 teams from 195 teams in 16 schools with total of 961 students.

The top three finalists in the groups of Chinese case analysis, English case analysis and microfilm group were selected after the fiercely competitive contest. Yuan Ze University, Taiwan University of Science and Technology and Southern Taiwan University of Science and Technology have the impressive achievements in the contest.
