Subject: Notification of qualified list of students applying for Minor/Double Major in 1131 semester.

說明:

一、113-2學期生效。

二、113-2學期選修學分數亦將一併增加6學分。本次輔系申請20人，雙主修8人，共28人。

Content:

1. Effective in semester 113-2

2. The elective credits for the 113-2 semester will also be increased by 6 credits. This time, 20 students applied for a minor, and 8 students applied for a double major, for a total of 28 students.
