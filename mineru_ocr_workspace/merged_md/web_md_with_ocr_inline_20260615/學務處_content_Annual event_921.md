Yuan Ze University Counseling and Career Development Section is the primary psychological service facility on campus. The Counseling and Career Development Section is located on the third floor within the Division of Student Affairs building. The Counseling and Career Development Section is open Mondays to Thursdays from 9:00 AM to 8:20 PM, and Fridays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Except for emergencies, all counseling services are available by appointment only. The Counseling and Career Development Section provides individual counseling and psychotherapy, group counseling, psychological assessment, crisis intervention, consultation and outreach programs. Although there are no specific limits on the number of counseling sessions available to individual students, our goal is to provide quality services in as short a time as possible. The Counseling and Career Development Section welcomes students from all backgrounds to come in.
