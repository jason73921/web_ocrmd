- **文章全文**: Holi Festival

The Holi Festival, also known as the Festival of Colors, is a joyful and exuberant celebration in India. 
During the festival, people throw colored powder and water at each other as an expression of joy and friendship. 
Holi symbolizes the victory of good over evil and the arrival of spring"s bountiful harvests. 
It is an feast that celebrates bonds of love and promotes togetherness through fun and vibrant revelry. 
People gather for Holi to dance, laugh, feast and set aside their differences in a display of harmony and affection.

The colorful powder used during Holi is called "gulal" and comes in a rainbow of bright and dazzling hues. 
As people run through the streets, they smear and drench one another with gulal until everyone is covered from head to toe!
It is an energetic celebration that brings people together.

International Food Festival

Various countries from around the world participated in the International Food Festival, including Vietnam, Thailand, 
Indonesia, India, Malaysia, Hong Kong, the Philippines, Japan, Pakistan, and Kyrgyzstan...etc. 
Students dressed in traditional attires of different countries and cooked hometown delicacies.

Through tasting foods from around the world and chatting face-to-face with international students, our students not only delighted their taste buds 
but also broadened global perspectives, deepening cross-cultural understanding and communication. 
This truly reflects Yuan Ze University’s goals of promoting campus internationalization and cultivating students’ global mobility.



Link

 

International Christmas Party

In the warmest month of the year, International Christmas party were held to invite Students and their companions to participate. 
While celebrating Christmas, students enjoyed music performances. Students from different countries dressed up and sang and danced enthusiastically on the dance floor, 
fully showcasing the festive atmosphere. Our students not only spent a joyful Christmas date full of belonging, 
but also deepened cultural exchanges and friendships. Every student who participated gained an unforgettable holiday memory.
