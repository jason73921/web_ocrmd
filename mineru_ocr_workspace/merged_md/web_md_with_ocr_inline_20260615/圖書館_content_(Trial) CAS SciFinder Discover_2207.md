- **文章簡介**: Platform Name: CAS SciFinder
Trial Period: From now until July 31, 2025

Overview:
Applicable research fields: Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, Biomedical Sciences, Biotechnology, Pharmaceuticals, Petrochemicals, Materials Science, Polymers, Cosmetics, Optoelectronics, and Agricultural Science.

The SciFinder Discovery Platform includes 3 integrated search tools:


CAS SciFinder® – Login URL: https://scifinder-n.cas.org/

Search scientific literature, substances, and reactions

Create and analyze retrosynthesis plans

View GHS hazard information




CAS Formulus® – Login URL: https://formulus.cas.org

Access formulations and procedures from journals and patents

Use Formulation Designer to develop safe and effective products




CAS Analytical Methods™ – Login URL: http://www.methodsnow.com/

Search detailed analytical methods, regulations, and suppliers

Utilize tools to quickly find applicable experimental methods





Usage Method: Cloud-based access. Users can log in using their personal account credentials within the authorized IP range or via YZU SSL VPN. Accessible via browser with no concurrent user limit.
