- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: The website, titled "Academic and Career Advancement – Career in Taoyuan" (URL: https://ycep-taoyuan.com.tw/), consolidates information on internship opportunities and industry collaborations across various departments of universities and colleges in Taoyuan City (Internship Info: https://reurl.cc/Omb1rR). It aims to help students understand industry trends and strengthen the connection between academic pursuits and career development.

The website covers internship collaboration details from 13 universities and colleges in Taoyuan, spanning industries such as semiconductors, smart manufacturing, information technology, AI applications, tourism and hospitality, and healthcare. It also promotes career exploration through diverse activities to enhance students’ willingness to participate in internships. Key promotional activities include:


Lucky Draw Event: Students who upload internship transcripts or relevant proof from the academic years 2024 or 2025 are eligible to enter a lucky draw, with total prizes valued at NT$500,000.

"AI Practical Workshop" Training Course: Career-oriented AI courses are offered to guide youth in mastering AI concepts and practical skills, enhancing their competitiveness in the job market.

"Light of Return – AI Youth Creativity Challenge": A competition based on course outcomes that encourages students to return to school and share their internship experiences, boosting career confidence and enthusiasm among peers.
