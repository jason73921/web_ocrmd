因考量非住宿生對衣物清洗的民生需求，

宿舍服務組已特別邀請洗烘衣機廠商上洋產業股份有限公司於女一舍地下室(亮美影印部外)新增一洗烘衣機專區，

歡迎有需要的人至洗烘衣機專區使用哦~~~

 

Announcement date: March 9, 2023 (Thursday)

Considering the needs of non-resident students for laundry cleaning, Student Housing Service has specially invited Shangyang Industrial Co.Ltd., a manufacturer of washing and drying machines, to add a special area for washing and drying machines in the basement of the first female dormitory (outside the Liangmei photocopying store) to serve all faculties and students.
Moreover, the cost of our laundry service is very affordable than other self-service laundry outside !

Welcome everyone to come and experience together~~~
