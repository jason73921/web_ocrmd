- **文章簡介**: In line with the university’s paperless policy, starting from the 2025 academic year (Academic Year 114), all work-study labor contracts will be processed and signed online.

Work-study students must review and complete the online signing of their labor contract before their first sign-in each month via the work-study system. Only after completing this process will students be allowed to begin their work.

If you have any questions, please contact the Life Guidance Section of the Office of Student Affairs.

Thank you for your cooperation. — Life Guidance Section
