# 網站文章資料_Transportation_70

## 一、文章基本資料

- **文章ID**: 70
- **文章標題**: Transportation
- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/index.php/tw/transportation
- **文章簡介**: ◎ By Train 

Southbound → Neili Station
Take a local train directly to Neili Station. Alternatively, take an express train to Zhongli or Taoyuan Station, then transfer to a local train or bus.

Northbound → Neili Station
From north of Hsinchu, take a local train directly to Neili Station. Alternatively, take an express train to Zhongli or Taoyuan Station, then transfer to a local train or bus.

From Neili Station to YZU
Exit the station and walk north (to the right) for about 5 minutes to the intersection of Zhonghua Road and Yuandong Road. Follow the YZU sign, turn right across the railway crossing, and continue along the right side for about 10 minutes to reach the campus.

Fare Reference:

☆Taipei → Neili (local train): NT$76
★Hsinchu → Neili (local train): NT$94

◎ By Bus 

Bus 155, 156 (Taoyuan Bus)
From Zhongli Station, take Bus 155 or 156 directly to Yuan Ze University (inside campus).

Bus 1 (Taoyuan Bus)
From Zhongli or Taoyuan Station, take Bus 1. Get off at “Yuan Ze University” stop (Zhongli → Taoyuan direction) or “Neili Railway Station” stop (Taoyuan → Zhongli direction). Walk along Zhonghua Road toward Taoyuan, turn right at the intersection with Yuandong Road, cross the railway crossing, and continue walking about 10 minutes to campus.

Bus 208 (United Highway Bus)
From THSR Taoyuan Station, take Bus 208 and get off at “Yuan Ze University” stop.

◎ By Car 

Northbound → Zhongli Interchange
On the freeway, after passing the Zhongli/Xinwu Interchange, keep right and take the Zhongli Interchange exit (58 km).

Southbound → Zhongli Interchange 
On the freeway, after passing Zhongli Service Area (around 54 km), keep right and take the Zhongli Interchange exit (56 km).

From Zhongli Interchange to YZU
After exiting, go straight about 3.6 km, connect to Provincial Highway No.1 (Zhonghua Road), and turn left toward Taoyuan. Continue about 2 km to Neili Station. About 500m further, turn right at the intersection of Zhonghua Road and Yuandong Road, cross the railway crossing, continue about 500m, and YZU will be on the left.

From Neili Interchange to YZU
Southbound at 56 km or northbound at 57 km, exit at Neili Interchange. Take the main road toward Taoyuan, pass Neili Station, then follow the YZU sign to turn right.

From South Taoyuan Interchange to YZU
Exit at South Taoyuan Interchange. Head south via Wenzhong Road or Longshou Street, or north via Guoji Road, then connect to the main highway. Follow road signs: turn right toward Zhongli, then left to reach YZU.
