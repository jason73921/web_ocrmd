說明：

一、 依本校111學年度行事曆修正，112年4月3日（一）民族掃墓節調整放假，於112年3月25日（六）補課。

二、 112年3月25日（六）當天補課教室安排原則如下：

（一）1112學期原排定於星期一或星期六上課之課程，以原教室上課為原則；

（二）如教室有衝堂者，除系上另行排定外，以星期六之課程優先安排於原教室；

（三）如教室因其他單位於修正行事曆公告前借用以辦理活動且無法取消而衝突者，課務組將協助安排其他教室；

（四）當天上課排定之教室，請參閱下表。

教務處／課務組 敬啟

【Announcement by Office of Academic Affairs】

 

Subject: Declaration on the Scheduling of Make-up Classes on March 25, 2023 (Sat.)


According to the calendar of academic year 2022-2023, classes on April 3, 2023(Mon.) which are adjusted as Tomb Sweeping holiday should be made up on March 25, 2023 (Sat.).


The scheduling of make-up classes on March 25, 2023 (Sat.) is based on the following principles:

Classes on Monday or Saturday in the 2023 Spring Semester will, in principle, be held in the original classroom.

In case of overlap, except for the classroom arranged by the department, the classes on Saturday will be given priority in the original classroom.

If there is a conflict in the classroom due to other units borrowing for activities before the revised calendar announcement and cannot be canceled, the Curriculum Section will assist in arranging other classrooms.

Please refer to the table below for the classrooms scheduled on the day.
