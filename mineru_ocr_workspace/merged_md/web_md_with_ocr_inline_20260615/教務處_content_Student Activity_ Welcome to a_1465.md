【112-1Theme - based Study Group】

The new semester has started, and we invite students to join the Theme-based Study Group. You can freely choose a topic of interest, such as the 50 classics that Yuan Ze students must-read, or original novels, psychology, literature , and more. Through the book club, you can team up with your classmates to arrange a reading plan, cultivate teamwork spirit, and further develop a reading habit. If you like reading, grab this opportunity. This activity provides book purchase subsidies, a reading reflection contest, and a prize of up to 🏆NT$12,000! The application is open now, so hurry up and form a team with your classmates. Let’s read together. 🙋‍♀️🙋‍♂️📚
