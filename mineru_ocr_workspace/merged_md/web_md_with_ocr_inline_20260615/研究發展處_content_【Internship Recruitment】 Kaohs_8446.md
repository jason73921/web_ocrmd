- **文章簡介**: The Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts (KMFA) is now accepting applications for its 2026 Summer and Autumn Internship Programs. Please review the following schedules and requirements carefully before applying.

 

I. Internship Schedule (Attendance Mandatory)

Applicants who cannot commit to the full duration of the internship should not apply.



Summer Internship:



Period: June 30 (Tue) – August 28 (Fri), 2026.




Working Days: Monday to Friday OR Tuesday to Saturday.







Autumn Internship:



Period: June 30 (Tue) – November 27 (Fri), 2026.




Working Days: Monday to Friday OR Tuesday to Saturday.







Important Note on Hours: The daily internship consists of 8 hours. According to the KMFA Internship Regulations, an official Internship Certificate will NOT be issued if the total internship hours are less than 200 hours, or if total leave (including personal leave, sick leave, and unexcused absences) exceeds 64 hours.

 

II. Application Timeline



Deadline: All applications must be submitted or postmarked by April 30, 2026.




Submission: Mail or hand-deliver your application to the "Education and Public Services Department" at KMFA. Please mark "Internship Application Materials" on the envelope.




Result Notification: Admitted candidates will be announced on the KMFA official website (www.kmfa.gov.tw) by June 1, 2026.




 

III. Eligibility and Required Documents

Applicants must possess basic computer skills and follow the KMFA Internship Regulations. The following documents are required:



Internship Application Form (one copy).




Official School Letter (properly formatted from the university/department) OR Two Letters of Recommendation.




Autobiography (including reasons for applying; format is flexible).




Internship Proposal (including goals, projects, methods, and duration).




 

IV. Parking Information

The KMFA surface parking lot on Meishu East 2nd Road is a paid facility. Interns who choose to park cars here are responsible for the parking fees. For detailed regulations, please visit: https://reurl.cc/DKzQge.
