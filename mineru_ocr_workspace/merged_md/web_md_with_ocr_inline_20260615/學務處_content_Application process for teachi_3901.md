Before the end of each semester (by May 31st for the first semester and by November 3Oth for the second semester), submit the "Club Mentor Teaching Record," "Mentor Receipt for Teaching," and "Club Account Information" documenting the approved number of subsidized sessions for that semester to the Extracurricular Activities Section for case closure.
