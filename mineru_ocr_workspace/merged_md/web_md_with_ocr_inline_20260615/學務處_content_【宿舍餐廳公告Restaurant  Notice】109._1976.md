## 餐廳公告

學校對於校內餐廳、廚房、員生消費合作社之飲食負有安全衛生維護及管理之責·因此學校基於風險管理·校園禁止校內餐廳使用含瘦肉精之食材·並貫徹校園學餐食材政策如下：

1.餐廳優先採用國產食材。

2.所供應之肉類及其他相關產製品食材皆符合可溯源食材政策且不得檢出乙型受體素（瘦肉精）。

3.商家須於販售區張貼公告肉類及其他相關產製品之食材來源產地·並保證或切結宣示不使用含有瘦肉精之生鮮肉品證明。

4.商家於販售區張貼餐廳的衛生管理流程並落實食材管理。

學務處宿舍服務組、衛生保健組敬啟學生事務處宿舍服務組StudentHousingServiceSection

## Restaurant Announcement

The school is responsible for the safety and health maintenance and management of the restaurant. Therefore, based on risk, school prohibits on-campus restaurants from using of containing ractopamine.

1. Restaurant prefers to use domestically produced ingredients.

2.  The meat and other related products are in compliance with the traceable food policy and no beta receptor (ractopamine) is allowed to b detected

3. Restaurant must post an announcement of the origin of the meat and other related products, and guarantee not to use fresh meat containing ractopamine.

4. Restaurant post the hygiene management process and implement food management.
