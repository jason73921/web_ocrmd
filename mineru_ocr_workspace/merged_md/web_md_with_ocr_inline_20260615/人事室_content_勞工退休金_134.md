- **文章簡介**: 因應教育部來文指示：私校退撫新制自99年1月1日起實施，本校專任工友不適用私校退撫新制，回歸勞基法。

In response to the Ministry of Education’s directive: The new pension system for private school faculty and staff has been implemented since January 1, 2010. However, full-time custodial staff at our school are not subject to this new system. Instead, they are covered under the Labor Standards Act.
