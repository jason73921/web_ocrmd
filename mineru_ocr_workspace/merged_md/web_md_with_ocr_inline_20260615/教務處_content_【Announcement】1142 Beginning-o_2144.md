 To: 12:00 PM, Wednesday, Apr. 1, 2026

🧭【How to Access the Survey】

Path: Personal Portal → New Course Survey → My Learning Survey

🎁【Priority Course Selection for 1151 Semester】

Complete both the:


End-of-semester survey for 1141 semester

Beginning-of-semester survey for 1142 semester


You’ll have a chance to gain priority course selection for the 1151 semester!

Applicable to: Elective courses only 
 Reserved quotas for priority selection:


Courses with ≤ 35 students: 5 quotas

Courses with 36–70 students: 10 quotas

Courses with ≥ 71 students: 15 quotas


⚠️【Important Notes】


Please be mindful of the survey period and complete it on time through the Portal.

Before the survey begins, any courses you have applied to withdraw from but are still pending instructor approval must also be included in the survey!

The survey is anonymous. Please answer honestly, but do not include inappropriate remarks that could violate the law.


Thank you!
