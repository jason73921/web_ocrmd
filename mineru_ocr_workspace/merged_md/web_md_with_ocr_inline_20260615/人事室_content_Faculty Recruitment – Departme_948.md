Position Title: Tenure-track or non-tenure-track Assistant Professor or above

Number of Openings: 2

Anticipated Start Date: February 1, 2026


Note: For applicants who are unable to obtain a valid work permit or Employment Gold Card within the required timeframe, the appointment will be deferred to August 1, 2026.


Qualifications:


Applicants must hold a Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Semiconductors, Green Energy, Optomechatronic Systems, Optical Information, or a closely related field from a domestic or international institution. Candidates must demonstrate:


Strong research performance

A passion for teaching and academic service

Team spirit and excellent interpersonal skills

The ability to teach in English



Areas of Specialization:



Semiconductors and Green Energy:
 Research expertise in nanophotonics, semiconductor materials and devices, microelectronics engineering, biomedical optoelectronics, , and other semiconductor related fields.

Optomechatronic Systems and Optical Information:
 Specializations in system-on-chip and optoelectronic applications for IoT, silicon photonic integrated circuits and packaging, LiDAR, optical imaging and detection, digital image processing, AI-based image recognition, and IoT-related technologies.

 📅 Application Deadline: September 30, 2025

Curriculum Vitae and Autobiography
 Include details of academic background, teaching and research experience (at the university level or higher), and areas of expertise.

Representative Publications (Past 5 Years)

Full-text PDFs of journal papers

List of publications with sources

Summary of research experience and major academic achievements




Two Recommendation Letters

One letter from the Ph.D. advisor

Letters need to be sent directly by referees via email




Proposed Courses and Future Research Plan
