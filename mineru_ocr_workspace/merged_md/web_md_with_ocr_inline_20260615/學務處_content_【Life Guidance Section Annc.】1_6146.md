Details:


Application Period: 2024/09/08 to 2024/09/26 Students should apply through their department or relevant unit by filling out the application form and attaching their transcript or supporting documents. Applications will be recommended by the evaluation units.


Important Notes:


Gold and Silver Awards: Applications should be based on achievements completed during the 113th academic year (2024/08/11 to 2025/07/31). Students graduating early may submit achievements completed before the application deadline in accordance with the Student Award Regulations. The same achievement may only be used to apply for either the Gold or Silver Award—not both. If multiple achievements are submitted for the same type of award, only one award will be granted.

Achievement Award: Graduating students may apply based on achievements accumulated throughout their academic years.

Award Distribution: Award funds are allocated based on a fixed ratio of the annual budget: 60% for Gold Awards and 40% for Achievement Awards. Prizes for each category will be evenly distributed among recipients, subject to the maximum limit per award. Any remaining funds may be reallocated as needed.

Disbursement of Gold Awards: Gold Awards granted in the 114-1 semester will be combined with those from the 114-2 semester and Achievement Awards, and all will be distributed at the end of the 114-2 semester.

Application Guidelines: Each paper or competition project may only be submitted once for award consideration. For co-authored publications or group competitions, one representative should apply on behalf of the team and indicate individual contributions. A consent form titled "Academic Award Application: Consent Form for Co-authored Publications / Group Competitions" must be attached.

Certificates and Prize Money: One original certificate will be issued. All co-authors or group members will have their names listed on the certificate. If additional certificates are needed, they can be copied at one’s own expense. Prize money will be granted to the applicant, who is responsible for distributing it among team members based on contribution.
