The Yuan Ze University Alumni Association will host a special lecture on June 28th. As humans, our lives are sustained by the food we eat, and, inevitably, we occasionally fall ill, which can be a persistent concern. Choosing the right doctor, asking the right questions, and receiving the correct treatment is crucial. To address these concerns, we have invited a neurosurgeon from the Taipei Hospital under the Ministry of Health and Welfare to provide valuable insights.

Additionally, Israel has been frequently featured in international mainstream media lately. Despite being smaller in population and land area than Taiwan and surrounded by powerful nations, Israel continues to lead globally in fields such as technology and finance. To shed light on this, we have invited the Dean of the Industrial Academy at Yuan Ze University, an expert on Israel, to unveil the mysteries of this remarkable country.
