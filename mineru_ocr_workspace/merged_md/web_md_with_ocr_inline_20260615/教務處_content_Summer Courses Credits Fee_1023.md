- **文章簡介**: Payment Standard: (According to the Tuition standard announced by General Accounting Office)

A. Summer Courses: Fees charge standard accords to the summer course credit fees standard posted in the accordingly academic year.

a. Undergraduate Program: College of Engineering & College of Informatics & College of Electrical and Communication Engineering NT$ 1510/per credit; College of Management NT$1400/per credit; College of Humanities and Social Sciences NT$1390/per credit.

b. Graduate Program: College of Engineering & College of Informatics & College of Electrical and Communication Engineering NT$1610/per credit; College of Management NT$1510/per credit; College of Humanities and Social Sciences NT$1500/per credit.

B. Prerequisite Courses: Credit fees standard for Two-year Undergraduate Program and Pro-longed study students accords to tuition standard (2026).   The credit fees of Prerequisite Courses will be charged in the following semester (1151 Semester).

C. Credit fees standard of Cross-Department Course Selection Program accords to Credit Fee Standards which is approved by Ministry of Education in the Letter Tai-(90)-Kao-4- No.90037114.
