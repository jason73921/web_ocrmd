Undergraduate Student Dorm and Graduate Dorm

Located in a spot with beautiful views and fresh air, the dormitory is equipped with an access control system to provide students with a safe environment. There are a desk, a chair, a wardrobe, and an internet port for each student in the spacious dorm rooms. However, the internet curfew starts from 12:00 am to 6:00 am every day. In the communal area in the dorm, one can find a student lounge, a fridge and a microwave oven available for students. The shower room is connected to the balcony where coin operated laundry machines and a free-of-charge spin dryer are available, and clothes can be sun-dried. To reduce carbon footprint, the central heating boiler will operate to provide hot water for shower from 6:00 pm to 12:00 am only. Hot water is still provided by electric heaters. 

 

 

1. Male Dormitory #1 (Including male graduate dorm rooms)

The seven-story building with a basement is equipped with a lift. There are 240 4-bed rooms, which are able to accommodate 960 students in total. There are also accessible rooms for students with disabilities.

 

 

2. Female Dormitory #1 (Including female graduate dorm rooms)

The nine-story building with a basement story is equipped with a lift. There are 183 4-bed rooms, which are able to accommodate 732 students in total. There are four rooms on the first floor specifically designed for students with disabilities.
