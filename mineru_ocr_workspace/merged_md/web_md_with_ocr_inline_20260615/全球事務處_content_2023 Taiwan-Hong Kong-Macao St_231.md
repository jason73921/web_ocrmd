To promote cultural understanding and facilitate mutual exchange among students from Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao, Mainland Affairs Council has entrusted the Youth and Children Caring Association of Kinmen County to organize the event.

The event will take place in two sessions: from August 13th to 18th and from August 20th to 25th, 2023.

Students from Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao who are interested in participating are encouraged to sign up and actively take part in this event.

## 活動介紹
