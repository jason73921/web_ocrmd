Four Core Strategic Fields 



 Still wondering if your current major truly aligns with your future passions? Break free with the Yuan Ze University (YZU) Bachelor’s Degree Program! Join a learning revolution centered on student autonomy. 

 If you have the ambition to cross boundaries, you can navigate seamlessly between AI Technology, the Semiconductor Industry, Innovation & Entrepreneurship, or ESG Sustainability. 

 YZU Bachelor’s Degree: Transitioning you from a passive learner to the active architect of your own career blueprint. 




 

Curriculum Framework 



 *After selecting your specialized track (Choose one: AI, Semiconductors, Innovation & Entrepreneurship, or Sustainability):


Electives: A minimum of 10 credits required.

Concentration Credits: A minimum of 20 credits must be completed within your selected field.
