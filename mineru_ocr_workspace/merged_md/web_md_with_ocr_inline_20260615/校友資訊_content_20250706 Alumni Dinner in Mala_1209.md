Alumni dinner in Johor Bahru, Malaysia (July 6, 2025)





Alumni dinner in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia (July 12, 2025)






During a recent visit to Kuala Lumpur and Johor Bahru for an education fair, Maureen Seo and Dorrie Zhang from Yuan Ze University’s Global Affairs Office hosted two alumni gatherings in Malaysia. A lunch was held in Johor Bahru, followed by a dinner in Kuala Lumpur. These events provided valuable opportunities to reconnect with local alumni, share university updates, and strengthen the bond between YZU and its graduates in the region.

We extend our sincere thanks to all Malaysian alumni who joined us and continue to support the YZU community worldwide.
