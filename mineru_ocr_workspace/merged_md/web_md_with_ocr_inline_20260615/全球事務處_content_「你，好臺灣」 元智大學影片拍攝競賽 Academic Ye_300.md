## Academic Year 112 Students Video Contest Competition Proposal

Goal: This video project aims to provide a unique and authentic perspective on Taiwan through the eyes of students who have lived here. ## Application and Submission of Video Entries:

## Step 1: Submit the online application form 112 AY YZU Students Video Contest Competition.

Step 2: Shot a video. The video should be between 3 and 5 minutes in length. Must comply with the Regulations Governing the Classification of Motion Pictures and Trailers and the Use of advertisements and promotional materials for a motion picture and copyright and privacy provisions of this contest.

Step 3: Please upload your video to GAO Google Drive by January 24, 2024. GAO will then upload the video to their YouTube channel before March 19, 2024.

Step 4: Upload the video to GAO Google Drive and wait 3-5 days for GAO qualification review. Video entries that passed the qualification review will be directly shown on GAO’s YouTube (YZU Global Affairs Office).

i. After GAO upload your video, click SHARE and hashtag 「# YZU Students Video Contest & #YZUGAO」

ii. Make your video available to the public and share it on Facebook and tag the GAO fan page @YZU Global Affairs Office.

iii. Please share your videos on Instagram and tag @yzugao

iv. Share with your friends and family to vote for you.

v. Voting was limited to one vote per Google account.

## Qualification Review:

➢ All the participants (including the people who show up in the video ) should submit the Authorization Letter and Portrait Consent Statement in Google Drive.

➢ GAO will review the video entries by Regulations Governing the Classification of Motion Pictures and Trailers and the use of advertisements and promotional materials for a motion picture and video storyline. Video entries found to be inappropriate for public viewing, defamatory towards YZU, or discriminatory towards others will be disqualified.

➢ Video entries that passed qualification review will be shown on the GAO YouTube channel.

➢ This adversely affects the reputation of the YZU or any of its faculty, students, and partners.

➢ Depict hatred, defame, abuse, harass, stalk, threaten a specific person or community in the society or incite violence or conflict, or otherwise violate the legal rights (such as rights of privacy and publicity) of others.

➢ The disqualified materials or information contain infringing, obscene, indecent, or unlawful material or information

➢ You will be disqualified without submitting the video title, concept and caption.

➢ Disqualified entries will be deleted directly without notification.
