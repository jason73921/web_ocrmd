一、 Subject: List of 1121-Semester Unqualified Prerequisite Students.

二、Statement：

（一） See the list of unqualified prerequisite students who are not allowed to take the course referred and should drop it at the Stage III online course sign-up period.

（二）If  errors occurred on the list referred, individual students should contact the Curriculum Section, Office of Academic Affairs, and provide relevant evidence by 2023/09/12 (Tue.) 12:00 p.m.

（三） The course(s) mentioned below will be dropped by the Office of Academic Affairs after the deadline.

　　　2023/09/04（Mon.）：The first day is only open to the senior students and the prolonged graduation students among the undergraduate program and graduate students.
