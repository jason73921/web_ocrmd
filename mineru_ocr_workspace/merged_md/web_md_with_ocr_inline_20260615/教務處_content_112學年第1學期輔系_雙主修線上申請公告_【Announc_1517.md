                                      112學年第1學期輔系/雙主修線上申請公告

 


  申請時間：自112.11.20起至112.12.01止。

  申請路徑: 元智首頁→個人portal→學習檔案→輔系/雙主修申請。

  申請經核定通過者，須依各學系112學年度應修科目表修課。

  相關申請事項請參閱：元智首頁→學校行政業務→教務處→學生專區→雙主修/輔系相關規定。

                                      【Announcement】apply for the minor /double major in 1121semester

 


  Deadline for application：Nov. 20th ~Dec.1 st,2023

  Online Application : YZU homepage → Portal →Study File →Minor  /Double Major Application


  Students who have been accepted as Minor / Double Major should take the courses on the planlist of Minor /Double Major    required by the department in 112 academic year.



  For further relevant statements of application, please check the website and follow the path：
