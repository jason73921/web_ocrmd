Description:



To support universities in promoting EMI courses, dispel common misconceptions about EMI, and address the challenges encountered by instructors in EMI teaching, our EMI Resource Center will organize the Inter-University EMI Lecture Series. Guided by the principles of resource sharing and collaborative learning, this initiative aims to enhance both the willingness to teach EMI courses and their overall effectiveness across institutions.




The lecture series will be held in northern and central regions of Taiwan. Please refer to the attached file for the agenda of each session.




Registration Information: (1) Eligibility: Faculty and staff from universities and colleges nationwide (individual online registration required) (2) Registration: Open from now on. Please refer to the attachment for registration links for each session.
