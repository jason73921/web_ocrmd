- **文章網址**: https://www.yzu.edu.tw/alumni/index.php/tw/20190601
- **文章簡介**: Yuan Ze University held 2019 Commencement on June 1st which carried the theme “Dream High” reflecting every graduate as like a paper plane with positive energy. Taoyuan Mayor Cheng Wen-Tsan came to deliver a remark and Dr. Huang-Qian Liang, who is General Manager of Wiltrom Medical and also the graduate in 1998, shared his entrepreneurial experience in the ceremony.

Mayor Cheng Wen-Tsan encouraged graduates that graduation is a new beginning and don’t give up easily when meeting difficulties; keep on pursuing challenges and better goal when being successful.

President of Trustee Board Douglas Hsu congratulated all the graduates and hope them to appreciate parents’ support and assistance. He reminded graduates to catch up with the times but meanwhile they should adjust themselves and look for the changes continually. Otherwise they will be washed out cause the world changes too quickly. Chairman Hsu also suggested graduates to choose a good company which industry can keeps up with the world pace.
