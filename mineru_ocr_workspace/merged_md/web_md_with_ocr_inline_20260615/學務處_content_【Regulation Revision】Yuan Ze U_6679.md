Article 1 To award scholarships to outstanding undergraduate and graduate students, these review regulations are established in accordance with Article 6 of the "Yuan Ze University Academic Scholarship Establishment Methods" (hereinafter referred to as the Establishment Methods).

## Article 2 The application and disbursement procedures are as follows:

1. Students who meet the eligibility criteria set out in Article 2 of the Establishment Methods should submit their application forms and relevant academic records within two weeks after the start of the academic year, following the announcements made by their respective departments or equivalent units. This scholarship is applied for once per academic year and is disbursed in two installments, one each semester.

2. Students who meet the eligibility criteria set out in Articles 3 and 4 of the Establishment Methods should submit an application form each semester during their first to third academic years.

3. Students who meet the eligibility criteria outlined in Article 5 of the Establishment Methods should apply according to the regulations.

4. If an applicant withdraws from the university during the academic year, any disbursed funds will be reclaimed, and their eligibility for this scholarship is forfeited without objection. Students who take a leave of absence may retain their eligibility for up to one year upon approval by the Scholarship Review Committee.

## Article 3 The review standards are as follows:

1. Students who meet the first criterion of Article 2 in the Establishment Methods will be reviewed based on the original copy of their college entrance examination

results by their departments, which will also submit a photocopy for reevaluation by the Scholarship Review Committee.

2. For students meeting the second and third criteria of Article 2 in the Establishment Methods, proof of scholarship eligibility is based on photocopies of graduation certificates from their respective schools.

3. For students who meet the fourth criterion of Article 2 in the Establishment Methods, proof of eligibility for this scholarship shall be based on the original employment certificate issued for that semester.

4. For second- to fourth-year students applying under the first criterion of Article 2 in the Establishment Methods, the review principles shall follow those set out in the first criterion of Article 5.

rticle 4 Each college completes the preliminary review according to the above regulations, compiles a list detailing the amounts to be disbursed, and submits it to the Student Affairs Office for consolidation. After reevaluation by the Scholarship Review Committee and approval by the Dean of Student Affairs, the Accounting Office will disburse the funds.

Article 5 Students who are entitled to the benefits specified in the first criterion of Article 3 of the Establishment Methods are limited to six semesters.

Article 6 These regulations shall be implemented following approval by the Scholarship Review Committee, and the same applies to amendments.

In case of any discrepancy between the Chinese and English version, the Chinese version shall prevail.
