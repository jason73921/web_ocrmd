- **文章網址**: 
- **文章簡介**: According to the Implementation Measures for Service Learning Education Article 9, the credit deduction method of students in Yuan Ze. 


* Those who have studied other university Service Learning related courses more than 1 credit can apply for credit deduction. 

* For 0 credit courses, need to follow the original school course content and actual hours, applying for the deduction course or activity hours, up to 18 hours. Service Learning courses include the same subject name and course content, or the subject name is different but the course content is the same.


Prepare the following two documents and send them to the Extracurricular Activity Section (R8301) for credit deduction procedures.


(1) Download the credit application form from the website of the Student Affairs Office and fill in the credit deduction information.

(2) Attached a copy of the original school Service Learning transcript.


Application time: Proposed within two weeks at the beginning of each semester (if there is a regular holiday, it will be postponed by 1 day).
