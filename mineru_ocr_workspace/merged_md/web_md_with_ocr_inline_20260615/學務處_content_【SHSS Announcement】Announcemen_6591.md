International students are not required to pay the dormitory deposit again. The previously paid deposit will be retained.

Bed assignments will follow the current arrangement. Any adjustments will be announced separately by SHSS if necessary.

Dormitory fees for undergraduate students do not include winter and summer breaks. If accommodation is required during these periods, please apply according to the relevant announcements.

Dormitory fees for the next semester will be adjusted. Please refer to the related announcement (Dormitory Fee Adjustment for AY 115) for details.

Attch1. Dormitory Renewal Results for International Students (Fall Semester, AY 115)


續住申請通過取得住宿資格名單請參閱附件一。（附件一連結）

境外生無需重複繳納住宿保證金，將沿用先前已繳之保證金。

床位將依現行安排辦理，如有調整需求，宿服組將另行通知。

大學部住宿費不包含寒暑假期間，如有寒暑假住宿需求，請務必依公告另行申請。

下學期住宿費將有所調整，詳情請參閱相關公告（115學年度宿舍費用調整）。
